Michael Joseph Jackson (August 29, 1958 – June 25, 2009) daa nyɛla Amerika yiliyiinda, yila sasabira, wa' waribiiŋa ka lahi nyɛ ŋun sɔŋdi pam. ka sokam daa mi o  "King of Pop", o nyɛla sokam ni saɣi ti so diyi kana yila polo zaŋ gbaai 20th century. Yuum-pihinahi shɛli din gari la, o tɔhibu zaŋ chaŋ yila, waa, ni o biɛhigu zaŋ chaŋ salo chɛmi ka dunia zaa baŋ o ni nyɛ so. Jackson daa nyɛla ŋun chɛ ka niriba pam zaɣa labina yila puuni. ŋuni n-nyɛ yiliyiindi so ŋun deei pina nyaŋ yiliyiindiba zaa dunia.
