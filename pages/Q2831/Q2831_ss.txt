Michael Joseph Jackson (watalwa ngo 29 ÍNgci 1958 eGary, eIndiana, eMelika – 25 ÍNhlaba 2009 eLos Angeles, eCalifornia, eMelika), lodvume ngaMichael Jackson, bekangumculi we-Pop yaseMelika. Watiwa nge-"The King of Pop".
Umculo wakhe uvame kuhlukaniswa nge-Pop, i-R&B, iSoul, iRock kanye ne-Disco.
