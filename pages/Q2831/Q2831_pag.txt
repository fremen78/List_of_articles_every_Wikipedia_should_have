Si Michael Joseph Jackson (Augusto 29, 1958 - Hunyo 25, 2009) et sakey ya Amerikanon managkansion, songwriter, managsayaw, tan pilantropo. Sikatoy kabkabat bilang "King of Pop", tan sikatoy sakey ed saray sankamanunaan ya karakter ed kultura nen koma-20 a siglo. Legan na apatiran dekada ya impanbilay to, sikatoy nagmaliw a sankamundoan a persona diad popular a kultura lapud saray kontribusyon to ed musika, sayaw, tan fashion, pati say personal ya bilay to. Si Jackson so angimpluensyaan ed saray artista ed dakel a genre na musika; panamegley na saray stage tan video performance, pinalapagan to iray komplikadon sayaw a kagagawa a singa say moonwalk, a sikato so angiter na ngaran, ontan met ed robot.
