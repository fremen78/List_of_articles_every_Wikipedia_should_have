Tang Dynasty (Chinese: 唐朝; Pinyin: Táng Cháo) (18 June 618–4 June 907) China ke ek imperial dynasty rahaa jon ki Sui Dynasty ke baad me rahaa aur iske baad Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms Period rahaa. Iske Li (李) palwaar suruu karis rahaa, jon ki Sui Empire ke khalaas hoe ke baad power me aae rahin. Ii dynasty ke kuchh din talak Zhou Dynasty (周) (16 October 690–3 March 705) bagal kar diis rahaa jab Empress Wu Zetian raj karis.
