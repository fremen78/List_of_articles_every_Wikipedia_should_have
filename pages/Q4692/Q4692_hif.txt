The Renaissance, Europe ke itihaas me uu time hae jon ki 1400 me suruubhais rahaa aur baad ke Medieval period, jiske Middle Ages ke naam se bhi jaana jaawe hae. 
"Renaissance" sabd French bhasa se aais hae aur iske matlab "rebirth" hae.
