Ta lhune çheet er jough lajer ta lhiasit liorish imlagh as braghey starkaghyyn, ta çheet ass grineyn–ass oarn vraghee son y chooid smoo, agh ta lhuneyn lhiasit ass curnagh, grine buigh as reise dy mennick myrgeddin. Ta blass lus y lhionney currit ayns y chooid smoo dy lhuneyn, as ta'n blass shen cur sharrooid rish y lhune, as ec y traa cheddin, ta'n lus shen gobbraghey myr freaylleyder najooragh. Dy cadjin, ta lhuneyn rang-oardit ayns daa horçh–y lhune freillagh, as y feer-lhune ta imlit dy h-ardjynagh er lheh. Ta'n sorçh shen dy lhuneyn rang-oardit ayns sorçhyn elley lheid as y lhune buigh, y lhune doo as y lhune dhone.
