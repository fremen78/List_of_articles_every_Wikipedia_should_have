Rũm-yãansã rɩta b rɩɩbo, wa rũm-rũm-dãmb a taabã. B sã n wa rɩtẽ, b rɩt-a lame n pa wãbd-a ye. B sã n wa rɩt rɩɩbã, b basd-b lame tɩ b lebg n wa, tɩ b sẽn boond-b masã tɩ zõosã wã, b sãbgd-b ne zõosã, n wãbd-b tɩ b lebg bõn-buudu. Rẽ poore, b leb n rɩt-a lame, tɩ bõn-vɩɩs a taab sẽn be yĩn-gãongẽ wã leb n wãbd-a. Bõn-kãens n kɩtd tɩ zuloɛɛzã la ko-loɛɛs a taabã lebg zũud sẽn pa tõe n yɩll n lebge, tɩ rũmsã rɩkd-b n dɩt-a. Bõn-vɩɩs nins sẽn be yĩn-gãong pʋgẽ wã leb n maand-a-la ne protein sẽn pa proteɛn ye. B sã n wa lebg n yaa toor-toore, bõn-vɩɩs nins sẽn kʋʋlã kiidame, tɩ b selil-dãmbã ket n be rɩtgã pʋgẽ. Rẽ poore, b sã n wa rɩt-b ne b yĩnsã, b tõe n dɩ-b lame. Woto kɩtame tɩ rũmsã tõe n dɩ tɩɩs la tɩɩs a taaba.
Rõgbã yaa bõn-vɩɩs sẽn tar bẽed sẽn yaa kãsenga, sẽn yaa b sẽn mi bũmbu, la b yaa b sẽn tar bũmbu.
B boondga pʋg-paal sẽn bɩ tɩ na-kõos la b boond-b tɩ namse.
B zãada rũng n dɩgd n dɩgd namse (sẽn yaa namse bɩ namse, bɩ b ges rũng sẽn yaa namse), ne piim (sẽn yaamse) la ne peels sẽn yaa b namse.
La sã n yaa ne rũmsã, b tõe n dɩka b tɩt n naan tɩ b naan yɩ bur bɩ tɩ b naan naan yɩ burã.
