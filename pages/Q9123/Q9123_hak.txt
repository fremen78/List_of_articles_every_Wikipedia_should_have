Thi 1 thoi Nelson ke Rutherford Nàm-chiok Ernest Rutherford (Yîn-ngî: Ernest Rutherford, 1st Baron Rutherford of Nelson, 1871-ngièn 8-ngie̍t 30-ngit – 1937-ngièn 10-ngie̍t 19-ngit) he New Zealand vu̍t-lí-ho̍k-kâ, ha̍k-vu̍t-lí chṳ̂ fu. Kî thûng-ko fat-hien lâu kié-sṳt kîm-phok sṳ̍t-ngiam chûng ke Rutherford san-sa, fat-hien ngièn-chṳ́ ke chang thien-hò chhiòn si̍p-chûng chhai khi̍t-séu ke ngièn-chṳ́-ha̍k, fat-chán chhut Rutherford ngièn-chṳ́ mù-hìn. 1908 ngièn, kî tet-tó Nobel Fa-ho̍k Chióng.
