Asif n Kungu neɣ Asif n Zair, d wasif n Tafriqt Talemmast, d wasif wis xemsa i ɣezzif akk deg umaḍal ed wasif wis sin i ɣezzif akk deg Tafriqt (akked ahat 4,700 km), deffir Nnil.
Aɣbalu-s seg unẓul-usamar n Tagduda Tamegdayt n Kungu, yessuneɣ-d yiwen n uḥric seg tillist-is akked Kungu, ed i yettawi ɣer Agaraw anaṭlas.
