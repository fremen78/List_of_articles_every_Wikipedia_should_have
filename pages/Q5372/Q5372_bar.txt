Basketboi (dt.: Basketball) is a Monnschoftsspoat, wo af an rechteckadn Fejd gspuid wead. As Zui is an Boi in an Koab z weafa, dea wo af jeda Schmoiseitn van Fejd af on Breddl 3 Meta hoch montiad is. Baketboi zejhd za de beliabdasdn Spoatoatn vo da Wejd. Es gibd uma 450 Milliona Leit, de wo Basketboi spuin.
Basketboi ghead zu de wening Boispoatoatn, de wo vo am oanzlnan Menschn eafundn worn is. Des Spui is anno 1891 van kanadischn Trainer James Naismith ois Hoinspoat entwicked worn.
