Basketbaw or basketballs is a team sport pleyed batween teu teams o' five pleyers that try tae score pynts agin ane anither bi pittin a baw throu a net that is 3m heich unner certaint rules. Basketbaw is ane o the maist popular sports in the warld. Pynts are scored bi pittin the baw throu the net an this is wirth different nummers o pynts dependin on certain factors. The baw is fordert bi dribblin (booncin' it) or passin it batween teammates. Pheesical contact isna alloud an thare is rules on hou the baw is haundled.
