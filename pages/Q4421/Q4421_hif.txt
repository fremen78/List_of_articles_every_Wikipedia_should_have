Jangal ek jagha hae jahaan pe ghanaa (dense) perr] ke samudae (community) hae. United Nations ke Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) jangal ke ii rakam se define kare hae" "Jamiin jon ki 0,5 hectares se jaada hae aur jisme perr 5 netres se jaada hae aur jisme perr ke canopy 10 percent se jaada area pe chhaae hae. Isme uu jamiin nai hae jon ki jaada kar ke kisani khaatir kaam me laawa jaae hae, nai to jahaan pe urban area hae"" 2020 me dunia me 4.06 billion hectares (10.0 billion acres; 40.6 million square kilometres; 15.7 million square miles) ke jangal hae. Iske matlab hae ki dunia ke 31% land area jangal hae.
