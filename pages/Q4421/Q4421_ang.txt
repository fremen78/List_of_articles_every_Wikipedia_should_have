Weald oþþe wudu is stow þe hæfþ manige beamas. Nean an teoða dæl þære eorðan is weald (þe is 30 hundteontigiðena dæla þære eorðan þæs landes). Wealda sind hefige on manigum landum to wicum manigra wihta, wæterhringemnettende, grundhealdendum, and sind swiðe heifge on þære lifendebyrdnesse. Þeah þe hit is gewunelice gesmead þæt treow sind þa anlican mearca wealda, þær sind eac maniga oðra plantan þa sind hefiga on wealdum, and eac deor, and timberweorc.
