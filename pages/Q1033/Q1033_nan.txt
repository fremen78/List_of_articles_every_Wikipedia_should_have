Nigeria Liân-pang Kiōng-hô-Kok (Eng-gí: Federal Republic of Nigeria) tiàm-tī Hui-chiu sai-pō͘, sī Hui-chiu jîn-kháu siāng chē ê kok-ka. Keh-piah ū Benin (sai), Chad (tang), Cameroon (tang), Niger (pak). Lâm ū Guinea-oan.
Nigeria chit-ê miâ kap keh-piah kok Niger kāng Latin gí-goân, chí tong-tē ê Niger-hô.
