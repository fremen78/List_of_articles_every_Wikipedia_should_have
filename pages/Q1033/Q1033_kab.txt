Nijirya (isem-is unṣib s teglizit: Federal Republic of Nigeria), d yiwet n tmurt deg Tefriqt n Utaram, d tamurt tameqqrant deg Tefriqt s wugar n 204 imelyan n yimezdaɣ. Tezzin-as-d Niger deg ugafa, Benin deg utaram, Chad d Kamirun deg usammer, d Afrag n Guinée deg unẓul. Tamaneɣt n Nigeria d Abuja, tamdint-is tameqqrant d Lagos. Timura i yettwaxedmen deg tmurt n Nigeria d naira. Aselway n tmurt n Nigeria d Muhammadu Buhari.
Nijirya tesεa tadamsa ijehden akk deg tefriqt. petrol yettɛawan mliḥ i bac ad teɣli Nijiriya ɣer tdamsa-ya. Tamurt n Nijiria deg-s kraḍ n yigduden imezwura: Awṛuppa, Igubu d Hausa
