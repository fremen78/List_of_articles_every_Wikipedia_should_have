Inorganic chemistry, rasayan vigyan ke ek hissa hae jon element aur inorganic conpound ke adhyan (study) kare hae. Inorganic chemistry ke ii bola jaae sake hae ki ii uu chij ke adhyan hae jon ki organic chemistry me nai hae. Organic chemistry me khaali carbon ke compound ke adhyan karaa jaae hae, tab inorganic chemistry me uu compound ke adhyan karaa jaae hae jon ki carbon copound nai hae.
