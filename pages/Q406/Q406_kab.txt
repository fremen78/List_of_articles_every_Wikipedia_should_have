Taɣṛemt n Isṭanbul Sṭambul d taɣṛemt  tis snat n Tmurt n Tuṛkya . Zik tella tamanaɣt ines.
Tesɛa 13 imelyan n imezdaɣ (akken i d-tenna TUIK g useggʷas n 2007) 
Tesɛa yun uḍar deg Turuft, wayed deg Asya, ulac taɣṛemt nniḍen deg umaḍal amm nettat yebḍan ɣef sin xas azgen i meqren yezga-d g Asya, maca Isṭanbul tecba mlih tiɣeṛmin n Turuft

= Tarakalt =
Tamdint n Sṭambul tezgad gar Asia d Turuft ygezmits g'ulemmas ilel n Marmara d ilel Aberkan abrid b'w aman Bosfor yessiweḍ garasen, tamdint n Sṭambul tmoqer aṭas tura g yal snat timiwin n'u Bosfor tswadlent s tamdint n Sṭambul.
