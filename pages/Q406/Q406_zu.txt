Istanbul' (/ˌɪstænˈbʊl/ Template:Respell,   /[unsupported input]ˈɪstænbʊl/ Template:Repell; I-Turkish: İstanbul [isˈtanbuɫ] ( listen)), eyayaziwa ngokuthi [[[Constantinople]], ingu-idolobha elikhulu kunawo wonke Turkey kanye nesikhungo sezwe sezomnotho, amasiko kanye nomlando. Leli dolobha linqamula i-Bosporus istrait, futhi likhona kokubili [[[Europe]]] kanye [[[E-Asia]], nezakhamuzi ezingaphezu kwezigidi ezingu-15, ezihlanganisa u-19% wabantu baseTurkey.< ref name="Population of Turkey"/> I-Istanbul idolobha elinabantu abaningi eYurophu, kanye nezwe idolobha leshumi nanhlanu ngobukhulu.
Yasungulwa njenge-Byzantion ngamakoloni Megarian ngekhulu lesi-7 BCE, futhi yaqanjwa kabusha ngu-Constantine Omkhulu kuqala ngokuthi IRoma Entsha ( I-Nova Roma) phakathi nokunikezelwa ngokusemthethweni kwedolobha njengenhloko-dolobha entsha WamaRoma ngo-330 CE, i asheshe wayishintsha yaba iConstantinople (Constantinopolis ), idolobha lakhula ngobukhulu nomthelela, laba isibani se-Silk Road kanye nelinye lamadolobha abaluleke kakhulu emlandweni. Yasebenza njengenhloko-dolobha yombuso cishe amakhulu eminyaka ayishumi nesithupha, phakathi Roman/Byzantine (330-1204), Latin (1204-1261), [[Byzantine]. Umbuso ongaphansi kobukhosi be-Palaiologos|Byzantine]] (1261–1453), kanye Ottoman (1453–1922) imibuso. Idolobha laba neqhaza elikhulu ekuthuthukisweni kobuKristu ngezikhathi zamaRoma nezaseByzantium, lasingatha ezine (kuhlanganise ne-Chalcedon (Kadıköy) ohlangothini lwase-Asia) [[kuqala] imikhandlu yenkolo eyisikhombisa]] (yonke eyayiseTurkey yanamuhla), ngaphambi kokuguqulelwa kwayo ekubeni inqaba yamaSulumane elandela Ukuwa kweConstantinople ngo-1453 CE, ikakhulukazi ngemva kokuba ikomkhulu [I-Ottoman Caliphate]] ngo-1517. Ngo-1923, ngemva Impi Yokuzimela YaseTurkey , I-Ankara yathatha indawo yedolobha njengenhloko-dolobha yezwe elalisanda kwakhiwa Republic of Turkey. Ngo-1930, igama laleli dolobha lashintshwa ngokusemthethweni laba yi-Istanbul, igama elithi Greek elalisetshenziswa kusukela ngekhulunyaka leshumi nanye.

Igama lokuqala elaziwayo ledolobha lithi Byzantium (Greek: Βυζάντιον}, Byzántion), igama elanikezwa kulo ekusekelweni kwalo ngu-[[Megara|Megarian] ] amakoloni cishe ngo-657 BCE. Amakholoni aseMegaran athi umugqa oqondile obuyela emuva abasunguli bedolobha, i-Byzas, indodana kankulunkulu u-Poseidon kanye ne-nymph Ceroëssa. idolobha elibalekayo. IConstantinople isuka egameni lesiLatini elithi Constantinus, elisuselwa egameni uConstantine Omkhulu, umbusi waseRoma owasungula kabusha leli dolobha ngo-324. CE. I-Constantinople yahlala iyigama elivame kakhulu ledolobha eliseNtshonalanga kwaze kwaba ngawo-1930, lapho iziphathimandla zaseTurkey ziqala ukucindezela ukusetshenziswa kwe-"Istanbul" ezilimini zakwamanye amazwe. Kostantiniyye (Ottoman Turkish: قسطنطينيه), Be Makam-e Qonstantiniyyah al-Mahmiyyah (okusho "Indawo Evikelwe yaseConstantinople") kanye nethi İstanbul kwakungamagama asetshenziswa ngenye indlela. ngama-Ottoman ngesikhathi sokubusa kwawo.
Igama elithi İstanbul (Ukuphinyiselwa kwesiTurkey: [isˈtanbuɫ] ( listen), colloquially Ukuphinyiselwa kwesiTurkey: [ɯsˈtambuɫ]) livamise ukubanjwa ngokuthi lisuselwa ku IsiGreece SaseNkathi Ephakathi ibinzana "εἰς τὴν Πόλιν" (liphinyiselwa Ukuphinyiselwa kwesiGreki: [is tim ˈbolin]}), okusho ukuthi "edolobheni" futhi yindlela iConstantinople eyayibhekiselwa kuyo amaGreki endawo. Lokhu kubonise isimo salo njengedolobha elikhulu kuphela endaweni. Ukubaluleka kweConstantinople emhlabeni wase-Ottoman kuphinde kwaboniswa isiteketiso sayo sase-Ottoman esithi Der Saadet okusho "Isango Eliya Empumelelweni" ngesi-Ottoman Turkish. Okunye ukubuka ukuthi igama livele ngqo lisuka ku- igama elithi Constantinople, elinohlamvu lokuqala nelesithathu liyehliswa. Eminye imithombo ye-Ottoman yekhulu le-17, njengokuthi Evliya Çelebi, ilichaza njengegama elivamile lesiTurkey isikhathi; phakathi kwekhulu le-17 nasekupheleni kwekhulu le-18, yayisetshenziswa futhi ngokusemthethweni. Ukusetshenziswa kokuqala kwegama elithi Islambol emalini kwenzeka ngo-1730 phakathi nokubusa kukaSultan Mahmud I. Ngesimanje Turkish, igama libhalwe ngokuthi İstanbul, elinechashazi elithi İ, njengoba izinhlamvu zezinhlamvu zesi-Turkish ihlukanisa phakathi kwe-dotted kanye ne-dotless I. . NgesiNgisi ukucindezeleka kuselungeni lokuqala noma lokugcina, kodwa ngesi-Turkish kuselungwini lwesibili (tan). Umuntu ovela edolobheni uyi-'İstanbullu (ubuningi: İstanbullular); I-Istanbulite isetshenziswa ngesiNgisi.
