Karachi, Pakistan ke sab se barraa city, National City aur Sindh province ke capital hae. Karachi ke log ke Karachiite bolaa jaawe hae. Ii city The city Pakistan ke financial aur commercial centre hae. Hian pe dui khaas sea ports hae. Ii city Arabian Sea ke coast pe hae aur Indus River delta ke west me hae. Iske population 12 million hae.
