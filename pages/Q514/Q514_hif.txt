Anatomy jinda chij, jaise ki admii, jaanwar aur perr ke adhyan hae. Anatomy me dehin ke ang jaise muscle, gland aur haddi ke adhyan hoe hae. Anatomy ke adhyan kare waala ke anatomist bola jaae hae.
Anatomy 1600 BC me Egypt me suruu bhais rahaa. Uu log kareja, spleen, kidney aur dil ke baare adhyan kare rahin aur lymphatic system ke baare me bhi jaankari paae rahin.
