I Andy Warhol (Agosto 6, 1928 – Febrero 22, 1987) metung yang Americanung artist a manimunang kayanib ning pangimut king visual art a awsan dang pop art. Sisyasatan da reng kayang obra ing pamiyugnayan ning pamagbandu king arte (artistic expression), kultura da reng sikat o celebrity, ampong patalastas o advertisement, a mibababo na anyang dekadang 1960. Kaybat ning matagumpeng karera antimong talapaglarawan pangkalakal (commercial illustrator), megi yang artist a sikat at neng kayi kontrobersyal i Warhol. Ing The Andy Warhol Museum king kayang tubung lakanbalen, ing Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, atin yang maleparan a permanenting koleksyon da reng kayang obra ampong archives. Iti ing pekamaragul a museo king Estados Unidos a makaparikil king metung mung artist.
