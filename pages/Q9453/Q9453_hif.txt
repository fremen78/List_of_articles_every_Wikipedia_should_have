Philosophy me dialectic, dui different group ke log ke biich me matbhed hae. Isme matbhed ke baat kar ke khalaas kare ke kosis karaa jaae hae.
Dialectic me tiin khaas bichaar hae:

Sab chij birodhi taagat/ birodhi pakchh (sides) se banaa hae.
Dhire-dhire hoe waala badlao se uu morr (turning point) aae hae, jahaan ek taagat duusre ke dabaae (overcome) de hae.
Parwartan spiral me chale hae, circle me nai.
