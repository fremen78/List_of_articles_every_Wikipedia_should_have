Bienhcingqfap (辩证法, Dialectic), dwg gvanhyih doiqdingj doeng'it、bujbienq lenzhaeq caeuq bienqva fatcanj dih cietyoz yozsoz. Youq seizneiz yungh youq baugvaq seiveiz、swhyienz、lizsij sam aen lingxyiz dih cungj cietyoz ginva dih gaiqniemh, lij cij caeuq hingzwzsangyoz dox doiqdingj dih seiqgyaiqgvanh caeuq fuengfaplun.
Bienhcingqfap miz sam cungj lizsij hingzsik: gujdaih bujsoq dih bienhcingqfap、veizsim bienhcingqfap caeuq veizvuz bienhcingqfap.
