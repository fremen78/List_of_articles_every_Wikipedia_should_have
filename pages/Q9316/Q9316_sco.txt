Sikhism is a releegion at began in Northren Indie in the 16t Century wi the teachins o a Guru kent as Nanak, an the nine Gurus at follaed oan fae him.
The releegious beliefs o the Sikhs are deep-rootit in North Indie, an hae similarities tae baith Islam an Hinduism. Houanever, Sikhs insist that thair releegion cam direct fae God himsel.
