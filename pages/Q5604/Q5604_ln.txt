Frank Lloyd Wright ( 8 Yuni 1867  - 9 Aprili 1959 ) Azalaka motongi, ya mboka ya Amerika.


== Mayé Azala ==
Frank Lloyd Wright abótámákí na engumba ya Richland Center na mokolo ya 8 Yuni 1867.


== Bowéyi ==
Frank Lloyd Wright akufaka na Phoenix o mokolo ya 9 avril 1959.
