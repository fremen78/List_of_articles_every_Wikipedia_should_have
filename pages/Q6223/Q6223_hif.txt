Fascism ek "far-right" rakam ke sarkar hae jisme des ke bahut sakti  khaali ek saasak (ruler), nai to ek chhota dal, khaali ek party ke niche, rahe hae. Fascism ke niche des ke economy aur samaj ke aur hissa ke sarkar kaabu me rakkhae hae. Sarkar hinsa (violence) aur police ke taagat ke kaam me laae ke log ke dabaae ke rakkhe hae.
Chaar barraa fascist des rahin Italy, Benito Mussolini ke niche, Germany, Adolf Hitler ke niche, Japan, Hideki Tojo ke niche aur Spain, Francisco Franco ke niche.
Mussolini, fascism ke 1910s me Italy me suruu karis rahaa aur 1930s talak ii puura laabu hoe gais rahaa. Uu 1922 me Italy ke neta banaa aur mid-1920s talak puura dictator ban gais rahaa, aur sab parrty ke band kar ke aur election ke dhang ke badal ke ki uske Fascist Party ke sab se jaada seat milii.
