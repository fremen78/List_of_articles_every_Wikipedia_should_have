Agelmim n Baykal (s tarusit : Озеро Байкал, Ozero Baïkal), d agelmim yezga-d deg unẓul-usamar n Sibiriya, tajumma-s 3,15 n yimelyunen n yihikṭaṛen, d ugelmim aqbur akk yellan deg umaḍal (20 ar 25 n yimelyunen n yiseggasen) ed d ugelmim-a akk i d alqayan n umaḍal (1 700 m), deg-s 20% n waman n umtiweg.
Agelmim n Baykal yettwajerred sɣur UNESCO am Tigemmi tamaḍlant deg useggas n 1996.
