Jalal-ud-din Muhammad Akbar (15 October 1542 - 27 October 1605, jiske jaada kar ke Akbar the Great, nai to Akbar I, ke naam se jaana jaawat rahaa, tiisra Mughal samraat rahaa, jon 1556 se 1605 talak raj karis rahaa. Akbar aapan pitaji, Humayun ke maut ke baad samraat banaa jab uu khaali 14 saal ke rahaa. Suruu me uske regent Bairam Khan ke madat se uu India bhar me Mughal raj ke taagat ke majbuut karis.


== Timeline ==
25 October 1542 -  Janam  Rajput Fortress, Umarkot , Sindh me bhais rahaa jahan pe uske abba Sher Shah se luka rahaa.
14 February 1556 - Akbar ke Mughal ke Shahanshah banawa gais.
5 November 1556 - Samrat Hem Chandra Vikramaditya ke harae ke Delhi ke aapn niche kar liis.
