Ahkezbah (1542 nienz 10 nyid15 hauh ï½ž 1605 nienz 10nyied 27 hauh) dwg boux vuengzdaeq daih'sam Movowj Digoz. De dwg lwg Vuzmajyung, seng youq Sindwz. Bi 13 de dingj vuengzvih, Bailam danghcwng. 1560 nienz Ahkezbah caenndang danghcwng. Ahkezbah haengj Yindugauqduz camcwng, caueq Vunz Lahgizbuj'wz lenzmungz. Youq seiz de dungjci, gij diegguek Movowj baenz lai lai. De lij laeb dau guenjleix gihgou, haengj ginghci hoengh.
