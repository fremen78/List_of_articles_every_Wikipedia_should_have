Samuel Langhorne Clemens (1835 nî 11 goe̍h 30 – 1910 nî 4 goe̍h 21) sī Bí-kok ê sim-sek-ka (humorist), khoe-sé-ka (satirist), chok-ka kiam ián-káng-ka (lecturer), Mark Twain sī i ê pit-miâ. Huckleberry Finn ê Mō͘-hiám (Adventures of Huckleberry Finn) kap Tom Sawyer ê Mō͘-hiám (The Adventures of Tom Sawyer) sī Mark Twain chin ū-miâ ê 2 phō siáu-soat. William Faulkner chun-chhûn Mark Twain sī "Bí-kok bûn-ha̍k ê khai-ki-chó͘".
