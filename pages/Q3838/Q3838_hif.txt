Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of the Congo ke capital city hae. Ii Africa ke tiisr asab se barraa city hae. Hian pe saat aur aadha million log rahe hae. Kinshasa city, Democratic Republic of the Congo ke Kinshasa Province me hae.
Henry Morton Stanley, 1881 me ii town ke suruu karis rahaa aur iske naam Léopoldville (King Léopold II of Belgium ke naam ke baad) rakkhaa gais rahaa. 1966 me iske naam badal ke Kinshasa rakkhaa gais.
Kinshasa, Congo Naddii ke ek taraf hae.
