I-luqeqesho olunjongo yalo ikumbutho we-anthropized space apho abantu bahlala khona. Ukwenza lula, sinokuthi ngokuyintloko kubhekiselele kuyilo kunye nokwakhiwa kwepropati okanye indawo eyakhiweyo . Imiba yobugcisa kunye nezobugcisa zidibanisa kuyo. Kunye kunye nesculpture, yinxalenye yento ebizwa ngokuba yi-plastic visual arts.
Ekubeni umntu enobuchule obunjalo bokukwazi ukuzilungelelanisa ngokwempucuko, ubugcisa bokwakha bebusoloko bukho. I-Architecture yazalwa kuqala ukwanelisa iimfuno zebhayoloji zomntu ezifana nokukhuselwa kwi -agent ye-atmospheric, kwaye ngenxa yesi sizathu iphakathi kwezifundo ezikhoyo kuzo zonke impucuko. Kuphela kamva, kunye nophuhliso lolwahlulo lwabasebenzi kuluntu, imisebenzi yesibini yongezwa kumsebenzi oyintloko kwinani elihlala likhula.
Ngokubonakala kweempawu zobuhle kwakukho ukuzalwa kwezakhiwo kwakhona njengobugcisa obubonakalayo, nangona kunjalo inikwe iimpawu zayo ezikhethekileyo. Kuya kuba kunciphisa kwakhona ukuthetha malunga nexabiso lobuhle kuba uyilo olulungileyo luhlala lusisiphumo seenqobo ezisesikweni kunye nesifundo se-anthropological.
Ukuchaza i-architecture kunzima njengoko i-architectural phenomenon isoloko ikhona kwinkcubeko yabantu, ukufumana iimpawu, iinkcazo, imisebenzi, indawo kunye nemiba yokwakha ehlala ihluke okanye ihluke kwimpucuko ukuya kwimpucuko okanye ukusuka kwixesha ukuya kwixesha.
