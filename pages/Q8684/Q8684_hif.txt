Seoul (Korean bhasa me, 서울), Republic of Korea (South Korea) ke sab se barraa city aur capital city hae. 1945 se pahile , ii puura Korea ke capital rahaa. Iske abaadi 10,276,968 hae aur area 610 km² hae. Ii dunia ke 6th sab se barraa city hae.
Seoul, Korean Peninsula ke biich me, samundar ke kinare Han River pe hae.
