Botany me Perr ya darakht  aisan paudha ke bolaa jaae hae jiske patti, thorraa barraa howe hae aur lamba howe hae. Sakhat matlab me sirf barre lakrri waala pudha nai to keraa ke bhi perr maan liya jaye hae. Perr ki lakrri furniture, ghat, bridge banaye ke kaam aaye hae. Perr barish barsane ke kaam kare hae aur mausam ke thanda rakhe hae. Ii ke ek mufeed chij samjha jaye hae.
