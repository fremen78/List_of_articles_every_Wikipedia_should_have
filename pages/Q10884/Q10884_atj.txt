
== Manawan/Wemotaci/Opitciwan ==
Enko neki ka matcekitcik notcimik nac e micta mitcetitcik matci apitcihakiniw kitci mikiwamkaniwok kaie kotik kekocica e ocitakiniwok matci ke kaie kitowokanon kecpin awik e siktcitc. Enko tca neki mictikok ota kitaskinak ka tatcik itekera. Miti kirika ocitakaniwon kitci kotohonaniwokw mikiwamik matci kaie rikasimonik.
