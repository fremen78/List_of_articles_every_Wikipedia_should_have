Ing pun dutung (tree king Ingles) metung yang magkaluat a marutung (perennial woody plant). Keraklan, ing bibie rang kabaldugan kaniti, metung a tanaman a marutung a dakal a sangang kaduang uri (secondary branches) a daralan king babo ning gabun king metung a manimunang tangkeng malinong makababo kabilian (having clear apical dominance). Mamie lang pekamababang kátas deng aliwang sinulat,  manibat 3 m anggang 6 m; ating sinulat a babie pekamababang kátas a 10 cm a lapad ning tangke (30 cm girth). Deng marutung a tanaman a e makapante kaniting kabaldugan uling dakal la tangke o kulang la lapad o dagul, ausan da lang palungpung (shrubs king Ingles). Nung pakiyanti ro kareng aliwang tanaman, makaba la bie deng pun dutung, at miraras lang pilang libung banua deng aliwa, at miraras lang anggang 115 m (379 ft) king kátas.
Maulaga lang dake ning kapadurutan deng pun dutung uling sasaup dang lisian ing pangatibag at babie lang kapadurutan a protectadu o makakambil king panaun king kilub ampong lalam da reng karelang bulung. Sasaup la murin king pamaglalang king oxygen ampong pangabawas king carbon dioxide king alapap (atmosphere), ampo ing pamanatili nang kasantingan ning pali o temperatura king gabun. Kayabe la murin king landscaping ampong agricultura, uling king karelang itsura ampo kareng tanaman kareng tamnanan (orchard) anti reng mansanas. Ing dutung ibat kareng pun dutung gagamitan ya king pamangawang bale ampong gusali, at manimuna yang pikukuanan enerhia kareng dakal a susulung a bangsa (developing countries). Maki papil la murin deng pun dutung king mitolohia da reng dakal a lugal king yatu.
