She corys dowanagh eh yn Eddyr-voggyl ta jeant magh jeh moggylyn co-earrooder kianglt rish y cheilley, as ad jannoo ymmyd jeh'n Chynney Reillyn Eddyr-voggyl (TCP/IP) son eddyr-obbraghey. She "moggyl jeh moggylyn" eh, as millioonyn dy voggylyn ayndaue, goaill stiagh moggylyn persoonagh, theayagh, dellal, ard-scoillaragh as reiltysagh, as adsyn co-chianglt liorish strengyn cobbyr, frauaig hilleydagh, kianglaghyn gyn streng as nyn lheid.
