Mot is staðollīc gemet andtimbres þe stent on þiccum middlum cyrnele be þǣm gǣþ ƿolcen  afōndlīce bespearcodra spearcincla. Motes cyrnel hæfþ gemang sendendlīce bespearcodra frumgrota and spearccræftiglīce middelra middelgrota (būton ƿætertimberes forman fadunge, þe hæfþ þone ānne stedefæstan cyrnel lēas middelgrota). Motes spearcinclu sind gebunden mid þǣm cyrnele þurh spearclādmægen. Ēac mid speardlādmægene, hēap mota cunnon ƿesan eall gebunden þurh gesceaftbendas, þe ƿyrceþ gemot. Mot þe hæbbe efen rīm spearcincla and frumgrota is spearccræftige middel, elles is hit oþþe sendendlīce þe afōndlīce bespearcod.
