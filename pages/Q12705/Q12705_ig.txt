REScoop.eu bụ European federation of renewable energy cooperatives, nke e guzobere na 2011.
As of April 2016 it has 20 member organisations (either individual REScoops or regional REScoop federations) in 11 European Member States, representing 1,240 individual REScoops and their 300,000 citizens.
Ọ bụ ụlọ ọrụ Belgian Ecopower na-ahụ maka nlekọta ya.
Na Disemba 24, 2013, European federation of groups and cooperatives of citizens for renewable energy (REScoop.EU) guzobere n'ụzọ iwu n'okpuru iwu mba Belgium na oke Europe. Omume iwu a bụ isi okwu iji kwalite ọrụ nke European federation ma bụrụ ntọala iji wuo njikọ aka siri ike nke European renewable energy.
REScoop bụ oge dị mkpirikpi maka Renewable Energy Source Cooperative.


== Ọnọdụ na mkpọsa ==
REScoop.eu na-akwado maka usoro ike dị iche iche, nke a na-eme ka ọ dị ọhụrụ, nke dị irè, nke dị ọcha na nke na-adịgide adịgide na ụmụ amaala na isi ya, na-ezo aka na mgbanwe ike dị ka "mgbanwe ike na ọchịchị onye kwuo uche ya".
