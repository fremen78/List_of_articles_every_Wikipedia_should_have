Nawaakarniye shakti, nawaakarniye saadhan se aae hae. Ii fossil fuels se nai aawe hae aur iske kaam me laae se greenhouse gases aur duusra pollutants nai bane hae. Kuchh renewable energy hae hawaa, paani ke bahao, perr ke tel, jaanwar ke waste aur suraj ke roshni. Har saal jaada electricity ke renewable resources se banaawa jaae hae.
2004 se nawaakarniye shakti 10-60% se barrhaa hae. Hawaa ke kaam me laana, 2009 se bahut jor se barrha hae aur ii saal hawaa ke kaam me laana aur sab technology se jaada rahaa.
