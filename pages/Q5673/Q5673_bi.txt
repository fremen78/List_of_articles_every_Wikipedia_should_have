Hans Christian Andersen (2 Epril 1805 – 4 Ogis 1875) em i bin wan raeta (man blong litorasa) blong Denmark.  Em i bin raetem plante stori.
Ol stori we em i bin raetem:

The Princess and the Pea (1835)
The Tinderbox (1835)
Thumbelina (1835)
The Little Mermaid (1837)
The Emperor's New Clothes (Niu Klos blong Emperor) (1837)
The Steadfast Tin Soldier (1838)
The Wild Swans (1838)
Ole Lukøje (1841)
The Swineherd (1841)
The Angel (1843)
The Nightingale (1843)
The Ugly Duckling (1843)
The Little Match Girl (1845)
The Red Shoes (Ol Red Sus) (1845)
The Shepherdess and the Chimney Sweep (1845)
The Snow Queen (1845)
The Fir Tree (1845)
The Shadow (1847)
The Story of a Mother (Stori blong wan Mama) (1847)
