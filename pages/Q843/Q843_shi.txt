Pakstan tga yat tagdudant tanslmt ɣ Wasya, tdru iwtta  nns d kkuẓt n tmizar, Lhnd d Ccinwa d Wafɣanistan d Yiran, Pakstan llan dars kra ɣ wafatn n idrarn da akkʷ attuynin ɣ ddunit. 
Tamaẓunt n Pakstan tga tt Islamabad da ittyawbnan ɣ ulatay wis ṣḍiṣ n tasut 20, imma tiɣrmt n Karatci lli tt inn ikkan tga tamaẓunt taqbuṛt hati tga ɣilad tiɣrmt lli tnt akkʷ yugrn ɣ turrut, yili gis afttas lli mqqurn n tmazirt. 
Tuggt n Pakstan ar tsmaẓlay ixf nns s yan ungigt izwan, s tagrst iṣmmiḍn d yat tnbdut irɣan, d yan unẓaṛ idrusn macc kra n tikkal ar ittili ugusif d ittawin angi، gn isaffn asagm agaman lli ilan atig ɣ tmazirt, imma middn ar sawaln yat tutlayt mi ism Urdu.
