Li Television (anc brevi: TV; del grec τῆλε(tèle)= distant e del latin visio= vision) es un telecomunication medium de se movent fotos in nigri-blanc o in colores e in du o tri dimensiones.


== Historie ==

Un del unésimes mecanic televisiores esset li Nipkow disco, quel esset patentar in 1884
In 1928 li Baird Television Development Company/Cinema Television transmisset quam li unési un transatlantice-signale.
In 1934 in Berlin Manfred von Ardenne presentat li unésim tot electric television
In 1953 li Eurovision, li europan organisation por television, ha esset instituet
In 1956 Ampex presentat li unésim video-recorder
In 1982 Sony presenat li unésim Camcorder
In 1995 aparit li unésim DVD
In 2010 aparit li unésim tri dimesiones capabil televisiores
