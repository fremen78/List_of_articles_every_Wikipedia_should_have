Television (jiske TV, telly nai to tube bhi bola jaae hae) ek machine hae jisme ek screen rahe hae. Televisions broadcast signals ke le ke iske chhapa aur awaj me badle hae. "Television" sabd tele (Greek jiske matlab far away hae) aur vision (seeing) se aae hae. Yahii kaaran hae ki Hindi me television ke Duurdarshan bola jaae hae.
