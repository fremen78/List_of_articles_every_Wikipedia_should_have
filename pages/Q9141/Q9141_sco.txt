The Taj Mahal (/ˈtɑːdʒ məˈhɑːl/ eften /ˈtɑːʒ/; Hindi: ताज महल, frae Persie/Urdu: تاج محل‬‎, ultimately frae Arabic, "crown of pailaces", pronounced [ˈt̪aːdʒ mɛˈɦɛl]; an aw "the Taj") is a white marble mausoleum locatit in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, Indie. It wis biggit bi Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory o his third wife, Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is widely recognised as "the jewel o Muslim airt in Indie an ane o the universally admired masterpieces o the warld's heritage".
