Taj Mahal (/ˌtɑːdʒ məˈhɑːl, ˌtɑːʒ-/; lit. 'Crown of the Palace')  Archived 2023-06-15 at the Wayback Machine</ref> ek kabar hae jiske 17th century me Mughal badhshah Shah Jahan, aapan favourate aurat, Mumtaz Mahal ke yaad me banais rahaa. Ii Agra, Uttar Pradesh, Republic of India me hae. Ii dunia ke "Seven Wonders" me se ek hae aur bahut log ii soche hae ki ii dunia ke sab se sundar building hae. Ii building Mughal architecture ke ek example hae jisme - Islamic Indian, Persian (old Iran), aur Muslim art mixed hae. Dhe log India iske dekhe ke khatir jaawe hae lekin abhi ii building "Acid Rain" ke kaaran barbad hoe lagaa hae.
