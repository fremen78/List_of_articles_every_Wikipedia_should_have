Noka ke metsi a tlholego a a elelang, gantsi ke molapo wa metsi a a foreshe, a a elelang mo godimo ga lefatshe kgotsa mo teng ga magaga go ya kwa metsing a mangwe a a kwa tlase, jaaka lewatle, lewatle, kgogometso, letsha, lefelo le le bongola kgotsa noka e nngwe. Ka dinako tse dingwe, noka e elela mo mmung kgotsa e a kgala fa e fitlha kwa e felelang teng mme e sa fitlhelele metsi a mangwe. Dinoka tse dinnye di ka bidiwa ka maina a a jaaka noka, molatswana le noka e nnye. Ga go na ditlhaloso tsa semmuso tsa lereo la noka jaaka le dirisitswe mo dithulaganyong tsa thutafatshe,le fa mo dinageng dingwe kana mo baaging, molapo o tlhalosiwa ka bogolo jwa one. Maina a le mantsi a dinoka tse dinnye a amana le lefelo la tsone; dikai ke "run" mo dikarolong dingwe tsa United States, "burn" kwa Scotland le kwa Bokonebotlhaba jwa Engelane, le "beck" kwa Bokone jwa Engelane. Ka dinako tse dingwe noka e tlhalosiwa e le e kgolo go feta molatswana, mme ga se ka metlha e leng jalo; puo eno ga e tlhalose dilo ka botlalo.
