She coorse ushtey najooragh, jeant ass ushtey glen dy mennick, ta roie lesh faarkey, logh, mooir, ny awin elley eh awin. Ayns cooishyn ennagh ta awin çhirmaghey roish y çheet eck lesh corpane elley d'ushtey. Foddee dy vel awinyn beggey er enney fo enmyn elley goaill stiagh branlaig, strooan, gullad, glasheen, glass, a.r.e.
Ta awinyn nyn gooid jeh Çhymshal yn Ushtey. Ta ushtey çheusthie jeh awin çhymshit liorish frassaghey trooid teaumey, aalughtey ushtey thallooin, farraneyn, as skeaylley magh ushtey va tashtit ayns rio najooragh as sniaghtey (m.s. veih awinyn rioee).
