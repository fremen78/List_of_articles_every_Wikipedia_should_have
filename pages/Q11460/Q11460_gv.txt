Ta eaddeeyn ny eaddagh çheet er ny nheeghyn ta deiney ceau dys y corp dy choodaghey, tro ta ry-akin ayns dagh fer dy heshaghtyn deiney noa-emshiragh. She foaynoo myr coadey veih'n emshir y phreeu-chooish d'eaddeeyn. Ta eaddeeyn cooney lesh sauçhys car obbraghyn gaueagh lheid as troagyraght as coagyraght, as ny h-eaddeeyn jannoo seose scarrey eddyr y chrackan as y çhymbyllaght. Ta eaddeeyn kiarail scarrey slayntagh as ad freill foddeeaght eddyr tomaineyn as y corp as cur cagliagh er ym-skeaylley ny bunniagyn.
Ta eaddeeyn cloie paart ayns foaynooyn sheshoil as cultooragh chammah. Myr sampleyr, ta cullee eaddee cur enney er feallee hivoil lheid as poleenyn, sidooryn, fwirran, a.r.e. Ayns ram sheshaghtyn ta cliaghtaghyn mychione eaddeeyn aahoilshaghey stundayrtyn er feeudys, craueeaght, keintys, as staydys sheshoil. Foddee dy vel eaddeeyn cloie paart myr cummey jesheenaghey as dreagh dy hosheeaghtyn ny fassan persoonagh.
