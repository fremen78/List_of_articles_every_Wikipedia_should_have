Sanchaar (Communication) uu samay (time) hoe hae jab jaankari (information) ke ek "medium" ke kaam me laae ke bheje waala se mile waala ke lae pahuchaawa jaae hae. Ii medium, dekhe waala hoe sake hae, jaise ki "body language" aur isaara (gesture), awaaj, mahak, chhuuna jaae sake hae.
Sanchaar ke bola jaae sake (sabd ke kaam me laae ke), nai to bina bole hoe sake hae, jaise ki muhuraana (smile).
