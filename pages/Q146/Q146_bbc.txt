Huting (goar ilmiah: Felis catus) i ma sada binatang sanga pinahan mamalia karnipora, na boi mangallang sude sipanganon, sanga pe suansuanan manang jagal.
Goar ni '"huting" somalna marlapatan tu huting na nunga dipahan jolma, agiat pe sipata marlapatan tu "huting na balga" songon singa manang babiat.
