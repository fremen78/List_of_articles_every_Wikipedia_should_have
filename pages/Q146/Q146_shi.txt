Iɣ ur igi wad amgrad lli tsiggilt, kcm s tasna n Amucc bac ad tẓrt akkʷ imgradn nna dar illa yism zun d wad !

Amuc nɣ Amucc nɣ Amaccu (s talatinit: Felis silvestris catus) iga yan ɣ imudar lli d inkkrn ɣ gr ifgann. Gin imucciwn ttusgrdin.  Itfrtn ɣ tudrt nnsn zun d iyḍan d ifullusn d ifunasn d ikrwan. Mac llan imuccaiwn ibaṛṛayn lli d nkkrnin ɣ tagant yaggugn f ufgan. Ntta imẓẓiy mac iwala i yat tawja d ugrzam, d uɣilas d waksil.
