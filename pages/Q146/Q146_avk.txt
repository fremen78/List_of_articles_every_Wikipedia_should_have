Kwaf Karvol (Felis catus) tir tana moukolafa katca ke Felis oxi vey Felidae yasa ( Felinae volveyyasa ) ke CARNIVORA veem ( FELIFORMIA volveyveem ). Gan Linnaeus bak 1758 taneon zo pimtayar.


== Pimtara ==
Karvol va dzaveso is terafo alto noveso va glabafa is tuzafa lizira
sodir ; beweem sotir trelackaf kapbure alto vox tigaf isen iniotsa tir tiguafa. Taka tir anamkafa isen pezay bargentimaf. Va abrotcapaf nyoxeem yoltkiraf gu kuga vamoe ticef kutc sodir, i gu kuga tadlesa va tana pestafa vilapa. Oblakeem tir pinaf isen iteem gijaf. Witca is pezitca tid dalafa.
Inaf im, gubeon vaf is idulaf, kare zaava arapeniwer


== Vexala dem apteem ==
Sedme Mammal Species of the World (siatos ke 2005), bata katca tir aptiskafa.
