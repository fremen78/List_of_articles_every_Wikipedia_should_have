Niau-á (Felis silvestris catus), iá-niau (Felis silvestris) ê a-chéng, ū-ê hun-lūi mā to̍k-li̍p chi̍t chéng Felis catus, sī 1 chióng jîn-lūi kúi-ā-chheng nî lâi poê-ióng hō͘ lo̍h-sèng ê sió-hêng bah-si̍t tōng-bu̍t. It-poaⁿ jīn-ûi siōng chhin ê iá-seng chéng sī Hui-chiu iá-niau (Felis silvestris lybica).
Niau sī thong-sè-kài siāng phó͘-ki̍p ê thióng-bu̍t chi it.
