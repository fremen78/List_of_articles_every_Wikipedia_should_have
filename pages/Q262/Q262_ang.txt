Algeria is land in Affrice. Hēr spricþ man Algerisc-Arabisce, Berber and Frencisce. Ðis rices heafodburg is Algiers. Se Deawyke Sand oferwriehð eall þæs rices landscipe buton þæm særime.
Algerie lēodweard is cynewīse.
Algeria wæs ofersǣwisc dæl Franclandes fram 1830 oþ 1962, and manige Francan setledon on norðernum Algerie. Hie āfliemedon æfter 1962.
