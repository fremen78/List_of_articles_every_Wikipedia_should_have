Leonardo da Vinci, an haife shi a ranar 15 ga Afrilu, 1452, a Vinci, (Italiya). Mahaifinsa Ser Piero da Vinci, ya kasance mai ban sha'awa, mahaifiyarsa, Caterina, ta kasance manomiya. An haifi Leonardo ne ba tare da aure ba, kuma dangin mahaifinsa ne suka yi ta renonsa. Duk da irin ƙalubalen da ya ke fuskanta, Leonardo ya ci gaba da zama ɗaya daga cikin masu faɗa-a-ji da ƙirƙire-ƙirƙire a tarihi, ya mutu a ranar 2 ga watan Mayu  Amboise (Faransa) a shekara ta alif 1519 Yana da shekara 67. Ya kuma rayu a lokacin Renaissance. Ya kasance shahararren mai zane, injiniya, masanin kimiyya da fasaha. Duk da cewa anfi sanin shi a matsayin mai zane, har ila yau ya shahara da ayyukansa na littafi, inda yake zane da rubuce rubuce akan abubuwa daban daban da suka shafi nazarin halittar dan Adam, taswira,  ilimin jikin mutum zuwa jirgin sama, labarin ƙasa da makamantansu. Abubuwan da ya ƙirƙira sun bar tasiri mai ɗorewa. Leonardo da Vinci sanannene ne a tarihi. 
Leonardo masanin Polymath ne na Italiyanci na Babban Renaissance wanda ya kasance mai aiki a matsayin mai zane, draughtman, injiniya, masanin kimiyya, masanin ilimin tunani, sculptor, da gine-gine. Yayin da shahararsa ta farko ta dogara ne kan nasarorin da ya samu a matsayinsa na mai zane, ya kuma shahara da littattafan rubutu, inda ya yi zane-zane da rubuce-rubuce kan batutuwa daban-daban da suka haɗa da ilmin jikin mutum, ilmin taurari, ilmin halitta, zane-zane, zane-zane, da ilmin taurari. Ana ɗaukan Leonardo a matsayin haziƙi wanda ya kwatanta manufar ɗan adam ta Renaissance, kuma ayyukansa na gama gari sun ƙunshi gudummawa ga ƙarni na gaba na masu fasaha wanda ya dace da na ƙaramin ɗan'uwansa Michelangelo. 
An girmama shi don basirar fasaharsa, ya tsara injinan tashi sama, nau'in motar yaƙi mai sulke, ƙarfin hasken rana, injin da za a iya amfani da shi a cikin injin ƙara, da ƙwanƙwasa biyu. Kaɗan daga cikin zane-zanensa da aka gina ko ma sun kasance masu yiwuwa a lokacin rayuwarsa, saboda hanyoyin kimiyya na zamani game da ƙarfe da injiniyanci sun kasance a cikin ƙuruciyarsu a lokacin Renaissance. Wasu ƙananan ƙirƙira nasa, duk da haka, sun shiga duniyar masana'anta ba a bayyana ba, irin su bobbin winder mai sarrafa kansa da na'ura don gwada ƙarfin ƙarfin waya. Ya yi bincike mai zurfi a cikin ilmin jikin mutum, injiniyan farar hula, hydrodynamics, geology, optics, da tribology, amma bai buga bincikensa ba kuma ba su da wani tasiri kai tsaye kan kimiyyar da ta biyo baya.
