Tha Yird is tha thrid planet oot frae tha Sun. It is tha mukkilest o tha solar seestem's fower stanie planets, an tha ae bodie that modren syense kens ti haud lyf. Tha planet cam thegither aboot 4.57 thoosan million yeir syn. Nae lang eftir (4.533 thoosan million yeir syn) it wis jyn'd bi tha Muin. Tha solar seestem is made up o echt planets.
