George Gordon Byron, 6th Baron Byron (22 January 1788 – 19 April 1824) ke jaada kar ke  Lord Byron ke naam se jaana jaawat rahaa. Uu ek English poet rahaa.  Byron ke ksvita me kuchh famous waala rahaa, She Walks in Beauty, When We Two Parted, So, we'll go no more a roving aur Childe Harold's Pilgrimage aur Don Juan. Usk Europe ke ek sab se barraa poet mana jaawe hae.
