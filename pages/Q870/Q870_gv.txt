She straih dy charbid railley chianglt ta broojit fo bree charbyd er lheh er raad yiarn son lughtyn ny troailtee y ymmyrkey eh traen.
Ta jeshaght-charbyd er lheh ny jeshaghtyn er lheh ayns yl-unnidyn hene-vroojit kiarail bree ghleashagh. Ga dy row ee yn ghaal-vroo dy row broojey traenyn dy shenndeeagh, jiu she traenyn deesyl as lectragh ny sorçhyn dy hraenyn smoo cadjin. Ta farraneyn bree elley goaill stiagh cabbil, caabil, yn ym-hayrn, aer broojit, battreeyn, as turbeenyn gas.
Ta raillaghyn jeant seose, er y chooid smoo, jeh daa, tree ny kiare raillaghyn, lesh unskelfyn as bollee-stiuree floaddey magnaidagh.
Ta'n fockle "traen" çheet magh ass trahiner y Çhenn Rangish trooid y Vaarle, veih trahere ("tayrn") y Ladjyn.
