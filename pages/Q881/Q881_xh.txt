IVietnam lilizwe elikwi-peninsula ye-Indochina, kufutshane naseLaos, eCambodia nase China. Idolophu enkulu yeli lizwe yiHanoi kwaye iziko lezoqoqosho yiHi Chi Minh City. Indawo yolawulo lwaseVietnam ngolandelo-lusezantsi: I-Central - Iphondo (isixeko esiphantsi kwegunya eliphakathi) - iSixeko - iSithili (idolophu) - Umbutho. Inani labemi baseVietnam ngonyaka ka-2020 bamalunga nezigidi ezingama-97.
