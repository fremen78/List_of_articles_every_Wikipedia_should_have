Kolosyum neɣ Koliseum neɣ Amsiswar Aflavi (s talatint : Colosseum, s taṭalyanit : Colosseo), d amsiswar ameqqren yezga-d deg Ṛuma, bnan-t deg tallit n umenkad arumani Vespasianus Augustus (gar 70 ed 72), Kolosyum yesεa uqus (ṛebεa) n wannagen ed yezmer ad yawi 50 000 neɣ 100 000 n yimferrjen.
Kolosyum sseqdacen ɣer imenɣan n imennaɣen (gladiatores), imennaɣen mgal iɣersiwen ed imenɣan n lebwabeṛ (navalia proelia).
Deg Tallit-Tanammast, yettuɣal taglisya, u d yiɣrem n snat n twaculin tarumanit Frangipane ed Annibaldi.
Deg 2007, Kolosyum yettwaxtar-d em yiwet seg Sa n leεǧubat timaynutin n umaḍal.
