Bukhomanisi lihlelo lepolitiki lesimo senhlangano yemphakatsi lapho imphahla kanye netidzingancanti tilawulwa 
tiphindze tabelwe emalunga emphakatsi wonkhe. 


== Umlandvo ==
Indlela yekubusa ngebukhomanisi yacalwa ingcwele kutefilosofi wase Fransi Victor d'Hupay la abhala khona ebhukwini lakhe lelatiwa nge (Project for a Philosophical Community) ngemnyaka wa 1777. Hupay uchaza ngetintfo letingenta kutsi kube nekulingana kwebantfu emphakatsini.
