
== Manawan ==
Wapatarew e tatotipahikaniarik pisimwa e tipihikioketc. Enko kirika ka ici tapahikawkaniokw awik e otamirotc, tato tipahikan pitikew kirika e ickwa otamirotc kiskeritam pitc e ickwa awiketc. Kaskina wiec ici miro apitisiw tipahipisimon, enko cakotc ka kiciweritak pimatisiwiniw. Kinowapamakaniw kotc kekocic e actek kitci ititoskaniwok. Apitc ke kockosinaniwok, apitc ke nipaniwok, apitc ke mitcisonaniwok, apitc ke matcekaniwok, kaskina sa kotc kekocic.
