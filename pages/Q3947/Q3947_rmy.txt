Yek kher (vay khâr) si yek than kay beshen tay  jipen le manusha, kerdo te garuvel len katar brishind, balval, tatipen, shil vai biamala. Si les zidurya (pashavre), uchhardo tay  yek vay buteder livnya (kherkotora, sobe, besharnya). Les si les yek vay buteder udara, pale varesave purane khera (sar kay Chatalhöyük) labyarden uchharde tay shopnya te den andre. Vi si les dikhavne (filyastre) te del andre o Khamesko dud tay  te dikhel pes avri.
O lav si Indo-Aryano (Hindi: घर - ghâr).
