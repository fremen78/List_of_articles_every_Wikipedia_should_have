A hoose (or houss) is a biggin that fowk bide in. It is kent as thair hame an aw. Fowk that dinnae huv a hoose are kent as hameless fowk.


== Chaumers an rooms ==
Hooses can huv monie different rooms:

Loonge
Kitchen
Bathroom
Bedroom
Sitooterie
Spence
