Shanghae, China ke sab se barraa city hae.
Ii Yangtze (Chang Jiang River) Naddii ke bank pe hae. 
Shanghae 1800s me western countries se trade kare khatir ek khaas port banaa rahaa. ii abhi dunia ke sab se barraa wharf hae.
