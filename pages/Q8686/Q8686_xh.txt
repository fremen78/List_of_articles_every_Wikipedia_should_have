Shanghai ingcaciso yeyokuba esi sixeko ekuqaleni sasimi phezu kolwandle okanye, ngokutsho kwabemi balapho, yayiyeyona ndawo iphakamileyo apho ulwandle lwalufikelela khona.
I-Shanghai ibonwa njengenkunzi yezoqoqosho yase-China: ngenxa yophuhliso lwamashumi eminyaka adlulileyo, i-Shanghai yindawo enkulu yezoqoqosho, yezemali, yorhwebo kunye nonxibelelwano lweRiphabhlikhi yaseChina. Izibuko layo, lelokuqala kweli lizwe, lelinye lawona axakekileyo emhlabeni kunye neSingapore kunye neRotterdam . Ngo-2010 yodlula iSingapore ngomthamo wezithuthi. 
Ezinye zeziteketiso zedolophu zidumileyo, kubandakanywa: " I-Paris yaseMpuma", "uKumkanikazi waseMpuma", "iPearl of the Orient". Ukuqinisekisa oku kwakhona, uphando olugunyazisiweyo lubeka iShanghai phakathi kwezona dolophu zisefashonini e- Asia kunye neyeshumi elinesine kwihlabathi.
