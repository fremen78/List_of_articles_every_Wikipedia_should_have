Mumbai, jisko 1994 talak mein Bombay(BOM) ke naam se jaana jata rahaa hae, India ke west coast pe ek natural harbour aur Maharashtra state ki capital city aur India ke financial capital rahaa hae. ii India ke sab se barraa city, aur dunia ke sab se jaada abaadi waala city hae. Iske India ke commercial capital bolaa jaawe hae. Hian pe Bombay ke Stock Exchange hae. Mumbai ke mausam monsoon jaisa hai aur kabhi garmi aur thandi hoti hai.
