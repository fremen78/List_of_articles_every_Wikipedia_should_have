Yangtze Naddi, nai to Yangzi, nai to Chang Jiang, China me ek naddi hae. Ii Asia ke sab se barraa naddi aur dunia ke tiisra sab se lamba naddi hae(Amazon aurNile ke baad me).
Ii naddi 6,300 kilometers (almost 4,000 miles) lamba hae aur China ke west me (Qinghae Province) se East China Sea, jon ki Pacific Ocean ke ek bhaag hae, me jaae ke nikle hae.
