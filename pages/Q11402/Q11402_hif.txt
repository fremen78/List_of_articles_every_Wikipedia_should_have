Physics me taagat (force) uu chij hae jon ki ek kharaa nai to ghasate rahe chij ke  raftaar badle hae. 
Newton ke second law of motion ke anusaar :

F = ma
jahaan pe F taagat hae, 
m chij ke wajan hae, 
aur a chij ke raftaar ke badlao hae.
Jab a standard gravity g, rahe tab duusra formula bane hae:

W = mg
jisme W chij ke wajan pe force, 
m chij ke wajan, 
aur g gravity ke acceleration jon ki lagbhag 9.8m/s^2 hae.
