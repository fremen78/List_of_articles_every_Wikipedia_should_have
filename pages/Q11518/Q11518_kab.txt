== Amezruy ==

Asekkud-a yettwassen s yisem n Pythagore, amusnak agrigi i yedderen gar 570 d 495 send talalit n Ɛisa. Maca, aṭas n yimusnawen ɛerḍen ad d-sbanen belli asekkud-a yella yettwassen uqbel Pythagore, ladɣa sɣur Ibabuliyen d Yimaṣriyen iqburen. Akken yebɣu yili, d Pythagore i t-yessuffɣen-d s wudem ummid, yerna yefka-yas anzaten yesdukklen.
Seg tallit-nni ar ass-a, asekkud n Pythagore yuɣal d allal agejdan deg tusnakt, ladɣa deg tanzeggit (géométrie) d tussniwin n usihel (sciences de l'ingénieur).
