Venus bi di sekond planet from di Sun. E get di densest atmosfe of all di roki bodis for di Sola System, so dens sotey for surfais levul and 92 atmosfes, e bi a supakritikal fluid. Di planet surfais dé dominated bai volkanos – 167 Venusian volkanos wey dey ova 100 km (60 mi) across na am dey. Venus get a diameta rofly equal to Earth at 12,104 km (7,521 mi) and dey klassifaid as a terrestrial planet.
Di surfais of Venus get uniq feachors of volkanik orijin, such as 'farras' wey bi waiyd pankaek-laik lava doms and 'araknoids' wey bi konsentrik strukchor wey resembul spaida web. Som tink say di planet  krud intanal krust dé riliz heat thru aktiv volkanisim and shayp di surfais with laj resurfaisin. Venus atmosfe get a rof komposishion of 96.5% kabon daioxaid, 3.5% naitrojin, and reflektiv sulfaurik acid klaud kova. Venus get a wik indiuzd magnetosfe.
