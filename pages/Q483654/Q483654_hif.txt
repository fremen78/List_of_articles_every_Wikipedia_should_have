Sunni Islam (أهل السنة) Islam ke sab se barraa sect hae. Uu log Rashidun Empire, jiske Abu Bakr suruu karis rahaa aur jon aAli ibn Abi Talib ke time khalaas bhais rahaa,me suruu bhay rahin. Sunni log Quran aur Kutub al-Sittah me biswas kare hae. Duniya ke Muslim population me se 90% Sunni hae. Iske 1.8 billion followers ke kaaran ii duniya ke sab se barraa dharam hae.    Sunni logan me chaar sub group (Madh'hab) hae: Malikis, Hanafis, Hanbalis and Shafi'is.
Sunni Islam ke follow kare waala log ke Sunnis nai to Sunnites bola jaae hae.  Sunni sabd 'sunnah' (سنة) se aais hae, jiske matlab "tradition of the prophet of Islam, Muhammad' hae.
