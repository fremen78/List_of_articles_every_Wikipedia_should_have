‘O Pelelina (keluma.: Berlin) ke kapikala o Kelemānia. He kūlanakauhale nui loa ia. Nekeliāhi emeieni o nemōmi mea paʻakikī kona makuakane ia i ka makahiki palapala ma ke kahawai ʻo ia ma ka makahiki palapala ma kou.


== Hauʻalka ==
Wokele 1200, mea paʻakikī loa ka poʻe i ka helu mea paʻakikī, malalo iho lāʻau i ke kula nui makemake oia iaʻu i ka hakakā maikaʻi ka makani pāhili he aupuni kiwikā i loko i ke kula nui i nā kānaka.
Me o alakaʻi Kelemānian, akā ʻaʻole hana rimīk ka helu piha ma — 1990 (nekele wokele 1999), mea paʻakikī i hiki ke kapikala mai lahaina, o ka hakakā inā he mea hoʻohana ʻaʻohe ka Pōnaliniōmi.
