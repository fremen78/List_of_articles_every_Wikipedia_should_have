Politikis em i we long manmeri wantim i ken tingting na mekim disison long gavman na narapela kaim samting tu. Yu ken painim politikis long olgeta kain ples we manmeri wantaim toktok na tingting na mekim disision. Politikis i no bilong gavman tasol.
Narapela we long toktok long politikis tu emi i stadi bilong gavman
Em i gat planti narakain tingting long politikis. Tupela bikpela tingting long politikis i tingting olsem left politikis na narapela mo i tingting olsem right politikis, na planti mo i tingting insait na outsait long tupela bikpela tingting.
