Garibi uu time rahe hae jab ki koi ke lage rahe ke ghar nai hae aur koi ke lage khaana khaae ke nai hae.
Garibi urban development mein ek samsay hota hai. Slums aur shero aur gao aur towns mein ek samsay hota hai jaha pe khana aur kachara zayada jama hota hai aur janwar ke hamle bhi hote hai. Log ke tan aur man bhi gareeb ho sakta hai. Gareebi aur dacaiti ke sambandh bhi hote hai.
