Fotosintesis (/ˌfoʊtəˈsɪnθəsɪs/ FOH-tə-SINTH-ə-sis)  bi a baiolojikal proces wey plenti oganisims dey yuz to konvat lait eneji into kemikal eneji, wey go kom dey stod for oganik kompaunds wey fit dey metabolaizd afta thru cellular respiration to fuel di oganisim ativitis. Di tem yuzuali dey rifa to oxygenik fotosintesis, whe oxygen dey produs as a byprodut and som of di kemikal eneji wey dey produsd dey stod for kabohydraet molekuls.
Although fotosintesis dey hapun difrentli for difrent specis, di proces aulweis dey bigin wen eneji from lait dey absobd bai proteins wey wi don dey kol riakshon centas wey kontaen fotosintetik pigments or kromofors. For plants, diz proteins bi klorofil (a porfirin deraivaetiv wey dey absob di red and bleu spektrums of lait, tus dey reflekt green kolour) for insyd cetain oganels.
