Khwajeh Shams al-Din Muhammad Hafez-e Shirazi (jiske Hafiz ke naam se bhi jaana jaawe hae) (janam 1310 aur 1337 ke biich me, biswas karaa jaawe hae ki 69 saal ke umar me maris) (Persian: خواجه شمس الدین محمد حافظ شیرازی) nai to Hafez ek Persian poet rahaa. Hafez ke janam Shiraz, Iran me bhaes rahaa. Hafez, Quran ke by heart, chhota umar me sikhis. Uske kavita ke Divan e Hafez ke naam se jaana jaawe hae. William Jones uske kavita ke 1771 me English me translate karis rahaa.
