Rōm is Eotolƿara hēafodburg. Sēo is micel burg. In gēardagum arærede sēo Rōmānisc cyneƿīse þæt Rōmānisc Rīce, þæs hēafodburg ƿæs Rōmburg. Rōmburg ƿæs hēafodstōl þāra Romaniscan casera oððæt ðe hie asettedon hiera stōl to Megelane in 286 under Diocletiane.  Fore cristentīd ƿyrhteden Eotolƿare eallhālgung ofer Rōm sƿa gyden.
Rōm ƿeard Italie hēafodstōl in 1871 æfter ðe Uictores-Emmanueles Cyninges here gehiersumode þēos ceastre of þǣm Pāpan.
