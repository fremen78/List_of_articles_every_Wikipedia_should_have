Dimitirii Mendeleev (Raashiyaaniffa: Дми́трий Ива́нович Менделе́ев; IPA: [ˈdmʲitrʲɪj ɪˈvanəvʲɪt͡ɕ mʲɪndʲɪˈlʲejɪf] (dhaggeeffadhu); A.L.A 8 Gurrandhala 1834 – 2 Gurrandhala 1907 O.S. 27 Amajjii 1834 – 20 Amajjii 1907) kemistii fi kalaqaa lammii Raashiyaa ti. Mendeleev Seera Peredikii baase, gabatee peredikii elementotaa mataasaa uumme, kanattis fayyadamuun amaloota elementota sana dura argaman sirreesse, amaloota elementota saddeet kan yeroo sana hin argamin ammoo raage.
