Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev (er ny hessen-lettyraghey myr Mendeleyev, Mendeleiev, ny Mendeleef keayrtyn; Rooshish: Дмитрий Иванович Менделеев,; 8 Toshiaght Arree [SA 27 Jerrey Geuree] 1834 – 2 Toshiaght Arree [SA 20 Jerrey Geuree] 1907), she kemmigagh as ynlaghteyr Rooshagh v'eh. Ta enney share er er-yn-oyr dy nee eshyn ren formlaghey yn Leigh Reiltagh as ren croo lhieggan jeh taabyl reiltagh ny bunstooghyn. Ghow eh ymmyd jeh'n Leigh Reiltagh dy chiartaghey troyn ram bunstooghyn va doltit ec y traa shen, lheid as ablid chianglee as trimmid vreneenagh yn uraanium, as ren eh faaishnaghey troyn tree bunstooghyn nagh row feddynit magh foast (germaanium, gallium as scandjum).
