Seizgan, lij heuh sizgenh.
It bi  miz  seiq geiq（四季）. Cin hah caeuq　cou doeng. Cibngeih ndwen guh bi.　Ndwen miz  samcib  ngoenz.　Doxca saek  song  ngoenz.　Ngoenz ngeihseiq  diemj cung. Banhaet coh banringz. Banhaemh coh gyanghwnz. Ngoenzbonz daengz ngoenzlwenz. Ngoenzneix daengz ngoenzcog. Seizgan gvaq ndaej vaiq. Raeuz aeu lai  insik.
