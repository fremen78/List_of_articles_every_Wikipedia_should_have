Osman Daeqgoz(Ottoman Turkish: دولتْ علیّه عثمانیّه Devlet-i Âliye-yi Osmâniyye, Yendai Turkish: Osmanlı İmparatorluğu or Osmanlı Devleti) dwg youz Duhgiezyinz laeb, bouxlaeb dwg Osman I. Codaeuz gyoengqde youq Cunghya, sinyangj Islam gauq, doeklaeng senj daengz Iq-yasihya, ciemhciemh maenh'ak. Seiz ceiq hwng seiqlig daengz ouh, ya, fei sam daihcouh, doeng daemx Lijhaij caeuq Bohswhvanh, baek daemx Austria caeuq Slovenia, namz daemx Sudan caeuq Yemen. Daireag Doenglozmaj Daeqgoz liux, dinghcinj soujduh Istanbul, gag nyinhnaeuz dingjfaenh lo Lozmaj Daeqgoz caeuq Islam vwnzva. yungzhab le doeng sae vwnzmingz.
