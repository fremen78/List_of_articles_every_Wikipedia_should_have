Ta Sydney ny h-ard-valley smoo 'syn Austrail as ny preeu-valley er y steat New South Wales. Ta sleih-earroo harrish er 4.3 millioonyn cummaltee as eaghtyr mygeayrt 12,000 kilometeryn kerrinagh eck. Dy mennick, ta'n ennym "the Harbour City" (Y Balley Purtagh") currit urree. T'ee coontit mastey ny h-ard-valjyn smoo yl-chultooragh 'sy theihll, as eh shen aahoilshey magh y paart eck myr kione jurnaa da ram arreydee stiagh dys yn Austrail.
