Seo Frenciscu Onwendung wæs wræchwil þærin Franclandes rice wæs toworpen in uphebbinge and in wige and in þæm hwile wæs Francena midwist oferwearp mid hiera leodwearde. Þeos ield begann ðan Parises scopgemot stod wið ðone Cyning  and þy be niede geben Hloðwig XVI Cyning his rices eald witenagemot se is þa États Généraux in 1789.  On 14. dæg Mædmonðes 1789 wæs onræs be folcgeþrang in Paris se inswog Bastille ða festnis.
In 1792 ðæs cyniges wiþercoran fang þone witenagemot and asettede hiera hlaford be swidome.  An winter æfterweard hie beheafde þone cyning.
Seo onwendungield endode þan niw  leodweard settede ofer þæ leode þrim Consulas þærof wæs Napoleon Bonaparte heafodconsul.
