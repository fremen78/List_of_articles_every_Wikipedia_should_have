She eash dy chaghlaaghyn sheshaghtagh as politickagh fraueoilagh 'sy Rank va Bun-chaghlaa yn Rank (Frangish: Révolution française), ta enmyssit Irree Magh y Rank ny yn Irree Magh Frangagh chammah. Hoshee eh lesh Steatyn Theayagh 1789 as haink jerrey rish lesh cummey Consyllaght y Rank ayns Mee Houney 1799. Ta ram jeh ny h-eieyn ayn coontit myr leeideilee raueoilagh y deynlaghys libraalagh, as va abbyrtyn lheid as liberté, égalité, fraternité ry-chlashtyn as ry-akin ayns irreeyn magh elley, lheid as Bun-chaghlaa yn Roosh ayns 1917, as chur ad rish faghtyssyn son dolley yn cleabaght as co-huffrageys. Ta ny feeuidyn as ny h-undinyssyn haink magh as foast jannoo kioneys er y pholitickaght Rangagh derrey'n laa t'ain jiu.
