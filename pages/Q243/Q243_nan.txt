Eiffel Thah (Hoat-gí: Tour Eiffel) sī tī Hoat-kok Paris Champ de Mars kóng-tiûⁿ téng-bīn ê thah, 1889 nî kiàn-siat ôan-sêng, siat-kè-chiá sī Gustave Eiffel. Eiffel Thah ê kôan-tō ū 324 m, iōng-tô͘ sī hòng-sàng kap kéng-koan. Tùi 1889 nî kàu 2009 nî, lóng-chóng ū chhiau-kòe 2-e̍k ê chham-koan jîn-chhù, sī Hoat-kok tī thong-sè-kài ê tāi-piáu-séng kiàn-tio̍k chi it.
