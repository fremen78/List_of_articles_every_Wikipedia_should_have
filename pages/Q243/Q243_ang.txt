Se Eiffel Torr (Frencisc: tour Eiffel) is geƿrohten īsern torr se stent on þǣm Marsƿongum in Paris, Francna hēafodbyrig. He hæfþ his naman æfter Gustave Eiffel, þæs ǣbod asettede þone torr and hine timbrode.
Ðone torr timbrode Eiffel in 1889 to bēon geat þæs 1889 Ƿorulde Gēarmarcet.
Se torr is 1,063 fēt hēah.  He is Parises hēasta gebold and se mǣste visited gemynding in eallum Middangearde: 6.98 þūsenda þūsen clymmede hine in 2011.
