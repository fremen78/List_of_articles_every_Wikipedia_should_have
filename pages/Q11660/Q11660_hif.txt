Artificial Intelligence, jiske AI, nai to A.I. bhi bola jaae hae, ek computer program, nai to masiin hae jon ki insaan ke rakam nawaa chij sikhe sake hae. Kabh kabhi AI ke neural network aur deep learning ke baare me baat kare khaatir bhi kaam me laawa jaae hae.

Kuch jaana-maana AI ke kaam me laae waale hae; search engines (jaise ki Google), video streaming (jaise ki YouTube, Netflix), human speech( jaise Google Assistant, Siri), creative toola (jaise ChatGPT, Apple Intelligence), strategy games (jaise chess).
Alan Turing pahila jan rahaa jon ki ii field me research karis rahaa jiske uu "machine intelligence" bolis rahaa. AI ke 1956 me University me parrhae ke suruu karaa gais rahaa.
