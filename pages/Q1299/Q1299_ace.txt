The Beatles nakeuh saboh kawan (grup) meusep aleran nu metal ngön pop/rock nyang meuasai di nanggroe Inggréh. The Beatles ka lheueh jipeuteubiet 13 boh album studio, nyakni Please Please Me, With the Beatles, A Hard Day's Night, Beatles For Sale, Help!, Rubber Soul, Revolver, Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band, Magical Mystery Tour, The Beatles, Yellow Submarine, Abbey Road ngon Let It Be. The Beatles meujaya nibak jipeuturi lagu-lagu lagee Love Me Do, A Hard Day's Night, Can't Buy Me Love, Help!, Yesterday, Yellow Submarine, Lucy In The Sky with Diamonds, Ob-La-Di Ob-La-Da, Helter Skelter, Strawberry Fields Forever ngon Hey Jude. Meunyo totaljih, album-album Beatles ka lheueh geupubloe trok 'an 1300 juta krek.
