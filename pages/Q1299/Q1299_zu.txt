Ama-Beatles kwakuyibhande elivela eLiverpool e-United Kingdom. Abadlali babo bomculo kwakunguJohn Lennon, uPaul McCartney, uGeorge Harrison noRingo Starr. Basizile ukwenza umculo othandwayo ngawo-1960s, futhi bayaphawuleka ngokusebenzisa izinhlobo eziningi ezahlukene zomsindo, ngokwesibonelo umculo wezidakamizwa waseNdiya noma wezengqondo. Kwakuyizinkanyezi emhlabeni wonke jikelele, nabantu abaningi benza 'Beatle mania' ngawo-1960. Bayibhande elithengwe kakhulu kunawo wonke esake futhi baba nenamba eningi.

UMcCartney noStarr basaqhubeka nomculo emhlabeni. Kodwa-ke, uLennon wabulawa ngo-1980 kwathi uHarrison wabulawa yisifo samaphaphu ngo-2001. Ngenxa yalezi zinto ezingezinhle, iqembu alisekho.
