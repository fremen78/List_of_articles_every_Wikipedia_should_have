She possan craa-chiaullee Sostynagh va The Beatles. Haink y possan ry-cheilley ayns Lerphoyll, Sostyn, 'sy vlein 1960. She nane jeh ny possanyn kiaullee smoo rahoil rere traght ayns shennaghys y phop-chiaull v'ayndaue. Va'n possan jeant seose jeh John Lennon (gitar ryddim, arraneyder), Paul McCartney (dord-ghitar, arraneyder), George Harrison (gitar stiuree, arraneyder) as Ringo Starr (drummyn, arraneyder).
