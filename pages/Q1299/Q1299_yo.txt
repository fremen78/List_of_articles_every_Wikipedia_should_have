The Beatles je egbe olorin Geesi rock, O je diddasile ni Liverpool ni 1960 be sini o je ikan ninu awon olorin to yori si rere julo ati ti o gba ayewo daada julo ninu itan orin togbajumo. Lati 1962 egbe olorin yi ni John Lennon (rhythm guitar, olukorin), Paul McCartney (bass guitar, vocals), George Harrison (lead guitar, vocals) ati Ringo Starr (ilu, vocals).
