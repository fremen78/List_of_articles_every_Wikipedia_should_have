The Beatles sī England Liverpool chhut-sin ê pop kap rock ga̍k-thôan. Chú-iàu ê sêng-ôan ū John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison chham Ringo Starr. Beatles sī 1960 nî-tāi hoat-chek, 1970 nî kái-sàn, tī chôan-sè-kài ê chhiòⁿ-phìⁿ siau-liōng chhiau-kòe 10-ek tiuⁿ. It-ti̍t-kàu 21 sè-kí, Beatles ê éng-hióng iah-koh chiâⁿ tōa.
