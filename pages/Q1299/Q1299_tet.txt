The Beatles ne'e grupu músiku pop rock ida husi Liverpool, Reinu Naklibur. The Beatles mós suksesu liu ho haklekar sira nia kansaun hanesan Hey Jude, Yellow Submarine, Yesterday, We Can Work It Out, Lucy in the Sky With Diamonds no Let It Be.
