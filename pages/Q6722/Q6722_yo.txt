Johann Carl Friedrich Gauss (pípè /ˈɡaʊs/;   listen , Látìnì: Carolus Fridericus Gauss) (30 April 1777 –  23 February 1855) je ara Germany onimo-mathematiki ati onimosayensi ti o ko ipa to se koko ninu opo omo ijinle, bi iro nomba, statistiki, ituyewo mathematiki, Jeometria oniyato, geodesy, geophysics, electrostatics, itorawo ati iriran. Won pe ni Princeps mathematicorum (Latini, fun "Arole awon Onimo-mathematiki" tabi "asiwaju awon onimo-mathematiki") ati "onimo-mathematiki olokikijulo lati atijo", Gauss ko ipa gidigidi ninu opo papa imo mathematiki ati sayensi. He referred to mathematics as "the queen of sciences."
