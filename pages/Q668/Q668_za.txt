Yindu roxnaeuz Yaenq Doh (印度, Vah Yindi:भारत गणराज्य) dwg aen guekgya youq baihnamz yacouh ndeu. De miz gijgvangq 316 fanh, daengx seiqgyaiq ndawde baiz daih caet. Daengz bi 2022, haeujvunz miz 14 ik, baiz daih ngeih. Baihnamz de caeuq Yinduyangz gaenhlaenz, baihbaek laenz Ahlahboz Haij, haijansen miz 7517 goengleix. Baihsae de dwg Bahgihswhdanj, baihbaek miz Cunghvaz Yinzminz Gunghozgoz, Nizbozwj caeuq Budanh. Soujduh de dwg Moq Dwzlij.
