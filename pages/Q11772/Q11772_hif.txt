== Purana itihaas ==
8th century BC me Greek logan parrhe aur likhe sikh liin rahin. Ii logan ek aur purana logan, Phoenicians se parrhe aur likhe sikhin. Ii ek des nai rahaa lekin dher chhota chhota des rahaa jiske "city states" bola jaawat rahaa.

Jab Greek logan ke number jaada hoe gais tab uu logan duusra jagha jaae ke aur nawaa city suruu karin. Pahila nawaa city Anatolia, fir Black sea me, fir Cyprus me, fir southern Italy, fir Sicily, aur baad me Libya me bhi banawa gais. Uu logan ek city, Naucratis, Nile naddi me bhi banae rahin. Aaj ke city Syracuse, Naples, Marseilles, aur Istanbul, Greek city , jiske naam Syracusa, Neapolis, Massilia aur Byzantium baad me bhais.
6th century talak kuchh cities aur important hoe gais, jaise ki Corinth, Thebes, Sparta, aur Athens.
