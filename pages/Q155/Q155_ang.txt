Brasil on mæðelword hatte Brasil seo Gebundene Cynewise (Portuglisc: República Federativa do Brasil), is þæt mǣste land on Sūðamerican. Manig land ymbligaþ hit norðweard, westƿeard and sūðweard. Ēastweard is sē garsecg. Þis land hæfþ 201,000,000 lēoda. Hēr sprecþ man Portuglisc. Brasilia is his hēafodburg.
Amāzanēa, micel flod, iernð ðurh ðis landes norþdæl fram Andes þæm beorgum oð þæm sæ. Geond þisre ea landum is deop weald.
