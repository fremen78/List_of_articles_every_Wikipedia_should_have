Pa-se (巴西), ia̍h-sī kiò chò Peh-la̍t-se-ní (伯剌西爾), choân-miâ Pa-se Liân-pang Kiōng-hô-kok (Phû-tô-gâ-gí: República Federativa do Brasil), sī Lâm Bí-chiu siāng tōa ê kok-ka, tī Lâm-bí tāi-lio̍k ê tang-pak-pêng. Tû-liáu Ecuador kap Chile í-gōa, kî-thaⁿ ê Lâm-bí kok-ka lóng ū piⁿ-kài hām Pa-se sio-tú.
