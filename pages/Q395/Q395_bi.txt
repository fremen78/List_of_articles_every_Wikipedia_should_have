Matematikis (Long Greek μάθημα máthēma, “stadi, laning”) em i stadi blong ol namba, jenis, ol shape, mo ol patern.
Pipol blong Matematikis ol i yusum Lojik blong solvem ol problem.  Ol i yusum dedaksen blong faenem niu samting we ol i faenem tru aot long ol samting we ol i faenem tru long bifo.
Matematikis inkludum ol stadi blong:

Namba: olsem wanem yumi kountem ol samting
Straksa: olsem wanem ol pipol organaes ol samting.  Nem blong pat ia blong matematikis Aljebra.
Ples: ol ples blong olgeta samting mo ol mesurmen blong saes mo distans.  Nem blong pat ia blong matematikis Jiometri.
