Mairiage, is a socially or ritually recognised union or legal contract atween marraes that estaiblishes richts an obligations atween them, atween thaim an thair bairns, an atween thaim an thair guid-faimlie.
Oothru historie, mairiage haes taken on sinderie forms, an in mair recent times, because o the gay richts muivement, mairiage atween marraes o the same sex or gender becam legal in monie kintras athort the warld.
