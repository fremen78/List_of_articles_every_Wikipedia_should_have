Kogin Indus shine mafi girma kogin gẽfen rãfi na yamma na kudu Nahiyar Asiya. Yana daya daga cikin koguna bakwai masu tsarki na mabiya addinin Hindu. Ita ce asalin asalin wayewar kwarin Indus . Ita ce kogin Pakistan mafi tsayi. Tana fitarwa kimanin mita dubu 6,600 a sakan daya. Yana gudana ne ta cikin China (Western Tibet), India da Pakistan. Yana ɗayan manyan kogunan Tekun Indo-Gangetic
Kogin yana da tsayin kilomita 3180. Itace kogin Pakistan mafi tsayi. Kogin yana da duka yankin magudanan ruwa da ya wuce 1,165,000 km2 (450,000 sq mi). Yawan kudinta na shekara-shekara yana kusan 207 km3 (50 cu mi), yana mai da shi babban kogi na ashirin da daya a duniya dangane da yawan gudana shekara.
