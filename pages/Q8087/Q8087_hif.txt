Geometry, Mathematics ke ek hissa hae, jisme chij ke size, shape aur position ke parrha jaae hae. Geometry me shapes ke 2D (plane), 3D (solid), 4D , 5D, 6D.... me study karaa jaae hae. Plane geometry ke kuchh shapes hae square, triangle and circle, aur solid geometry ke kuchh shape hae cube, cylinder aur sphere
