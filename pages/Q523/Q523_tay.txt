bzngah (星星) 
yasa qu pkaki' kya babaw kayal ka ttaypuq balay ru pyax, pyax smyax ktan.  pnawngan pintkaykay' na nbkis sraral hya' qu bzngah qani hya' ga ramu' na wagi' ka wal bun na squliq maha ma. yalaw gi minsazing qu wagi' mru mkilux iayl qu kayal lmru, stu' naha' sa sazing mrkyas mlikuy musa' smbu' lma, nanu' bun naha' qu wagi' lga mprpus tay babaw kayal qu ramu' niya' lru yasa qu nyux ta' ktan ka bzngah qani la.
