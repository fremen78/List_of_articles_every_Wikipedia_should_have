Sima Qian (Chinese bhasa:司马迁), Han Dynasty me Emperor Wu ke niche ek itihaas ke likhe waala jan rahaa. Uu Xia Yang (aaj ke Shan Xi Province, Han City) me paida bhaes rahaa. Uske pitaji, Sima Tan, bhi ek itihaas ke likhe waala jan rahaa.


== Jindagi ==
Jab Sima Qian 10 saal ke rahaa, tb uu Classical Chinese me likhe aur parrhe sakat rahaa.
Uu China bhar ghum ke dher jagha dekhis jab ki uu 20 saal ke rahaa.
108 BC me, jab uske pitaji ke maut bhaes, tab uu uske historian ke kaam kare laga.
104 BC me, Sima Qian, Taichu Calendar ke banae ke khalaas karis.
99 BC me, Sima Qian, Li Ling Affair me fas gais. Uske jahel bheja gais aur uske chaahe maut ke sentence mile ke rahaa nai to castration. Kahe ki uske lage paisa nai rahaa, uu castration ke accept karis aur aapan lindagi ke akhri 19 saal aapan baare me buk likhe me bitais.
