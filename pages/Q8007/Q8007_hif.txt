Franklin Delano Roosevelt (30 January 1882 - 12 April 1945) New York state ke governor aur America ke 32nd President rahaa.

Roosevelt 1932 ke election me President Herbert Hoover ke harae ke President banaa. Hoover, Great Depression ke kaaran bahut  unpopular rahaa.
