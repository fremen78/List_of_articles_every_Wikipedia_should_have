Inyama ukudla okuvela esilwaneni. Jikelele lokhu kusho imisipha yomzimba kuhlananisa namafutha awo.

Inyama yinyama yesilwane edliwa njengokudla. [1] Abantu baye bazingela futhi babulala izilwane ukuze bathole inyama kusukela ezikhathini zangaphambi komlando. Ukufika kwempucuko kwavumela ukufuywa kwezilwane ezinjengezinkukhu, izimvu, onogwaja, izingulube nezinkomo. Lokhu ekugcineni kwaholela ekusetshenzisweni kwabo ekukhiqizeni inyama esikalini sezimboni ngosizo lwendawo zokuhlatshwa.
Inyama yakhiwa kakhulu ngamanzi, amaprotheni, namafutha. Iyadliwa iluhlaza, kepha imvamisa iyadliwa ngemuva kokuthi iphekiwe futhi ifakwe umunyu noma icutshungulwe ngezindlela ezahlukahlukene. Inyama engalungisiwe izokonakala noma ibole kungakapheli amahora noma izinsuku ngenxa yokutheleleka nokubola kwamagciwane nesikhunta.
