Raffaello Sanzio, jiske jaada kar ke Raphael ke naam se jaana jaawe hae (6 April 1483 - 6 April 1520) ek Renaissance painter aur architect rahaa. Leonardo da Vinci aur Michelangelo ke saate, uu High Renaissance ke ek bahut achchhaa painter rahaa.
Raphaelke janam ek chhota Central Italian city, Urbino jon ki Marche region me hae,  me bhaes rahaa, jahan pe uske pitaji, Giovanni Santi ek court painter rahaa.  
Uske Madonna aur Christ Child aur Vatican in Rome, Italy ke painting khatir jaana jaawe hae.
