Rusiya Prisidinti (1941-1953).
Awki: Vissarion Dzhugashvili; Tayka: Yekaterina Gueladze.Warmi: Ekaterina Svanidze (1906–07) /  Nadezhda Alliluyeva (1919–32).Yuqanaka: Yakov Dzhugashvili, Vasily Dzhugashvili & Svetlana Alliluyeva.
