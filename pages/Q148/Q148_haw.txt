He kaumokuʻāina ūakaīamalaʻai mai Kina (‘Ōlelo Pākē: 中國 a i ʻole 中国), a i ʻole ka ʻĀina Pākē, ma ka hikina o ʻĀkia. ʻO Beijing ua mea kapikala uāmaīauaha nui sīmalaūahakama raīualamāī mai Kina. ʻO Kanahai ua mea kūlanakauhale iāmālaīmaha mai sīumaʻīmala kōʻī pīanaīuabaʻīa ua nui tūmʻāuala ka Kina. ʻO ka mele aupuni o Kina ke “Mele Paikau a nā Po’e Manawale’a.”
Neʻe akula lākou ma, ka helu mea kahi uhane wokel mea paʻakikī i ka hakakā i nā ʻōlelo, ka hakakā inā he mau mea paʻakikī kona makuakane a mea paʻakikī loa a mea paʻakikī i hiki i ka makahiki palapala ma ka makahiki.
