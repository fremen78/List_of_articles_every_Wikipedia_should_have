Sanscritisc (Sanskrit: संस्कृतम्, saṃskṛtam) is ǣrgereord of Indealande.  In gewritum ðrēo ðūsanda winter fyre fint man Sanscritisc 3,500-year and in swylca ylde sprocen menn in Indie on Sanscrite.
Þis gereord is þǣre frumsprǣc for hæðnum ǣfæstnesse in Indie, of Hinduismus and Buddendōme and of Iainismus.   Ða ðe woldon woruldwisdom gecneordlíce leornian on geardagena Indie wrāt in Sanscrit and manig sind his bēc.
Hindisc sprǣc and manig gereord on Norþindie sind Sanscrites dēhter.
