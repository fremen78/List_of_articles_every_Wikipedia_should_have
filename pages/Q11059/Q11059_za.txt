Vahfan（संस्कृता वाक् saṃskṛtā vāk,  संस्कृतम् saṃskṛtam） gvi Yindu-yajlizan yijcih duh Yindi - Yilangj yijcuz. Caeuq Vahlahding ityiengh, vahfan gaenq baenz guh yozsuz coenggyau dih cienmonz yunghyij.
Bouh sawging Yindugyau 《Veda》 couh dwg yungh sawfan sij baenz. Gij fapvah caeuq fatyaem cungj hawj yawj guh cungj gveigawj coenggyau ndeu baujrom.
