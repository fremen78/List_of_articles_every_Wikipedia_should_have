Shakanhi, Shanhi (nge. sheet of paper; paper) kana bepa, chinhu chitete chakagadzirwa ne marise ehuni akatsindirwa pamwe chete. Vanhu vanonyora pashakanhu nepenzura kana karamu, uye man'wadhi ne mamagazini anogadzirwa ne mashakanhi. Shakanhi rinogona kunwririra mutuvi senge mvura, saka vanhu vanogona kuchenesa zvinhu neshakanhi. Kune mhando dzakawanda dzeshakanhi. Vanhu vakatanga kugadzira mashakanhi waive Machayina.


=== Mamwe mashoko ===
MuChiswahiri - Bepa rinonzi karatasi kutora shoko reChigiriki Χαρτί kharti rakabva wo kuChiarabhu قرطاص qartas.
MuChishona rinenge richinzi kaharati kutora shoko reChigiriki kana karatasi futi kutora shoko reChiarabhu.

Xani / Kġani (kureva a form of paper) - kubva paChizulu chinoti ikhasi, kana kungoti fomu sepaChishongirishi.
