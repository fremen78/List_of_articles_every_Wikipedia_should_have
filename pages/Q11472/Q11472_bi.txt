Pepa i wan ting, pipol ol i mekem long fiber long wud mo rags.  Pipol i raet long pepa, mekem olgeta buk long pepa.  Pepa absorbs wora, pipol i usum pepa blong wasem ol ting.

Pipol i bin mekem pepa fastaem long Jaena long 105 B.K.  Long Jaena, pepa i kamtru long India, long Middle East, mo long Itali.  Long Inglan i bin mekem wan masin blong mekem pepa before 1812.
