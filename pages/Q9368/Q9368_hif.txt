== Kaam ==
Kareja dehin me kuchh khaaskaam kare hae:

Kareja bile banae hae
Kareja glucose  ke store kare hae.
Kareja protein aur fat ke glucose me badle hae.
Kareja kuchh fat aur cholesterol bi banae hae.
Kareja khuun me ke dher cij ke turre hae, jaise ki:
hemoglobin
proteins, jaise ki enzymes, insulin, aur serum amyloid a
Ammonia
poison ke dehin me se nikale hae
Liver vitamins aur minerals ke store kare hae
Liver protein banae hae:
protein, jon ki khuun ke clot  kare hae, iske coagulation proteins bola jaawe hae.
protein, jaise ki albumin
