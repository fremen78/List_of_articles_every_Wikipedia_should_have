Tasa tga yan usknaf amqqṛan g udis n ufgan, tga yat tgzzumt sg ungraw ahḍm. Ar takka tasa yan uḥruray ilan s wassaɣ Tawraɣt (Aḥruray awraɣ), ar tskar tasa ma izrin 300 n twwri, sgitsnt kraḍt twwriwin imqqurn : Tawwri n Tarda, tawwri n tanɣruft d tawwri n uxzan. Tga tasa yan uwlsis ittɛdaln Tawraɣt d tugt n tɣawsiwin yaḍni. Dars yat tilalt bahra imqqurn g taɣaṛt n idammn . Tga tasa asknaf g ggutn iẓɣṛan.
