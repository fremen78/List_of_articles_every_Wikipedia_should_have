Tasnudert neɣ tudrussna d yiwet n tɣult n tussna i d-ilhan ɣef yakk imuddiren ama d iɣersiwen neɣ d imɣan neɣ d adalen. Taɣult-is tejmel-d tussna n ugama d imuddien s umata, maca imi tudert ugten-t deg-s talɣiwin d isellumen i ɣef tebna, tasnudert tetteṭṭef-d seg uswir alayan, ɣer win n tsilunt, d wudus, alamma d tagudt d unagraw awenḍan.
