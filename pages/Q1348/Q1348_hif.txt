Kolkata (Bengali: কলকাতা), jiske 1 January 2001 se pahile Calcutta naam rahaa, India ke West Bengal state ke capital ciy hae. Iske abaadi (2001 census) 4,580,544 rahaa. Ii India ke duusra sab se barraa city (Mumbai ke baad) hae. 
Ii  Hooghly Naddi ke East bank pe hae.  Kolkata, British Raj ke niche India ke capital 1911 talak rahaa.
