Ing Karayatmalatan (Ingles: Ocean, Tagalug: Karagatan), o Kadayatmalatan, metung yang katáwan ning danum king yatu. Ating limang karayatmalatan: Pasipiku (Pacific), Atlantiku (Atlantic), Indiku (Indian), Kemaulian (Southern) at Artiku (Arctic).
