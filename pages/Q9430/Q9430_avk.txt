Welfa wetce divatcepe dem lavok wale toloya ok loa tadava tawavapon zo tentur. Dere tickir lavakrotc dun tuwarzana gan birasalt. Mon 70.8 % ke tawavafa welma tir besanafa gu welfeem. Agvavon, va aluboya welfa ( is jontika bira ) patat.
Mialukafe aludeve ke welfeem tir vas 3682 m-. Ane midafe titsuye vas 11 020 m- koe Marianna kela tigir.
