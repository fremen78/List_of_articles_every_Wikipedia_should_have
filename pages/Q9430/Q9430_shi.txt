Iga ugaru (Ar nttini ula yill nɣ d yill amaḍlan) yan ugraw n waman amaraɣ da ittamẓn attayn n 70.8% n wakal, yamẓ 97% n waman n wakal. 
Ismawn myanawnin ar ttusmrasn afad ad ttussann smmust n tmnaḍin myanawnin n ugaru: wad irggign, aṭlanti, ahindi, iffus d arktik (kullu imẓẓin). Aman n ugaru yumẓ attayn n 361,000,000 km2 n wakal.
