The Tale of Genji,jiske Genji Monogatari ke naam se bhi jaana jaae hae, 11th century me likha gais Japanese literature hai. Iske ek noblewoman, kavi aur rani ke personal assistant likhis rahaa. isme ii time ke raja logan ke ghar me rahan sahan ke baare me bataawa gais hae. 
Iske ek puraana bhasa me likha gais rahaa. 20th century ke suruu me Genji ke Japanese bhasa me anuwaad karaa gais rahaa aur 1882 me iske English me anuwaad karaa gais.
Ii novel me Emporer ke larrkaa ke baare me likha gais he. Kaaheki karrka ke maiyaa low-rank ke rahii, Emperor uske line of succession me se hatae diis rahaa, uske ek saadharan admi ke title diis aur uske naam ke bhi badal diis rahaa. II itihaas ke pahila novel hae jiske ek aurat likhis hae aur Japan ke aaj-kal Shakespeare ke rakam maana jaae hae.
