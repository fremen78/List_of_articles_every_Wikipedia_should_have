Cape Town, South Africa ke legislative capital city hae.
Ii South Africa ke southern coast me hae.
1486 me Portuguese explorer, Bartolomew Diaz iske naam "Cape of Storms" rakkhis , lekin Portugal ke raja iske naam badal ke "Cape of Good Hope" rakh diis.
Ii city ke khaas landmark, Table Mountain hae.
