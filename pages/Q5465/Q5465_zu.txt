IKapa (isiBhunu: Kaapstad; isiXhosa: iKapa) yisidlavela elikwisifundazwe  sentshonalanga Kapa, eNingizimu Afrika. IKapa esinye sesidlavela ezintathu eziyiziqongo, futhi iKapa liyikomkhulu lendlu yoMgwamanqa waseNingizimu Afrika. Liyisiqongo sokushaywa komthetho ezweni, liyisidlavela esidala ezweni, lilele isibili lilandela isidlavela saseGoli. Liteketiswa ngokuthi isidlavela esinguMama, liyisidlavela esikhulu eNtshoanalanga Kapa, futhi liphethwe uMkhandlu waseKapa. Ezinye izidlavela eziyiziqongo, iPitoli, okuyisiqongo sesiGungu sezwe, lapho uMongameli ehlezi khona,  kanye ne-Bloemfontein, okuyisiqongo sezokwaHlulela, lapho iNkundla ePhakeme yokuDluliswa kwamacala ihlezi khona.


== Umlando ==
Ekapa abantu bakhuluma isiXhosa, isiBhunu nesiNgisi. Baningi abantu khona.
