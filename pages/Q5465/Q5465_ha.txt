Cape Town birni ne, da ke a ƙasar Afirka ta Kudu. kuma ɗaya daga cikin manyan birane kwara uku na kasar ta Afirka ta Kudu (wasu biyu suna Pretoria da Bloemfontein). Shi ne babban birnin lardin Western Cape. Cape Town yana da yawan jama'a 3,740,026, bisa ga ƙidayar shekarar 2011. An gina birnin Cape Town a shekara ta 1652.
