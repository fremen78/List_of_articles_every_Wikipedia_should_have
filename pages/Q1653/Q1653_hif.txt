Danube, Europe ke duusra sab se barraa naddi hae. (Volga sab se lamba hae). Ii European Union ke sab se lamba naddi hae. Danube 2,860 km (1,777 mile) lamba hae. Danube 10 des me se bahe hae: Germany, Austria, Slovakia, Hungary, Croatia, Serbia, Romania, Bulgaria, Moldova aur Ukraine. Ii naddi Black Sea me jaae ke nikle hae. 
Kuchh city jon ki iske kiare hae: Ulm aur Regensburg, Germany me ; Linz aur Vienna, Austria me; Bratislava, Slovakia me, Budapest Hungary me; Novi Sad aur Belgrade Serbia me.
