Asif n Danub (s talmant : Donau, s tasluvakt : Dunaj, s tahungarit : Duna, s tabulgart : Dunav, s tarumanit : Dunărea, s tukṛaniyt : Dunay), asif n Turuft talemmast, d wasif wis sin i ɣezzif akk deg Turuft deffir Volga akked 2 850 km deg teɣzi.
Aɣbalu-s seg idurar n Tiẓgi Taberkant deg amalu n Lalman ed i yettawi ɣer Ilel Aberkan, yetteɛeddi ɣef 10 n timura : Lalman, Tutrict, Sluvakya, Maǧar, Kruwatya, Serbya, Bulgarya, Rumanya, Muldavya, ed Ukrayin, yetteɛeddi daɣen ɣef timanaɣin timeqqranin n Turuft em Vin, Budapest, Bratislava d Belgrad.
