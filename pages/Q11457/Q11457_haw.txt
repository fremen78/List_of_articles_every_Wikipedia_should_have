ʻO ka māno i ke kaopa, he mea hoʻokaheispai e ulu i loko o kahi, a i ʻole hihia e pale ai, a puni nā hua o nā mea kanu laʻa o ka genus Kokipim (lākina.: Gossypium) i ka ʻohana mallow Malwāea. ʻAkahi ponoʻī kāwili pili ʻana i ka melīaʻie. Ma lalo o nā kūlana kūlohelohe, e hoʻonui ka pipi wili i ka palu i nā hua.
