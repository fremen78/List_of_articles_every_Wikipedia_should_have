Han Samrajya 202 BC me taagat me aais. Ii logan Confucianism aur Legalism ke maanat rahin. Iske Han Sythesis bola jaawat rahaa. Ii dynasty ke niche, China arts aur science me tarakki karis. Ii samrajya barraa hote gais. China duusra des se trade kare lagaa. Merchant log Silk Road le ke China pahunchin. Ii dynasty ke time China me Buddhism aais.
