Chigiriki (mu Chigiriki cheikozvino: Ελληνικά (Elliniká); Chigiriki chekare: Ἑλληνική (Hellēnikḗ) ibazi rakazvimirira remhuri yemitauro yeIndo-Yuropu, yakazvarwa kuVugiriki, Saipurasi, Arubhanya, mamwe matunhu eMedhiteraniya rekuMabvazuva neBhahari Tema. Iyo ine yakarebesa yakanyorwa nhoroondo yechero mupenyu Indo-Yuropu mutauro, inotora angangoita makore mazana matatu nemazana mana ezvinyorwa zvakanyorwa.


=== Bumbiro re mabhi eChigiriki ===
Tarisai mahatikoru: Bumbi reMabhi eChigiriki uye Horutogirafi ye Chigirki
Chigiriki chine mabhii makumi maviri ne ina (mambiri-ina), rimwe ne rimwe rine fomu re nyorwauzuru (uppercase) ne nyorwadzasi (lowercase). Bhii ra sigima rine wedzerwa re fomu re nyorwadzasi (ς) rinoshandiswa muchinzvimbo ye kupedzisira/pakuguma.
