Al khwarizmi  (arabic philosophy) koo Muhammad Musa Al khwarizmi (0 nyaɗɗo maareɗo hiitande ndunngu c.780-c850) e persian scholar ngaaɗo kowmgal keemiyaaje nder anndal nyalɗe e limngal too  les astronomy e nder tsarewal ndaatal kowtarku ɓendetol jannde geografiwol


== yimmɓe ==
Muhammad ibn Ahmed Al khwarizmi 10th century e encyclopedist ngannɗo benndol mafatih ulum(mabburngol sciences
Abu bakr Muhammad b.Al abbas Al khwarizmi o arabic poet e nder ɓenndol(934-93)
Al khwarizmi Al lati 11th century alchemist
Shuja Al khwarizmi (d.861) o innajo abbasid caliph ja'far Al mutawakkil


== Ennare daygo ==
Al khwarizmi (crater) taaguɗo tar sise of the moon waatu anndal lewru Innde maako ɓaawo Muhammad ibn Musa Al khwarizmi
Khwarizmi o heebeti shaidawol international award taa anndal bincikeje keengal jaaɓego e nder anndal science e anndal technolog
