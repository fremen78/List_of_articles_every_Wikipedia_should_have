Enrico Fermi (1901 nî 9 goe̍h 29 ji̍t – 1954 nî 11 goe̍h 28 ji̍t) sī Í-tāi-lī ê bu̍t-lí-ha̍k-ka, i kiàn-li̍p tē 1 chō goân-chú-lô͘ (nuclear reactor), tùi liōng-chú le̍k-ha̍k, hu̍t-chú bu̍t-lí-ha̍k (nuclear physics), lia̍p-chú bu̍t-lí-ha̍k kap thóng-kè le̍k-ha̍k ê hoat-tián lóng ū toā kòng-hiàn. Fermi tī 1938 nî the̍h tio̍h Nobel Bu̍t-lí-ha̍k Chióng.
