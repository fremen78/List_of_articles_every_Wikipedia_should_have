IParisi (|p|ær|ɨ|s|; isiFulentshi: |paʁi|) iyisiqongo saseFulentshi. Ngesikhathi pambuso iRhomani babiza ngokuthi "Lutetia". Namhlanje igama lakhe ngu-"isidolobha ekhanya" (isiFulansi: "La Ville-Lumière").


== Umlando ==
Ngo-52 BC, Julius Caesar wahlula amaParisii. AmaRoma aqambe Parisii "Lutetia".
