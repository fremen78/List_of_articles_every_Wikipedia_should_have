Empire State Building, New York City, United States me ek bahut uuncha building hae.  Ii 381 meters (1,250 feet) uuncha hae aur ii 102 storey ke hae. Chalis saal talak ii dunia ke sab se uuncha building rahaa. Iske naam New York ke nuckname, The Empire State se aais hae. Iske 1931 me banae ke khalaas karaa gais rahaa.
