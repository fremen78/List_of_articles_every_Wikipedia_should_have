Tamnekda Tarumanit, (s talatint :  Imperium romanum), d tallit deffir Tagduda Tarumanit, d tamnekda taqburt (27 s.t.-S-Ɛ – 395 / 476 / 1453), tamanaɣt-ynes tamezwarut d Roma, asmi teǧhed maḍi deg 117, tamnekda-nni tella d tilin tasertant ed tanmettit tameqqrant akk deg taɣerma utaram.
