þæt Rōmānisce Rīce (Læden: Imperium Romanum) ƿæs þrāg þǣm stǣr þāra ealdra Rōmāne mid selfƿealdende ƿieldung. Hit folgode æfter þǣre Rōmāniscan Cyneƿīsan (510 BC - 1e gearhundred BC), þæt æfter sacu betƿeonan Gaius Marius and Sulla and ingefeoht betƿeonan Gaius Iulius Caesar, Pompeie, and Marcus Brutus ƿæs ƿāc.
