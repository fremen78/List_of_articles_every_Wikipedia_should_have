Wine ek nasaa kare waala drink hae. WIne jaada kar ke anguur se bane hae lekin kabhi abhi plums aur balckberies se banaa dring ke bhi wine bola jaawe hae. 
Niche ke suchi me uu des hae jahan pe sab se jaada wine piwa jaae hae.:
Luxembourg, France, Italy, Portugal, Croatia, Switzerland, Spain, Argentina, Uruguay, and Slovenia.
Des jahan pe sab se jaada Wine banawa jaae hae: 
France, Italy, Spain, USA, Argentina, Germany, Australia, South Africa, Portugal, and Chile.
