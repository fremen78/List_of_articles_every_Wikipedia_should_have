Tola yito leembota vertebrata poikilotermik, u hetumula to taluhu wawu hemohilawo lo insang. Tola botiya leembo'a lo vertebrata u ngohuntuwa, jumulaliyo limbata to'u 27.000 to duniya. Tola hebiyahu lo tawu pohamala tapuliyo.
Damango tola herupa-rupawa monto u kikingo 7 mm sambe u damangaa da'a 14 meter.


== Ekologi ==
Tola mowali tumumula to nga'amila taambati otaluhu, taluhu deheto taluhu bulalo, to taluhe mooti meyalo taluhe modelomo.
