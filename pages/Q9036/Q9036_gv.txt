She ynlaghteyr, jeshaghteyr lectragh, jeshaghteyr obbrinagh, as foddey-reayrtagh va Nikola Tesla (Serbish: Никола Тесла; 10 Jerrey Souree [SA: 28 Mean Souree] 1856 – 7 Jerrey Geuree 1943). She seyraanagh Americaanagh jeh sluight Croitagh-Serbagh v'eh, ta share er enney kyndagh rish e cho-hoyrtyssyn rish kiaddey yn chorys kiarail lectraghys fo stroo kirkinagh (AC).
