Sętal [ˈsɛntal] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dywity, within Olsztyn County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Dywity and 13 km (8 mi) north of the regional capital Olsztyn. It is located in Warmia.
