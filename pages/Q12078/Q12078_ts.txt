Fukuzana i vuvabyi lebyi hlaselaka no hombolokisa swiaki swa miri naswona byithlela byi hangalakela eka swirho swin'wana swa miri. Ahi marhumba hinkwawo yalendzeni lama vangiwaka hi vuvabyi bya fukuzana, naswona marhumba lama khumbekeke ama hangalakeli eka swirho swin'wana. Swikombiso swin'wana swa vuvabyi lebyi swihlanganisa kupfelela ka rhanga emirini, ku huma mongolo, kukhohlola loku nga heriki, ku ondza loku nga hlamuselekiku na ku chuluka. Hambileswi swikombiso leswi swingava swihlavutela vuvabyi bya Fukuzana, swinga thlela swi komba mavabyi man'wana. Kuna mixaka ya kutlula dzana, ya vuvabyi bya Fukuzana.
