ibisakuzo, amavumvu, amasare, amahigi, amagorane, ibitongero (mu kuragura, guterekera, gutukura umwana, kwambika imana zeze,

guhura, kugangahura, guhanura, kugombora, kuroga nâ€™ibindi)
