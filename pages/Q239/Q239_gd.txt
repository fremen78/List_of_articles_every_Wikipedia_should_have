'S e cleasaiche às A' Bheilg a tha ann an Jean-Claude Van Damme (ainm bho thùs: Jean-Claude Camille François Van Varenberg). Rugadh e 18 an Dàmhair 1960 ann am Berchem-Sainte-Agathe faisg air a' Bhruiseal. 
Seo cuid de na filmichean as ainmeile anns an robh e' cluich: 

Bloodsport (1988)
Kickboxer (1989)
Double Impact (1991)
Universal Soldier (1992)
Hard Target (1993)
Timecop (1994)
JCVD (2008)
