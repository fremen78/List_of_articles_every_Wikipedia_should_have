Makai,nai to Indian corn, ek rakam ke anaaj hae jiske pahile Central America ke log bowat rahin. Makai ke insaan khaae hae, jaanwar ke bhi kkhawaaya jaae hae aur ethanol banaawa jaae hae. II Mexico, South America aur Central america ke log ke khaas khaana hae.
Makai ke patti se lapeta kernels rahe jisme biyaa hae. Ii ek "angiosperm" hae jiske matlab hae ki iske biyaa biyaa fal nai to shell ke bhittar hae. United States aur Canada me iske corn bola jaae hae. 
Dunia bhar me wajan ke hisaab se ii sab se se jaada paida karaa jaae waala anaaj hae. 2020 me dunia ke production, 1.1 billion tonnes rahaa.
