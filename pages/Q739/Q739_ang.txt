Colombia (Spēonisce: Colombia), seo on mæðelword hatte Colombia seo Cynewise (on Spēonisce hātte República de Colombia), is norþƿesterne land on Sūðamerican, þe hæfþ rīcu on American Middelgeard. Hit gebyrdaþ norþƿesterne Panama, easterne Feneswela and Brasil, and hit gebyrdaþ sūðerne Ecuador and Peru. His hēofodburg is Bogotá.
