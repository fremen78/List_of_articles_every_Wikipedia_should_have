Tga Kulumbya nɣ Tagduda n Kulumbya s wudm amaddud yat tmazirt tamnanaḍt illan ɣ Tmrikt n Unẓul. Tili tmazirt ad ɣ ugzzum agafan n tmnẓawt, ilkm ubuju nns 1,141,748 Kilumitr².
Nttat waḥdu tt gr tmizar n Tmrikt n Unẓul ad iṭṭafn iftasn nna nn ittaggʷan f Ugaru Irggign d Yill Akaribi.
Timizar da ṭṭafnin igmirn d Kulumbya gant tnt: Vinizwilla (agmuḍ), Biru (anẓul), Ikwaḍuṛ (anẓul utrim) d Banama (ataram). Timizar da ṭṭafnin igmirn d Kulumbiya gant tnt: Nikaragwa, Banama, Kuṣṭa Rika, Gwatimala, Hunḍuṛas, Jamayka, Hayiti d Tgduda Taduminikant.
Uggar n 51 id mlyun n ummdnu ad izdɣn ɣ Kulumbya. Tga tiss kraḍt s wuṭṭun n imzdaɣn ɣ Tmrikt Talatint ḍaṛat n Miksik d Bṛazil.


== Anamk ==
Tettusmma tmazirt ad f yism n Kristufr Kulumbus da igan iminig nna issntan asdurrri awṛubban n Tmrikin.
