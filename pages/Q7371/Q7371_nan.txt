Federico Fellini (1471 nî 5 goe̍h 21 ji̍t – 1528 nî 4 goe̍h 6 ji̍t) sī Ì-tāi-lī ê tiān-iáⁿ tō-ián kiam pian-kio̍k. I-ê hong-keh to̍k-te̍k, pī jīm-ûi sī 20 sè-kí siōng-kài ū éng-hióng-le̍k ê úi-tāi tiān-iáⁿ chè-chok-jîn chi-it. Fellini tī Ì-tāi-lī ê Rimini chhut-sì, āu-lâi tī Roberto Rossellini ê pang-chō͘ hā, khai-sí chham-ú tiān-iáⁿ ê chè-chok. Fellini chan-keng sian-hō͘ 5 pái tit-tio̍h Oscar Chióng ê khéng-tēng. 1993 nî 10 goe̍h 31 hō, i in-ūi sim-chōng khām-khe̍h kap ho͘-khip hē-thóng kong-lêng-sèng chiòng-gāi put-tī, tàm Lô-má ê pēⁿ-īⁿ óng-seng. Āu-lâi, Ì-tāi-lī chèng-hú choan-bûn ūi i kí-hêng chi̍t tiûⁿ kok-chòng.
