Nit is a general tairm faur the muckle, freuch, glittie seeds or fruit o wheen plaunts. While a braid kynd o dried seeds an fruits are cried nits, anely a certaint nummer o thaim are conseedert bi biologists tae be richt nits. Nits is an important springheid o nutrients faur baith fowk an wildlife.
Aw nits is seeds, bit no aw seeds is nits. Nits is baith the seed an the fruit, an cannae be separatit. Seeds came frae fruit, an can be tirred fae the fruit, liek awmonds, cashews, walnuts, an pistachios, whilk wis yince inby fruit.
