Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) ek international rajnaitik aur aarthik sangathaan hai dakshin - poorv eshiya ke deesh ke liye. 8 August 1967 me Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Singapore, aur Philippines ASEAN ke banais rahaa. Uske bad aur sadassa ko bhi srikar kya giya tha. 
Iye wo desh ka aarthik babshta ko aur accha karne ke liye hai. Uska motto hai "One Vision, One Identity, One community" 
Pahile khaali paanch des ASEAN me rahaa . Brunei, 8 January 1984 me ASEAN ke join karis. Vietnam, 28 July 1995 ke, aur Laos aur Myanmar 23 July 1997 aur Cambodia 30 April 1999 ke ASEAN ke join karis. 
Dunia ke lagbhag 8% population ASEAN des me rahe hae. Lagbhag 240 million Muslims ii des me rahe hae, jaada kar ke Indonesia, Malaysia, aur Brunei me. Lagbhag 170 million Buddhists jaaada kar ke Thailand, Myanmar, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam aur Singapore me rahe hae.
