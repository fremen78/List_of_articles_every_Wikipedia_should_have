The Ajose awon Orile-ede Guusuilaorun Asia (Geesi: Association of Southeast Asian Nations), to je ke kuru si ASEAN (pípè /ˈɑːsi.ɑːn/ AH-see-ahn, occasionally /ˈɑːzi.ɑːn/ AH-zee-ahn ni Geesi, to je ede isise won), je agbajo oloselu jeografi ati tekonomi awon orile-ede 10 ti won budo si Guusuilaorun Asia, to je didasile ni 8 August 1967 latowo Indonesia, Malaysia, awon Philippines, Singapore ati Thailand.
