Flamenco (Spaingie pronunciation: [flaˈmeŋko]) is an airtform an genre o muisic an dance native tae the soothren Spaingie regions o Andalusie, Extremadura an Murcia. It includes cante (sangin), toque (guitar playin), baile (dance) an jaleo, which refers tae the vocalisations an rhythmic soonds o palmas (haundclappin) an pitos (finger snappin).
