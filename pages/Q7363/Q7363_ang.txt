Nasu is ōst on bodige hrycgbānedra dēor þe hæfþ þā nasþyrelu, þe innimaþ and ūtsendaþ lyfte ēðunge ætgædere mid mūþe. Beæftan þǣre nase is sēo gestences dylstan rind and þā pohhan. Beæftan þǣm nasholhe; lyft nīehst gǣþ þurh þā fōreþrote, þe is gemǣnsumod mid þǣra meltunge dǣla gæderunge, and þǣræfter on þā ēðunge dǣla gæderunge. On mannum sitt sēo nasu on þǣm andƿiltan; on manigum ōðrum sycedēorum sitt hēo on þǣm uferran ende þæs ceafles
