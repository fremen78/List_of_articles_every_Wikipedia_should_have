IsiXeko saseVatican, ngokusemthethweni iSixeko saseVatican (ngesiLatini : Status Civitatis Vatican√¶ ), ekwabizwa ngokuba yiVatican (okanye ukugqwesa kweSaint Peter's, okanye ngokungafanelekanga iHoly See), sisixeko esivaliweyo sosingasiqithi weItali, elona lizwe lincinane elizimeleyo ehlabathini ngokumalunga nabemi (abemi abangama-869)  kunye nokwandiswa kommandla, eyesithathu ngokobuninzi babantu.  Njengohlobo lorhulumente bubukhosi obunyuliweyo obupheleleyo . 
Isixeko -state, esakhiwe nge - 7 kaJuni 1929 kunye neLateran Pacts, esayinwe nge - 11 kaFebruwari waloo nyaka mnye phakathi kukaBenito Mussolini kunye noNobhala weKhadinali welizwe uPietro Gasparri, ngokulandelelana abameli boBukumkani baseItali kunye neHoly See, ' I-enclave kumhlaba weRiphabhlikhi yase-Italiya, efakwe kwilaphu ledolophu yesixeko saseRoma . EbuRhulumenteni kukho ulawulo lobukumkani obupheleleyo , olukhokelwa nguPontifi Owongamileyo weCawa yamaKatolika . Ulwimi olusemthethweni sisiTaliyane, ngelixa isiLatini silulwimi olusemthethweni lweHoly See .
URhulumente ukhupha imali yakhe, kodwa, ngenxa yezithethe kunye nomanyano lwemali kunye neItali, wamkela i- euro, ethi kwiimvaba ezisibhozo zeengqekembe zentsimbi ziphethe isambatho sengalo sikaPopu olawulayo, okanye abantu baseVatican, icala lesizwe, kwaye likhupha ezalo izitampu, ezisebenzisekayo kwinkonzo yeposi kwihlabathi liphela (kodwa ngokucacileyo kuphela ngothutho olusuka kwiPosi yaseVatican ). Uluhlu lwesikhumbuzo lweengqekembe kunye nezitampu zibalulekile, zifunwa kakhulu ngama-numismatists kunye nama-philatelists  . IVatican ikwapapasha iphephandaba lemihla ngemihla, iL'Osservatore Romano, elasekwa ngo-1861, yaye ukususela ngo-1931, isikhululo sikanomathotholo, iVatican Radio, siye sasebenza ngeelwimi ezahlukahlukeneyo.
