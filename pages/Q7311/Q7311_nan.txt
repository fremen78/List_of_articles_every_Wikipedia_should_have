Giacomo Antonio Domenico Michele Secondo Maria Puccini (1858 nî 12 goe̍h 22 ji̍t – 1924 nî 11 goe̍h 29 ji̍t) sī Í-tāi-lī ê chok-khek-ka. I siāng chhut-miâ ê koa-kio̍k chok-phín ū La bohème, Tosca kap Madama Butterfly; chia-ê chok-phín iā sī tong-kim thong-sè-kài chia̍p-chia̍p ián-chhut ê koa-kio̍k.
