Canada is land in Norðamerican. Þis land hæfþ 40 þusenda þusend menn. Niwe Englisc and Frencisc sind ambihtlica spræca.
In norþ Niwu Scotlande folce spræc Scyttiscgaelisc and Niwe Englisc.


== Weald ==

Canada is cynerice. Se Gemænelican Geræswan is þæs Cyninges forwyrhta in Canadan, and undergeræswa in ælcum underrice. Canadan heafodstol is Ottawa in Ontarioe underrice. Þær sitteþ Gebindes Witenagemot and seo Styrung.
Canada hæfþ tiene underrice and þreo landscipas:
