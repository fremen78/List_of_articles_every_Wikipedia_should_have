International Monetary Fund (IMF) ek international sanstha hae. 189 des,last desh noru hai International Monetary Fund ke member hae. Iske headquarters Washington, D.C., USA me hae. By. 2401


== Kaam ==
IMF ke kaam hae des ke biich me paisa ke len-den me madat kare ke. 
Iske kuchh khaas kaam hae:

International trade me madat.
Exchange rates ke dekho
Balance of payments ke dekho
Member des ke economic development me madat
