E Ukrayna (o pinjardo anav: Україна, Ukraynikani chib) si yek Them andi Mashkaruni Europa. Kapitalya Kiyev. Pe sudutni rig si Kalo tay Azovikano doryav (more)


== Anav ==
Ando traditsiyalno gindo anday Rusiya thay Polsha, o anav le Ukrainyako avel anday phurani slavyanski vorba ke akhardi "them ande granitsa" vay "them". But adyesune ukrainyake istoriyatora phenen ke o akharimos mothol pe "u-kraina" sar "ando them", "kheresko them" vay "amaro them".
