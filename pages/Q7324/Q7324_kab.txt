James Cook neɣ Captain Cook (s tanglizit : James Cook) (27 tubeṛ 1728 - 14 fuṛaṛ 1779) yella d abehri d umnadi Aglizi, yettwaḥesben d yiwen seg imnadiyen Iurufen i yesɛan azal ameqran deg tallit n zik, yexdem kraḍ n yinigen deg ugaraw Ahadi, yura aṭas n tkartidin n temnaḍt-a  yexdem ula aṭas n tmusniwin. Yufa n tegzirin n Hawaii d Taseyult Tajdidt. Yetwassen deg umezruy segmi yemmut Magellan deg useggas n 1768 yexdem kraḍ n isikelen: amezwaru gar (1768-1774) wis sin deg tallit gar (1772-1774) wis tlata deg useggas n 1776 i d-yessufɣen tagzirt n Haway.
