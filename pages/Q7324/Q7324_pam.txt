I Capitan James Cook FRS RN (27 October 1728 (O.S.) - 14 February 1779) metung yang Ingles a talapaglakbe, talapaglayag ampong talagawang mapa, a mitas king ranggu ning Capitan king Royal Navy. I Cook ing minunang ginawang mapa ning Newfoundland bayu karing atlu nang pamaglakbe king Pacific Ocean, nung kapilan megi yang mumunang Europeung miras king pampang ning Australia ampo Isla ning Hawaii, ampo ing mumunang misulat a pamaglayag padurut king New Zealand.
Mikiabe ya i Cook king merchant navy ning Britania aniang binatiliu ya pamu at mikiabe ya king Royal Navy aniang 1755. Mekilaban ya king Seven Years' War, at kaibat na nita, sikdan ne ampong gewang mapa ing maragul a dake ning piluluban (entrance) ning Ilug Saint Lawrence aniang pamaglusub ning Quebec. Uli na niti, ikua nang arapat General Wolfe ing kayang kasebian a pasalikut a pamaglusub (stealth attack) king Plains of Abraham. Ini ing migdala kaya king pansin ning  Admiralty ampo ing Royal Society. Mikatagun a aniang a miliari iti aniang ustung panaun king kayang karera, ampo king pamanuclas na kareng aliwang labuad ning Britania, ining pamamansin kaya, at minuli iti king pamanuldu kaya antimong pekapun (commander) ning HM Bark Endeavour para king mumuna kareng atlung pamaglakbe king Pacifico.
Mengalat yang impormasiun tungkul kareng dakal a lugal i Cook, at ginawa yang mapa da reng dakal a isla ampong bebe dayatmalat para kareng mapa da reng Europeu king mumunang pamikatagun. Ayabut nang Cook ing kayang tagumpe uling kareng dakal a bage, kayabe na ing kabiasnan na king dayatmalat (seamanship), pangamatenakan king pamaniukad lugal (surveying) ampong pamangawang mapa, tepangan king pamanuklas kareng mapanganib a karinan bang patutuan ing impormasiun (alimbawa, ing ulit-ulit nang pamamunta king Antarctic Circle ampong pamaglakbe padurut king Great Barrier Reef, kagiwan a manibala kareng tau lipat ning mangasákit a kabilian, ampong kapangangasan (boldness) king leparan da reng kayang pamaglakbe ampong kaburian a lagpusan ing kautusan na kaya ning Admiralty.
Mete ya i Cook king Hawaii aniang akapate da la reng Hawaianu aniang katlu nang pamaglakbe ning pamanuklas king Pacifico kanitang 1779.
