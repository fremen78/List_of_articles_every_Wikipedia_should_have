Marxism is an economic an sociopoleetical warldview an method o socioeconomic inquiry that centers upon a materialist interpretation o history, a dialectical view o social chynge, an an analysis an critique o the development o caipitalism. Marxism wis pioneered in the early tae mid 19th century bi twa German philosophers, Karl Marx an Friedrich Engels. Marxism encompasses Marxian economic theory, a sociological theory an a revolutionary view o social chynge that haes influenced socialist poleetical muivements aroond the warld.
