Charles Robert Darwin (1809 nî 2 goe̍h 12 – 1882 nî 4 goe̍h 19), Eng-tē chū-jiân-ha̍k-ka. I siāng kài chhut-miâ ê sêng-chiū tō-sī chhoē chhut chèng-kì chèng-bêng seng-bu̍t-chéng ùi chìn-hoà kái-piàn lâi chō-chiâⁿ, koh thê-chhut kho-ha̍k lūn-soat kái-soeh kóng, chit khoán kái-piàn ê ki-choán (mechanism) sī chū-jiân soán-te̍k. Chit ê lí-lūn sī hiān-tāi seng-bu̍t-ha̍k ê 1 ki tē-ki-kha.
