Shinto, Japan ke native dharam hae. "Shinto" sabd dui Japanese sabd, shin, jiske matlab aatma, nai to bhagwaan hae, aur to, jiske matlab rastaa hae, se aais hae. Iske matlab ii hae ki Shinto ke matlab 'Bhagwaan talak ke rastaa' (The way to the Gods) hae.
Shinto me dher riiti-rwaaj (rituals and customs) hae, aur isme se koi koi ke har roj karaa jaae hae. Kuchh log Shinto aur Buuddhist ke milae ke puuja kare hae.
World War II se pahile State Shinto Japan ke khaas dharam rahaa. 1869 se 1945 talak, JApan ke sarkar Shinto ke aapan parchaar (propaganda) khaatir kaam me laais rahaa. Sab Shinto pandit sarkar khaatir kaam karat rahin aur larrai ke ek pavitra kaam (sacred duty) maana jaat rahaa, aur Japan ke Emporer ke Bhagwaan maana jaat rahaa.
