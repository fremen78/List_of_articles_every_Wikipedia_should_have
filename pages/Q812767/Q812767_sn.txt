Ushintho kana Cshintho (Chijapani: 神道, chiroma: Shintō; "nzira yavamwari" muChijapani) chinamato chekuJapani. Chinopangurwa sechinamato chekuEzhiya ye Mabvazuva ne maaskola echinamato, vatendi vayo vanoiona sechinamato chidza cheJapani uye sechinamato chizvarwa. Dzimwenguva maskora vanodana vatendi vayo kuti Vashinto .


== Chindau: -Ntho ==
