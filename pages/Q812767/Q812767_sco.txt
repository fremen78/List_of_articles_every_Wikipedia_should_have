Shinto (神道, Shintō), an aa kannagara-no-michi, is the indigenous spirituality o Japan an the fowk o Japan. It is a set o practices, tae be carried oot diligently, tae establish a connection atween present-day Japan an its auncient past. Founded in 660 BC, Shinto practices wur first recordit an codified in the written heestorical records o the Kojiki an Nihon Shoki in the 8t century.
