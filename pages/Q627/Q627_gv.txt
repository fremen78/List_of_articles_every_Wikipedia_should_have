Ta neetragien ny neetrageen ny vunstoo lesh y chowrey N, yn earroo breneenagh 7 as y trimmid breneenagh 14.0067 g·mol-1. Ta'n neetragien bunneydagh ny ghas daa-vreneenagh neughleashagh son y chooid smoo gyn daah, gyn soar, gyn blass. T'eh ny vunstoo smoo lheeadagh ayns aeraght ny Cruinney as eh jannoo seose 78.08% jeh'n aeraght eck rere thummid. Ta ram co-vunstooghyn çhynskylagh scanshoil, lheid as ammoiney, sharroo neetragh, naitraidyn orgaanagh, as keeaneedyn goaill stiagh neetragien. V'eh feddynit magh 'sy vlein 1772 liorish y fishigagh Albinagh Daniel Rutherford.
