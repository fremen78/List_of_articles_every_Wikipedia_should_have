Ing Rio de Janeiro ( /ˈriːoʊ deɪ ʒəˈnɛəroʊ/ o /ˈriːoʊ dɪ dʒəˈnɛəroʊ/; Pangayagkas a Portugues: [ˈʁi.u dʒi ʒaˈnejɾu], Ilug ning Enero January), a keraklan ausan da mung  Rio, ing lakanbalen a tungku o syudad a kapitolyu/kabisera (capital city) Estadu ning Rio de Janeiro, ing kadwang pekamaragul a lakanbalen o syudad ning Brazil, ampo ing katlung pekamaragul a karinan a metropolitan ampong agglomeration king Mauling America, a maki boasting approximately 6.3 milyung tau king kabalenan ning lakanbalen, at uli na niti yang ka-6 a pekamaragul king Americas, ampo ing ka26 king yatu.  Ing Rio de Janeiro karinan ne ning World Heritage Site a maki lagyung "Rio de Janeiro: Carioca Landscapes between the Mountain and the Sea", as granted by UNESCO on 1 July 2012 in the category Cultural Landscape.
Adwa yang dilanwa o siglung tungku o kapitolyu/kabisera Brazil, manibat 1763 anggang 1815 anyang panaun a kolonyal a Portugues, at 1815 anggang 1821 antimong tungku ning [Misanmetung a Kaarian ning [Misanmetung a Kaarian ning Portugal, Brazil ampong Algarves|Misanmetung a Kaarian ning Portugal, Brazil ampong Algarves]], ampong 1822 anggang 1960 antimong timawang bangsa. Ing Rio maki palayo ya o bansag a Cidade Maravilhosa o "Marvelous City" ("Makapagmulalang Lakanbalen").
