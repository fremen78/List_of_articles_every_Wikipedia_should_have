Himalaya, Asia ke ek pahaarr ke range hae. Ii Pakistan aur Jammu and Kashmir ke north me, Republic of India ke Himachal Pradesh aur Uttaranchal states, Nepal, Sikkim, aur Bhutan ke north; aur Tibet ke south me hae. Dunia ke 15 sab se uunch pahaarr Himalayas me hae jisme se khaas hae Mount Everest, K2, Annapurna, aur Nanga Parbat. Mount Everest, jon ki 8,848 meters uuncha hae, dunia ke sab se uuncha pahaarr hae.
