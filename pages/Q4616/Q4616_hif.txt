Marilyn Monroe, jiske paidaais ke naam Norma Jean Baker rahaa,  1 June  1926 ke paida bhaes rahaa. Uu ek jaana maana America ke abhinetri, gawaiya aur model rahii. Uu  29 films  me act karis rahaa. Uske dher award milaa, jisme tiin golden globes aur ek best actress oscar uske Some Like it Hot ke role khatir milaa. 1950s me uu pachchhim dunia me ek  sex symbol rahii. Uske tiin dafe saadi bhaes rahaa.
1999 me uske American film insitute sab time ke sixth greatest film star declare karaa gais.
