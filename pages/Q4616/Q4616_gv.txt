Marilyn Monroe (ruggit myr Norma Jeane Mortenson; 1 Mean Souree 1926   4 Luanistyn 1962) she ben-aghteyr, craishteyder as arraneyder Americaanagh v'ee. V'ee mie er enney son cloie karaghteyryn bwoyagh fynney as haink urree dy ve fer jeh ny cowraghyn keintyssagh smoo ennoil car ny 1950yn as ny 1960yn leah as v'ee coontit myr cowrey jeh caghlaa mooar keintyssagh y lhing shen. Ren ny filmyn eck tayrn stiagh $200 millioon (corrym rish $2,000 millioon ayns 2020) roish my dooar ee baase ayns 1962. Foddey lurg e baaish, ta Monroe foast coontit myr jalloo mooar y phop-chultoor.
