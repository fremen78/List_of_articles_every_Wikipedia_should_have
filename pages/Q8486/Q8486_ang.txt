Caffig is drinca þe is geƿorht of þǣm ahyrstedan sǣde caffigplantan (Coffea canephora and Coffea arabica sind oftost gesett). Þēah þe his bēana sind oftost tō drincan geƿenda, hit biþ ēac nytlic in birgung sƿōtǣtes and geƿealdenǣtes. Cāfe menn brūcan þæs sǣd self sƿā geƿealdenǣt, oft ymbhammen mid ceocōlāte.
