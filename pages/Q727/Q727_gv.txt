She Amsterdam preeu-valley yn Çheer Injil. V'eh bunnit 'sy 12oo eash myr balley beg çheerey eeasteyraght, er broogh ny h-awin Amstel. Jiu, t'eh ny valley smoo 'sy çheer, as balley scanshoil rere cultoor as argidys. Ta ammyryn er feie y valley as ram thieyn tashtee ard-ghooagh (myr sampleyr y thie tashtee d'Anne Frank). Ta'n balley ard-ennymagh son ny leighyn libraalagh t'ayn mychione druggaghyn as keintys.
