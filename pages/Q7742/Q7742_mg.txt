Louis XIV dia mpanao politika teraka ny 5 Septambra 1638 ary maty ny 1 Septambra 1715


== Ny fiainany manokana ==
I Louis XIII of France no rainy. Ny vadiny dia Marie Thérèse of Austria, Françoise d'Aubigné, Marquise de Maintenon. Ny zanany dia Louis, Grand Dauphin, Marie Thérèse, Madame Royale, Philippe Charles, Duke of Anjou, Princess Anne Élisabeth of France, Princess Marie Anne of France, Louis François, Duke of Anjou, Françoise Marie de Bourbon, Louis Alexandre, Count of Toulouse, Louise Françoise de Bourbon, Duchess of Bourbon, Louis Auguste, Duke of Maine, Louis, Count of Vermandois, Louis César, Count of Vexin, Louise Marie Anne de Bourbon, Marie Anne de Bourbon, Louise de Maisonblanche, Charles de La Baume Le Blanc. Ny rahalahiny sy/na ny rahavaviny dia Philippe, Duke of Orléans.
