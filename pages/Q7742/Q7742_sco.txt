Louis XIV (5 September 1638 – 1 September 1715), kent as Louis the Great (Louis le Grand) or the Sun King (le Roi-Soleil), wis a monarch o the Hoose o Bourbon who ruled as Keeng o Fraunce an Navarre frae 1643 till his daith. His reign o 72 year an 110 days is the langest o monarchs o major kintras in European history. He weas succeeded bi Louis XV, but Louis' nephew an son-in-law Philippe d'Orléans, Duke o Orléans acted as regent till the young king became o age in 1723.


== Issue with Queen Marie Thérèse ==
Louis o Fraunce, Dauphin o Fraunce, "Le Grand Dauphin" (1 November 1661 – 14 Aprile 1711)  mairit Maria Anna Victoria o Bavarie an haed issue. Mairit again tae Marie Émilie de Joly (in saecret) but haed nae issue
Anne Élisabeth o Fraunce (18 November 1662 – 30 December 1662) died in infancy.
Marie Anne o Fraunce (16 November 1664 – 26 December 1664) died in infancy or the Black Nun o Moret
Marie Thérèse o Fraunce"Madame Royale" (2 Januar 1667 – 1 Mairch 1672) died in infancy.
Philippe Charles o Fraunce (5 August 1668 – 10 Julie 1671) Duke o Anjou, died in infancy.
Louis François o Fraunce (14 Juin 1672 – 4 November 1672) Duke o Anjou, died in infancy.


== Issue with Mademoiselle de La Vallière ==
Charles de La Baume Le Blanc (19 December 1663 – 15 Julie 1665) died young.
Philippe de La Baume Le Blanc (7 Januar 1665 – 1666) died young.
Louis de La Baume Le Blanc (27 December 1665 – 1666) died young.
Marie Anne de Bourbon (2 October 1666 – 3 Mai 1739) mairit Louis Armand de Bourbon, Prince o Conti but haed nae issue.
Louis de Bourbon, Coont o Vermandois (2 October 1667 – 18 November 1683) died in adulthood.


== Issue with Madame de Montespan ==
Louise Françoise de Bourbon (1669-1672) died young.
Louis Auguste de Bourbon, Duke o Maine (31 Mairch 1670 - 14 Mey 173) mairit Louise Bénédicte de Bourbon an haed issue.
Louis César de Bourbon, Coont o Vexin (20 Juin 1672 – 10 Januar 1683) died in infancy.
Louise Françoise de Bourbon, "Mademoiselle de Nantes" (1 Juin 1673 – 16 Juin 1743) mairit Louis de Bourbon, Duke o Bourbon, Prince o Condé an haed issue.
Louise Marie Anne de Bourbon, "Mademoiselle de Tours" 18 November 1674 – 15 September 1681) died young.
Marie Françoise de Bourbon, "Mademoiselle de Blois" (4 Mey 1677 – 1 Februar 1749) mairit Philippe d'Orléans, Duke o Orléans, Regent o Fraunce an had issue.
Louis Alexandre de Bourbon, Coont o Toulouse (6 Juin 1678 – 1 December 1737) mairit Marie Victoire de Noailles an haed issue.
