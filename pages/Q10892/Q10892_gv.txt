She possan rang-oardree ass ymmyd dy vynvioee eukaryotagh un- ny beggan-chillagagh gyn sur-stoo kirpey ad protista.  Ta'n possan goaill stiagh algaghyn as protosoa.
Hoshiaght, liorish Ernst Haeckel (1866), hie ad er rang-oardraghey myr reeriaght Protista, possan dy vioagyn un-chillagagh (son y chooid smoo) nagh vel cooie da possan elley.  Ren oayllee aarheynn ad rere caslyssyn rish ny reeriaghtyn elley: protosoa "baaghagh", protophyta "lussagh" as lheeahid glooie "fliughaneagh".  Cha nel shen goit rish ayns rang-oardraghey jeianagh.  Rish yn eie jeianagh, she possan neuoikoil dy mysh 30-40 phyla t'ayn, gyn faggys-mooinjerys eddyr oc, as shimmey scansh t'eddyr ny dooieyn.  Er lhimmey jeh'n chummey bunneydagh oc, cha nel monney caslys eddyr protista.  Ta'n strughtoor oc, gyn stoo kirpey as sur-obbyr echey, ad y eddyraghey veih eukaryota elley.
Ta protista cummal ayns çhymbyllaght erbee as ushtey fliugh aynjee.  Ta kuse vooar jeu co-haaghey sollysh, as t'adsyn ard-chrootee ayns eggochoryssyn mastey plankton elley.


== Troyn ==
Ta protista jeant jeh plasmey killag as ta far-chrackan killag mygeayrt oc.  Cha nel agh beggan roshtyn mooadys ymmodee millimeadar.  Ta un çheshvean killag oc son y chooid smoo, agh ec beggan jeu ta ymmodee çheshveanyn.  Ta mitochondria 'sy phlasmey, as ec protista ta co-haaghey sollys, ta chloroplastyn ayn chammah.  Foddee kuse jeu jannoo coadagh sheaneagh dys coadey ad noi drogh-staydyn çhymbyllagh.
