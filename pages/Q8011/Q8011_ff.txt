Ibn sina ( o nasaranko'en no'udirtirta avicenna) o laatiiɗo ɓeeɗo leydi persia kumma o go'onijo taa anndal bincikeje ɗe kowngal magannije e anndingo'o anndal kowngal kowtarku nder falsafaje ɗe zamanuje kadimma o go'onijo winndande e nder hitaande ndunngu yamtango darajare diina musulunci jaaɓeti too

duniiyaaru e ɗon waaɗite naamo lakabiire e dimma babajo magannije zamanuje sajjad H rizui o inniree mo sina e dimma anndudo anndal nder falsafaje ɗe zamanuje haande e dimma ɓurduɗo komoi jaaɓe'ogo e nder howterde jeytul littafije ɗe o winnde kama 240 go'nde e haande haa jooni kowtuɗe e 150 bullitoje e dou jeytul anndal scientific philosophy 40 bulwoje e dou magannije zamanuje diga nder go'onare kowngal kowɗe kamma the book of healing e philosophical and scientific encyclopedia e the canon of medicine e medical text taa ɓurna medieval univesities kumma o faatiyesu e amfaanago haa ɓaawo hiitande 1650 e nduungu alif 1973 avicenna canon of medicine ɗon sakiitare googo ngal e gelle new york ɓaawo philosophy e medicine kundiro ibn sina
