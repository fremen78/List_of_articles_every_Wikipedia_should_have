Abū ʿAlī al-Ḥusayn ibn ʿAbd Allāh ibn Sīnā (ابو علی الحسین ابن عبدالله ابن سینا); c. 980 Bukhara me, Khorasan – 1037 Hamedan me), jiske Ibn Seena ke naam se bhi jaana jaawe hae aur jiske jaada kar ke English me uske Latinized naam Avicenna se jaana jaae hae (Greek  Aβιτζιανός), ek Persian rahaa Muslim polymath and the most important physician and Islamic philosopher of his time. He was also an astronomer, chemist, Hafiz, logician, mathematician, poet, psychologist, scientist, Sheikh, soldier, statesman and theologian.
Ibn Sīnā lagbhag 450 treatises dher subject pe likhis rahaa,jisme se 240 abhi talak hae. Khaas kar ke, 150 treatises philosophy ke baareme aur 40  medicine ke baare me hae.
