Daga cikin mafi shaharun ayyukansa akwai The Book of Healing, a philosophical and scientific encyclopedia, da The Canon of Medicine, a medical encyclopedia wanda yazama standard medical text a mafi yawan medieval universities kuma yacigaba da amfani har karshen 1650. a shekarar 1973, Avicenna's Canon Of Medicine ansake bugata a birnin New York.

Bayan philosophy da medicine, kundin Ibn Sina na kunshe da rubuce rubuce akan astronomy, alchemy, geography da geology, psychology, Islamic theology, logic, mathematics, physics da kuma ayyuka aika poetry.
