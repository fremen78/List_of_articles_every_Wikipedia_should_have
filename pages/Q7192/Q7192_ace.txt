Qin Shi Huang (Hanzi: 秦始皇) (Buleuen Siblah atawa Buleuen Duwa Blah 260 SM - 10 Buleuen Sikureueng 210 SM), lahe ngon nan Ying Zheng (贏政), geuhey cit Shi Huang Di nyang hareutoejih nakeuh Raja Keuphon, nakeuh raja nanggroe Qin nibak thon 247 SM troh 'an thon 221 SM, 'oh lheueh jipeusaboh Cina ngon jipeutaklok 6 boh nanggroe la'en, lheueh nyan jipeudong Dinasti Qin ngon jipeu'ek droe jeuet keu raja Cina nyang meusaboh - nibak thon 221 SM trok 'an thon 210 SM - meutahta ngon neuhey Raja Keuphon.
