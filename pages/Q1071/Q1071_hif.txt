Geography, Dunia ke, iske features aur loag ke baare me study hae. Iske "features" me continent, samundar, naddi aur pahaarr hae. Isme rahe waala sab log aur janwar hae. Isme juwaar, hawaa aur bhudhaul hae. 
Ek jan jon ki geography ke expert hae ke geographer bola jaawe hae. Geographer logan dunia me ke sab chij ke baare me jaane ke kosis kare hae, aur ii jaane ke kosis kare hae ki sab chij kaise suruu bhais aur kaise badlis.  Geographer logan map ke baare me bahut chij jaane hae.
