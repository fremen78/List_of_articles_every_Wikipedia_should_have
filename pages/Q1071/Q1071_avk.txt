
= Tawavopa =
Batu bu ta tadlera is grustara va direfi tuveli lospas va kot tawavopaf teliz zo ickriler.
Banvielu, ta aykara va gluyava van toleodafu bu ixam suteyenu nelkon zanudar.


== Welfeem is bireem ==
→ Vexala dem bireem ke tamava


== Gluyaxa van toleodaf teliz icde tawavopa ==
→ Wafra vexalasa va ravlem vegungaf gu voa.
→ Wafra vexalasa va ravlem vegungaf gu lavaxo.
