Geografie (or geography) is the scientific studie o the locational an spatial variation o baith physical an human phenomena on the Yird. The wird cums frae the Greek wirds γη or γεια ("Yird") an γραφειν ("ti descrieve" an "ti scrieve,").
A feuw geografie terms include river, loch, stream, muntain, ridge, bay, sea, plateau, mesa, desert, an forest, tae juste nam a feuw.
