George Washington (* Westmoreland, 1732 mara 22 uru Anata phaxsin yuritayna - Mount Vernon, 1799 mara 4 uru Jallu qallta phaxsin yuriña ),  Istadus Unidos jacha marka militar & anakithiri. Istadus Unidus Prisidinti (1789 - 1797).
Awki: Agustine Washington; Tayka: Mary Ball Washington.Warmi: Martha Dandridge Custis.
