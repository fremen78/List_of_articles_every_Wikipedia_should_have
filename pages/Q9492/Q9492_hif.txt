Anumaan, applied Mathematics ke ek hissa hae.
Probability is a part of applied mathematics. Ii mauka hae, jiske matlab ii hae ki ii saait hoe sake nai to nai hoe sake hae.
Jaise ki ham anumman lagae sakte hae ki agar ek coin ke uppar hawaa me bigaa gais aur iske jamiin pe gire jaae dewa gais, tab aadha time ii head pe land hoi aur aadha time ii tail pe land hoi.

Agar ek dice ke roll karaa jaae hae tab ham anumaan lagae sakte hae ki 1/6th time ii 1 pe land hoi. Yahii rakam se, ham anumaan lagae sakte hae ki 1/6th time me ii aur koi number (2,3,4,5,6) pe girii.
