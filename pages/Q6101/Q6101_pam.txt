I Marco Polo (September 15 1254 – January 9 1324 ing pekamaranun dapot e mas tauli kesa king June 1325) metung yang Venetian a negosianti ampong talapaglakbeng mekilala uling king kayang pamaglakbe king yatu, a misulat king librung Il Milione ("Ing Miliunn" o Deng Pamaglakbe nang Marco Polo) a ausan da muring Oriente Poliano  (ing Aslagan da reng Polo) ampo ing Pamilarawan ning Yatu (Description of the  World).
Kayabe ning tata nang i Niccolò ampo ing bapa nang i Maffeo, i Polo ing metung kareng mumunang tau ibat king Albugan a mekadalan king Silk Road papunta king China (a inaus nang Cathay, uling king lahing Khitan) at manapa o magbisita king Great Khan ning Imperiung Mongol, i Kublai Khan (apú nang Genghis Khan).
