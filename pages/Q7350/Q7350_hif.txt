Panama Canal, Panama (Central America) ke ek waterway hae jon ki Atlantic Ocean aur Pacific Ocean ke jorree hae. Panama Canal tiin locks ke kaam me laae ke paani ke level ke control kare hae. Har saal 14,000 jahaj ii canal se jaawe hae. The France 1880 ke iske banae ke kosi skaris lekin nai khalaas kare paais aur 1914 me United States, das saal ke construction ke baad, banae ke khalaas karis. ii canal 77 km (48 miles) lamba hae.
