Rufangu (Acceleration)- iri izwi rinoreva kuchinja kweSpidhi paumwe hwechiyero chenguva. Rufangu rune chiyero (m/s2). Rufangu runowanikwa nekugova spidhi pane nguva.
Tsazaniso inoti Rufangu = (kuchinja kwemuchacha/nguva), (m/s2).


== Mitauro yeBantu ==
