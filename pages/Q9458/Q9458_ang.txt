Abu al-Qasim Ibn Abd Allah Ibn Abd al-Muttalib Ibn Hashim oþþe efne Mahomet ƿæs Arabisc cēapmann se ƿearþ brego of ealla Arabena on Arabea and se ongann þāra Araba geƿeald þæs ēastes. Man alīefð ðe Mahomet ƿæs Alladōmes staðolere and for manna þǣre ǣfæstnesse is hē Godes ƿītega.
For mannum þā healdað tō Alladōme is Mahomet þǣre ǣfæstnesse ƿītega. Hē is on Alladōme hālig gesmēad. Se nama Mahomet (oþðe Muhammed) gemǣnþ "Herigendlīc mann".
Stǣrbēc ne secgaþ nāht be þissum menn būton þe þā "Cƿidas þæs Ƿītegan" secgað ðe Abu al-Qasim Ibn Abd Allah Ibn Abd al-Muttalib Ibn Hashim ƿæs geboren on Meccan on þǣm 570. gēare æfter Crīste. Æfter þēaƿum, is sēo Curānbōc sēo gæderung his ætīeƿunga.
