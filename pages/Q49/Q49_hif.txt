North America, dunia ke north Pacific Ocean ke east, Atlantic Ocean ke west, Arctic Ocean ke south aur South America ke north me ek continent hae. Ii the Americas ke north hissa hae.
North America ke barraa des ke suchi north se south me niche hae:

Canada
United States of America
Mexico
Greenland, jisme Denmark ke raaj hae, ek barraa island Canada ke northeast me hae.
Central America ke des jiske North America ke hissa maana jaae hae: 

Guatemala
Belize
Honduras
El Salvador
Nicaragua
Costa Rica
Panama
Caribbean ke sab des aur island North America me hae.
