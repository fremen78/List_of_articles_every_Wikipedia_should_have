Amelika wa Kumpoto ni nthunthu la vyalo(continent)  ilo likusangika kumpoto kwa chalo cha pasi.
Amelika wa Kumpoto walikupanga mphaka na nyanja ikulu ya Arctic Ocean ku mpoto,nyanja ikulu ya Atlantic Ocean ku mafumilo gha dazi,South America na  Caribbean Sea kummwera,kweneso  Pacific Ocean ku manjililo gha dazi.


== UKULU WA NTHUNTHU LA AMELIKA WA KUMPOTO ==
Amelika wa Kumpoto ukulu wake ni  24,709,000 square kilometers (panji titi : 9,540,000 square miles),
Amelika wa Kumpoto ni nthunthu lachitatatu  pa ma nthunthu gha chalo 9,nga ni umo tikumanyila kuti nthunthu likulu chomene ni Asia(biggest continent).
