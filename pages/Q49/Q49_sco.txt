North Americae's a continent in the northren hauf o the warld, locate tae the east o the Pacific Ocean, the wast o the Atlantic Ocean, the sooth o the Arctic Ocean, an the north o Sooth Americae. It's the northrenmaist pairt o the Americaes. 
Northren Americae includes thir muckle kintras, listit frae north tae sooth:

Canadae
Unitit States o Americae
Mexico
The kintras o Central Americae is thocht tae be pairt o North Americae an aw. They are: Guatemala, Belize, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, an Panama. 
Aw the kintras an islands o the Caribbean is pairt o North Americae.
Greenland, governt bi Denmark, is a muckle island tae the nor'east o Canadae, an is thocht tae be pairt o North Americae an aw.
