Atheism is the lack o belief in onie deities. Abody that hauds this view is cried an atheist.
Some atheists follae religions withoot onie gods, lik Buddhism or Taoism, whiles monie dinna follae onie religion at aa.
Atheism differs fae agnosticism. Agnostics are o the view that there isnae onie wey tae ken whither gods exist or no.
