== Biografi ==


=== Masa kikingo ===
Thomas Alva Edison pilotutu to Milan, Ohio, Amerika Serikat to tanggal 11 Pebruari 1847. To'u masa kikingiyo to Amerika Serikat, Edison layito hemo'otapu nilai moleeto to sikolaliyo. Sababu uwito tiyo ma bilerenti li mamaliyo to sikola wawu ma hebalajariyoliyo to bele. To bele te Edison kikingo luwasi mobaca tili lahepa lobuku, sambe buku-buku ilmiah dewasa wawu ma lolumula mohutu percobaan ilmiah lohihilawo.
To uumuru 12 lo taawunu tiyo ma hemokaraja mopotaliya lo koran, hungo lo ayu wawu gula-gula to kereta api. Lapatiyo ma'o tiyo ma lowali operator lo telegraf.
To kota New York tiyo lowali ta'uwa lo masina telegraf. Masina-masina boyito u hemolawo habari bisnis ode nga'amila perusahaan uda'a to New York.
