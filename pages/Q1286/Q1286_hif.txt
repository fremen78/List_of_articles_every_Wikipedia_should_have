Alps (German: Alpen; French: Alpes; Italian: Alpi; Slovenian: Alpe), Europe ke ek barraa paharr ke range hae.


== Geography ==
Alps, Austria aur Slovenia ke east me se lae ke, Italy, Switzerland, Liechtenstein aur Germany se France talak ke west jaawe hae.
Alps ke sab se uuncha pahaarr Mont Blanc hae (4,808 m), jon ki French-Italian border me hae.
