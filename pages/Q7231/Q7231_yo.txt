Rosa Luxemburg (Rosalia Luxemburg, Pólándì: [Róża Luksemburg] ; 5 March 1871  –  15 January 1919) jé oludero Marksisti, onimoye, onímò okòwò àti alákitiyan omo Júù Pólándì tó di ára Jámánì. Ni telentele o di omo egbe Social Democracy of the Kingdom of Poland and Lithuania, SPD Jemani, Independent Social Democratic Party ati Egbe Komunisti ile Jemani.
