Rosa Luxemburg (Polska-gí: Róża Luksemburg, 1871 nî 3 goe̍h 5 ji̍t - 1919 nî 1 goe̍h 15 ji̍t) sī Tek-kok ê siā-hoē-chú-gī chèng-tī lí-lūn-ka kap o̍ah-tāng-chiá. I kap Karl Liebknecht chòe-hoé chhòng-li̍p kek-bēng cho͘-chit Spartacus Tông-bêng (Spartakusbund). I mā-sī āu-lâi ê Tek-kok Kiōng-sán-tóng ê léng-tō-chiá.
Rosa Luxemburg sī Iû-thài-lâng, i tī Polska ê Zamość chhut-sì. Aū-boé hō͘ tìn-ap Spartacus Khí-gī ê Freikorps thâi-sí.
