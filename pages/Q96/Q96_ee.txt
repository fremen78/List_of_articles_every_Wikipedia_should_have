Mexico, United States le se nu, nye dukɔ si le Dziehe Amerika ƒe dzigbe gome. Eƒe lolome nye kilometa 1,972,550 (761,610 sq mi),[enye dukɔ gãtɔ 13 lia le xexeame le eƒe lolome nu; esi eƒe amewo ade miliɔn 130 kloe ta la, enye dukɔ 10 lia si me amewo sɔ gbɔ ɖo wu eye Spaingbe dolawo sɔ gbɔ wu le xexeame.Wowɔ ɖoɖo ɖe Mexico ŋu abe dukɔa ƒe dukplɔsedziɖuɖu si me dukɔ 31 kple Mexico City, si nye eƒe fiadu kple du gãtɔ kekeake, si le xexeame ƒe dugã siwo me amewo sɔ gbɔ ɖo wu dome. Dukɔa ƒe anyigba ƒe liƒo kple United States le dziehe gome, eye Guatemala kple Belize le dzieheɣedzeƒe gome; kpakple ƒudziliƒo siwo le Pasifik Ƒua le ɣetoɖoƒe gome, Caribbean Ƒua le dzieheɣedzeƒe gome, kple Mexico-ƒuta le ɣedzeƒe gome.
