Mexico nye dukɔ si le Dziehe Amerika ƒe dzigbe gome, eye United States do liƒo le eƒe dziehe gome, Mexico-ƒuta kple Caribbean Ƒua le eƒe ɣedzeƒe gome, Belize kple Guatemala le eƒe dzigbeɣedzeƒe gome, eye le ɣetoɖoƒe kple dzigbe gome Atsiaƒu si me Ŋutifafa le.


== Nyadzɔdzɔ ==
Ŋutinya deŋgɔ aɖe le Mexico si tso blema dekɔnu siwo nɔ anyi do ŋgɔ na Columbia, abe Aztektɔwo kple Mayawo ene, dzi va ɖo Spaintɔwo ƒe dutanyigbadziɖuɖu si dze egɔme le ƒe alafa 16 lia me eye wòva xɔ ɖokuisinɔnɔ mlɔeba le ƒe 1821 me.


== Dekᴐnu ==
Mexicotɔwo ƒe dekɔnuwo nye anyigbadzitɔwo, Spaintɔwo, eye le mɔ sue aɖe nu la, Afrikatɔwo kple Asiatɔwo ƒe ŋusẽkpɔɖeamedziwo ƒe tsakatsaka. Wonyae ɖe eƒe nuɖuɖu, si UNESCO buna be enye Amegbetɔ ƒe Dekɔnu Domenyinu Makpɔmakpɔ, eƒe azãɖuɖuwo abe Ame Kukuwo ƒe Ŋkeke, eƒe hadzidzi, aɖaŋudɔwo kple xɔtuɖaŋuwo ta.


== Ga ŋuti nya ==
Mexico nye dukɔ evelia si ƒe ganyawo le sesẽm wu le Latin Amerika eye wònye dukɔ si le ŋgɔ yim eye wòle ŋgɔ yim. Dɔwɔƒe vovovowo le esi si me dɔwɔƒewo abe ami, adzɔnuwo wɔwɔ, agbledede, tsaɖiɖi kple dɔwɔnawo le.


== Tsaɖiɖi yi teƒeteƒewo ==
Tsaɖiɖi nye Mexico ƒe ganyawo ƒe akpa vevi aɖe, eye teƒe siwo amewo lɔ̃a yiyi abe Cancun, Riviera Maya, Los Cabos, Mexico City, kple dutanyigbawo abe Oaxaca kple San Miguel de Allende ene le afima.


== Sukudede kple lãmesẽnyawo ==
Dukɔa kple ame ŋutɔ ƒe sukudede ƒe ɖoɖo le Mexico, eye wòdze agbagba vevie be yeana agbalẽnyanya kple suku kɔkɔ dede nanyo ɖe edzi. Lãmesẽnyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ ƒe ɖoɖoa hã nyo ɖe edzi le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe, togbɔ be kuxiwo doa goe le alesi woakpɔe kple eƒe nyonyome gome hã.
