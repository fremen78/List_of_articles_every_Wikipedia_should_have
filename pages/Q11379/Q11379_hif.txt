Shakti (Energy) ek sabd hae jiske ek se jaada matlab hae. Bhautik vigyan (Physics) me shakti, matter aur space ke ek property hae. Shakti ke ek chij se duusra chij me karaa jaae sake hae , lekin iske banaawa aur barbaad nai karaa jaae sake hae. Economics me shakti ke matlab "energy industry" hae, jaise ki, fuel aur electricity. Daenik upyog me shakti ke matlab hae koi lively aur vigour se baat kare hae.
