Ally Energy bụ ụlọ ọrụ mmepe na-arụ ọrụ n'ịntanetị maka ndị ọkachamara na ụlọ ọrụ ike gburugburu ụwa. E hiwere ụlọ ọrụ ahụ na 2014 na Houston, Texas, na mbụ n'okpuru aha Pink Petro. 
Ụlọ ọrụ ahụ na-enye ohere ọrụ na netwọk site na ndepụta nke ọrụ na ihe omume metụtara ike. Ọ na-enyekwa ihe onwunwe ụlọ ọrụ, nghọta na mmepe nkà. 


== Akụkọ ihe mere eme ==
Ọ bụ Katie Mehnert, onye isi ike na ụlọ ọrụ dị iche iche  yana Ngalaba Ike nke United States na atụmatụ ya Equity na Energy, guzobere Ally Energy.
O nwetara echiche maka Pink Petro na 2013. Site na nkwado sitere n'aka Cindy Patman, onye isi nchịkwa nke ihe dị iche iche na ụlọ ọrụ na Halliburton na nkwado sitere na Royal Dutch Shell, ọ malitere ikpo okwu n'ịntanetị dabere na teknụzụ mmekọrịta igwe ojii site na Jive Software. Mgbe ahụ, onye isi oche nke Jive, Elisa Steele mepụtara mmekọrịta iji malite obodo ahụ. Onye bụbu onye isi obodo Houston, Annise Parker kwupụtara Pink Petro Day na Mee 2, 2015, iji mee emume na ihe karịrị ndị isi 300 na ndị ọkachamara.  na nkwado nke Shell, Halliburton, Jive Software na KPMG.
Na Machị 2016, Pink Petro haziri HERWorld Energy Forum, nzukọ ụbọchị niile gbasara ihe ọhụrụ, teknụzụ, iwu na ndị ọrụ.   N'afọ 2017, a malitere ebe ọrụ mbụ maka ụmụ nwanyị na ike, Experience Energy, na ikpo okwu na-anabata ihe dịka ọrụ 500. N'afọ 2020, e jikọtara Pink Petro na Experience Energy ma gbanwee aha ha iji mepụta Ally Energy.
Na 2021, Ally Energy nwetara Clean Energy Social na-agbasawanye nha nha nha na nsonye ya iji tinye nyocha nke nwere ike inye aka na-ewe ndị njikwa.[1] Dịka nke 2022, Ally Energy nwere kansụl atọ, ALLY, ERG na ESG. ALLY bụ maka ndị isi CHROs na ndị isi na-agwa ndị ọrụ ike ike, ERG bụ maka otu akụrụngwa akụrụngwa iji lebara ọdịbendị anya, yana ESG bụ maka ndị isi na-ahụ maka ihu igwe na nkwado..
