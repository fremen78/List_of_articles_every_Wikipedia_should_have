Mita (Amurka, metre ) shine ainihin ma'aunin dake nuna tsayi a tsarin ma'aunin SI. Alamar mita ita ce m. Ma'anar farko (a cikin juyin juya halin Faransa ) shine kashi ɗaya cikin miliyan goma na nisa tsakanin ma'aunin duniya da Pole ta Arewa tare da Paris Meridian. A yanzu an ayyana mita a matsayin nisan hasken da ke tafiya a cikin sarari samaniya 1/299,792,458 na daƙiƙa. 
An fara bayyana ma'anar mita a shekarar 1793, a matsayin daya daga cikin nisa kimanin triliyon goma daga equator zuwa North pole na zagayen duniya.
A cikin tsarin ma'auni na daular, yadi ɗaya yana da mita 0.9144 (bayan yarjejeniya ta duniya a shekara ta alif 1959), don haka mita yana kusa da 39.37 inci : kimanin ƙafa 3.281, ko 1.0936 yadudduka.
