Tsunami (seg ajapuni 津波, tsu nami, meskuli « tayyugt n usagen ») d amazrar n timdeswalin n tallit tameqrant aṭas yetzreɛen seg tawennaḍt n waman (agaraw, ilel neɣ agelmim), yussad seg taswiɛt kan n awliwel n ubleɣ ameqran n waman, yettwaweḍen sɣur tizenzelt, tuccḍa n wakal adu-awlal neɣ aṭṭerḍeq n uburkan, dɣa yezmer ad yuɣal tayyugin tisedramin timeqranin.seba tamezwarut n tsu nami d zenzela iwaken asma ad yetru aherek n thedrin n umadal yetili deg ljiha lebher ma yella Almessi ye9reb ad yali lemwaj n waman .
