Ko te pirikahu, o te hipi (takea mai te kupu Pākehā sheep), he whakangote nui. Ka ingoatia ia mai te "kahu" mātotoru piringia (piri-) i tōna tinana. Ko te ingoa pūtaiao he Ovis aries. He pango o te waewae me te kanohi ētahi, he tea te waewae me te kanohi ētahi. Ka raratia tēnei kararehe i te Rohe Awarua Āhia ki-te-Uru, ka whakarua ia nō Hēmi Kuki ki Aotearoa i te tau 1773.
Ka kutētētia waiū mai tēnei kararehe ki te hangahanga o te tīhi i ngā motu Ūropi. E kūtia ōna huruhuru mō te rawa taketake ki te hanga o ngā pūweru mahana ("wūru", takea mai te kupu Pākehā wool).
