Euxinus is sǣ ymblegen mid land būton æt ānum nearƿum nearunesse. His rīman sind ƿiþ sūðēasternum Europan, þāra Caucasus beorgum and þāra Lǣssan Asie (Turcland).
Euxinus hæfþ sǣfæreld mid Ƿendelsǣ (Egiscan Sǣ) þurh Bosporos (æt Constantinopolim), Proponditis and Ellaspontus. Þes ƿæter bedǣlaþ Europan ƿiþ Asie.
In Euxines norþerne healf is þæt brāde land þæt ābrædaþ ēastƿeard to bedǣlenne þone Euxines miclan bodig ƿiþ þǣm Meotediscan Mere, sē is Danais mūða.
