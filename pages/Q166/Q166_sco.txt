The Black Sea is a sea in sooth-eastren Europe. It is bund bi Europe, Anatolie an the Caucasus an is ultimately connectit tae the Atlantic Ocean via the Mediterranean an the Aegean Seas an sindrie straits. The Bosphorus Strait connects it tae the Sea o Marmara, an the Strait o the Dardanelles connects that sea tae the Aegean Sea region o the Mediterranean. Thir watters sinders eastren Europe an wastren Asie. The Black Sea is connectit tae the Sea o Azov forby, bi the Strait o Kerch.
