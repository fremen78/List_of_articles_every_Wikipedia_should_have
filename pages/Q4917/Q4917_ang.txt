Geandra Rica daler (US$) is seo ambehtlicu feh ðara Geanedan Ricu America and missenlice þeodas brycaþ hine eac.
Seo Mynet Gesetnes of 1792 inbelæde ðone daler efenlice mid þæm Speoniscan seolfrene dalere, and bedældede hine in hund centum, and bidd þe seo mynetsmiððe slea sceattas in dalerum and in centum.
