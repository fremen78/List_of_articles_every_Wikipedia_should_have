United States dollar, nai to American dollar, United States of America ke sarkari currency hae. Likhe ke time American dollar ke ($) chinh ke kaam me laae ke likha jaae hae. Dollar ke USD (U.S. Dollar) ke naam se bhi jaana jaae hae.

America ke ek dollar note me George Washington ke chaapa hae. Kagaj ke note 1, 2, 5, 10, 20, 50, aur 100 dollar me chhaapa jaae hae.
Dollar coins bhi hae jisme se kuch silver aur kuchh copper hae. Vending machines jaada kar ke dollar coins ke change me de hae, kaahe ki machine ke coin dewe me kaagaj ke currency se aur jaada sahaj rahe hae. Jaada log kagaj ke dollar ke kaam me lawe hae. 
Ek American dollar me 100 cents rahe hae. Cent (jiske American log "penny" bhi bole hae) U.S. ke sab se chhota kimat ke coin hae. Aadha-dollar waala coin bhi rahe hae. Coins me hae 50 cents, Quarters (jon ki 25 cents hae), dimes (jon ki ten cents hae), nickels (jon ki five cents hae) aur pennies (jon ki ek cent hae). Sab coins aur kagaj ke paisa me jaana maana Americans ke chaapa hae.
