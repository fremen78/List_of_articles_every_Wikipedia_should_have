Idola laseMelika (uphawu : $ ; ikhodi yemali : USD ; futhi yafushanisa i-US$ ukuze ihlukanise kwezinye izinhlobo zemali zedola ; ebizwa ngokuthi idola , idola laseMelika , idola laseMelika noma i-colloquially buck ) iwuhlobo lwemali olusemthethweni eMelika nakwamanye amazwe amaningana . Umthetho we-Coinage Act ka-1792 wethula idola laseMelika ngokulingana nedola lesiliva laseSpain , walihlukanisa laba amasenti ayi-100 , futhi wagunyaza ukugaywa kwezinhlamvu zemali ezinamadola namasenti. Amanothi ase-US akhishwa ngendlela ye- Federal Reserve Notes , edume ngokuthi ama-greenbacks ngenxa yombala wawo oluhlaza kakhulu.
