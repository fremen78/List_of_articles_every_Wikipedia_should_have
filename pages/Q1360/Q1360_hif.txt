Arthropods (phylum Arthropoda) uu janwar ke bola jawe hae jisme  backbone nai rahe hae. Kuch athropod hae;  Kirwa, makrraa, kekrra, jhinga, gojar, aur centipedes.  
Jaada kar ke arthropod jamiin pe rahe hae, lekin kuchh paani me bhi rahe hae. Iske aur sab jaanwar ke hisaab se sab se jaada species hae. Arthropod duusra jaanwar ke khaatir khaana hae. Kuchh insaan bhi iske khaae hae. Jaada arthropod ke species, kirwaa hae.
