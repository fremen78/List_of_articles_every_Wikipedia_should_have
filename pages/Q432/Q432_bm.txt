Silamɛya walima silamɛya, fana Mahamadu (Arabukan na: الإسلام, a kɔrɔ kɔrɔ ye ko majigilenya, majigin, kololi Ala kelenpe ani a sago) ye diɲɛ diinɛ dɔ ye min jɛnsɛnna kosɛbɛ, min sigira senkan san kɛmɛda 7nan na Arabu jamana na. Kira Muhamadu (570-632) jate bɛ kɛ a sigibaga ye.
Silamɛya bɛ jira iko Ala kelenpe diinɛ min ju bɔra Ibrahima la. Silamɛya nɔfɛmɔgɔw bɛ wele silamɛw. Sisan, nin diinɛ in nɔfɛmɔgɔw bɛɛ lajɛlen hakɛ bɛ diɲɛ kɔnɔ, o bɛ daminɛ miliyari 1,1 na ka se miliyari 1,8 ma, o bɛ silamɛya kɛ diinɛba filanan ye kerecɛnya kɔfɛ. Silamɛw ye jamana caman ka jamanaden fanba ye Azi ani Afiriki. Silamɛya ka jogo dɔ ye a jɔyɔrɔ ye sigida ni laadalakow ɲɛnabɔbaga ye. Silamɛw ka jɛkulu kɔnɔ, hadamaden ka ɲɛnamaya ni sigida fan bɛɛ bɛ labɛn diinɛ fɛ.


== Daɲɛ in bɔcogo (Origin) ==
Silamɛya, n’a bamanankanna ka bɔ arabukan na, o kɔrɔ ye majigilenya ye — o kɔrɔ ye ko ɲɛnamaya min bɛ bɛn diɲɛ ma, ɲɛnamaya hɛrɛ la ni Sebaayabɛɛtigi ye, ka kolo a sago ye. Daɲɛ min bɛ Arabukan na, o ju ye slm ye — daɲɛ minnu ju ye kelen ye:
salam - hɛrɛ, hɛrɛ, .
salim - hɛrɛ, hɛrɛ, .
salima - ka hɛrɛ, silamɛ - silamɛ - mɔgɔ min ni diɲɛ bɛnna, ka a yɛrɛ majigin Kɔrɔtalenba (Ala, Allah) kɔrɔ.
Yan, a bɛnnen don fana ka silamɛ foli fɔ - Salam alaikum walima assalamu alaikum, o kɔrɔ ye ko Hɛrɛ ka kɛ aw kan !
Sìlàmɛya (الإسلام) bɛ diinɛ.
