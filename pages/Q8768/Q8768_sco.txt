Henry Ford (30 Julie, 1863 – 7 Aprile, 1947) wis the foonder o the Ford Motor Company an is creditit wi holpin mak a middle cless in American society. He wis ane o the first til applee assembly line manufacturin ti the mass production o cheap caurs. This feat didna juist revolutionise industrial production in the United States an the lave o the warld, but haed a fluence ower modren cultur ti sic an extent that monie social theorists identifee's this phase o economic an social history as "Fordism."
