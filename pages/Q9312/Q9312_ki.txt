nĩ Mũndũ Mũgo (Philosopher) mũjĩrĩmani ũrĩa agwetetwo kaingĩ ta ũmwe wa eciria a bata mũno gĩtarũ-inĩ kĩa Rũraya.

aciarirwo 22 april 1724 handũ hetagwo konisberg, ũthamaki wa prussia (riũ wĩtagwo kaliningrad, Racia) na no kuo akuĩre tarĩki 12 february 1804 ena mĩaka 79.

rũrenda rwa mawĩra make mahutĩtie iharo nyandandũku kuma Ũgo (logic), Mĩguto (ethics), ginya Nyagathanga (aesthetics).
