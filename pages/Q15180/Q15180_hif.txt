Soviet Union, jiske sarkari naam Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (USSR) rahaa, ek russian des rahaa, 1922 se 1991 talak. Iske 14 Soviet socialist republics aur ek Soviet Federative Socialist Republic (Russian SFSR) rahaa. Jon des isme rahin lekin abhi ajaad des hae ke suchi hae:

Armenia
Azerbaijan
Belarus
Estonia
Georgia
Kazakhstan
Kyrgyzstan
Latvia
Lithuania
Moldova
Russia
Tajikistan
Turkmenistan
Ukraine
Uzbekistan
Soviet Union, Russian Revolution ke lagbhag paach saal baad banaawa gais jab Vladimir Lenin leaser banaa. Lenin ii des ke communist banais jisme sarkar sab rakam ke utpaadan pe control rakkhis. Ii union ke sab se barraa des Russia rahaa, duusra sab se barraa des Kazakhistan aur tiisra sab se barra des Unkaine rahaa. Iske capital Moscow rahaa.
Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union des kae kaanun banaawat rahaa, lekin asliyat me communist party ke General Secretary ke isme puura haanth rahaa. Communist Party ke general secretary ke suchi hae:

Joseph Stalin (3 April 1922 - {16 October]] 1952)
Nikita Khrushchev (7 September 1953 - 14 Octobe 1964)
Leonid Brezhnes (14 October 1964 - 10 November 1982)
Yuri Andropov (12 November 1982 9 February 1984)
Konstantin Chernenko (13 February 1984 - 10 March 1985)
Mikhail Gorbachev (11 March]] 1985 - 24 August 1991)
