Tadukli n Tegdudiwin Tinemlayin Tisuvyitiyin neɣ Tadukli Tasuvyatit (s tarusit: Союз Советских Социалистических Республик (asusru )), d awanak aqbur n Turuft ed n Asya, seg 1922 ar 1991, tamanaɣt-ines Musku, tajumma-ines ahat 22 400 000 km². Tadukli Tasuvyatit teṭṭef-d seg yilel n Baltik ar yilel Aberkan ed ugaraw Amelwi. Deg iseggasen-is ineggura, xemsṭac n tigdudiwin tinemlayin tisuvyitiyin i yellan deg awanak-nni : Arminya, Azerbijan, Byilurusya (Byilurusya n tura), Isṭunya, Jurjya, Kazaxestan, Kirgizistan (Kirgizistan n tura), Latvya, Litwanya, Muldavya (Muldavya n tura), Rusya, Ṭaǧikstan, Turkmenistan, Ukrayin ed Uzbekistan.
