Alemanya or Germany, say Federal Republic na Germany odino Bundesrepublik Deutschland (diad salitan German), naanap ed Pegley na Europe. Aliktoben na Baybay Dayat, Denmark tan Baltic Dayat ed baybay, Poland tan Czechia ed bokig, Austria tan Switzerland ed abalaten, san France, Luxembourg, Belgium tan Netherlands ed sagor. Say kabesera et Berlin.
Say Alemanya so adeskribe bilang baleg a pakapanyari a walaan na mabiskeg ya ekonomiya; walaan na sankabalgan ya ekonomia ed Europa, komapat a sankabalgan ya ekonomia ed mundo diad nominal a GDP tan komalima a sankabalgan diad PPP. Bilang sankamundoan a pakapanyari ed saray sektor na industriya, siensia tan teknolohiya, sikato so komatlo a sankamakelkan eksportador tan importador ed mundo. Bilang sakey ya amaaligwas a bansa, itatarya toy social security, universal health care system, tan tuition-free university education. Say Alemanya et membro na United Nations, European Union, NATO, Council of Europe, G7, G20 tan OECD. Satan so komatlon sankarakelan ya bilang na UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
