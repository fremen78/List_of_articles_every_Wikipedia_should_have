Aristotle (Greek Αριστοτέλης Aristotelēs) (enianak 384 BC diad Stagira, ed Macedonia, Greece; enatey Marso 7, 322 BC diad Chalicis, Euboea, Greece), sakey ya daan ya Greek philosopher. Para ed dakel ran totoo, sikato san si Plato, so sankaimportantean ya philosopher ed kanunotan na West. Wala ray mangibabaga ya akapansulat si Aristotle na 150 libro ed Philosophy.
