Aristotile (Chigiriki: Ἀριστοτέλης Aristotélēs; 384–322 BC) aive mugondamano uye chisepe cheVugiriki Yekare. Zvinyorwa zvake zvinotaura ndaba dzizhinji dzakasiyana kusanganisa guruzivo, ugondamano, ururimi, upanganhundu, hutonganyika, ruambandanga uye zveuseza. Semuvambi we Chikoro che Ruperipati cheugondamano muRikeuma (kureva Lyceum) muAthina Yekare, akatanga tsika yakafaruka ye Huaristotile wakatoodzwa, ndiyo yakave bwaro rekuumba guruzivo yezvarizvino.
