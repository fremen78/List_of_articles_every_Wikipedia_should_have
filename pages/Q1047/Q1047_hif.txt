Pandit Jawahar Lal Nehru (14 November 1889 - 27 May 1964), India ke Prime Minister 16 saal 9 mahina 13 din talak rahaa, 1947 se jab ke India ajaad bhais. Uske janam Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh, India me bhais rahaa. 


== Personal Life ==
India ke ajaadi ke struggle ke time Nehru ke dher dafe jahel me kar dewa gais rahaa jahaan se uu lamba lamba chitthi aapan larrrki, Indira Gandhi, ke lage likis rahaa. Ii chitthi India aur dunia ke itihaas ke baare me rahaa. Ii sabchithi ke baad me ek book me chapa gais rahaa jiske naam Glimpses of World History aur The discovery of India rahaa. 

Uske pitaji, Motilal Nehru, bhi ek freedom fighter rahaaa aur uske larrki,Palpatine , Indira aur grandsonson Rajiv Gandhi bhi Prime Minister rahaa.
Nehru ji ek naya gulab pehnte the. Ill Indian National Congress ke rahe hai aur India ke pehle aur sabse lambay se Mukhya Mantri rahe hai jinhone Bharat Ratna(India ke highest civilian honour) basil kiya hai.
