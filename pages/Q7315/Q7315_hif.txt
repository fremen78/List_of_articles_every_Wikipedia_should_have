Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky (Russian: Пётр Ильич Чайковский, Pëtr Il’ič Čajkovskij; (janam: Kamsko-Votkinsk, 7 May 1840; maut: St Petersburg, 6 November 1893) ek Russian composer rahaa jon ki Romantic period ke rahaa. Uu sabse popular Russian composer rahaa. Uu Western Europe ke music se bahut chij sikhis rahaa. Uske compositions me 11 operas, 3 ballets, orchestral music, chamber music aur 100 se jaada gaana rahaa. Uske ballets (Swan Lake, The Nutcracker  aur Sleeping Beauty) ke classical music ke khaas music maana jaawe hae.
