Kong Hu Cu meyalo Konfusius, matiye ma'o bo he tanggulalo Kongcu (Hanzi: 孔夫子、孔子, hanyu pinyin: Kongfuzi、Kongzi) (551 SM – 479 SM) yito guru ngota meyalo ta motota boli o tinepo masahuru wawu filsuf sosial to Tiongkok. Filsafatliyo momaralu moral lohihilawo wawu ulipu, wawu ma lowali masahuru sababu asasliyo u mototoheto to sipati tradisional Tionghoa. Ta hito'opuwa to agama Kong Hu Cu, ma longaku tiyo botiye nabi.
