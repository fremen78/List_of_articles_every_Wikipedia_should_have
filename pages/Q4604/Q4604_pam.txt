I Confucius (Chinese: 孔子; pinyin: Kǒng zǐ; Wade–Giles: K'ung-tzu, o Chinese: 孔夫子; pinyin: Kǒng Fūzǐ; Wade–Giles: K'ung-fu-tzu), " ( Septiembri 28, 551 B.C.E. – 479 B.C.E.) metung yang Isik talaisip ampong pangaguman philosopu, nung nu ing kayang tuturung philosopu ayasag nong malalam deng Isik, Koreanu, Japon ampong Vietnamese keng pamisip ampong bie.
