A tower is an high edifice. Kwimbali yoyilo lwezakhiwo, iinqaba zibaluleke kakhulu kwihlabathi liphela, njengazo zombini izinto ezisebenzayo, (indawo yokujonga okanye ukubona, iintsimbi zentsimbi, iiminarethi, iinqaba zewotshi, iinqaba zomoya, njl. njl.) ngokufuthi ukudibanisa iimpawu ezimbini nangaphezulu zosetyenziso zibe sisiqalelo esinye.
