Þē trum (Niwenglisc: drum) is a gesundmacian. Hit is gesundmacian mid þæm handum, hwīlum ġebrūcende a druma sticca (a sticca tō ūtstriciane a druma). Āne gefērscipe druma and cimbala is ġecīged druma cȳt, oþþe druma sittan.
Trumas sint ġebrūcade tō āsacan stēadan swātan on a lēod. Hīe ġiefan mūsical manig cynna ænne andfealdnys. For Þām mēodon, tō dōn a sang tō be swāwl oþþe hrædlīce, Þā druma spēla swærr oþþe hrædor.
