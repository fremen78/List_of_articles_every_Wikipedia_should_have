Enzymes, cells ke protein molecules hae jon ki catalyst ke kaam kare hae. Enzymes, chemical reaction ke aur jor kar de hae lekin apne se react nai hoe hae. 


== Enzyme are catalyst, meaning they speed up the rate at which reactants interact to form product in a chemical reaction.While not being consumed in the reaction. they physically combine chemical reactants in the way that lowers the energy required.. ==
