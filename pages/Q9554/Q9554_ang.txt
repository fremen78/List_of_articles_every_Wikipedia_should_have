Martin Luther (10 Blōtmōnaþ 1483 oþ 18 Solmōnaþ 1546) ƿæs staþolede þā Cirice Ednīƿunge þærbe ƿæs þære Cirican micel dæl forsceappen and of þæs lār cumaþ þā Oncƿeðende Cirican.  He ƿæs, sƿa secgð man, se 'Fæder of Oncƿeðenesse'.
Luther ƿæs geboren in Eisleben in Seaxlande (se ƿæs rīce on Germanie).  His fæder, se hātte hans Luther, heald lǣnland of oran.  In 1505 ƿeard Martin munuc þæs Augustine munuchāde, and in 1512 ƿæs lārēoƿ  mid setl in Ƿittenbeorges Eormengyld.
Geƿæmnednes in þære Cirican and ƿōh in hiere ƿord ƿiþ þone Godspell drāfon Luther to brēcan þærmid.  On 31 Ƿinterfylleþ in 1517 he næglede 95 Þeses on þære Castelcirican dure in Ƿittenbearge þær ƿæs he lārēoƿ þærin oncƿæþ he ƿiþ gedƿolnesse and geƿæmnednesse and þes dǣd ƿæs þære Ednīƿunge anginn.
Lutheres lār amanode þæs pāpadōmes ealdornes and he gesēðede þe sēo Biblioþēce is se ān frūma of æfæstnesse ealdordōm and þe eall Crīsten mid fulluhte sind eallic prēosthād. Be Lutheres lār is hǣlu frēoh giefu fram Gode, afangen ǣnlice be true behrēoƿsung and trēoƿe in Iesus sƿa Messiah, se is alēafa gegiefen be Gode and nis be þære Cirican.
