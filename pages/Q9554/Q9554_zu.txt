Martin Luther  (10 ngoLwezi 1483 – 18 ngoNhlolanja 1546) ubengumfundisi useJalimani. umchazithixo (theologian), umbhali, umqambi wamahubo (hymnwriter), kanye noSolwazi. Umzalwane wangaphambili wama-Augustinian, waziwa njengomholi oqavile eziNguqukweni zabaNqathuzi kanye nokuqanjwe ngaye ibandla lamaLuthela.
