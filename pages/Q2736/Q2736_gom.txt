Futbol zaun asa sounsaracho sogleantlo avodlolo khell, zaka 200 voir desanim, 250 million log khelltat. Ho khell ek moidanant khelltat, zachea donui vordank gol asat. Khellacho udexyo mhonnlear dusrea teamachea golant futbol bhitor ghalunk. Futbolak akhea sonvsaran FIFA rajkaran choloita, zalear Indiek All India Football Federation choloita, ani Goeant, Goa Football Association. Futbolacho prosidh mukhel spordha zaun asa World Cup.
Goenkarank futbol khup avodta. Goeant GFA mukhel spordha choloita tem Goa Professional League. Desachea sthorar, Goem futbol team Santosh trophint khelta zalear FC Goa Indian Super Leagueant, and Goenche teami I-Leagueant ui khelltat.
