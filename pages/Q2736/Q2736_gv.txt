She spoyrt fwirranagh eh sockyr ny soccer. Ny smenkey, ta sleih cur bluckan coshey er myr enmys cadjin, ga dy vel bluckan coshey currit er gammanyn cosoylagh elley. T'ad cloie yn gamman marish bluckan runt eddyr daa wirran as 11 bluckaneyr er dagh fwirran. Ta mysh 250 millioon dy vluckaneyryn ayns ny smoo na 200 çheeraghynas thallooyn-croghaneagh as, rere shen, she yn spoyrt smoo cadjin er y theihll t'ayn. Ta'n gamman cloiet er faaie kiare-chuilleigagh as maidjaghyn dean ec dagh kione. She dean y ghamman na'n bluckan y ghleashaghey harrish linney dean y chondaigys as eh y yannoo ny smoo na'n condaigys hene çheusthie jeh 90 minnidyn.
She FIFA (Frangish: Fédération Internationale de Football Association) lught-reill eddyr-ashoonagh y ghamman as t'ad soit ayns Zürich, yn Elveeish.


== Reillyn y ghamman ==
Ta daa wirran lesh un chloieder jeig ayn y wirran as she yeearree y ghamman y bluckan dy chur harrish linney dean y phossan elley. T'eh meelowal, magh as freilleyder y dean, dy ventyn rish y bluckan.
