United Nations (UN) ek international organization hae. Iske World War II ke baad, 1945 me, suruu karaa gais rahaa. Iske headquarters hai Geneva, Switzerland aur New York, United States of America mein. UN sab continent mein maujood hae.  Inke international charitable organization bhi kehlaya jata hai aur duniya ke seva karne me maiez hae. Ill ke speciality hai scientific research, gareebi mitana, bacha mar logo ke madat karna. Ill dusre desho ke saath morche nikalte hai dusre level par jaise ki schools mein, head quarters mein aur assemblies mein. Li zyada tar population, gareebi mitana, aur empowerment mein maeiz hai aur politics mein bhi shamil hae. Ill ke heritage sites bhi hote hai duniya bhar.
