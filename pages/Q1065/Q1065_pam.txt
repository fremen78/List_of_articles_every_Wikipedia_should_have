Ing Misasanmetung a Bangsa (UN; French: Organisation des Nations Unies, ONU) o United Nations king Ingles, metung yang aguman da reng bangsa, nung nu kayabe kareng kayang makasulat a parasan ing pamipasulung king pamisaup-saup king leying internasyunal (international law), seguridad a internasyunal (international security), panyulung a ekonomiku (economic development), panyulung a pangketawan (social progress), katulirang pantau (human rights), katulirang sibil (civil rights), katimawang sibil (katimawang pampulitika), demokrasya (democracy), ampo ing pangmalwatan a karatunang pangyatu (world peace). Mitátag ya ing Misasanmetung a Bangsa anyang 1945 kaybat ning Kadwang Gerang Pangyatu ba yang alilan ing after World War II to replace the Liga ding Bangsa (League of Nations), bang mayari ing gera da reng bangsa, at bang magsilbing plataporma ning pamisabi-sabi. Dakal ya susuportang aguman o organisasyun ba lang papalyari deng kayang misyon.
