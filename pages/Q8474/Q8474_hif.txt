Suleiman I (سليمان Sulaymān, Süleyman; almost always Kanuni Sultan Süleyman Turkish me) (6 November 1494 – 5 September  1566), Ottoman Empire ke 10th Sultan rahaa. Uu 1520 se 1566 talak raj karis rahaa. UAke West me Suleiman the Magnificent ke naam se jaana jaawe hae  aur Islamic world, me the Lawgiver (in Turkish Kanuni; Template:Lang-ar, al‐Qānūnī), kahe ki uu Ottoman legal system ke badal diis rahaa.
