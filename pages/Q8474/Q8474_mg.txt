Suleiman I dia pôeta teraka ny 6 Novambra 1494 ary maty ny 5 Septambra 1566


== Ny fiainany manokana ==
I Selim I no rainy. Ny vadiny dia i Roxelana, i Mahidevran, i Gülfem i Hatun, ary i Fülane Hatun. Ny zanany dia i Şehzade Mustafa, i Şehzade Mehmed, i Mihrimah Sultan, i Selim II, ary i Şehzade Beyazıt.
