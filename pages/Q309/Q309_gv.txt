Ta shennaghys çheet er studeyrys as docamadys y traa hie shaghey. Ta taghyrtyn roish cummey coryssyn screeuee coontit myr y roie-hennaghys. She enmys fascaaidagh eh "shennaghys" ta goaill stiagh taghyrtyn shaghey chammah as cooinaghtyn, feddyn magh, çhaglym, reagheydys, toyrt, as soilshaghey ny taghyrtyn shen. Ta screeudeyryn shennaghys shirrey fyssyree bentyn rish y traa hie shaghey liorish feanishyn shendeeagh lheid as docamadyn screeuit, coontyssyn beill, jeantane ellyn as stoo, as cowraghyn egg-oaylleeagh.
