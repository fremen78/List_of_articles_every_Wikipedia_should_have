Amezruy d tazrawt n tidyanin yeḍran, d acu kan, Amezruy mačči d tussna am tiyaḍ axaṭer ur nezmir ara ad nessen akk tiɣawsiwin, daɣnetta, nzemmer a nemmeslay ɣef tamusni n tiẓri n tidyanin.
Amezruy yesseqdac tazrawt n titeggarin (Tateggart: document), asmi ur nesɛi titeggartin, nemmeslay ɣef azarmezruy (préhistoire).
