iNyanga (uphawu: ) ngumzulane omncane ozungeza umhlaba wethu uNomkhubulwane. INyanga iyiDuna lesihlanu ngobukhulu kuzungezohlelo lukaNomkhubulwane, futhi inkulu kunanoma yisiphi isiThomiyana. Iswela umoyambulunga, amanzimbulunga kanye nobuwonga. UmNyondo osebusweni bayo ungange ngxenye eyodwa kweziyisithupha zikaNomkhubulwane (0.1654 g). INyanga  kaMkhulukhulwana uLo yaziwa njengeDuna elikuNozungezilanga elinomNyondo ophakeme kanye nesiluthi.
