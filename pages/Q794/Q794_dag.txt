Iran, din yuli lahi booni Persia bee Islamic Republic of Iran, nyɛla ti!ŋgbani shɛli din be West Asia. Di dila tarisi ni Iraq mini Turkey zaŋ chaŋ, Azerbaijan mini Armenia zaŋ chaŋ northwest, Caspian Sea mini Turkmenistan zaŋ chaŋ north, Afghanistan mini Pakistan zaŋ chaŋ east, n-ti pahi Gulf of Oman ni Persian Gulf zaŋ chaŋ south. Di tiŋgbani yɛliŋ nyɛla 1.64 million square kilometres (0.63 million square miles), ka di zuɣu chɛ ka di nyɛ tiŋgbani din dɔ pishi ata ka tiŋgbani galisim polo. Ka di salo galisim nyɛ 86.8 million, Iran n dɔ pishi ata ka zuɣu tiŋsi din yuli du polo, ka nyɛ tiŋ'shɛli din pahiri buyi galisim polo Middle East. Di tiŋ'zuɣu mini tiŋ'titali n-nyɛ Tehran.
