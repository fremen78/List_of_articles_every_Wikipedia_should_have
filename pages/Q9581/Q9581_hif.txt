Confucianism ek philosophy hae jon ki Confucius (511 BC - 479 BC), jon ki ek Chinese philosopher rahaa, ke teachings pe aadharit hae.
Confucianism ii biswas kare hae ki log duusra log ke baare me soche, paisa aur imaarat ke baare me nai. Uu log  ii biswas kare hae ki janata ke maane ke khaatir tagrraa niyam (rules) hoe ke chaahi. Confucianism ii maane hae ki, log me paanch rista (relationshp) ke niyam hae, aur usme bhi aapan aapan niyam rahe hae. Dui jan hoe sake hae:

Rajkumar aur janata
Pitaji aur larrka
Admii aur aurat
Bujurg (elder) aur larrka
dost
II sab traditional rista hae jiske "five prototypes" bola jaae hae. Confucianism me ii sab sambandh (relationship) me log ke niyam ke paalan kare ke parri. Jaise ki, janata ke rajkaumar ke sune ke parri, lekin rajkumar ke bhi janata ke sune ke chaahi, uske achchha se raj kare ke khatir.
