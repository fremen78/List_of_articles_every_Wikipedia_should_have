American Civil War United States me 1861 se 1865 talak larraa gais rahaa . Gyrah southern gulaam ke rakkhe waala states ek hoe ke, jiske Confederate States of America (CSA) bola jaawat rahaa, ajaadi ke khatir United States ke sarkar ke larris rahaa. Uu logan "the Union" se larrin rahaa, jon ki U.S. federal government rahaa jiske aur sab state support karat rahaa. Union States (jiske kabhi kabhi "the North" bola jaawat rahaa) uu sab state rahaa jahan pe slavery legal nai rahaa.
