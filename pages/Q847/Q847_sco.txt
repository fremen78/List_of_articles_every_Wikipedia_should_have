Tennis is a sport uisually played atween twa players (singles) or atween twa teams o twa players ilk o thaim (doubles). Ilka player uises a racket that is strung tae sclaff a hollae rubber baw covered wi felt ower a net intae the opponent's court. The goal o the gemm is ti muiv the baw so that the ither player canna hit richtlie. The player who canna hit richtlie will gennae point, while the ither player will.
Tennis is an Olympic sport an is played at aw levels o society at aw ages. The sport can be played bi onybody that can haud a racket, includin fowk in wheelchairs.
