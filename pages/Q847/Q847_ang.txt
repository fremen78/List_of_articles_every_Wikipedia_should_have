Tennis oþþe nettgeardþōðer is plega þe is gepleged betweoh oþþe twǣm mannum oþþe twǣm hēapum þāra ǣlc hæfþ twegen plegendas. Ǣlc plegend brȳcþ nettbatt tō swingenne holne þennungteorwenne þōðer þe hæbbe feltenne brǣdels ofer nett in þæs winnan geard. Se ende þæs plegan is tō forþsendenne þone þōðer swā þæt se winna ne cunne sendan hine eft. Ǣnig þe mihte healdan batt cann tennis plegan, ēac hādas on hweogolstōlum.
