I Sir Peter Paul Rubens (Pangayagkas a Holandes: [ˈrybə(n)s]; 28 Junio 1577 – 30 Mayo 1640), metung yang talapintang Flemish Baroque, ampong talataguyud ning metung a "gastarul" (extravagant) a istilong Baroque a mititimid king kimut, kule, ampong sensualidad. Kilala ya kareng Counter-Reformation a altarpiece, larawan, landscape, ampong larawan ning amlat (history painting) da reng paksang ibat king mitolohiya ampong alegorya.
Bukud king pamanibala king maragul a studio king Antwerp a maglalang pininturang sikat kareng aristokrata (nobility) ampo kareng pamitipun ning arte o kalalangan king mabilug a Europa, menigaral ya king klasika i Rubens a iskolar a humanista, talatipun arte, ampong diplomatikung gewa rang kabalyero o suging Felipe IV, Ari ning Espanya, ampong Charles I, Ari ning Inglaterra.
