Va Charles André Joseph Marie de Gaulle (1890–1970)  ny eaghtyrane as n'eniagh caggee ny Frank. Rug eh ayns Lille as v'eh ynsit ayns Collège Stanislas ayns Paarys. Chur eh Forseyn ny Frangee Heyrey er bun 'sy vlein 1940 rish y Nah Chaggey Dowanagh. Ayns y vlein 1959 v'eh reiht myr y 22oo eaghtyrane ny Frank. Chur eh pobblaght noa er bun, y 5oo phobblaght. Hooar eh baase giense thie ayns Colombey-les-Deux-Églises 'sy vlein 1970.
