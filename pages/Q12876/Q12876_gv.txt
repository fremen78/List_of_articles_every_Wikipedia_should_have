She carbyd caggee eillit eh yn tank. T'eh kiaddit myr bun-wappin commyssagh ayns caggey thallooin 'sy linney toshee. Ta tankyn kiaddit as geill currit da niart gunn lajer, eilley lajer, as so-ghleashaght vagher caggee vie ta kiarit liorish queeyllyn oltit as jeshaght chummaghtagh; son y choois smoo, ta'n ard-arm soit ayns tooran. T'ad ry-akin dy mennick ayns forseyn thallooin 'sy 20oo as 21d eashyn as t'ad coontit myr ayrn ogheragh jeh'n chaggey co-armit.
