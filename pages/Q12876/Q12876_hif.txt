Tank ek armoured larre waala gaarri (vehicle) hae jiske jaada kar ke dharti pe aakrraman kare khaatir kaam me laawa jaae hae. Tank me track rahe hae, jisse iske weight jaada area me gaila rahe hae aur ii matti me dhase nai hae. Tank me ek taagatwar toop (cannon) aur ek nai to jaada machine gun rhae hae.
