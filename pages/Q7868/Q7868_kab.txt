Takwat neɣ Tabniqt d tigget tamesniyt d twurayt deg imuddiren, Acku yal amuddir yettwasni seg yiwet n tebniqt neɣ ugar, ttufarsen-t tebniqin seg unegzum akawtan seld n gemmu-ynes.
Tikwatin ttugezmen-t ar tekwatin tamɣawt d tekwatin taɣersawt akken i llan-t daɣen tgezzumin n iden irewsen (yettemcabin) deg wesnay ixeddmen (akked) kra n twuri deg umuddir imezṭi asnudran, Takwatt deg-s tifekkiwin meẓẓiyen fell-as isem-is takwegmant am tifekkiwin n Golgi d uɣwaw yettafdamen s agensu-ynes (daxel-is) Angal asmmezray DNA.
