ILondon iyinhlokodolobha yoMbuso Ohlangeneyo. Ngesikhathi pambuso iRhomani babiza ngokuthi "Londinium".


== Umlando wegama ==
Umlando wegama "iLondon" ungabaza. Iyigama idaladala: Yatholakala kungaba 121 AD kwa-Londinium, okhombisa umdabu weRhomani. Incasiselo ilinga ukuqala, idelela manje, kwa-Geoffrey weMonmouth e-Historia Regum Britanniae. Yacabanga Inkosi Lud, yaqamba idolobha Kaerlud.
Emuva kwa-1899 yamukela igama unendabuko waCeltic yisho indawo ngendoda uqamba *Londinos. Richard Coates usho ngo-1998 okhombisa umdabu we-Yurobhu Idaladala *(p)lowonida, yisho 'umfula ubanzi ngokweqile ukuwela'.

Phambile kwa-1889 igama "London" ngokusemthethweni Idolobha weLondon kodwa manje upheca Isifunda weLondon na manje ILondon Kakhulu.
