Lundene (Lǣden: Londinium, Niwenglisc: London) is hēafodburg þæs Geānedan Cynerīces and Englalandes, and is on ealre ƿorulde þāra grīetestra burga ān. Hit hæfþ seofon þūsendu þūsenda lēoda in Grēatrum Lundene (hātte Lundeneras).
His fyrmesta nama ƿæs Londinium, hēafodburg þæs Rōmāniscan underrīces þæs naman Britannia ƿæs, and hit ƿierþ hēafodburg þæs Bryttiscan Rīces. 
Lundenes ǣrest tūnscipe ƿæs se stede þǣre ealdan Romaniscan ceastre in þǣm ƿeallum. Under Ælfrede cyninge setledon þā Lundenƿare in þǣm ƿeallum fram hira ealdum ƿic, se is Ealdƿic. Oþþæt þā ƿealla habbaþ geƿiðged, sēo stōƿe, Lundenceaster, ābīdeþ sƿā Lundenes heorte and cēapung hēafodburg tō Bretene and þǣre ƿorulde.


== Māre Lunden ==

Þæt micele ceasterisce land ymbe Lundene is Māre Lunden. Hit befēhþ nēan ealle Middelseaxe, micele dǣlas geond Sūþrigan, Cent, and Ēastseaxe and Heortfordscīre 
dǣlas.
Ofer Māran Lundene rīcsaþ þæt Māran Lundene Gerec, þæt is þæt Lundene Burgƿeald and sēo Lundene Ymbcyme.
