Thailand, bɛ ni booni shɛli Kingdom of Thailand ka taarihi puuni di nyɛ Siam, nyɛla tiŋgbani shɛli din be Southeast Asia zaŋ chaŋ Indochinese Peninsula. Di salo galisim ni miri kamani 70 miliyɔŋ,Di tiŋgbani yɛliŋ nyɛla 513,120 square kilometres (198,120 sq mi). Thailand di la tarisi ni north  Myanmar mini Laos, zaŋ chaŋ east  Laos mini Cambodia, south Gulf of Thailand mini Malaysia, n-ti pahi west Andaman Sea; Di lahi di la mɔɣ'tarisi ni Vietnam zaŋ chaŋ southeast, ni Indonesia mini India zaŋ chaŋ southwest. Bangkok n-nyɛ tiŋgbani maa tiŋ'zuɣu mini tiŋ'titali.
