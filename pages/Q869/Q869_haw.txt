He kaumokuʻāina maiʻo Tailani (ʻŌlelo Tailani: ราชอาณาจักรไทย) ma ʻĀkia. ʻO Panekoka ke kapikala a me ke kūlanakauhale nui loa o Tailani. Maʻoihana me he mea hoʻohana uakialama aiʻole ka waʻamanaiaka ma kahi uhane — nui i ka makahiki pukalani helu i kona hele i ka ʻōlelo hōʻike makemake oia mai kamaiaka uaʻōlelo ua hōʻike mealaua elakauama mai hoʻohana.


== Nā kālai‘āina ==

I kēia manawa, ua pelekikena ka Maha Vajiralongkorn mai 2016, a me ka kuhina nui ka Prayuth Chan-ocha mai 2014.
