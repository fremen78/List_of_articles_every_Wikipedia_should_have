William Shakespeare (1564 nî  – 1616 nî ) sī Eng-tē ê si-jîn kap ke̍k-chok-ka. Shakespeare sī ēng Eng-gí lâi siá-chok ê toā chok-ka, iok-liōng ū siá 38 pún kha-pún, 154 siú cha̍p-sì-choā-si (sonnet), kap chi̍t-koá pa̍t khoán ê si. Romeo kap Juliet, Hamlet, ... téng-téng lóng sī Shakespeare chin ū-miâ ê chok-phín.
