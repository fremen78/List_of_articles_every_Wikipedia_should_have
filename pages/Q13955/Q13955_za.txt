Vahahlahboh (اَلْعَرَبِيَّةُ
, al-ʿarabiyyah [al ʕaraˈbijːa]) dwg Vahsaejcu giz Vahfeihcouh-Yacouh ranz gangj baihndaw Ahlahboh ciqyaiq. Gizhaenx dwg 32 leizcing giz Vahahlahboh.
Vahahlahboh dwg daihsam geij cienzbouh mbe hak vah yijhou Vahyingzgoz caeuq Vahfazgoz, it giz loeg hak vah giz Lianheguo, caeuq dwg cungqyauq vah giz Yihsihlancaeux.
Vahahlahboh dwg gangj ndaw 380 baijwanq gangjboux, liangjgeqdouh mbwnnamh caeuq mbouj mbwnnamh, suojyij Vahahlahboh daihhaj geij gangj vah baihndaw lajmbwn.
Vahahlahboh dwg raiz ndawj Vahahlahboh cizmuj,  aenlawz dwg Saw abjad caeuq dwg dajlaeng baih giz swx.
