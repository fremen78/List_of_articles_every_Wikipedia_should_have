Chiarabhu (Chingezi: Arabic; Chiarabhu: العَرَبِيَّة (al-ʻarabiyyah), zita refu al-luġatu al-ʿarabiyya) kana Chiarabhiki mutauro we Chishemu unoshandiswa nevanhu 206 miriryoni semutauro wekutanga uye nevanhu 246 miriyoni semutauro wechipiri. Kunyangwe paine mitauro mizhinji, Chiarabhu Chakajairika (الفصحى fuṣḥā) ndiwo mutauro wepamutemo wenyika 22 eLeague ye Arabhu uye neMusangano weUyisalamu uye mumwe wemitauro ye pamutemo ye Mubatanidzwa weMadhidhizha (United Nations). Iko kune zvakare kushandiswa semutauro we chinamato cheUyisalamu.
Nekudaro Chiarabhu ndiyo imwe yemitauro yakakosha pasirose inoverengwa pamitengo yakasiyana nemamiriyoni evatendi veUyisalamu munzira yavo yekudzidza nekunzwisisa Kurani. Semutauro wechitendero unokanganisa mitauro mizhinji pasi rose uye nehunhu netsika dzevanhu vakasiyana siyana pamwe nemadzinza eVasalama.
Chiarabhu chinowanzo nyorwa neKeruro ye Chiarabhu.
Iwo mutauro wakapfuma hukuru mazwi, kutsetsenura kutaura kukuru uye kudzora kukuru kwe girama (grammar) uye syntax.
