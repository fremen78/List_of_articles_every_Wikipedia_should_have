The chicken (Gallus gallus, whiles G. gallus domesticus), aft kent as a teukie frae the teuk teuk soond it maks, is a caif foul. Evidence newlin foond mynts that domestication o the chicken wis unner wey in Vietnam ower 10,000 year syne. Afore this discovery, conventional wit haudit that the chicken wis domesticatit in Indie.
A poullie is a young chicken for bylin in a pat.
