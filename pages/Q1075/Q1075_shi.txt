Aklu (mgt. ikʷlan) iga-t ɣikelli s ttannayen midden tifawt.
Ikʷlan imenza ɣ dar icelḥiyen gan-ten: aseggan neɣ usgin (aẓeṭṭaf), amellal neɣ umlil, azeggʷaɣ neɣ uzwiɣ, awraɣ d uzegzaw.
