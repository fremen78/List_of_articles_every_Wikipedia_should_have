Ek inductor, ek electrical device hae jiske electrical circuit me kaam me laawa jaae hae, kaaheki ii inertia ke rakam kaam kare hae. Jaise inertia koi chalte chij ke disaa badle ke roke hae, inductor, electric current ke badlao ke roke hae. Inductor ke jaada kar ke ek conducting chij, jaise ki copper taar jiske ek core (jon ki khaali hawaa nai to magnetic metal rahe hae) ke around lapeta gais hae, se banaawa jaae hae. Chhota chhota inductor ke integrated circuit me bhi lagaawa jaae hae.
