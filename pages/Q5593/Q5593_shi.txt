Pablu Pikassu (Ktubr 1881 - Bril 1973) iga yan unaẓur Asblyuniy yu-s n unaẓuṛ, ig bab n yan waddur mqqurn, ilul ɣ Malaga, d lliɣ ilkm 15 n usggʷas iga yad yan unaẓuṛ aḥrayfi, s twisi n babas yadlli, ittyawssan uggar s tikʷlutin nns, iswuri daɣ f usram, f ussunɣ. Issnflul yan ustiyl amaynu n tẓuṛi, ism as "Akyubizm" (Cubism), ɣ ustiyl-ad, ar issunuɣ Pablu mddn d imudar d tɣawsiwin yaḍni s talɣiwin n imkkuẓn d imkṛaḍn. 

Ɣ usggʷas n 1904, Rad sul izug s Fransa ɣilli ɣ rad ismd ma iɣaman ɣ tudrt nns, immut lliɣ dars tlla 91 n usggʷas.


== Iẓmmuẓẓal ɣ Tudrt nns ==
1881 - ilul ɣ Malaga assf n 25 n Ktubr 
1896 - Rad ikcm tinml n tẓuṛiwin fulkinin n Baṛcaluna. ￼
1904 - Rad immatti s Bariz ad gis izdɣ s yat tklit.  
1937 - Rad iṛṣm tannid akkʷ dars yufn lli mi ism "Girnika" f wudm n yimiɣ agʷnsan n Ṣṣblyun.   
1961 - Rad iskr zzman nns d yat Tfransist "Jaklin Ṛukk", assf n 2 Mars. 
1979 - Tamttant nns lliɣ as iqqn yan uẓuṛ ɣ turin, mayad ɣ Mugans.
