Nairobi ( /naɪˈroʊbi/ ny-ROH-bee ) un laamorde e kapital ɗem e gelle mawɗe ɗe leydi Kenya . Innde ɗen heeɓaama e ɗe ɓe massiwal gollid. Enkare Nairobi, e de fassire e ndiyam peudam, de nassiji ɗe mayo [Nairobi]  filtiingo gelle fuu.  Galliol (berniiji) ngol engol woodi ɗuɗirka yimɓe ha faadi 4,397,073 eder ɗi 2019 lingal waataagal. Haa ɗen bo jajirka leysdi ɗuuɗiri baano ɗum 9,354,580. Galliol ngol ɗum buri anɗirki kooreeji ɗun ɗe naage.
