Aṛjuntina (s wudm amaddud tga Tagduda n Aṛjuntina) tga yat tmazirt ɣ Marikan Tanẓulant. Tga tmazirt ad tis snat ɣ tmɣuri ɣ Marikan Tanẓulant d tis tamt ɣ umaḍal s timmad nns.
Taṣbnyult ad bahra sawaln mddn, tg tutlayt tamaddudt, maccan ar sawaln mddn s tutlayin yaḍnin. Llant kra n tdrusin sawalnin s  Tṭalyant, Talimant, Tinglizt, Takitcwat d Tawilct akkʷ ɣ Paṭagunya.


== Tasrtit ==
Tga Aṛjuntina yat tagduda tafidiralt. Ar sttin ayt Aṛjuntin yan unssixf afad ad tn inbḍ, d isinaturn, d imaggasn fad ad sawaln s yism nnsn, ar asn skarn islgnn. Anssixf ɣila iga t Xafyir Miliy zɣ Dissumbr n 2023.
