== Wekwin fɔngbemɛ tɔn lɛ ==
1-Wekwin asi gli lɛ:
a, i, u, e, ɛ, ɔ, o.
2-Wekwin asi gaga lɛ:
aa, ii, uu, ee, ɛɛ, ɔɔ, oo.
3-Awɔntinmɛwekwin asi lɛ:
an - aan
in - iin
un - uun
ɛn - ɛɛn
ɔn - ɔɔn
4-Wekwin Asu Yaya lɛ:
b, c, d, ɖ, f , g, h, j, k, l, m, n , p, r, s, t, v, w, x, y , z. 
5-Wekwin Asu Webɔwebɔ lɛ:
kp , gb , ny


== Gbedyɔxwi ==
Gbedyɔxwi atɔɔn wɛ ɖò fɔngbemɛ :
1-Gbewadò sin xwi:    ̀
2-Gbeyiji sin xwi:    ́
3-Gbe wado bo yi ji: '̌
4-Gbe yiji bo wa do:  ̂
5-Gbeɖobamɛ̵̵̵ sin xwi:  ̄
É nɔ zán Gbeɖobamɛ̵̵̵ sín xwì ɔ bleble a.
