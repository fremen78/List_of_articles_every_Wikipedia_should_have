Vasco da Gama (1469- 24 December 1524) ek Portuguese sailor rahaa. Uu pahila European rahaa jon ki India, Cape of Good Hope hoe ke gais rahaa. Uu India tiin dafe gais rahaa.
8 July 1497 ke uu chaar jahaaj (São Gabriel, São Rafael, Berrio, aur ek dhoe waala jahaaj) ke lae ke Lisbon se India ke aapan pahila yatra pe nikla. Kahe ki ii lagbhag Christmas ke time rahaa , da Gama ke crew South Africa ke coast ke Natal (jiske matlab "Christmas" hae Portuguese me), naam rakkhin jon ki abhi talak hae. 
January me uu logan Mozambique pahunchin, jiske coastland Arabs logan control karat rahin. Jab iipawa gais ki uu logan Mussalman nai hae tab uu logan ke huan se khder dewa gais aur uu logan north jaae ke Kenya ke utrin. September 1499 me aapan kuchh saathi ke India me chhorr ke Portugal laut gais. 
1502 me aapan duusra trip pe jab Calicut pahuncha tab uske pataa chalaa ki jon saathi ke uu chhorr ke gais rahaa, ke maar dewa gais rahaa. Ii trip pe uske Indian Ocean jetna Arab nai to Indian jahaaj mila, ke you plunder karis aur Calicut ke bhi aapan capture me kar liis.
