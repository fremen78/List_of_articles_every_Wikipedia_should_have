Niger is seo mæste ea on Westernum Africe. Heo is 2,600 milalang and geond 817,600 mila2 iernað streamas herin.
Nigres wielle is in þæm Guineaheahlandum in suþeasternum Guinean neah Sierraleonan landgemæro. Þurh fif ricums iernþ Niger: Guinea, Mali, Nigerrice, Benin and Nigeria. Hiere muþu oferwriehþ 27,000 mila2.
Niger is Affrice þridde-lengste ea: nan buton Nīl and Congo sind lengre. Hiere mægen 'gafellicu ea' is Benue seo Ea.
