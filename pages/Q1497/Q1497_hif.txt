Mississippi Naddi, United States ke sab se lamba naddi hae aur ii dunia ke bhi sab se barraa naddi hae. 
"Mississippi" naam ke matlab Native American me "big river" hae.
Mississippi ke sotii Minnesota state me hae jon ki Canada ke border me hae. Mississippi, United States ke biich me se bahe hae aur Wisconsin, Iowa, Illinois, Missouri, Kentucky, Tennessee, Arkansas, aur Mississippi state me se jaawe hae.
Mississippi ke muh Louisiana state ke New Orleans city me hae jahan se ii Gulf of Mexico me jaawe hae.
