Ta'n Vooir Chaspagh (Asserbajaanish: Xəzər dənizi, Kassaghish: Каспий теңізі, Pershish: دریای خزر, Rooshish: Каспийское море, Turkmenish: Hazar deňzi) ny corpane ushtey smoo er y theihll rere eaghtyr, as t'ee coontit myr y logh smoo er y theihll ny myr mooir hene. Ta'n logh 371,000 km2 er eaghtyr (gyn Garabogazköl Aylagy) as t'ee 78,200 km3 er thummid. T'ee ny meilley endorheiagh (gyn soo magh faagail magh gaalaghey) as t'ee caglit liorish yn Eeraan da'n jiass, liorish yn Asserbajaan as y Roosh da'n sheear, liorish y Chassaghstaan da'n twoaie as da'n shiar hwoaie, as liorish y Turkmenistaan da'n shiar as da'n shiar yiass.
Chred ny shenn-chummaltee dy row yn Vooir Chaspagh ny keayn, er-yn-oyr dy vel ee ny chorpane ushtey sailjey as er oyr y vooadys eck. Ta sailjys 1.2% eck, un trass sailjys ny faarkaghyn.
