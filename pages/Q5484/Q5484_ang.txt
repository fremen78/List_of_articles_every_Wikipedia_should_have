Caspia (Persisce: دریای خزر) is landbefæstan sǣ on Asie. He is þǣre ƿorulde mǣsta luh. Hiere mǣst dēop is 3,363 fēt.
Caspia is gefēdd by manigum strēamum, ac mǣst be Folga ēa. Ƿæter cymþ ūt fram þǣm sǣ be sunnan hāte.
Fīf rīciu ymbgāþ Caspia: Russland (be norþen and be ƿesten), Casahstan and Turcmenistan (be ēasten), Persealand (be sūðen) and Azerbaijan (be ƿesten).
