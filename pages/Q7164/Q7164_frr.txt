At Weltbeenk as en ferbant faan fiiw iinrachtangen, diar för stabiil finansen üüb a eerd surge skel. Hat sat uun Washington, D.C. (USA) an as 1945 grünjlaanj wurden, am a lunen efter a Ööder Weltkriich weder aptubauen. At Weltbeenk as en iinrachtang faan a UNO an nai mä di Internatsionaal Müntfond (IMF).


== Iinrachtangen ==

IDA (International Development Association)
IFC (International Finance Corporation)
MIGA (Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency)
