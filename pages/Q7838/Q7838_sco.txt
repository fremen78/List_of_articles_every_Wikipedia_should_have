Swahili (or Kiswahili - its ain name), is a Bantu leid that's spak in East Africae. The kintras whaur it's spak maist is Tanzanie, Kenyae an Uganda. Aboot five million fowk speaks it as thair mither-tongue, an fifty million ithers speaks it as a seicont leid.
