Mount Everest, dunia ke sab se uuncha pahaarr hae. Mount Everest, Himalaya pahaarr me hae. Ii 8848 m uuncha hae.
Iske choti Nepal me hae. Ii etna uuncha hae ki hian pe charrhe ke khatir aapan oxygen lae jaae ke parre hae.

 Everest pe pahila dafe May 1953 ke Sherpa Tenzing Norgay aur New Zealand ke Sir Edmund Hillary charrha rahin.
