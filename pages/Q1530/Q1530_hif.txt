Baghdad (Arbii: بغداد‎ translit: Baghdād), Iraq ke capital aur sab se barraa city hae. Ii Tehran ke baad Southwest Asia me duusra sab se barraa city aur Arab world me Cairo ke baad bhi duusra sab se barraa city hae. Iske population 2003 me 5,772,000 rahaa. Ii city Tigris Naddii pe 33°20′N 44°26′E me hae aur ek dafe Muslim world ke centre raha.

Ii city ek samthar jagha, jon ki River Naddi bisect kare hae pe banaa hae. Tigris, Baghdad ke dui hissa me baate hae, aadha east ke hissa ke Risafa aur west ke hissa ke Karkh bola jaawe hae.
Baghdad me duno arid aur subtropical  mausam hae. Agar jo garmi dekha jaae tab ii dunia ke sab se jaada garam jagha me se ek hae. Summer me,  June se August talak, hian ke average temperature 44 °C (111 °F) rahe hae aur iske saathe karraa khaam bhi rahe hae , lekin paani kuchh nai barse hae. 
Winter ke time,  December se February talak, Baghdad ke aximum temperatures 15.5 to 18.5 °C (59.9 to 65.3 °F) average kare hae, aur kabhi kabhi temperatur 70 °F (21 °C) talak chal de hae. Kamti se kamti temperature  January me 3.8 °C (38.8 °F)  me chal de hae.
Hian pe paani, November se March, talak barrse hae jab average me 120 mm (4.72 in) paani kire hae, lekin ii jaada se jaada h 575 mm (22.64 in) talak chal de hae aur kamti se kamti 23 mm (0.91 in).
