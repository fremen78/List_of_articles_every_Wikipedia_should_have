Rahim (Raḥīm رحيم, nakwa anglicized dị ka Raheem) bụ otu n'ime aha Allah na Islam, nke pụtara "Onye Ebere", site na mgbọrọgwụ R-Ḥ-M.  A na-ejikwa ya dị ka aha nwoke nkeonwe, nkenke maka Abdu r-Raḥīm "Ohu nke Onye Ebere".  Mpempe okwu gụnyere Rahim, Raheem, Rohim na Roheem.
