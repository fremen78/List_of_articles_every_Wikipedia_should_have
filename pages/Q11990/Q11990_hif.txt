Reproduction ke matlab hae koi chij ke banaana, nai to fir se banaana, jaise ki bachchaa. Jiu vigyan me dui rakam ke reproduction rahe hae; ii hae sexual aur asexual reproduction. Sexual reproduction me ek species ke dui jiu rahe hae aur bachhchaa ke aadha genes de hae. Asexual reproduction me khaali ek jiu rahe hae, aur ii cell ke divide kar ke reproduce kare hae. Jaada kar ke bacteria asexual reproduction se reproduce kare hae. Koi koi jiu duuni sexual aur asexual reproduction ke kaam me laae hae.
