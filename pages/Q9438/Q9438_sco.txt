Thomas Aquinas, OP (/əˈkwaɪnəs/; 1225 – 7 March 1274), kent as Thomas o Aquin or Aquino forby, wis an Italian Dominican friar an priest an an gye influential filosopher an theologian in the tradetioun o scholasticism, athin whilk he is kent as the "Doctor Angelicus", "Doctor Communis", an "Doctor Universalis".
