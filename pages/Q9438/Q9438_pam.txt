Y Santo Tomas de Aquino [Thomas of Aquin, or Aquino] (c. 1225 – Marsu 7 1274) metung yang pilosopu at theologian king  scholastic a kaugalian, a kebaluan bilang Doctor Angelicus, Doctor Universalis.  Iya ing maimunakeng talasulung ning ausan dang  natural theology, at tatang ning Thomistic pipagaralan a  philosophy, nung nu maluat na iya ing pilosopung gagamitan ning Roman Catholic a Pisamban. Ing gawa bantug ya bilang  Summa Theologiae. Metung kareng atlung pulu't atlung Doctor ning Pisamban, kikilanan de reng Katoliku bilang pekamatenakan a theologian ning Pisamban. Dakal lang pipagaralan a intstitusyun a mipalagyu kaibat na.
