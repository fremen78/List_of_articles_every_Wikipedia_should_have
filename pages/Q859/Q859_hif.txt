Plato ek khaas classical Greek philosopher rahaa. Uu 427 BC se 348 talak ii dunia me rahaa. Uu ka janam Athens me bhawa raha Classical Period ke dauran. Plato uuka pen name raha jaunke uke uke wrestling coach dehe rahe jaunki uke sharirik shakti ke darshawat raha kahe ki uke kandhe bahut chaude rahein. Uu Socrates ke student aur Aristotle ke teacher rahaa.
Plato, uuke teacher Socrates aur uuka Student Aristotle ke sath milke History of Philosophy ke sabse kendriya kirdar hae. Plato ke Neoplatonism se Islamic aur Christian philosophy bhi bahut prabhavit hae.
