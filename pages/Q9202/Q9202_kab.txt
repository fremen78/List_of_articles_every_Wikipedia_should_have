Asebdad n Tlelli (s taglizit : Statue of Liberty neɣ Liberty Enlightening the World, s tafrensist : Statue de la Liberté neɣ la Liberté éclairant le monde), d asebdad agehguh tesɛa 93 n lmitrat deg asawen-is, Fransa tefka asebdad-nni i Marikan deg 1886. Asebdad n Tlelli yezga-d deg usagen n New York,,.
Imuhal n lebni n usebdad bdan deg 1875 deg Fransa akked tenmehla n asreqqat Frédéric-Auguste Bartholdi, asebdad-nni yemmugen s Eugène-Emmanuel Viollet-le-Duc ed Alexandre-Gustave Eiffel.
Deg 1984, asebdad n Tlelli yekcem ɣar wumuɣ n Tigemmi tamaḍlant n UNESCO.
