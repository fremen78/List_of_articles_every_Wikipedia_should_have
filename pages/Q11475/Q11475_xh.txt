I-vacuum yindawo engena-matter. Isandi asinakuhambela ndawo kwi-vacuum, Oumphandle we-space iakuyovacuum igqibeleleyo, kuba kukho iqela lee-particles apho. 


== Ii-vacuums zoshishino ==
I-vacuum ifuneka kwiintlobo ezithile zokwenza imveliso zoshishino. Iimpompo ze-vacuum zisetyenziselwa ukumpompa umoya ziwukhuphela ngaphandle kwi-vacuum. apha ekuhlaleni i-100% akunto enokufane yenzeke, kodwa inkoliso yeempompo ze-vacuum ziyakwazi ukwenza i-vacuum eyi-99.9999%.  le ke ibizwa ngokuba y"ivacuum eyomeleleyo".  Inkoliso yeenjongo zoshishino azifuni vacuum iqinileyo; eyi-0.001% ilunge ngokwaneleyo.  
Ii-vacuums zoshishino zisetyenziswa kuphela :  

kushishino ngokutya
kushishino nge-Electronics 
kwi-Packaging
kwi-Manipulation
kwi-Coating and degasing
Ii-vacuum chambers zikwasetyenziswa kwimisebenzi emininzi yasez-ilaboratories. Eminye yale misebenzi ikwifiziki nakwi-Khemestri nyona ifuna ivacuum eyomeleleyo. 


== Eminye imisebenzi ==
Ivacuum yokucoca isebenzisa amandla amakhulu evacuum ukuze ikwazi ukufunxa uthuli nokungcola, icoce umgangatho wendlu.
