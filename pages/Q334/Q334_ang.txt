Singapore is īegrīce and ceasterrīce on sūþernum Asie. Menn of manigum fylcum onƿuniaþ Singapore and manigfealde sind hiere gereord, forðæm onmang him sprēcað Singaporeƿare in þǣm Nīƿengliscan gereorde.  Þæs þēodes mæsta dæl is of Seringum.
Singapore hæfþ ān īegland and manig īgoþa.  Hēo hæfð ān burg, and burg sƿa sƿa īeg hēt Singapore.  Ðes naman menþ Lēoceaster.
Miclu hȳð is Singapore and micel bysig taðolƿang for eormenum fēohhūscræfte þærof is þes þēod goldƿelig.
