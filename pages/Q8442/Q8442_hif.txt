Prince Otto Eduard Leopold von Bismarck-Schönhausen Duke of Lauenburg (1 April 1815 – 30 July 1898) 19th century Europe ke ek sttaesman rahaa. 1862 se 1890 talak uu Prussia ke Prime Minister rahaa. 1871 me uu chhota chhota state ke ek kar ke German Empire banais rahaa, jiske uu pahila chancellor banaa. 
Bismark ke monarchy se link rahaa aur uu democracy ke supporter nai rahaa.
