Ko te tiakarete he hanga kai reka whakanaoa mai te whakarano o te nehu kakano hua "kakawātara" (cacahuatl i te reo Nawātara, te ingoa pūtaiao: Theobroma cacao), me waiū, me te rehu "paka tō" o "huka". Ko te rākau whakatupu tēnei hua tiritiria i te paparahi Amerika-ki-te-Raki me te paparahi Āwherika. I pūtakeatia te tiakarete mai tētahi inu pārekareka o te tangata Mehika ki motu Mēhiko.
