Euclid(hihlaq saw:Εὐκλείδης — Eukleídēs) dwg aen soqyozgyah Hihlaq ciuhgeq,gyoengqvunz cwngh de dwg boh geijhozyoz.De swnghhoz youq Hellenistic Alexandria youq sauh Ptolemy I dungjci.Elements de sij dwg saw ceiq baenzgoeng youq soqyozsij,caiqlix dwg cujyaoq sawson son geijhozyoz dwkdaengz 19 seiqgeiq.Euclidean geometry dwg geijhozyoz cujyaoq,de youz siujsoq geij diuz goengleix doi caet.
