Franz Kafka (IPA: [ˈfranʦ ˈkafka]) (pilotutu 3 Juli 1883 – yilate 3 Juni 1924) botiya tawu ngota ta mollulade novel wawu silita limbu-limbu'o to delomo bahasaa Jerman to abad 20.
Gaya lo tuladiyo u deli-deli ma longohi pongaruh uda'a to sastra otolopa to wakutu boyito. Ilomataliyo u masahuru da'a towolotaliyo Die Verwandlung (Metamorfosis) wawu novel novel u diipo yilapato tiluladiyo Das Schloß (Kastil).
