Franz Kafka (IPA: [ˈfranʦ ˈkafka]; 1883 nî 7 goe̍h 3 – 1924 nî 6 goe̍h 3) sī 20 sè-kí ēng Tek-gí siá siáu-soat ê tiōng-iàu kak-sek. Kafka tī Praha 1 ê tiong-sán-kai-kip ê Yehudim ka-têng chhut-sì. Toā-hūn ê chok-phín tī koè-sin liáu-āu chiah chhut-pán, chhin-chhiūⁿ Sím-phoàⁿ (Der Prozess) kap Siâⁿ-pó (Das Schloß). Kafka ê hong-keh chin te̍k-sû, tùi se-hng bûn-ha̍k (Western literature) ê éng-hióng kài toā.
