Laozi (lafazi|ˈ|l|aʊ|ˈ|z|ɪ|ə}}; ko |ˈ|l|aʊ|ˈ|t|s|iː; a Amurkanci harshen sin|s= 老子, |làu̯.tsɨ; ma'ana "Tsohon Shugaba"), akan sunan a Lao Tzu (furucci|ˈ|l|aʊ|ˈ|t|s|uː ko |ˈ|l|aʊ|ˈ|d|z|ʌ) da kuma Lao-Tze (|ˈ|l|aʊ|ˈ|d|z|eɪ), ya kasance tsohon Chinese philosopher kuma marubuci. Shine shahararren mawallafin Tao Te Ching, kuma wanda ya Samar da filosafancin Taoism, da ubangiji a addinin Taoism and addinin al'adar sin.
