Va Igor Fyodorovich Stravinsky (Rooshish: Игорь Фёдорович Стравинский, Igor' Fjodorovič Stravinskij) (17 Mean Souree 1882 - 6 Averil 1971) ny chummeyder Rooshagh, as t'eh er credjal dy vel eh nane jeh ny cummeyderyn kiaull y 20-oo eash smoo cummaghtagh. Chammah as yn enney echey er son e chummaghyn, va ard-ghoo er myr cloieder pianney as stiureyder chiaull.
