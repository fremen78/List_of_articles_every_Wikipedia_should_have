Igor Stravinsky (janam: Oranienbaum (abhi Lomonosov), 17 June 1882; maut: New York, 6 April 1971) 20thcentury ke ek important composer rahaa. Uu Russia me baraa bhais rahaa, lekin jab Russian Revolution suruu bhais tab uu Switzerland aut huan se Paris chalaa gais rahaa, aur 1939 me jab World War II suruu bhais tab United States chal diis..
Stravinsky suruu me music aapan teacher Nikolai Rimsky-Korsakov ke rakam likhis. Baad me uu aapan style ke badal ke “neo-classical”.
