Gavman em i samting ol i pipol i makim long mekim gutpela samting long olgeta manmeri long wanpela kantri bilong ol. Gavman i save mekim administresen, na lo, na tu olgeta narapela polisi bilong em.
Em i gat planti ol kainkain stail bilong gavman long wol. Bikpela difrens long olgeta kainkain gavman em i hamas man o meri igat pawa long givim stia long gavnens.

Monarchy: Wanpela man o meri i gat pawa tasol, nem bilong em i "King," o "Emporer."
Oligarchy: Sampela manmeri, liklik lain olsem nobiliti, igat pawa.
Ripablik: Ol manmeri i makim sampela lain long mekim wok gavman olsem "reprisentativ" bilong ol.
Democracy: Olgeta manmeri igat pawa.
Na olgeta gavman igat narapela liklik difrens tu. Ol i gat difren pilosopi bilong em.
