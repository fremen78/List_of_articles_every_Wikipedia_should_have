Fransa (s tefransist: La France), s umarisan Tagduda tafransist (s tefransist: la République française), d awanak ilan timmuɣṛa taɣeṛmant di Turuft n Utaram d temnaḍt tagerlelyant deg yigarawen Ahindyan, Aṭlasyan aha Amelwi, aha di Antarktika d Temrikt n Wenẓul. Tamurt-a tla ibuda ikalanen akked Beljika, Luksemburg, Almanya, Swisṛa, Ṭṭalyan, Spanya, Munaku d Andura di Turuft. Aha tla ibuda ikalanen nniḍen akked Braẓil, Surinam d Timura-Yudaren di Temrikin. Fransa tla timnad tilelyanin deg yigarawen Ahindyan, Aṭlasyan aha Amelwi d yilel Agrakal, jjant-itt ad tellummeẓ s yiwet tɣanast tadamsant tameqqṛant akk deg umaḍal. Aha tekcem di Tamunt turufant (UE). Tutlayt tunṣibt ines d Tafransist.


== Amezruy ==
Awal n "Fransa" yekka-d seg yisem n wedrumen iǧermaniyen: "les francs" s Tefransist.
