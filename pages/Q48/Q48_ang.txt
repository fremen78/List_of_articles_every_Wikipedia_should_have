Asia is þǣre Eorðan grēatest ƿorulddǣl in grīete and in landƿare, licgende mǣstlic in þǣm norþernean and sūðernean healftryndlum. Hit þecþ 8.6% of Eorðan fulne (oþþe 29.9% of hire lande) and mid ymbe 4 ðūsenda ðūsenda ðūsenda onƿunenda, hit behabbaþ 60% þā Eorðan tidgenge landƿare.
Asia is gemænelīc mearcaþ sƿā dǣl of land in Eurasia — mid þǣm ƿesterneum þider ende sƿā Europe — æt tō Suez Gedelfes ēastne, tō Riffenge Beorgum ēastne and Caucasum þǣm Beorgum and hæfþ Caspia and Euxinus þā meras be sūþen. Hit is endede be ēasten æt þǣm Sericus Garsecge, besūþen æt þǣm Indiscan Gārsecge and benorþen be Īshæfe. Fram hire grīete and mise, is Asia māra ān mislic þing ymbhabbende manig landas and lēoda þonne ānne þing.
Asia þisses ƿelan tōscecgaþ micelne ongemang and ƿiþinnan his rīcu, forþæm his grīete and grēatu manigu cynd, drohtaþas, stær and lēodƿearda. For namede GDP, Iapan hæfeþ grēatest ƿelan ƿorulddǣles, mid ōðerum in þære ƿorulde, æfter þǣm Geānedum Rīcum American. In strengþu for geēhtende, hæfeþ Cīna grēatest ƿelan of grīete in Asia and ōðerum grēatest in þære ƿorulde.
