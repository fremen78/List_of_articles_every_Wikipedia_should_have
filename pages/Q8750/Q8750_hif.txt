Michael Faraday (22 September 1791-25 August 1867) ek English chemist aur physicist rahaa. 
Uske time pe uske rakam ke log ke natural philosophers bola jjawat rahaa.  Michael Faraday ii paais ki jab ek taar me bijli bahe hae tab uu ek rakam ke chumbak loha ban jaawe hae. Iske aajkal electromGnetism bola jaawe hae. Uu ii bhi paais ki kon rakam se bijli se chemical ke bhala jaae sake hae. 


== Zindagi ==
Michael Faraday ke janam South London, England me bhais rahaa. Uske palwaar ke lage bahut paisa nai rahaa. Uske pitaji ek lohaar rahaa.
