O Buddhism si ek religio orijinal ai filosofia. Kotse geli ande India le 6th century BC, ai nakhlia but anda Central ai Southeast Asia. Balo sas katar o sicharimos le Gautama Siddhartha (Buddha Shakyamuni), iek manush kai gindilas pa India beshelas mashkar o 560 BC ai 480 BC. Pala e vriama, o Buddhism parhudilo but dieluria, akana si ek religio kai si xulade la, bi iek shib swunto ai bi iek dogma kai si swunto. E amperetsia si ande India, Nepal, China, Japan, Sri Lanka, Korea, Mongolia, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia, Indonesia ai Malaysia. Sas pashte 600 milion pala sa e lumia, o Buddhism si e shtarto religio la lumiake pala Kristo, o Islam ai o hinduismo.
