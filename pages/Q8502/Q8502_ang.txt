Beorg is hēah land þe stent ofer þǣm nēan lande on mearcodre brǣdo. Þæt is, beorg is grēat hyll. Se mǣsta beorg þǣre Eorðan is Eferest Beorg, þe is 8,848 metera hēah and ligþ on þǣm Himālaia Beorgum on Asia. Se hīehsta gecūða beorg on þǣre Sunnlīcan Endebyrdnesse is Olympus Beorg on Þunore þe 21,171 metera hēah is.
