Tsauni ya kasance tudu ne babba na yanayin siffar kasa, wanda kuma yake da tsayi fiye da dukkan sauran bangarorinsa ko kewayensa, tsauni yakan zama mai fadi da kauri da kuma girma a wani sa'in.
Tsauni mafi tsayi a duniya shi ne Tsaunin Everest dake  Himalaya a yankin Asiya, wanda tsayinsa ya kai 

8,850 m (29,035 ft). Tsaunin da ya fi kowane tsauni a sararin samaniya tsayi shi ne Olympus Mons wanda ke Mars da tsayin kimanin 21,171 m (69,459 ft).
