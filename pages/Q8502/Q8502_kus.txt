
== Zuor gbin ==
Zuor anɛ zi'ekanɛ ka teŋ la dʋʋ an kʋŋ-kʋŋ, ka mɔr zulugʋr ti paae teŋin la. Zuor bɛ li kɔ' nɛ kʋnkʋn bɔzʋgɔ, zuori zu'oe laa, kʋnkʋn anɛ bi'ela.  Zuor yiti mɔri wʋʋ 300 metres (980ft) gat teŋ saaŋ la. Ti mɔr zuobibis nɛ zuotitara.


== Zuor tʋʋm ==
1. Zuor sʋ'ʋŋidi gʋ'ʋd waad zi'ibanɛ an waad babir la.
2. zuoya gʋ'ʋd kɔraad bɛɛ mu'an ka ba mɔr li tisid na'asaabugum.
3. zuoya sʋ'ʋŋid ka baŋɔ bɛ ka ti paamid taabu anina.
4. zuoya mɛ mɔr la'asʋma nwɛnnɛ salima, anzurifa nɛ bɛdigɔ.
5. zuoya ni mɛn kan nidib kɛnnɛ gɔsid yɛlsaamis
