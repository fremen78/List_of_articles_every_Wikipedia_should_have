Jainism, India ke ek bahut purana dharam hae, jiske sikchhaa (teaching) me jinda chij ke khatir ahimsa sikhaawa jaae hae. Jain ke philosophy, India ke sab se puraana philosophy hae aur ii dehin aur aatma ke biich ke antar ke jor de (emphasise) hae. Jainism ii batae hae ki brahmaand (universe) amar  hae aur sab jinda chij ke lage aatma hae jiske lage "all-knowing" hoe ke kabliyat hae. Ek aatma, jon ki bhittar ke dusman, jaise ki laalach, abhimaan (pride) ke jiit liis hae ke "jina" (victor) bola jaae hae. Jainism ke  pavtr kitaab (holy book) Pravachansara hae.


== Jain dharam ke biswas ==
Sab jinda chij ke lage aatma hae
Sab aatma me potentially divya (devine) hae, jiske lage janmajaat (devine) gunn hae, anant gyaan (infinite knowledge), dhaarna (perception), shakti aur anand (bliss) ke khaatir.
Brahmaand apne se chale hae aur sab ghatna ke apne se karaawa jaae hae. Sab aatma ke siddha (self consciousness) mile ke kabliyat hae, uske aapan mahinat se.
Koi ek param (supreme) divya nirmaata (creator), maalik (owner), aur banae (creator) aur nast (destroyer) kare waala nai hae.
