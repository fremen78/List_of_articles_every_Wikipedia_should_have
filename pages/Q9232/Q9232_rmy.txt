O Jajinipen, kai s as tradino akhardo Jain Dharma, si iek andal mai phure religiona ai philosophia la lumiake, inker sas les vuni ande mai anglal sar te zhal ande India. Sas tradino te mothol ke kodo pachamos sas phendo pala bish tai shtar manush kai avile pala o foro kai busholas Tirthankaras, ai porme o paluno sas o Mahavira; uni manush kai sas historia phende sas ke wo thodia o baro Jainismo ande kodia vriama.
