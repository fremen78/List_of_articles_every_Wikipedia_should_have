Uhwebo lubandakanya ukudlulisa izingudla nezinkonzo kusuka kumuntu kuya komunye, imvama kunanwa ngemali. Osomnotho babhekisela esimisweni noma uxhaxha oluvuma kube nohwebo ngokuthi indayi.
Uhlobo lwasendulo lohwebo, isinano, lwalwenziwa ngokwenanwa kwezingudla nezinkonzo ngokuqondile ngezinye izingudla nezinkonzo, okungukuthi, ukuhweba ngezinto ngaphandle kwemali. Abahwebi banamuhla ngokuvamile bakhoza ngokusebenzisa insiza kunana, efana nemali. Ngokomphumela, ukuthenga kuyahlukaniswa esenzweni sokudayisa, noma enzuzweni.
