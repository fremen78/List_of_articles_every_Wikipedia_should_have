François-Marie Arouet, bitmingz Voltaire, dwg boux cozgya、lizsijyozgya、cwzyozgya youq seizdaih buqmoengz Fazgoz ndeu. De sij le haujlai gij saw cujcangh gunghminz swyouz (baengzdaengz coenggyau swyouz caueq swyouz gaicawx), ndigah mizmingz raeuh.
Voltaire sij gij saw haujlai, baengzdaengz sawheiq、seigo、sawcuenh、sanjfeanz, caiqlij miz gij cucoz lizsij caeuq gohyoz. De sij saenq lai gvaq 2 fanh bien, saw lai gvaq 2000 bonj. De daemxcengj gaijgwz sevei.
