Christopher Columbus ek trader aur explorer rahaa. Uske janam Genoa, Italy me 1451 me aur maut 20 May, 1506 me bhaes rahaa. Pahile ii socha jaawat rahaa ki Columbus pahila goraa rahaa jon ki America gais rahaa lekin aaj kal ii maana jaawe hae ki Viking log pahile America gain rahaa.
Columbus, Atlantic Ocean paar kare ke kosis karis kahe ki uu janat rahaa ki dunia gola hae aur west ke bagal jjae se bhi India pahuch jaae sake hae. Jab ki Columbus Italian rahaa, uske trip khatir jahaaj aur paisa Spain ke Rani diis rahaa. Jab uu West Indies pahucha tab uu sochis ki uu India pahunch gais hae aur huan ke logan ke Indian naam rakkha diis.
