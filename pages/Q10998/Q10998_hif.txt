Aalu ek rakam ke tarkaari hae. Iske paudha chhota rahe hae aur iske patti barraa rahe hae. Aalu ke uu hissa khaawa jaae jon ki jamiin ke bhittar rahe hae. Aalu me dher starch aur carbohydrate rahe hae. Aalu jaada kar ke ujjar, nai to, piyar aur chilkaa brown nai to piyar rahe hae.
Agar aalu me anjor lage hae tab uu green hoe jaae hae aur jahar ban jaae hae.
Aalu, South America ke Andes pahaarr ke uuncha aur thanda jagha se aais hae. jab Soanish log South America aain tab ii log aalu ke Europe lae gain.
