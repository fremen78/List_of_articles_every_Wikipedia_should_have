Artape (Solanum tuberosum L.) yito pilomulo asali lonto suku Solanaceae u otapuliyo mowali aalolo. Tapu lo artape masatiya ma lowali u'aalo uda'a to lipu lo Eropa, openu to bohuliyo mayi artape botie asaliliyo lonto Amerika huliyaliyo.
Ta hepoleleyangiya lonto Spanyol wawu Portugis ta bungaliyo lodelo wawu lopohala'a artape to Eropa.
Pilomulo artape asaliliyo lonto Amerika huliyaliyo wawu ma hebiyahu limongoliyo anggadu lihu lotaawunu yilalu mayi. Pilomulo botiye batangiyo moluluhi'o, ja mototoheta odelo ayu, pilomulo ngosembo wawu motohila lo iklim motimboolungo.
