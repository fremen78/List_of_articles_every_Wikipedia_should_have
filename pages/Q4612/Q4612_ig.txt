Sylvaine Strike bụ onye na-emetụ ememe na-eme ihe nkiri na South Africa, onye edemede na onye nduzi ihe nkiri nke bi na Cape Town, South Africa.


== Oge ọ malitere ==
A mụrụ Strike na Pretoria.   Ọ gụrụ akwụkwọ na Mahadum nke Cape Town ma gụsịrị akwụkwọ na nzere na ihe nkiri na 1993. Strike gara n'ihu nke oru gụọ akwụkwọ site na 1998 ruo 2000 na Paris na Jacques LeCoq School ebe ọ gụsịrị diplọma afọ abụọ na-elekwasị anya na mime na clown.  


== Ọrụ ==


=== Ihe nkiri ===
Strike bụ onye nduzi nka nke Fortune Cookie Theatre ugbu a, nke ya na ya guzobere na 2000. Sitere mahadum nke n'aka ụmụaka na Fortune Cookie Theatre, Strike duziri ihe ngosi ndị dị ka Molière's The Miser (2012) na Tartuffe (2017)  na Chekhov-adaption, Tobacco and the Harmful Effects Thereof (2016).  N'afọ 2004, Strike so nsị ọbara nke anụ ọhịa otu Fortune Cookie ndị ọzọ mepụta ihe ngosi Black and Blue, nke ọ sokwa mee ihe nkiri. N'afọ 2015, kuki Fortune weghachiri mmepụta ahụ. 
N'èzí Fortune Cookie Theatre, Strike duziri ihe ngosi dịka Miss Dietrich Regrets (2015),  DOP (2019),  na ECLIPSED (2019).  Strike nyekwara aka n'ịgbanwe akụkọ Snow White ka ọ bụrụ Snow White - The Ballet for Joburg Ballet na 2017.  N'afọ 2023, ya na Damon Galgut rụkọrọ ọrụ iji gbanwee àgwà nke chárá na akwụkwọ akụkọ ya, The Promise ka ọ bụrụ ihe nkiri.


=== Ihe nkiri na telivishọn ===
Strike apụtala na ihe nkiri ndị mmadụ mata dị ka District 9 (2009).  Strike enweela a ọrụ na ihe ngosi telivishọn dị ka Those Who Can't, Black Sails, Mad Dogs, na The Hot Zone.


== Onyinye ==
E nyere Strike Rosalie van den Gucht Best New Director na 2004.   N'afọ 2006, e nyere iwu n'ụlọ mkpọrọ pụta Strike onyinye Standard Bank Young Artist Award for Drama. N'afọ 2010, ọ bụ otu n'ime mmadụ iri abụọ na ise a họpụtara maka Rolex Mentor Protégé Arts Initiative . 
Strike meriri Fleur du Cap Best Director Theatre Award maka mmepụta nke mkpọsa nke ọbọdọ ya nke Samuel Beckett's Endgame maka Baxter Theatre Centre, Cape Town. E nyekwara Endgame ihe nrite Best Production na Best Actor.   
Dị ka onye na-eme ihe nkiri, Strike enwetala Naledi Best Actress Award 2004 na 2006 maka ọrụ ya na Black and Blue na Coupé . A họpụtakwala ya na ngalaba onye ndụmọdụ ụfọdụ nke gọọmenti kwesịrị nduzi kacha mma na Naledi, Fleur du Cap na Woordfees Awards 2016 maka ntụziaka ya nke egwuregwu ndị a ma ama Tobacco na Dop. The Miser meriri ya Naledi Best Director Award 2012 na Best Production of a Play 2012.   
N'afọ 2011, e nyere mmepụta Strike nke Butcher Brothers Naledi Award maka Best-Cutting Edge Production.  N'afọ 2014, a matara Strike dị ka onye na-ese ihe na National Arts Festival na South Africa. Dị ka onye na-ese ihe, Strike rụrụ ọrụ na kọmitii alaka na Nwunye bute mmemme nka iji mepụta ihe ngosi nke ọrụ ya. E nyere Strike SAFTA nke afọ 2017 na ngalaba Best Supporting Actress - TV Comedy maka ọrụ ya na ndịda nke ọbọdọ ihe ngosi Those Who Can't .  A họpụtara Strike maka onyinye Kanna maka ntụziaka ya nke DOP na 2017. 
E nyere Strike Chevalier des Arts et des Lettres na 2018 maka enyemaka ya na nka.
