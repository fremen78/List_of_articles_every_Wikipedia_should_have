Marlene Dietrich (1901 nî 12 goe̍h 27 ji̍t – 1992 nî 5 goe̍h 6 ji̍t), chhut-sì-mâi Marie Magdalene Dietrich, sī Tek-kok chhut-sin, koh tī Bí-kok hoat-tián ê ián-goân kap koa-chhiú. Tī 1999 nî hō Bí-kok Tiān-iáⁿ Gián-kiù-īⁿ (AFI) soán chò 100-ê siāng úi-tāi ê lú-sèng ián-goân chi it. 
I tī Tek-kok ê Berlin chhut-sì; tī Hoat-kok Pa-lí koè-sin.
