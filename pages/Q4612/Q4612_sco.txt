Marie Magdalene "Marlene" Dietrich (/mɑːrˈleɪnəˈdiːtrɪk/, German pronunciation: [maɐ̯ˈleːnə ˈdiːtʁɪç]; 27 Dizember 1901 – 6 Mey 1992) wis a German actress an sangster wha held baith German an American ceetizenship. Throuoot her unuisually lang career, which spanned frae the 1910s tae the 1980s, she maintained popularity bi continually reinventin hersel.
