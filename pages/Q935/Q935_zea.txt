Sir Isaac Newton (4 jannewari 1643 – 31 maerte 1727) was 'n Iengelse weêtenschapper en filosoof.
In termen van wiskunde ao Newton stik vee bie'edraege an de wiskunde zoas die m'n vandaege de dag kenne. Isaac Newton is op 20 maerte 1727 (31 maerte 1727, vogges de moderne kalender) in Londen 'esturve nae 'n lang ziekbed.


== Wêrken ==
Vee artîkels in de Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society
1671: Method of Fluxions
1671–1675: Of Natures Obvious Laws & Processes in Vegetation, onuitgegeven werk over alchemie,
1684: De Motu Corporum in Gyrum
1687: Philosophiae Naturalis Principia Mathematica,
1704: Opticks
1707: Arithmetica Universalis
1701-1725: Rapporten van de Master of the Mint
Postuum uut'egeven:

1728: Short Chronicle, The System of the World, Optical Lectures, The Chronology of Ancient Kingdoms, Amended, De mundi systemate.
1733: Observations on Daniel and The Apocalypse of St. John
1754: An Historical Account of Two Notable Corruptions of Scripture
