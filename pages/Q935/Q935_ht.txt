Isaac Newton, ki fèt 25 desanm 1642 epi ki mouri  20 mas 1727 (Angletè), se yon matematisyen, fizisyen ak astwonòm angle. 


== Biyografi ==
Li ekri liv konsène syans. Li te eksplike twa (3) lwa mouvman.


== Zèv li yo ==
1669 : De analysi per aequationes numero terminorum infinitas (1669, pibliye 1711)
1671 : Method of Fluxions
Of Natures Obvious Laws & Processes in Vegetation (pa pibliye, c. 1671–75)
1684 : De motu corporum in gyrum
1687 : Philosophiæ Naturalis Principia Mathematica
1704 : Opticks
1701–1725 : Reports as Master of the Mint
1707 : Arithmetica Universalis
