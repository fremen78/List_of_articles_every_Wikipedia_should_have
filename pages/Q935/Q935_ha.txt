Sir Isaac Newton an haife shi ne a kasar Birtaniya. Ran ashirin da biyar ga Disamba, 1642 ya kuma mutu a ranar ashirin ga Maris 20, 1727. Sir Isaac Newton sananne ne a duk faÉ—in duniya, a kan ilimin kimiyya, Lissafi, Falsafanci, Falaki. Aikin shi da ya shahara a kai shi ne dokar nauyi, da kuma ilimin Lissafi. Shahararre ne saboda littafinsa na Falsafanci irinsu, Naturalis Principia Mathematica (1687).


== Shahara ==
Isaac Newton yana daga cikim mutane mafi shara a duniya wainda ake amfani da ilimun kimiyar wanda shine mafi sharan masu ilimin kimiya.
Ya kawo wasu kaidoji guda uku akan yanayin motsawan jiki kodai na mai rai kona mare rai sune
1- First Law of motion
2- Second law of notion
3- Third la of motion
wainda suke a cikin littattafan physics na sakandare da jamia.
