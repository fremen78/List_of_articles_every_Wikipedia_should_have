Ek computer ek machine hae jon ki achchhaa rakam se paribhasita niyama ka paalan kar ke jaankari banae hae. Aaj kal iske har field me kaam me laawa jae hae, jaise ki education (siksha), military aur aspataal . Is machine ko sabse pahile banaae waala aadmi ke naam hae Charles Babbage. Computer ke saathe ek CPU, Keyboard aur ek Mouse bhi rahe hae. 
Computer ki aaj tak paanch generation hae. 


== Uses ==
Computer ke sab field me kaam me laawa jaae hae. Ii machine me data ke sambhaal ke rakha jaae hae. School, office, aspataal, military aur sab jagah me iske kaam me laawa jaae hae.
