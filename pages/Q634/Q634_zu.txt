uMzulane uyimbhulunga enkulu yasemkhathini ezungeza inkanyezi thizeni, kodwa yona ayisiyo inkanyezi.  UZungezohlelo lweLanga lunemizulane eyisi-8 ngokwemigomo ebekelwe ukuchaza ukuthi yini engabalwa njengoMzulane: imizulan' ehlabathi ( uNomjubane, uNokhwezi, uNomkhubulwana, kanye noNotshezi) kanye nemizulana emhelezo (iNdonsa, uNomaxhama, uNomakhekheba, kanye noNolundwa). Isihlawumbhiselo senkonkowana esithi isigamu senkonkowana (ifu elikhulu ) siyafoceka ukuze kudaleke umbasa ozungezwa isicaba sothuli.  Kulol' uthuli ilapho khona kubumbeka imizulane ngokubuthelana koTho lubuthelwa uMnyondo, isona siphaca esamukelwa ngoSosayensi.
