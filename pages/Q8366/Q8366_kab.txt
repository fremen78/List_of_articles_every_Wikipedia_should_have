Alguritm neɣ Awarizm (asget : iwarzimen), (s taglizit : algorithm, s tafrensist : algorithme), d terbaεt n ilugan imahlanen, asnas-nsen yettaǧǧa ad yefru yiwen ugur akked umḍan n timhelin.
Awarizm yezmer ad yettwasuqqel ɣur uhil i yettwalkamen s uselkim, akked yiwet tutlayt n usihel.


== Isem ==
Isem-is yekkad seg isem alatini n amusnak n taɛrabt; Muḥemmed Ibn Musa al-Xuwarzmi qqaren as Xuwarzmi.
