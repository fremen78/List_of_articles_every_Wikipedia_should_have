Ek Algorithm ek aadesh ke suchi hae jisse logical aur mathematical problem ke solve karaa jaae hae, nai to aur koi kaam ke puura karaa jaae hae. Ek recipe ek algoritm ke achchhaa namuuna hae kaaheki isme jon chij kare ke hae ke step by step karaa jaae hae aur isme input aur ouutput hae.
Algorithm ke sadhaaran basa me likha jaae sake hae lekin computing me  pseudocode aur flow chart ke bhi kaam me laawa jaae hae.
