Medumweder (hāteþ on Nīwum Englisce climate) is þā tācnunga lyfthelmes ofer sumum gearde under langre hwīle. Þā tācnunga lyfthelmes under sceortre hwīle hātaþ weder. 
Geardes medumweder is geworht fram þǣre medumwederendebyrdnesse, þe hafaþ fīf dǣla: se lyfthelm, se wæterhring, se īsgeard, þæs landes andwlita, and sēo līfendebyrdness.
