Climate is the variety o wather that for ordinar prevails ower time in a particular geographic place. Meisures for climate includes temperatur, humidity, atmospheric pressure, wind, rainfaw, an ither meteorlogical data oot-through a year an ower time in general. Climate lippens on latitude, terrain, altitude, aspect in relation tae the sun, the praisance o watter, an mony ither variables that micht haud for the aurie in quaistion.
