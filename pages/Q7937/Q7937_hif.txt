Hawaapaani (Climate) ke matlab hae dunia ke ek jagha ke garmii (temperature), humidity, atmospheric pressure, hawaa (wind), baaris (rainfall) dher din talak. Ek aur dhabg se ii hae tiis saal ke average. Hawaapaani aur mausam ek chij nai hae, kaaheki mausam koi ek din ke condition hae.  


== Kuchh rakam ke hawaapaani ==
Polar climate, jisme lambaa aur ekdam thandaa winter aur chhotaa summer rahe hae, jaise North Pole aur South Pole
Temperate climate, jisme chaar season rahe hae, jaise ki jaada Europe ke des me.
Desert, jahan pe bahut jhuura rahe hae, jaise ki Sahara Desert
Tropical climate, jahaan pe garam rahe hae aur jahaan pe dui season rahe hae: bhiija aur jhuura
Mediterranean climate jahan pe summer me garam aur jhuura aur winter me thanda aur bhiija rahe hae.
