Waata (kimikal faamiula: H2O) a chansparant fluid we faam di wol schriimdem, like, uoshan ah rien, an a di mieja kanstityuent a di fluid a haaganizim.
Waata mek outa two Iydrojen ahn whan Oxijen malicyuul. Dem banded tugeda covaylentli.
Waata cyan hegsist in chree meyja fahm ina naycha: Salid, Likwid ahn Gyas. It a whan likwid at room tempicha but it cyan freez lickl rounah zeeroh digreez - 0 째C (32 째F) ahn tun gyas roun hunjid digreez - 100 째C (212 째F).
