Worta es kemikill kompornd lorngf' t' kemikill formyula H2O. A' worta molekuul contains wun oksijen an tuu hiidrojen etems konnektiid bai kovalent bonds. Worta es liquid a' t' standid ambient temperature an preshur, bat et uulii ko-iksists a' t' Erth lorngf' ets solid staiit, aes, an gaseous staiit (worta vapor ala stiim). 
Worta tuu iksists iin a' liquid crystal staiit neya hydrophilic surfaiisis. Dem krichas niid worta f' servaaew.
