Amazi ni igisucyika gishobora gukoreshwa mu bintu bitandukanye, urugero gukora isuku, guteka, kubaba. 2/3 by'isi bigizwe n'amazi ndetse n'umubiri wacu ni amazi.


== Ibigizi amazi ==
Amazi agaragara etat de l'eau mu buryo 3 :

Mu mwuka
mu bisucyika
imiterere ikomeye
Bitewe n'igihe bigeze amazi ashobora kuba umwuka cyangwa akomeye.


== Amazi ku kwezi ==

Abashakashatsi babonye amazi ku kwezi, ni amazi ari mukazenga gato, kuruhande rugaragaho izuba aho aho yabonetse hangana na m cube imwe.
