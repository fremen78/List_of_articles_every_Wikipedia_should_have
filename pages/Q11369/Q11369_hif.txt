Molecule sabse chhotaa chij hae jon ki hoe sake hae. Agar ek molecule ke chhota hissa me tuurra jjae hae tab uu aur koi molecule ban jaai. Molecule, atom se banaa rahe hae aur iske khaas shape rahe hae. Atoms  jon shape ke molecule banae hae, uske valency ke uppar nirbhar rahe hae. Jaise ki oxygen atomm duusra atom se dui bond form kare hae, carbon atom 4 bond banae hae aur nitrogen atom 3 bond ke banae hae.
Ek molecule me kon rakam ke aur ketnaa atom hae ke Molecular formula se likha jaae sake hae. jaise ki glucose ke molecular formula C6H12O<6 hae. Ii dekhaae hae ki ek molecule glucose me chhe carbon atom, 12 Hydrogen atom aur chhe oxygen atom hae.
