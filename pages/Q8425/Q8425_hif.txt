Kahe ki samaj me sab se jaada garib aur sab se jaada kamjor ke saathe saathe, sab se jaada dhani aur sab se jaada tagrraa ke madat kare ke chaahi, ek samaj citizenship, rights, ethics aur time limits ke bbare me dhyan de hae, jon ki fair rahe ke khatir khaas rakam hae.
