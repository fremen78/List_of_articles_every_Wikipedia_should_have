Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) ek international organization hae jisme dunia ke barraa petroleum export kare waala des hae. 1965 se iske headquarters Vienna, Austria me hae. 


== Members ==
11 des OPEC ke member hae. Ii des ke naam hae: 

Algeria
Indonesia
Iran
Iraq
Kuwait
Libya
Nigeria
Qatar
Saudi Arabia
United Arab Emirates
Venezuela.
