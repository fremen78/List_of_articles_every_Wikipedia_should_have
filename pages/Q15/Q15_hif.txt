Africa, dunia ke duusra sab se barraa continent aur isme dunia ke jamin ke 20% se jaada area hae. Ii continent me 61 des hae, aur dunia ke 14% abaadi hian pe rahe hae. Ii socha jaae hae ki insaan ke suruwaat pahile ii continent me bhais rahaa.
Ii continent

ke north me, Mediterranean Sea ke baad Europe hae;
ke east me Suez Canal aur Red Sea ke baad me Asia hae;
ke east me Indian Ocean ke baad me Australia hae; aur
ke west me Atlantic Ocean ke baad me the Americas hae.


== Jagah ==
Africa ke jaada kar ke log garib hae aur gaon me rahe hae.


=== North Africa (Saharan Africa) ===

North Africa ke jaada hissa jhuura aur garam hae, aur lagbhag sab desert hae. Saharan Africa me khaali kuchh naddi hae. Desert me jamiin ke niche se sooti se bhi paani mile hae. Ii dher dafe oasis ban jaae hae, jahaan pe perr bhi hoe hae. North Africa ke jon des Sahara desert me nai hae, me bhi garam aur jhuura hawa chale hae.

Ek dam north me, mausam, Europe ke Mediterranean des ke rakam thora thanda hae.


=== Sub-Saharan Africa ===
Ii jagha Afica ke uu hissa hae jon ki Sahara Desert ke south me hae lekin jiske South Africa nai hae. Hian ke log karia rahe hae aur Arab descent ke nai hae. Ii jagha me pahaarr, grasslands aur jungle hae.


== Janwar ==
Africa me dher rakam ke janwar hae, jisme [[cheetah], haanthi, sher, saamp, giraffe, aur dher rakam ke machchhri hae.
