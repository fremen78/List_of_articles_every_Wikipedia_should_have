Dunia tinsi pubu-pubu zaa, Afrika n-nyɛ din pahiri ayi zaŋkpa tiŋgbaŋ galisim ni daadam biɛligu.Tiŋgbani ŋɔ puuni, gbaŋsabila n-nyɛ ban zooi. Mediterranean Sea, Isthmus of Suez, Red Sea, Indian Ocean ni Atlan-tic Ocean zaa nyɛla ko' shɛŋa din gili Afrika ŋɔ tiŋgbana. Madagascar mini archipelagos balibunima zaa pahila tiŋgbani ŋɔ puuni. Di yi nyɛla tiŋgbani galisim polo, Algeria n-nyɛ tuuli amaa di yi nyɛla niribi galisim Nigeria n-nyɛ tuuli. Niribi pam sa.
