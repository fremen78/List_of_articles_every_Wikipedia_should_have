Affrica is þære woruldes oðer-storest and oðer mæst eardfæste worulddæl, æfter Asie. Æt ymbe 30.2 þusenda þusenda km² midgetellende togegeoddum ealandum, hit bebrædeþ 6% of Eorðan mid 20.4% of Eorðan lande. Seo worulddæl is behlænede þisse Wendelsæ benorþum, Suez Gedelf and Reada Sæ to norþwestum, se Indisca Garsecg besuþeastum, and Atlantic to westum. Affrica hæfþ manif ricu, and man hie tellaþ to beon 54 swylce mid Madagascar þæm ieglande and missenlica ealandisc mægþa.
Affrica, mæstlice on his eastan, is widlic arede witancræfte gefere swa beon þæt frumcynn land manna and þa greata apas, mid gewitscipe for ærlice hominidum and hira ealdefæderum fram ymbe seofon þusenda þusenda geras agon. Þa ærlicest menn wæron fram Sigelhwaraland, sum 200,000 winter agon.
