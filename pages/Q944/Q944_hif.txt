Quantum mechanics ("QM"), jiske quantum physics, nai to quantum theory bhi bola jaae hae, physics ke ek hissa hae, jisme matter aur energy ke adhyan karaa jaawe hae. Ek "quantum of energy", sab se kamtii energy hae jon ke chale hae aur react kare hae. Pahile ii socha jaawat rahaa ke atom sab se chhotaa particle hae, lekin aaj-kal ii jaana jaae hae ki atoms, proton, electron aur neutron se banaa rahe hae. Quantum mechanics ii batae hae ki ii partile kaise kaam kare hae. Quantum physics ii bhi batae he ki electromagnetic waves kaise kaam kare hae.
