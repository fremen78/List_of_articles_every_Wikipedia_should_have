Transport, nai to transportation, me log ke ek jagha se duusra jagha lae jawa jaae hae.  Transport ke tarika hae jamiin, hawaa, paani, cable, pipeline aur antariksh (space). 
Transport ke infrastructure, vehicles aur opearions me baata jaae sake hae. Infrastructure me hae; rastaa, pakki (railway), airway (flightpath), waterway, nahar (canal) aur pipeline, airport, railway station, busstation. Vehicles me hae; baggi, motar, bike, bus, train, truck, helicopter, hawai jahaaj
