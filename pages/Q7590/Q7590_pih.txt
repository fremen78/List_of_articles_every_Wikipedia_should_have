== Norufuk Ailen transport ==
Norfuk Ailen reliis moestly on tuu forms a' transport:

bai wata, in sheps and boets, an
bai air, in  pliens.
Dem sheps laen a' Kingston ulla Kaaskied piya, en pliens laen a' t' Norfuk Ailen Eyaport.
