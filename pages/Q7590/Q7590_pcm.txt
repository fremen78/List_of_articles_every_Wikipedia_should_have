Transpoteshon na di metod wey pipo, hanima or guds dey waka from one aria to anoda.
We fit sey na im bi di wey one tin waka from point A to point B.
we fit waka by air, land (for railway and for road), water,cable,pipeline an space.)
Waka waka muvment dey helep pipo buy an sell an e dey important for civilisation divelopment.


=== Impact of Transportashon ===
Waka waka dey important for specialisation
Na transpoteshon dey mek prodoshon an konsumshon dey posibu for difren difeen plesis.
