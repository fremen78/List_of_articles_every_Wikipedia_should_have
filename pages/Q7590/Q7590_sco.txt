Thare are mony furms o transport reengin frae traivelin on the grund tae ower watter or throu the air. Transport is a term remittin tae the muivement o, mainly, fowk an guids. Housomeiver, it remitts tae the muivement o information an aw, for ensaumple, the wab. The term comes frae the Laitin trans (athort) an portare (tae cairy).
