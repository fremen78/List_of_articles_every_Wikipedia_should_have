James Augustine Aloysius Joyce (2 February 1882 - 13 January 1941) ek famous Irish writer aur poet rahaa. Uu Dublin, Ireland ke rahaa. Uske ek aankhi me chot lagaa rahaa.
Uske book ek khaas style ke rahaa. Suruu me uu jindagi ke baare me likhis, jiske chota khissa ek book, Dubliners me chapaa. 
Uske aur book rahaa:

Dubliners (1914)
A Portrait of the Artist as a Young Man (1916)
Ulysses (1922)
Finnegans Wake (1939).
