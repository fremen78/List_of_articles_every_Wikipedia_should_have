Ko te tau te roa o te takahuri kotahi a Papa-tū-ā-nuku i te Rā. Tekau mā rua marama te roa o te tau kotahi, arā 365.24219 rā (365 rā, 5 hāora, 48 meneti, me 45 hēkene).
E 365 rā te roa o te tau noa; e 366 rā kē te roa o te tau pekerangi.
Ko tekau tau te roa o te tekau tau .

2001, te tau rua mano mā tahi, he tau noa; ko te rā tuatahi he Rāhina
2002, te tau rua mano mā rua, he tau noa; ko te rā tuatahi he Rātū
2003, te tau rua mano mā toru, he tau noa; ko te rā tuatahi he Rāapa
2004, te tau rua mano mā whā, he tau pekerangi; ko te rā tuatahi he Rāpare
2005, te tau rua mano mā rima, he tau noa; ko te rā tuatahi he Rāhoroi
