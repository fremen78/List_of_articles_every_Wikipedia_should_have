Holt is heard spēnig ƿebb þe maniga plantan habbaþ. Mann brȳcþ hit tō timbrunge and bærnete. Trēoƿnu timbrung hāteþ trēoƿƿeorc. Hit is līfig timber þe stent on geblendtimber clēoftimbrenra spēna þā licgaþ in gesetednesse holttimbres. On lifigendum trēoƿe birþ holt ƿæter and ƿelfōdan tō þǣm lēafum and ōðrum ƿeaxendum ƿebbum, and ēac aƿreðaþ þæt trēoƿ. For þissum cunnon holtplantan ƿeorðan grēata and gīet standan selflīce.
