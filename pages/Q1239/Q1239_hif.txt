Indian Ocean uu ocean hae jiske north me Asia, east me Australia aur Pacific Ocean, south me Southern Ocean, aur west me Africa aur Atlantic Ocean hae. Iske naam India se aais hae jon ki north me hae aur jiske sab beach Indian Ocean se mile hae. Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea, Persian Gulf aurRed Sea sab ii ocean ke bhaag hae.


== Indian Ocean ke border ==

Indian Ocean, southern Asia talak jaawe hae aur Africa aur Australia ke alag kare hae.
International Hydrographic Organization (IHO) ke 3rd edition, Limits of Oceans and Seas ,Indian Ocean (isme se chhota chhota samundar ke nikal dewa gais hae) ke ii rakam se define kare hae:

Uttar ke bagal, Arabian Sea hae, jiske ke dakchhin me  Lakshadweep Sea hae. Bay of Bengal ke dakchhin se,  East Indian Archipelago ke oachchhim se, aur Great Australian Bight ke dakchhin se.

Pachchhim me.  Cape Agulhas sen 20° long. East, aur ii meridian ke follow kar ke Antarctic Continent talak.
East me. South East Cape, Tasmania ke sab se dakchhin jagha se meridian 146°55'E Antarctic Continent talak.
South me. Antarctic Continent.
