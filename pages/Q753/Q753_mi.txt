Ko konukura ("Cu") he pūmotu. Ko te taungota, 29; ko te Takiwā, 4; ko te Rōpū, 11 . 


== Āhuatanga rongo ==
Taumaha ngota: 63.546 amu
Mārō: 3.0
Kiato: 8 920 kg/m3
Kahakawe iahiko: 59.6 106/(m·Ω)


== Ko ngā pūhui ==

Te konukura waihā (me hāora, hauwai): Cu0H
