Niyl Aldin Armstrung (S tanglizt: Neil Alden Armstrong) ilul ɣ 5 Ɣuct 1930, immut ɣ 25 Ɣuct 2012, ig yan umstara n usṭas Amirikani, ittyawssan is iga amddnu izwarn da igan aḍaṛ nns ɣ iggi n wayyur. Ɣ wass n 20 Yulyu n 1969 iggʷiz gis s tmssudut n Wabullu 11 (tamyiwnt n igidr),  lliɣ iga aẓmmuẓẓl nns inna Warmstrung awal nns: "Ayad iga ɣar yan uẓmmuẓẓl imẓẓiyn n yan ufgan, ig yat tukya imqqurn n tiffugna". Asmmigl ad nns iga yat tmatart immaln ar mani yufa ad ilkm wallxf n ufgan.
