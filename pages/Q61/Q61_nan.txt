Washington, D.C. chiàⁿ-miâ Columbia Te̍k-pia̍t-khu (Eng-gí: District of Columbia), hàn-miâ Hôa-sēng-tùn (華盛頓) ia̍h Hôa-hú (華府), sī Bí-kok ê siú-to͘, chham Virginia kap Maryland sio-chiap. Thóng-kè-siōng ê jîn-kháu ū 588,292, to͘-hōe-khu jîn-kháu ū 530 bān.
Washington, D.C. tû-liáu sī Bí-kok liân-pang chèng-hú chham Bí-kok Kok-hōe ê só͘-chāi-tē, mā-sī Sè-kài Gîn-hâng, Kok-chè Hòe-pè Ki-kim chham Bí-chiu Kok-ka Cho͘-chit téng-téng ki-kò͘  ê chóng-pō͘  só͘-chāi-tē.
