Johann Sebastian Bach (21 Werurwe 1685 - 28 Nyakanga 1750) yari umuhimbyi wâ€™Ubudage. Bach afatwa nkumwe mubahimbyi bakomeye mumateka yuburengerazuba. Yagize ingaruka ku bahimbyi nka Mozart, Brahms na Beethoven, kandi azwiho ubwiza n'uburemere bw'umuziki we. Ari mubahimbyi bazwi cyane, kandi umuziki we ukorerwa kwisi yose.
