Si Johann Sebastian Bach (b. Eisenach, 25 March 1685; d.Leipzig, 28 July 1750) so sankanayarian ya composer nen panaon to. Karanggo to ra Mozart san Beethoven ya higante na history na musica. Abilay nen utit na parte na Baroque period. Nanengneng ed musika nen Bach ya akapan-aral sikato na musikon Italian, French san Germany.
