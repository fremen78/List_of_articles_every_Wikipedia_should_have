Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche (1844 nî 10 goe̍h 15 ji̍t – 1900 nî 8 goe̍h 25 ji̍t) sī chi̍t-ê Tek-kok tiat-ha̍k-ka chham bûn-hiàn-ha̍k-ka, i chhòng-chok chin-chē iú-koan chong-kàu, tō-tek, bûn-hòa, kho-ha̍k kap tiat-ha̍k ê bûn-chiong chham chheh-che̍k, tùi āu-lâi ê tiat-ha̍k, iû-kî sī chûn-chāi-chú-gī (existentialism) kap āu-hiān-tāi-chú-gī (postmodernism) ū chin-chē éng-hióng.
