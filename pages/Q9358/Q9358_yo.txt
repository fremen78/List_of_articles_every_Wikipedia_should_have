Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche (October 15, 1844 –  August 25, 1900) (Pípè nì Jẹ́mánì: [ˈfʁiːdʁɪç ˈvɪlhɛlm ˈniːtsʃə]; in English UK: /ˈniːtʃə/, US: /ˈniːtʃi/) je ara Europe ni orundun 19 to je onimo oye ati onimo filologi ijoun. O ko iwe agbeyewo lori esin, iwuwa, asa igbana, imoye ati sayensi, nipa lilo ede Jemani otooto.
