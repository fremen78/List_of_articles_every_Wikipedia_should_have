Anton Pavlovich Chekhov (29 January 1860 - 15 July 1904) ek Russia ke writer rahaa jon ki short stories aur plays likhat rahaa.
1887 me Chekhov ek play Ivanov jiske uu nai like karis lekin aur log bahut like karin rahaa.
1890 me uu ek book, The Island Of Salakhin likhis jon ki uu island me prisoner log ke baare me rahaa.
