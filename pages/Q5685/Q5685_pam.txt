I Anton Pavlovich Chekhov   (29 ya ing Enero 1860 – 15 ya ing Julio 1904) métung yang Rusong talasúlat drama ampóng talasúlat makúyad a salítâ, a tutúring dang métung karéng mapiang díling talasúlat ning kábang panaun. Ápat la réng clasica karéng kayang sinúlat a drama, at mátas la paglaláwé déng talasúlat ampóng critico karéng kayang makúyad a salítâ. Along with Henrik Ibsen and August Strindberg, Chekhov is often referred to as one of the three seminal figures in the birth of early modernism in the theatre. Chekhov was a physician by profession. "Medicine is my lawful wife", he once said, "and literature is my mistress."
