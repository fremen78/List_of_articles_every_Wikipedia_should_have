Ta'n Rhine (Germaanish: Rhein, Frangish: Rhin, Ollanish: Rijn, Walloonish: Rén, Limburgish: Rien, Sursilvanish: Rein, Sutsilvanish as Surmiranish: Ragn, Rumantsch Grischun, Valladerish, as Puterish: Rain, Iddaalish: Reno, Germaanish Alemaanagh: Rhi(n) goaill stiagh Alsaasish/Germaanish Alemaanagh Injil, Ripuarish, Çhengaghyn Franconagh Injil: Rhing, Ladjyn: Rhenus) coontit myr fer jeh ny h-awinyn smoo 'syn Oarpey. Ta moir yn awin soit ayns cantoon Graubünden 'syn Elveeish ayns shiar yiass ny Sleityn Alpagh Elveeishagh as ta'n awin jannoo seose ny joaraghyn eddyr yn Elveeish as Liechtenstein, eddyr yn Elveeish as yn Austeyr, eddyr yn Elveeish as y Ghermaan as eddyr y Rank as y Ghermaan, as, eisht, t'ee roie my hwoaie trooid Çheer ny Rhiney 'sy Ghermaan as 'sy Çheer Injil roish my vel eh lhiggey stiagh ayns Mooir Hostyn.
