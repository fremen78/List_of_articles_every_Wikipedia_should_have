Portugal south-west Europe ke ek des hae. Iske west aur south me Atlantic Ocean aur north aur east me Spain hae. Portugal ek chhotaa des hae.
Atlantic Ocean ke tiin island group, Azores Madeira Islands aur Savage Isles Portugal ke hissa hae.


== Itihaas ==
Portugal, 1139 me ek kingdom banaa. Spain se border 13th century se balaa nai hae. Fishing aur trade ii des ke khaas dhandaa hae.
