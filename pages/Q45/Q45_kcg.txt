Portugat (di alyem aniet Potugat an ngyei ka: [puɾtuˈɣal] ⓘ), ma agwomnati, aRipobulik aniet Potugat ji. (di alyem aniet Potugat: República Portuguesa [ʁɛˈpuβlikɐ puɾtuˈɣezɐ]), abyin ka ni shyie di fam-abyin Aberiyan Peningsula mami Yirop AtakJenshyung. Shi gak abyin ka ni   
ku beang mi Makaroneshiyan susot bibyin akawatyia asakhwot si Azos nbeang Madira. Ku li fam-abyin Jenshyung mami vam-abyin Yirop, atsak abyin Jenshyung nggu hu nbeang Atak gak nggu hu mi Aza agbandang kyai asakhwot Atilantik nbeang mami Aza nbeang Atyin, gak Potugat-Sipen ji, bibya byia mang ajunjung lang gak nang kun swak mamai Mun apyia Yirop. Susot bibyin akawatyia asakhwot nggu hu wa bai mun agbandang fam-abyin afyiang nang ba byie agwomnati nba hu. Mami atsak abyin ka, fam abyin Aliyentejo hwa byie agbandang a swak akavwuo na a si yet afyiang mami ayabyibyin nang ba shyie mami Yirop nang ba byie badadai shi aniet. Lisibbwuon hwa yet akeangtung nbeang amgbii aswak akavwuo na tazwa shi aniet a si bi yet atsak akavwuo di fam abyin Porto nbeang Aligav nang sarai anietcong hyap aghyi ku cat bai nwuan kyang kun nat bwok ani
