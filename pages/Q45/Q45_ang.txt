Portugal biþ land on Europan. His hēafodburg is Olisipo.
Portugulƿare sprecað þone Portugliscan gereorde se is sibb mid þæm Galiciscum spræce on Spēnum.
Ǣr healdon Portugalƿarum brad ƿoruldrīce; Brasil ƿæs hiere landrīce, þær spricþ man giet Portuglisc, ond Angola ond Mosambice on Affrice ond Goa on Indeum mid byrigum on þæm ilcan landes særime, ond eac Macao, se licgþ on Seringum ond Ēasttimor þæs Indiscan iegena.


== Heafodlica byrig ==
Þa heafodlican byrig Portugales sind Lisbon, Oporto, Funchal, Braga, Coimbra, Ponta Delgada, Evora, Faro, Aveiro ond Setubal.
