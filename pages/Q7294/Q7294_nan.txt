Johannes Brahms (1833 nî 5 goe̍h 7 ji̍t - 1897 nî 4 goe̍h 3 ji̍t) sī Tek-kok ê chok-khek-ka, kǹg-khîm-ka kap chí-hui-ka, sī lōng-bān sî-kî chú-iàu ê im-ga̍k-ka chi it. I tī Hamburg chhut-sì, tī tōa-hūn ê im-ga̍k-ka seng-gâi tī Wien khiā-khí. 
Āu-sè kā i chham Johann Sebastian Bach koh-ū Ludwig van Beethoven hō chò "sam B".
