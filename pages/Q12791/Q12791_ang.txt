== Stǣr ==
Menn macodon and brucon stīelecg ƿǣpnu fram þǣre Brānse Ieldu and siþþan. Þæt sƿeord cymþ of þǣm cnīfe þǣr langa ecga mihtlica ƿurdon, fram þǣm æfterran 3. gēarþūsende BCE in þǣm middlan ēaste, ǣrst of copere, þonne of tinbrānse. Man fand þā ieldestan sƿeordlīcan ƿǣpnu in Arslantepe on Turcena lande.
