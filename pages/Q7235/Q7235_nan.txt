Sophocles (kó͘ Hi-lia̍p-gí: Σοφοκλής; kong-goân-chêng 495 nî – kong-goân-chêng 406 nî) sī kó͘ Hi-lia̍p 3 ê pi-chhut ke̍k-chok-ka lāi-té ê tē 2 ê, tī i chìn-chêng sī Aeschylus, tī i boé-āu sī Euripides. Kin-kì 10 sè-kí ê pak-kho-choân-su Suda, Sophocles siōng-bô siá 123 chhut hì, m̄-koh kan-taⁿ 7 phō oân-chèng thoân--lo̍h-lâi. Oedipus (Οἰδίπους) sī lāi-tiong siāng ū-miâ ê 1 phō.
