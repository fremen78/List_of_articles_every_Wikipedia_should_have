Va Alexander Sergeyevich Pushkin (Rooshish: Алекса́ндр Серге́евич Пу́шкин, focklit [ɐlʲɪˈksandr sʲɪˈrgʲevʲɪtɕ ˈpuʂkʲɪn]) (6 Mean Souree 1799 – 10 Toshiaght Arree 1837) n'ughtar Romansagh Rooshagh, as t'eh er credjal dy vel eh ny vard Rooshagh share as ny chiaddeyder er lettyraght Rooshagh noa-emshir.
Ruggit ayns Moscow, va'n chied daan echey soilshit as eh agh queig bleeaney jeig d'eash. Phoos eh Natalya Goncharova ayns 1831, agh 'sy vlein 1837 v'eh er credjal echey dy vel ee mooar lesh Georges d'Anthès. Hug Pushkin y lane fo d'Anthès as, rere caggey fer rish fer, hooar Pushkin baase.
