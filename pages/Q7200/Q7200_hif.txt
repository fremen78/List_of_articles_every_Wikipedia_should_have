Alexander Sergeyevich Pushkin (Janam: 6 June, 1799, Moscow; maut: 10 February 1837, St Petersburg) ek Russian  poet , novelist, dramatist aur short stories ke writer   rahaa. Log soche hae ki Pushkin Russia ke sab se barraa poet rahaa. Uu Russian literature promote karisrahaa. Pushkin uu rakam se likhat rahaa jon ki uu time talak koi Russian nai likhe rahaa. Uu Russian me uu rakam likhat rahaa jaise ki baat kaeaa jaawe hae.
