Feminism ek social aur political movement hae. Feminism me admi aur aurat ke janta kon rakam se dekhe hae ke badle ke kosis karaa jaawe hae. Feminism ii bole hae ki pahile aurat logan ke achchhaa se nai dekha jaawat rahaa aur ispe rok lagae ke chaahi. Jon koi feminism me biswas rakhe hae ke feminist bola jawe hae.
