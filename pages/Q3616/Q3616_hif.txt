Tehran (nai to Teheran) (تهران Farsii), Iran ke capital city  hae. Ii Tehran Province ke bhi capital hae. Tehran, Tochal mountain range ke kinare hae aur hian pe dher highway hae jon ki Asia ke aur des se bahut achchhaa hae.  Ii city  The city, Alborz slopes pe resorts, barraa museums, art centers, aur palace complexes khatir jaana jaawe hae.
Tehran, Middle East ke sab se barraa city hae aur dunia ke 16th sab se jaada abaadi waala city hae. Iske abaadi  8,429,807 hae. Tehran, Iran ke urban center me se sab se barraa hae. Iran ke jaada industry ke headquartes Tehran me hae, jisme manufacturing of automobiles, electrical equipment, military weaponry, textiles, sugar, cement, aur chemical products hae. Tehran ek khaas jagha hae jahan pe carpets aur furniture beja jaawe hae. City ke south me ek oil refinery bhi hae.
20th century, me Iran ke sab jagha se log aae ke Tehran me rahe lagin. Abi ii city me cher religious minorities hae, aur hian pe dher itihasik  mosque, churches, synagogues aur Zoroastrian fire temples dekha jaae sake hae. Abhi ke Tehran ek modern city hae jisme tall structures, jaise ki Azadi Tower aur Milad Tower Tehran ke ek chinh hae. Agar jo dunia bhar ke city ke ddelha jaae tab dunia ke sab se sasta capital city hae aur duursa sab se sata city (Karachi ke baad) hae. Population se ii dunia ke 14th sab se barraa city hae, 56th agar jo GDP dekha jaae aur  29th agar jo  metropolitan area ke abaadi dekha jaae tab..
