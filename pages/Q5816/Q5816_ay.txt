Mao Zedong (chino simplificado: 毛泽东, chino tradicional: 毛澤東, pinyin: Máo Zédōng, Wade-Giles: Mao Tse-Tung)(* Hunan, 1893 mara 26 uru Jallu qallta phaxsin yuritayna – Pekin, 1976 mara 9 uru Llamp'u phaxsin yuriwi),  China jacha marka anakithiri. 
Markani Republika China Prisidinti.
Awki: Mao Yichang; Tayka: Wen Qimei.Warminaka:   Luo Yixiu (1907-1910) / Yang Kaihui (1920–1930) / He Zizhen (1930–1937) / Jiang Qing (1939–1976).Yuqanaka: Mao Anying & Mao Anqing.
