Socrates (470 BC - 399 BC) ek khaas Greek philosopher aur teacher rahaa. Uu Greek city, Athens me rahat rahaa. Socrates ke ii kaaran se yaad karaa jaawe hae kahe ki uu ii pataa lagae ke kosis karis ki admii logan kaise soche hae. 
Socrates ke kabi kabhi "father of Western philosophy" bola jaawe hae.
