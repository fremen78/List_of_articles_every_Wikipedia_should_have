Intaba-mlilo ukugqabhuka kuqweqwe lwento enkulu yeplanethi, njengoMhlaba, evumela udaka olushushu, uthuthu lwentaba-mlilo, kkunye neegesi eziphuma kwigumbi le-magma elingaphantsi komphezulu.


== I-Etymology ==
Igama elithi volcano lithatyathwe kwigama leVulcano, isiqithi sentaba-mlilo kwiAeolian Islands yaseItali igama layo lisuka kwiVulcan, uthixo womlilo kwiintsomi zamaRoma. Uphononongo lweentaba-mlilo lubizwa ngokuba yi-volcanology, ngamanye amaxesha i-spelled vulcanology.
