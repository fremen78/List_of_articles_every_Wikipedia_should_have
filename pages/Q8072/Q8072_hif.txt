Jwala mukhi ek rakam ke pahaarr hae, jahaan pe  lava (garam taglaa patthar) jamiin ke niche se nikle hae. 
Volcano ke top pe me jaada kar ke  volcanic crater rahe hae. Jab jwala mukhi  active rahe hae tab usme se galaaawa gais chij nikle hae. Isme bhaap, sulphur ke bhaap, raakhi aur patthar ke tukrra rahe hae. 
Jwala mukhi uu time ufnae hae jab ki nichche ke pressure uske top ke blow kar de hae. Jwala mukhi keEarth, like the Olympus Mons aur Mars me paawa jaae hae..
