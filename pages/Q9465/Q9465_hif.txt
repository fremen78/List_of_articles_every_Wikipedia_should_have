Ethics, philosophy ke ek hissa hae, jisme achchhai aur kharaabi ke baare me baat karaa jaawe hae. Iske tiin hissa me baata jaae hae: "normative ethics, applied ethics aur methematics". 
Ethics ii rakam ke sawaal ke uttar de ke kosis kare hae:

Kon kaarya (action) achchhaa hae aur kon karya kharaab hae
Ham antar ke kaise jaane saktaa hae
Kaa Achchhaai aur kharaabi ekke chij hae
Ham kaise karraa nirnay (decision) le saktaa hae, jon ki duusre ke madat, nai to dard (hurt) pahunchaae sake hae
Hamaar karya duusre ke ke kaise prsbhawit (affect) kare hae.
