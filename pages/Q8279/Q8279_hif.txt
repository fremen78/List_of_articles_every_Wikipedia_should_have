Shahanama, jiske matlab Book of Kings hae, ek lamba epic kavita hai jiske 977 aur 1010 CE ke biich me, Persia me , likha gais rahaa. Ii duniya ke sab se lambaa epic poems me se ek hae. Iske Ferdowsi likhis rahaa. Ii Persia ke itihaas creation se Muslim conquest talak batae hae. Ii poem ke Azerbaijan, Afghanistan, Tajikistan me bhi parrha jaae hae.
