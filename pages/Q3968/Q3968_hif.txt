Algebra, mathematics (math) ke ek hissa hae jisme equations aur inequalities ke solve karaa jaawe hae. Algebra me, uu chij jiske value abhi nai jaana gais hae ke dekhaae ke khaatir "variables" ke kaam me laawa jaae hae. Ek saral equation ke namuuna hae: x + 3 = 5. Bhautik vigyan (Physics), engineering aur computer programming me algebra ke sab time kaam me laawa jaae hae.
