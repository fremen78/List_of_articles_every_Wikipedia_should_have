Lamanyu nɣ Aliminyum (s tfransist : aluminium) iga afrdis akruran nɣ afrdis akimyan ilan uṭṭun ablkam nɣ uṭṭun afnuzan 13 d usgzl Al. Illa g trabbut tiss 13 n tflwit n usuds azdawan n ifrdisn ikimyan, ili g tawja n imɣuzn imẓlaḍ. 


== Assaɣ ==
S tclḥit takuyasst ar ttinin iclḥin "Lamanyu", macc g tugt n iḍṛiṣn ar as ttinin imaratn "Aliminyum"
