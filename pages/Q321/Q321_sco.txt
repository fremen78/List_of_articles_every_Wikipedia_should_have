The Vatlant Streit (forbye cawed Watling Strete or The Milky Wey), whilies referred tae juist as "the Galaxie", is a baured spiral galaxy whilk furms pairt o the Local Group. Altho the Vatlant Streit is but ane o millions o galaxies in the universe, the Galaxie haes speicial significance tae humanity  acause hit is oor hame in the solar seestem, whilk is locatit naur the Orion Airm.
