Baltic Sea northern Europe ke Scandinavia, Finland, Russia, Baltic countries, Poland, aur Germany ke biich me ek samundar hae. Ii Atlantic Ocean ke ek samundar hae.
Bagal ke des me se dher naddi aae ke Baltic Sea me nikle hae. Baltic Sea, Atlantic Ocean se Danish straits se jurre hae. Ii samundar me nimak bahut kamti hae aur ii kaaran se winter me iske northern part jam jaawe hae aur ii ice ke uppar se motar Sweden aur Finland ke biich me jaawe sake hae.
