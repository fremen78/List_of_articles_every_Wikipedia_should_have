International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement ek movement hae jon ki insaan ke jindagi aur tabiyat ke bachae hae. 
Jab ki ii ek barraa sanstha hae, ii privately owned hae aur Red Cross pe koi sarkar pe control nai hae.
Ii movement me tiin chij hae:

International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) jiske 1863 me Geneva, Switzerland me suruu karaa gais rahaa. Iske kaam, international law ke niche, larrai ke victim ke jindagi aur dignity ke bachae hae.
International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) ke 1919 me suruu karaa gais rahaa. Ii sanstha ii kosis kare ke try kare hae ki Red Cross aur Red Crescent Societies ek saathe kaam kare ke kosis kare. The International Federation Secretariat bhi Geneva, Switzerland me based hae.
Dunia ke lagbhag sab des me Red Cross aur Red Crescent society hae. ICRC, 186 National Red Cross aur Red Crescent Societies ke recognise kare hae.
