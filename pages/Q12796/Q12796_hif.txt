Banduuk ek rakam ke hathyaar hae jon ki ek explosive charge ke kaam me laae hae aur jiske ek jan sahaj me uthae sake hae.
Pahila banduuk ke China me banaawa gais rahaa, jab baans (bamboo) tube jisme gunpowder aur pellet rahaa ke moto (spear) me jorra gais rahaa.
13th century me baans ke jagha metal ke barrel ke kaam me laae jaae lagaa. Ii technology Eurasia bhar me 13th century me fael gais.
