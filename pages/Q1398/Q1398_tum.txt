Publius Vergilius Maro (malinga na mitheto yake 15 Okutobala 70 ‡ 21 Seputembala 19 BC) wakaŵa mulembi wa ku Roma wa nyengo ya Augusto. Wakalemba sumu zitatu zakumanyikwa comene mu mabuku gha Cilatini: Eclogues (panji Bucolics), Georgics, na Aeneid. Mu nyengo yakale ŵanthu ŵakatenge ndiyo wakalemba mapowemu ghachoko waka agho ghali mu Appendix Vergiliana, kweni ŵasayansi mazuŵa ghano ŵakukayika usange ndiyo wakalemba mapowemu agha.
