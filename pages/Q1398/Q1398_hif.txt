Publius Vergilius Maro, jiske English bhasa waalas log Virgil nai to  Vergil ke naam se jaane hae, (15 October, 70 BC - 21 September, 19 BC) ek kavi rahaa, jon ki Latin me likhat rahaa. Virgil' ke sab se jaana maana kavita,  Aeneid hae. 
Virgil ke janam Andes, Mantua, Cisalpine Gaul me bhais rahaa.  Vidwaan log ii batae hae ki uske chaahe Etruscan, Umbrian nai to Celtic descent rahaa, uske ilaka ke ethnic marker ke study kare ke baad.
