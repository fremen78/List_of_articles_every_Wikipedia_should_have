Beijing (‘Ōlelo Pākē: 北京 — naīawaike bīnawīan: «bīunūaka») ua mea kapikala wāʻanoākama ikaʻanwaika lakuamaʻawa mai uaʻmauakauama ka wahiawaēwa mīacōaikama ua sūamawa mai Kina. Aia ka ōumawōika meahuaū ua kimuāwala meiwākua hūbaōunaʻmō eikaulācaka lapuama āwakiunalia ua ʻOlumepika Kauwela ma 2008 mai Beijing.
