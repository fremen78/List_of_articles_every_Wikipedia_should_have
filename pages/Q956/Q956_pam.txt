Ing Beijing (Chinese: 北京; pinyin: Běijīng; Wade-Giles: Peiching o Pei-ching; IPA: [peɪ˨˩ tɕɪŋ˥˥]; Chinese Postal Map Romanization: Peking; kabaldugan na "Pangulung Tungku"; pronunciation ), metung yang metropolis king pangulung Tsina, at iti ing tungku ning People's Republic of China (PRC).  Dati yang kebaluan keng amanung English bilang Peking (English pronunciation ).
