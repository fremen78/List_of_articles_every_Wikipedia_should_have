Ko te awa Naera (reo Ārapia: النيل, an-Nīl, [an'niːl], Bohairic Coptic: ⲫⲓⲁⲣⲟ [pʰjaˈro], reo Luganda: Kiira [ki:ra], reo Nobiin: Áman Dawū) tētahi awa hirahira e pūheke ana ki te raki i te raki-mā-rawhiti o Āwherika. E pūheke ana ki te Moana Rōwhenua. Ko te Naera te roa nui awa i Āwherika me i muri i whakaaro te awa roa nui i te ao, engari i nāianei e whakaaro koutou te awa Amahona te awa roa nui i te ao. Te pūheke iti o wai ia tau i te naera. E 6,650 km (4,130 mi) te roa. He 11 whenua i tōna riu manga: Te Whenua o Kōngo, Tānahia, Puruniti, Rāwana, Ukānga, Kēnia, Etiopia, Eritēria, Hūtāne-ki-te-tonga, Hūtāne me Īhipa.
