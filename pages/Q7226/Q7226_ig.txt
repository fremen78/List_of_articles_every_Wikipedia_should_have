Margie Sudre (Amụrụ n'abalị iri na asaa n'ọnwa Ọktoba n'afọ 1943) bụ onye ndọrọ ndọrọ ọchịchị Reunionese a mụrụ na Vietnamese na onye otu European Parliament maka "Outre-mer" nke France.
Tupu a họpụta ya na European Parliament (UMP-"les républicains"), o nwere ọtụtụ ọrụ ndọrọ ndọrọ ọchịchị, ma nke obodo ma nke mba: onye isi oche nke Réunion Island regional Council (1993 ruo afọ 1998, onye otu kemgbe afọ 1998), odeakwụkwọ nke steeti maka French-Speaking World (1995 ruo afọ 1997). Ọ rụrụ ọrụ iji mee ka French bụrụ asụsụ nke abụọ nke Atlanta Olympic Games.
Ọ bụ onye otu Union for a Popular Movement, nke bụ akụkụ nke European People's Party, ma nọrọ na Kọmitii European Parliament's Committee on Fisheries na Kọmiti ya na Regional Development.

Ọkachamara na anesthesia na resuscitation (1976)
Dọkịta nke ọgwụ (1977)

Onye na-akụnwụ ahụ (gụnyere mbilite n'ọnwụ) na Joan of Arc Clinic (La Réunion) (1977 Sri Lanka)
Onye isi oche nke Réunion Island Regional Council (1993[[1998)
Odeakwụkwọ nke steeti maka ụwa na-asụ French (1995-1997)
Onye otu European Parliament (ahọrọ 1999, 2004). Ọ bụghị onye na-azọ ọkwa na ntuli aka nke afọ 2009.
Knight nke Legion of Honour (1999)
Onye isi nke Legion of Honour (2010)
