Va George Frideric Handel (23 Toshiaght Arree 1685 – 14 Averil 1759) ny chummeyder va ruggit ayns y Ghermaan. Ta ard-ghoo er er son ny kiaulldramaghyn, oraatoiryn, as conshertoyn mooarey echey. Va Handel ruggit myr Georg Friedrich Händel (IPA: [ˈhɛndəl]) ayns Halle ny Germaan, agh cheau eh yn chooid smoo jeh'n seihll echey ayns Sostyn, as haink er dy ve currit fo reill y chrooin Goaldagh er 22 Jerrey Geuree 1727. T'ad ny h-ard-obbraghyn echey na Messiah, Water Music, as Music for the Royal Fireworks.
