Gawq gvangjyi yienghdoiqlun yawhyenz, youq ndawhoengq samgaiq miz meh cizlieng lai lai dih denhdij, heuhguh Conghlaep（Sawndip：𰇍𮄻 、 Sawgun：黑洞）, lij heuh Conghndaem、hwzdung. Cizlieng cuk caemh dih hwngzsing haedgawqbienq yienzliuh hauq liux couh bienqbaenz Conghlaep. De dih yinxligciengj cuk haenq, hawj rongh cungj mbouj ndaej biq. Youq Conghlaep seiqhenz dwg aen gaiqyawj, mbouj ndaej damqcaz.
