Agbbay asggan iga yat tmnaḍt n unsakud lli ɣ icqqa uẓẓug, d ur illi ma iẓḍarn, mqqar d tifawt nɣ tiḍḍangiwin yaḍn n tliktṛumɣnaṭiṭ, ad irwl i umnid n tmsart. Tiẓṛi n timmasɣt tamatayt tsmnni mas d yan ubddur ittyamaẓn mliḥ iẓḍar ad isluxcu ansakud issilɣ agbbay asggan. Ittusmma uwttu n war iwrri s Amnid n tmsart.
