Peter the Great, Peter I or Pyotr Alexeyevich ([Пётр Алексе́евич, Пётр I, Pyotr I, or Пётр Вели́кий, Pyotr Velikiy] ) (9 Juin [A.S. 30 Mey] 1672 – 8 February [A.S. 28 January] 1725) ruled the Tsardom o Roushie an later the Roushie Empire frae 7 Mey [A.S. 27 Aprile] 1682 till his daith, jyntly rulin afore 1696 wi his hauf-brither.
