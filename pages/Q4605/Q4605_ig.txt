O Xangô de Baker Street ( aha bekee: Samba maka Sherlock) bụ ihe nkiri Brazil-Portuguese nke 2001 nke Miguel Faria Jr. duziri, dabere na akwụkwọ akụkọ nke otu aha Jô Soares dere. Ebu ụzọ mara ọkwa ihe nkiri a na 1996, mana ịse vidiyo malitere na Septemba 1998 na Porto, Portugal, [1] na-adịgide ruo 1999.. 


== Atụmatụ ==
Rio de Janeiro, 1886. Onye na-eme ihe nkiri Sarah Bernhardt na-eme ihe nkiri na Ụlọ ihe nkiri obodo, na-adọrọ mmasị ndị na-ege ntị n'ógbè ahụ na omenala French. Obodo ahụ dị n'ụkwụ ya, ọbụna Emperor Pedro II na-abịa ịkwanyere ya ùgwù. Ọ na-akọrọ ya ihe nzuzo: mwepu nke violin Stradivarius dị oké ọnụ ahịa nke o gosipụtara n'ihu nwanyị di ya nwụrụ mara mma Baroness Maria Luiza. Onye na-eme ihe nkiri na-atụ aro ka eze were were enyi ya, onye ama ama Britain bụ Sherlock Holmes, dozie okwu ahụ. Mgbe nke ahụ gasịrị, ogbugbu obi ọjọọ juru obodo ahụ anya, wee hapụ onye isi ndị uwe ojii Mello Pimenta: egburu onye akwụna ma bibie ya, bepụ ntị ya ma tinye eriri violin n'ụzọ dabara adaba n'ahụ onye ahụ. Mgbe e mesịrị, n'okpuru okpomọkụ nke anwụ okpomọkụ, ndụ Holmes na Dọkịta Watson na-agbanwe ruo mgbe ebighị ebi, ka ha na-ahụ onwe ha n'olu n'olu na omenala omenala nke na-egosipụta ụkpụrụ niile nke Brazil.
