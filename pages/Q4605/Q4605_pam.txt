I Sarah Bernhardt (mibait king Paris, France aniang Octubri 23, 1844; mete Marzu 26, 1923) metung yang artista king teatro, a meyaus a "the most famous actress in the history of the world" (ing pekataniag a artistang babai king kasalesayan ning yatu). Sinikat ya king entabladu ning Europa aniang dekadang 1870. E migluat, lalage ne king Europa ampo king America. Mekilala ya antimong seriosung artista, at ikua ne ing palayong "The Divine Sarah" (ing mala-diosang Sarah).
