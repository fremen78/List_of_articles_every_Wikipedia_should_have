She marrinagh, ronseyder, diploamagh, ard-marragh lhuingys, as muilteen cooyrtey Sheenagh car eash leah reeraghtys Ming va Zheng He (Sheenish Aashagh: 郑和; Sheenish Hradishoonagh: 鄭和; pinyin: Zhènghé; Wade–Giles: Chêng-ho; 1371–1433 ny 1435). V'eh ruggit lesh lught thie Islamagh fo'n ennym Ma He as ny s'anmey ren eh doltey yn sliennoo Zheng va bronnit er liorish Impir Yongle. Fo barrantys Impir Yongle as Impir Xuande, hug eh currym er shiaght marrinyssyn tashtee dys yn Aishey Hiar Yiass, yn Aishey Yiass, yn Aishey Heear, as yn Affrick Hiar eddyr 1405 as 1433. Rere ny shenn-skeealyn, dymmyrk ny lhongyn mooarey keeadyn dy hiaulteyryn er kiare loutyn as va'd daa cheayrt ny s'liurey na lhong fuygh elley v'ayn rieau.
Myr reih Impir Yongle, chur Zheng cooney da ayns toyrt-mow Impir Jianwen, hie eh dys baare y cheimoilaght impiroil as haink er dy ve ny anneyder er y phreeu-valley jiass Nanjing.
