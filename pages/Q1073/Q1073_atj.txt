
== Manawan ==
Enko anihe wiaw wirtip ka icinikatek ote ici actew atamik octikwanik. Wiaw ka micta kictapatak misiwe e icinakositc awik e matisitc. Enko anihe ka micta kictapatak ekota meka e otciparitc kekocic ka iti matce aiatcinaniwok kekotc e matce pamitoneritcikatek. Ekota tekaci e pitakeparik kekwan ka kictapatak e ko pamitoneritaman kirika kitci petaman kirika kitci motein ekota nta wetciparik anihe kaskina awik ka matisitci tatam aniheriw iriniw peikon kaie awesis. Wir tipirowe pe otcipirin nehe itewin.
