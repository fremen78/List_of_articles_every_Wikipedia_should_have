She greie ee jeshaght. Dy cadjin, ta'n obbyr çheet er nhee co-oltagh ta jannoo obbyr (ny cooney sleih obbyr y yannoo) liorish ymmyd bree. Cha nel co-oltyn so-ghleashagh ec dagh fer; myr sampleyr, cha nel gleashaght ry-akin jeh co-earrooderyn.


== Sorçhyn dy yeshaght ==
Shoh kuse dy 'ir chadjin:

Ta jeshaghtyn bunneydagh caghlaa troa ny mooadys forse ennagh gyn ceau bree erbee. T'adsyn goaill stiagh y plaaney liargee, queeyl as essyl, maidjey, ullag, jeenys as cassey.
Ta ymmodee jeshaghtyn jannoo forse jeh bree, myr sampleyr, jeshaght lostee yn-veanagh ny gall-ghreie.
Ta turbeen goaill bree ass stroo fliughid ennagh as jannoo obbyr lesh, myr sampleyr, mwyllin geayee
Lesh gleashaghey trooid fliughid ennagh, ta edjag jannoo forse er jeeraghan y ghleashaght, as shen cummaght ardjee. T'adsyn goaill stiagh shiaull, skian, as stiur
