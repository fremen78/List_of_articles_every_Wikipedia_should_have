Karl Marx, lij heuh Gajwj Majgwzswh (Vahgun: 馬克思, 1818 nienz - 1883 nienz) dwg boux cietyozgyah、sevei ginghciyozgyah、ligsijyozgyah、cwngci lijlungyah caeuq gwzminggyah Daekgoz ndeu, de leabbaenz le gungcanjcujyi seizneix. Marx youq Senhyenz Gungcanjdangj  (1848 nienz okbanj) cungh daez ok le fuengfap de: "daengxmiz ligsij biengz cungj dwg doucwngh gaihgiz gij ligsij."
Marx nyinhnaeuz swhbwnjcujyi beijbaenz doenghbaez sevei ginghci heahdungj ityiengq, mbouj gojyij biet byaijcoh dairaeg. De saenq seveicujyi gyaek dap swhbwnjcujyi.
