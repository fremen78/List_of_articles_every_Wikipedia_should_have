She magher bea-oaylleeagh eh lus-oaylleeaght, bentyn rish lossreeyn.  Ta'n magher goaill stiagh studeyrys er fungyssyn as algey chammah.  Ta cooishyn lus-oaylleeaght goaill stiagh troggal, aase killagyn, geddyn sliught, as soe lossreeyn; gorlaghyn lossreeyn; aafilley lossreeyn; as troyn kemmigagh lossreeyn.  Va bun y vagher obbraghyn shenn sleih dy chur enney er lossreeyn yn-ee, lheeys as nieunagh.
