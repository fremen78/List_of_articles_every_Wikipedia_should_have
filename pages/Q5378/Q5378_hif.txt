Rugby football ek naam hae jiske duuno rugby union aur rugby league team khel ke milae ke bola jaae hae.
Rugby football, Rugby, Warwickshire, England me suruu bhais rahaa. jahaan pe iske niyam ke 1845 me likha gais rahaa. Ii rakam ke khel middle ages se khela jaae hae.  Rugby football, England ke public school system me faila, jahaan se ii British Empire bhar fael gais. 
1895 me ii dui rakam ke khel me vibhajit hoe gais jab North England ke 21 club rugby football union ke chhorr ke northern rugby football union ke banain. Isje 1922 me rRugby Football Leage ke naam dewa gais. Iske kaaran rahaa ki League ke member maangat rahin ki khilaari log ke talab dewa jaae. II pahila code rahaa jon ki professional bhais, aur Rugby Union 100 saal baad 1995 Rugby Worlsd Cup ke baad professional bhais.
