Sab se uppar oleic acid  aur cholesterol hae. Biich waala structure hae triglyceride jisme hae oleoyl, stearoyl, aur palmitoyl ke chains glycerol ke backbone se jurraa hae. Sab se niche hae saadharan phospholipid, phosphatidylcholine.]]Lipid ek rakam ke molecule hae, jiske jiu vigyan me jaada kar ke kaam me laawa jae hae. Ii tel ke rakam hae. Charbi ke lipid molecules se banaawa jaae hae. Lipid ke algae, biyaa, gos, cheese, butter aur machhri me paawa jaae hae. Lipids carbon aur hydrogen molecules ke lambaa chain rahe hae. Lipids ke simple aur complex me classify karaa jaae hae. Complex molecules ke namuuna steroids aur phospholipids hoe sake hae. Lipids ke kaam hae energy ke store karo.
