Mari Kuri (s tapulunit : Maria Skłodowska-Curie (asusru )), tllul ass n 7 wamber 1867 deg Waṛsu (deg Tagelda n Ukungri n Pulunya, di Tamnekda tarusit), temmut ass n 4 yulyu 1934 deg Fransa, d tamesfizikt (tasengama) ed tameskimit (takrura) tapulunit tuɣal tafransist deffir zzwaǧ-is akked umesfizik afṛansis Pier Kuri deg 1895, terbeḥ Arraz n Nobel di tfizikt deg 1903 ed Arraz n Nobel di tekrura deg 1911, d tameṭṭut tamzawrut i terbeḥ Arraz Nobel, d nettat kan i yellan d tameṭṭut wi terbeḥ arraz-nni deg snat taɣulin yemgaraden.
