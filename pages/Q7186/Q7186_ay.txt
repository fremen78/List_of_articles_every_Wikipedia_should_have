Maria Salomea Skłodowska-Curie (* Warszawa, 1857 mara 7 uru Awti phaxsin yuritayna - Passy, 1934 mara 4 uru Huillka kuti phaxsin yuriwi),  Puluña jach'a marka jaqinaka; huk Yatxatawiri, Kimika yachaq mantapas yachaqsi karqan. 
 1911 Nobel Pallaytas chaskirqan (Nobel Pallay Kimikapi).
 1903 Nobel Pallaytas chaskirqan (Nobel Pallay Pachaykamay).
Awki: Władysław Skłodowski; Tayka: Bronisława Boguska. Qusan: Pierre Curie Yuqanaka: 2 (Irène & Ève Denise)
