Turka tir patecta ke taltefa Asia wale Tcerna Bira is Mediterranea Bira, dem kimi ( 3% ke tawavo ) vagerone Europa.
Inafu kelu tir lenttaltisteon Ankara.
Vegungafa patecta ke Turka tid Ellasa is Balgaria koe Europa is Suria is Iraka is Irana is Xayastana is Sakartvela koe Asia.
