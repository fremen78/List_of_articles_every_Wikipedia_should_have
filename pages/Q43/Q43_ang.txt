Tyrcland is land in Europan and Asie. Hēr man spricþ on Tyrcisce. His hēafodburg is Ancyra.
Tyrclandes mǣsta dǣl is on Asie, of þǣre Lǣssan Asie ēastƿeard oð Persealandes mearc. Lytel dǣl is on Europan, se is sūþēasterum Tracie, bedǣled of Asie be Bosporose, Proponditis and Ellaspontus. On Bosporose stent Constantinopolis.
