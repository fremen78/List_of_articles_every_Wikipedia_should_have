Turkya, nɣ d tgdudant n turkya (s taturkit: türkiye d türkiye cumhuriyeti) tga yat tmazirt lli illan g iwtta n Asya d Urupa.  lla g iwtta nns lyunan d blɣarya g ugafa utrim, d jurjya d arminya g ugafa agmuḍan, d azarbijan (naxitcewan) d iran g ugmuḍ, d Lɛiraq (kurdistan n lɛiraq) d Surya ɣ unẓul agmuḍan. 
Tamaẓunt nns tga tt Anqaṛa sg 13 kṭubṛ 1923. Tbḍa turkya ɣr iẓẓlmd s yill asggan, ɣr tagut s yill n ijey i d ɣr tagut n iffus s yill amllal.
