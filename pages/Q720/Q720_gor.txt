Jenghis Khan (bahasa Mongolia: Чингис Хаан), meyalo Genghis Khan, Jinghis Khan, Chinghiz Khan, Chinggis Khan, Changaiz Khan, tanggulo unteliyo Temüjin, dieja Temuchin meyalo TiemuZhen (pilotutu to hu'idu Khentii, 1162 - yilate 18 Agustus 1227) botiya khan Mongol wawu ta'uwa lo militer ta lopotuwawu bangusa Mongolia, lapata'o lopotihulo Kekaisaran Mongolia wawu ma lopota'aluku lipu-lipuwalo to Asia, wayitiyo Tiongkok huliyaliyo (Dinasti Jin), Xia Barat, Asia Tengah, Persia, wawu Mongolia.
