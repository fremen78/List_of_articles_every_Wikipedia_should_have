uNomaxhama, (uphawu: ) ngumzulane wesithupha kusuka eLangeni futhi ungowesibili ngobukhulu ozungezohlelweni lwelanga emva kukaMkhulukhulwana. UyiQhakamela eliluhowo elinomugqazinga ovamile ophindwe izikhathi ezilithoba nengexenye kunolukaNomkhubulwane. Unengxenye eyodwa kweziyisiphohlongo zesiluthi esivamile kuNomkhubulwane; Kodwa, ngomthamo wawo omkhulu, uNomaxhama mkhulu izikhathi ezingaphezu kwezingu-95.
