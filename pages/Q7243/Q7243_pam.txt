I Leo Tolstoy, o kaya Conti Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy (Template:Audio-ru, Russian pronunciation: [lʲɛv nʲɪkɐˈlaɪvʲɪtɕ tɐlˈstoj]; Template:OldStyleDate – Template:OldStyleDate), metung yang Russian talasulat maleparan a makikilala bilang metung kareng pekamatenakan a novelista. Ding kayang obra maestra ilapin deng Guerra ampong Kapayapan (War and Peace) ampong Anna Karenina ila deng maglarawan keng lalam, lapad, ampong mabieng papakit ing pang19 dilanuang (siglu) bie king Russia ampong panlalawe, ing taluktuk ning realist fiction.[mangailangangan yang sitas/suporta].
