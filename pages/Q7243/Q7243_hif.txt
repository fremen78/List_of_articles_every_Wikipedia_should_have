Count Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy (1828-1910) ek Russian novelist rahaa, jon ki aapan book War and Peace aur Anna Karenina ke khatir famous bhais .
Tolstoy ke janam Yasnaya Polyana Tula, Russia me bhais rahaa aur uske saadi Sofia Andreevna Bers se bhais rahaa. 
Uu ek Christian rahaa aur non-violence me biswas karat rahaa. Uske book The Kingdom of God is Within You Mahatma Gandhi aur Martin Luther King se influence bhais rahaa.
