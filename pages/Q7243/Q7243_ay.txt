Lev Nikoláievich Tolstói (Лев Николаевич Толстой), Liyun Tolstoy (* Yasnaya Poliana, ~  1828 mara 96 uru Yapu apthapi phaxsin yuritayna -  Astapovo, 1910 mara 20 uru Awti phaxsin sarawayxarakitayna ),  Rusiya jacha marka qillqiri. 
Awki: Nikolái Ilich Tolstóy ; Tayka:  Mariya Tolstaya (Volkónskaya). Warmi: Sofia Behrs.Yuqanaka: .
