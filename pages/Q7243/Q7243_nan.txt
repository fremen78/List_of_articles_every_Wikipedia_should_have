Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy (1828 nî 9 goe̍h 9 – 1910 nî 11 goe̍h 20) (Lō͘-se-a-gí: Лев Никола́евич Толсто́й, IPA: [lʲɛv nʲɪkʌˈlajɪvʲɪtɕ tʌlˈstoj]  Tolstoy?) ia̍h Leo Tolstoy sī Lō͘-se-a ê siáu-soat-ka, tiat-ha̍k-ka, hô-pêng-chú-gī-chiá, kàu-io̍k-kái-kek-chiá. Chiàn-cheng kap Hô-pêng (Война и мир, Voyna i mir) chham Anna Karenina (Анна Каренина) sī Tolstoy siāng ū-miâ ê 2 phō siáu-soat. Chū 1902 nî kàu 1906 nî kî-kan, ta̍k-nî lóng siū Nobel Bûn-ha̍k Chióng thê-miâ; jī-chiá, i mā tī 1901, 1902, kap 1909 nî siū Nobel Hô-pêng Chióng thê-miâ. M̄-koh chòe-āu lóng bô tek chióng, ín-khí siā-hōe kok-kài cheng-lūn.
