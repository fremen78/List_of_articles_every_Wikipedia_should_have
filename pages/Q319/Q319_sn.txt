Vhenekeratsvimborume  (chikwangwani: ; pachidi rinoreva kuti "chinovhenekesa zhira yetsvimborume" ku Chingezi: Jupiter) kana Mveniki (mukupfupisa zita) ndicho chindeya chechishanu kubva kuzuva, uye chakakurisa kudarika zvimwe zvindeya zviri mu mutandiro we mushana. Mveneki chindeya chegasi, uye ndicho chiro chemuchadenga chakavheneka kwazvo chichienderana nevamwe vacho senge Mwedzi na Hweva.
