Merge with uMkhulukhulwana

uNdonsakusa (noma uNdosa; uphawu: ) u. uMkhulukhulwana umZulane omkhulu kuneminye esimisweni sikaNozungezilanga. UngumZulane wesihlanu kusuka elangeni. uMkhulukhulwana uyiqhamela lohowo, kokubili ngenxa yokuthi mkhulu futhi wakhiwe ngalo uohowo. Amanye amaqhamela ohowo anguNomaxhama, uNomakhekheba noNolundwa.
