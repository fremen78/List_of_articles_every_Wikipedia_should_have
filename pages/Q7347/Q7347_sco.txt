The Great Lochs is a collection o fresh watter lochs locate in eastren North Americae on the Canadae - Unitit States mairch. Conseestin o Lochs Superior, Michigan, Huron, Erie, an Ontario, thay form the lairgest group o fresh watter lochs on the Yird. Thay are whiles referred tae as the "Third Coast" bi some ceetizens o the Unitit States.
