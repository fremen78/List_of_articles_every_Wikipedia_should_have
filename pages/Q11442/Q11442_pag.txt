Say bisikleta, a tatawagen met a pedal cycle, bike, push-bike odino cycle, et sakey a sasakyan ya uusaren na too odino uusaren na motor ya ontutulong, uusaren na pedal, sakey-track, a walay duaran dalig ya akasakey ed sakey a frame, sakey ed beneg na sakey. Say manbibisikleta et tatawagen a cyclist, odino bicycle rider.
Saray bisikleta et inletneg ed Europa nen koma-19 a siglo. Diad kagapo na koma-21 a siglo et wala lay masulok a 1 bilyon. Sarayan bilang et mas baleg nen say karakel na saray kotse, balanglan diad karakel tan diad karakel na saray inproduksyon modelo. Sikaray manunan paraan na panagbiyahe ed dakel a rehyon. Sikaray popular met ya panagligliwaan, tan inadapta da iratan pian nausar bilang dulaan na ugugaw, general fitness, militar tan pulis, courier services, bike racing, tan bike stunts.
Say manunan porma tan konfigurasyon na tipikal a maptek odino "seguridad a bisikleta", et daiset so anguman manlapu la nen impawala so sankaunaan ya modelo ya inusar so chain nen manga 1885. Balet, dakel ya detalye so apaaligwas, lalo la manlapu la'd insabi na modernon materyal tan computer-aided design. Saraya so amawala na dakel a espisyal a desinyo parad dakel a klase na panagbisikleta. Diad koma-21 a siglo et nagmaliw ya popular iray electric bicycle.
