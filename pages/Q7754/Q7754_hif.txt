Mathematical analysis, mathematics ke ek hissa hae. Iske kabhi kabhi khaaki analysis bhi bola jaae hae. Isme functions, sequences aur series ke adhyan karaa jaae hae. II engineering me chchhaa se kaam e aae hae. Mathematical analysis ke adhyan ke baad calculus parrhanaa saral hoe jaae hae. Gottfried Wilhem Leibniz aur Isaac Newton ii vibhag me pahila kaam kare rahin.
