Va Wilhelm Richard Wagner ny chummeyder Germaanagh (22 Boaldyn 1813 – 13 Toshiaght Arree 1883). Ruggit ayns Leipzig ny Germaan 'sy vlein 1813 eh. Va sym echey ayns y chiaull chouds v'eh aeg, as hie eh stiagh 'syn Ollooscoill Leipzig 'sy vlein 1831. Va'n jerrey scruit echey lesh e chied obbyr chiaull, Die Feen ("Ny Ferrishyn").
