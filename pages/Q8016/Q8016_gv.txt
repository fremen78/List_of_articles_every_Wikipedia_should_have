Va'n Reejerey Winston Leonard Spencer-Churchill (30 Sauin 1874 – 24 Jerrey Geuree 1965) ny pholitickeyr Goaldagh, as enney share er er son y leeideilys echey er y Reeriaght Unnaneysit car y Nah Chaggey Dowanagh. V'eh ny ard-vinishter ny çheerey veih 1940 derrey 1945 as reesht veih 1951 derrey 1955. Chammah as steateyr as oraatagh ard-ghooagh, va Churchill ny offishear ayns yn Armee Ghoaldagh, ny screeudeyr shennaghys, ny screeudeyr as ny ellyneyr.
