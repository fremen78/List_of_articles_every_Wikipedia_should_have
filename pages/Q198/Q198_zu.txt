Impi ingxabano eshubile yezikhali  phakathi kwemibuso, ohulumeni, imiphakathi, noma amaqembu ezempi njengamabutho, abashokobezi, namasosha . Ngokuvamile kubonakala ngodlame oludlulele, ukucekelwa phansi, nokufa, kusetshenziswa amabutho avamile noma angajwayelekile . Impi isho ubuqhebeqhebe obujwayelekile kanye nezici zezinhlobo zempi, noma zezimpi zijwayelekile.  Impi ephelele iyimpi engakhawulelwe kulokho okuyimingxibishe yezempi kuphela, futhi ingaholela ekuhluphekeni okukhulu kwezakhamuzi noma okunye okungekona ukulwa kanye nokulimala kwabantu .
