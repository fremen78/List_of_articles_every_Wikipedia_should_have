Ostiriya (kureva Austria; Chijerumani: Ã–sterreich sokuti Us'tyeria paChishona) kana Awustiriya, uye pachihofisi ichinzi Rudhende rwe Awustiriya, inyika yakakomberedzwa inowanikwa chikamu chekuchamhembe kwe Yuropu Yepakati, yakagadzikwa kuMaharupusi (kureva Alps). Ifedherari ye mavuso mapfumbabwe, imwe yayo iri gutaguru Vhiyena (kureva Vienna), gutaguru uye muvuso ne huwandu hwe vanhu. Nyika iyi yakaganhurwa ne Jerumani kumaodzanyembaemadokero, uye Rudhende rwe Cheki kumaodzanyemba, Sirovhakya kumaodzanyembaemabvazuva, Hangariya kumabvazuva, Sirovheniya uye Itari kuchamhembe, ne Vusvwisi uye Rishenistayini kumadokero. Inotora ruwa rinosvika of 83,879 km2 (32,386 sk mi) uye ine huwandu hwe vanhu unosvika 9 miriyoni.
