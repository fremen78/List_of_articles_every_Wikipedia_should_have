Ing Austria metung yang parliamentary representative democracy at ating yang siyam a federal states at metung ya kareng aduang bangsa king Europa a kinulyo a pilming neutral, ing metung ing Switzerland. Ing Austria kayanib ya keng United Nations (manibat 1955) at keng European Union (manibat 1995). Keng mumunang kapitna ning 2006, ing Austria ati yang luklukan keng Pamuntukan ning EU.
