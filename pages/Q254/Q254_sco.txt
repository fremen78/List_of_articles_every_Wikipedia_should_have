Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart (27 Januar 1756 – 5 December 1791) wis an Austrian componer. He wis born in the ceety o Salzburg on the 27 Januar 1756. He componed aboot 700 warks, includin the operas Don Giovanni an Die Zauberflöte. He dee'd on 5 December 1791. It isna kent the reason Mozart dee’d, houaniver maist doctors nou thinks it wis frae the rheumatic fivver.
