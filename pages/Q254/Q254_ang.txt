Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart (hātte be fullum naman Joannes Chrysostomus Wolfgangus Theophilus Mozart; ƿæs geboren þæs 27. þæs Æfterran Gēolan þæs 1756. gēares on Sealtbyrig- cƿæl þæs 5. þæs Ǣrran Gēolan 1791. gēares on Uigenna) ƿæs drēamƿrītere Europisces ealdƿīslices drēames. His grēat ƿeorc is brēme geond þa ƿorulde and is ongemang þǣm brēmostum on Europiscum ealdƿīslicum drēame.
