Pama prezidenti ghose gha chalo cha United States,uyo yekhaa ndiye walikupokela ntchindi za kuluska chifukwa ndiye wakamazga ukapolo( slavery),kuyambiska umoza wa vyalo vakupambana (union) kweneso wakalutiska chuma cha chalo chake panthazi.


== MBIRI YAKE MWAKUDUMULA ==
Lincoln wakababika mu mwezi wa Febuluwale ,pa  12, mu chaka cha 1809,mwana wachiwili wa  Thomas Lincoln na  Nancy Hanks Lincoln, mu nyumba ya chidimbo  pa munda wa Sinking Spring pafupi na Hodgenville, Kentucky.
