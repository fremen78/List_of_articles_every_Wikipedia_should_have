Abraham Lincoln (1809 nî 2 goe̍h 12 ji̍t – 1865 nî 4 goe̍h 15 ji̍t) sī Bí-kok tē-16-ê chóng-thóng, jīm-kî sī tē-19 chham tē-20-kài, 1861 nî 3 goe̍h 4 ji̍t chiūⁿ-jīm, 1865 nî 4 goe̍h 15 ji̍t sià-jīm.
Lincoln sī thâu 1 ê Kiōng-hô-tóng-chek ê Bí-kok chóng-thóng.
