Abraham Lincoln (/ ˈlɪŋkən/ LINK-ən; Febụwarị 12, 1809 – Eprel 15, 1865) bụ onye ọkaiwu America na onye ndọrọndọrọ ọchịchị onye jere ozi dị ka onye isi ala 16th nke United States site na 1861 ruo mgbe e gburu ya na 1865. Lincoln duuru mba ahụ n'ofe mba ahụ.  Agha Obodo America wee nwee ihe ịga nke ọma n'ichekwa Union, kagbuo ịgba ohu, ịkwado gọọmentị etiti, na imeziwanye akụnụba US.
