ʻO Abraham Lincoln, i hānau ʻia ma Pepeluali 12, 1809 ma Hardin, ʻAmelika Hui Pū ʻIa, ʻo ia ka Pelekikena 16 o ʻAmelika Hui Pū ʻIa mai Malaki 4, 1861 a hiki i ʻApelila 15, 1865. ʻO ia ka Pelekikena Pelekikena mua loa ma ka moʻolelo o ka ʻāina. Hoʻopau ia i ke kauā. Ua make ʻo ia i pepehi ʻia ma ʻApelila 15, 1865.
