Diina Yahudo (ɗon diina  ibrananci) diga nder ndatal diina ibrahimiya ɓurngol hiidongo e nder duniiyaru diina yahudanci e woodi duɓɓe 4.000 kama heebeti assali diga lesdi isra'il e woodi takkoɓe ndaatal jeytuugol diina hudanci kima yimmɓe miliyanji 15  e ɗon noudiraɓe yahudanko'en ɗon diina tauhidi burka nyamgo e nder littafiwal attaura ɗe littafiwal jooɗi 5 ɗe arannol taa tsarki 

Diina ibrananci e al'adaje taa bolle woɓɓe moodiɓɓe nder hadisije ɗe ɓolletal finnatawa a'rannde kama diga ɓaawo ɗon winndande de nder mishnah talmud e kowride kowɗe diina kiristanci e 
musulunci sa'rii e de Allah e nder yahudanci ɗe diinaje ɗe jaaɓe jaaɓetul jaaɓanɗe imannako too Allah gooto diga hoolotango e dabiya littafiwal ngal tsarki taa ibrananci (alkawal arannde) kautongal attaura


== Imannako ɗon waali ==

Mawɓe imannako kowtarɗo geɓe tati 3 e nder caakaje diina yahudanci ɗon tauhidi e alkawal e yarjejeniya hakkode Allah e yimmɓe Allah e kolletol ngal muhimmanci e nder diina yahudanci e woodi Allah joumiraawo go'otojo giiɗo yimmɓe gaadita kowtarko  kowje ɗe adalci e kuma juyayin diina yahudanci e hollita nyaɗɗo kowtarku Allah taa kolletol littafije  ɗe tsarki e wango'o kowɗe ekketol kama kowɗe al'adaje e dabi'a yahudanci e ikketa yiingo fat yimmɓe nder ayyaje Allah ɓe cancantaki takkirengo daraja e daraja


== Allah go'tojo ==
Mawɗe ikketande diina yahudanci too ɗou Allah e go'otojo e baawurde diina yahudanci Allah ke tanguɗo duniiyaaru kuma Allah nee go'oto o ikko e nder duniiyaaru kuma Allah nee go'oto yahudawa e ɓe innira Allah e innde burde tsarki taa Allah e nder diina yahudanci hakkowke yahweh ko koma ɓurna yahudanko'en e kolleta Allah ɗon o ruhi na ba ɗon zahiri ba 


== Leysdi isra'ila ==
Leydi isra'ila ɗon lisde ɗe tsarki e nder diina yahudanci ɗon Allah taageti jeytuugol duniiyaaru diga dutse moriah e urushalima e nder lesdi isra'il kuma Allah o burti heekago ɗe leydi isra'il kodiyi yahudanko'en
