She gorley gowaltagh cadjin as gaueagh eh gorley shymlee. She bacteyryn 'sy chynney Mycobacterium bun yn asslaynt, as Mycobacterium tuberculosis ayns deiney son y chooid smoo. T'eh soiaghey er ny scowanyn dy cadjin, agh foddee eh jeeylley organeyn elley. T'eh skeaylley trooid yn aer liorish creddal, streighyraght as ceau shelley. Son y chooid smoo, ta doghan follit ec deiney surransagh, agh ta mysh fer 'sy jeig caghlaa dys gorley breeoil.
