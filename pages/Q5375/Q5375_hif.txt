Cricket ek rakam ke game hae jiske gyrah khilaari ke dui team ke beach me khela jaawe hae. Ii khel duniya mae 120 million khilaari khelat rahiis hae, jis waja se ii khel duniya mai dui sab se prasidh khel baan gaya rahiis hae. Cricket Australia, Indian subcontinent, England, West Indies kay desho mae aur Southern Africa kay desho mae bohot pasand kiya jata hae.
