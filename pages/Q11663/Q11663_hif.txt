Mausam ke matlab hae ki , garmii, humidity, atmospheric pressure, hawaa, baaris, aur duusra meteorological patterns dunia me . Chaar rakam ke mausam hae; tropical, desert/dry, polar aur Mediterranean.
Dunia bhar me weather station mausam ke alag-alag hissa ke naape hae, jaise ki hawaa ke raftaar, kon taraf hawaa chale hae, garmii aur namii (humidity). Meteorologists ii sab naap ke aage ke mausam ke predict kare hae.
Mausam badle hae, kaaheki dunia ke alag lag jagha me ek rakam suraj ke garmii nai mile hae. Tropics me jaada garmii mile hae aur poles me kamtii garmii rahe hae. Garam hawaa uppar jaae hae aur iske jagha ke thanda hawaa le hae, jiske "convection" bola jaae hae.
