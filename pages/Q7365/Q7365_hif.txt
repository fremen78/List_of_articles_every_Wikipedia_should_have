Muscle, jaanwar aur insaan ke denhi ke ek hissa hae. Iske kaam hae denhi ke part jaise haanth aur gorr ke move karo. Muscle, cells se banaa rhe hae. Tiin rakam ke muscle hae: Skeletal muscle jon ki haddi se jurraa rahe hae, smooth muscle, jaise ki blood vessels aur bladder au cardiac muscle jiske dil me paawa jaae hae.
