The African Union (abbreviated AU) ek international organization hae jisme fifty-three African member des hae. Iske Durban me July 2002 me banawa gais rahaa, aur ii Organisation of African Unity (OAU) aurAfrican Economic Community (AEC) ke jagha pe banaa rahaa. 
Aage chal ke AU, Africa khatir ek currency ek army aur ek sarkar maange hae.

== Suspended member ==
Mauritania - jiske 2008 ke coup ke baad suspend karaa gais rahaa.
Guinea - jiske 2008 ke coup ke baad suspend karaa gais rahaa.
Madagascar - jiske 2009 Malagasy political crisis ke baad suspend karaa gais rahaa
Eritrea - apne se African Union se nikal ke United Nations Security Council se Union ke uppar UN sanctions ke maang karis.
