Bangkok (s tataylandit : กรุงเทพมหานคร, Krung Thep neɣ Maha Nakhon. Krung Thep anamek-is s taqbaylit : tamdint d tamanaɣt, Maha Nakhon s taqbaylit : tamdint n yineglusen), d tamanaɣt n Ṭayland, d Bagkok kan i d tamdint deg tamurt i tesɛa n tudrin kan.
Bagkok tezga-d deg delta n wasif n Čaw Fraya, tebɛeb s ahat 40 yikilumitren ɣer ubagu n Ṭayland.
