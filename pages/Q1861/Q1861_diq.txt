Bangkok (be Taiki: กรุงเทพมหานคร), sûka Taylandaya tewr gırda û paytextê dewleto. Serra 2010ıne dı gorey amarê resmi nıfusê Bangkoki 8,280,925 nefero. 
Namey sûke Tayki dı 166 herfe ra "Krung Thep Mahanakhon Amon Rattanakosin Mahinthara Yuthaya Mahadilok Phop Noppharat Ratchathani Burirom Udomratchaniwet Mahasathan Amon Piman Awatan Sathit Sakkathattiya Witsanukam Prasit"o.
