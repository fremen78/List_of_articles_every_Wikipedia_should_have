IBangkok, ngesiThai ngokusemthethweni ibizwa ngokuba  Thep Maha Nakhon okanye iKrung Thep ikomkhulu kunye nesona sixeko sikhulu nesona sinabantu abaninzi eThailand, emi ecaleni koMlambo iChao Phraya, kufutshane neGulf of Thailand . Ngokobalo-bantu luka-2020, bekukho i-10.539 yezigidi zabemi, i-15.3% yabemi belizwe, ngelixa ngaphezulu kwe-14 yezigidi (22.2%) behlala kwingingqi ye- metropolitan ngo-2010, equka namaphondo angqongileyo.  Phakathi kwezona zixeko zinabantu abaninzi kunye nezona zixakekileyo ehlabathini,  kunye nenye yezona ndawo zithandwayo kukhenketho lwehlabathi, iye yafumana uphuhliso olukhawulezayo lwemizi-mveliso ukususela kwisiqingatha sesibini senkulungwane yama-20, emele esinye sezona zixeko zinamandla kwezoqoqosho. kuMzantsi-Mpuma Asia .  Uqoqosho lwaseBangkok lubekwe kwindawo yesithandathu ngobukhulu phakathi kwezixeko zaseAsia ngokwe- GDP ngomntu ngamnye, emva Singapore, Hong Kong, Tokyo, Osaka - Kobe kunye neSeoul ukususela ngo-2010, nto leyo eyenza ukuba ibe yenye yezona zixeko zibalulekileyo e-Asia.
