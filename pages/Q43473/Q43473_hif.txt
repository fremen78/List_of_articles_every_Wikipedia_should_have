Angkor Wat, Cambodia me ek mandir ke complex hae. Iske 12th century me Hindu raja Suryavarman II, aapan des ke capital aur raj ke mandir khatir banais rahaa. Baad me ii ek Buddhist mandir hoe gais rahaa, lekin 14th century ke baad ii barbaad hoe lagaa. Ii dunia ke sab se barraa dharmik building hae.
