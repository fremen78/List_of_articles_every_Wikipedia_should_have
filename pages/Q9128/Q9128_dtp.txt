Naau, nawau, navau, toi ko' ninaau, ninawau, om ninavau nopo nga' kawo do ginumbang dorudginid (electromagnetic radiation) ii okito do tulun. Naau nopo nga' poingion id rinulud okito (visible spectrum) om koubasan pointongkop mantad ninaru 400 gisom 700nm om kopiampai do sinaru mantad 750 gisom 420THz. Rinulud naau poingion id piolitan do rinulud saauragang (infrared), ii kininaru lobi anaru om kisinaru lobi aranggang, om rinulud lapusohug (ultraviolet) ii kininaru lobi oniba om kisinaru lobi asarok. Tolu ginumbang diti, ii saauragang, naau, om lapusohug nopo roitan do ginumbang dounmato (optical radiation).
