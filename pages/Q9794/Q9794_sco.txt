Reggae is a style o muisic that oreeginatit in Jamaica in the late 1960s. The term an aa denotes the modren popular muisic o Jamaica an its diaspora. A 1968 single bi Toots and the Maytals "Do the Reggay" wis the first popular sang tae uise the wird "reggae," effectively namin the style an introducin it tae a global audience.
