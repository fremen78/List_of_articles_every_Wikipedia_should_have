Alfred Hitchcock (13 augustus 1899, Londe, Ingeland - 29 april 1980, Los Angeles, Californië) waor 'nen Ingelse regisseur dee veural bekind is vaan zien thrillers. Zien bekindste films zien: The Lodger (1927), The Lady Vanishes (1938), Rebecca (1940), The Man Who Knew Too Much (1934), Rear Window (1954), Vertigo (1958), North by Northwest (1959), Psycho (1960), The Birds (1963), Topaz (1969) en Frenzy (1972).
Daoneve heet heer ouch 'n TV-show gemaak, naomelek: Alfred Hitchcock Presents. Heer waor 52 jaor actief, vaan 1922 tot 1976.
