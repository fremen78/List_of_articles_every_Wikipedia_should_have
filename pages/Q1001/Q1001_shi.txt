Mahatma Gandi ula Muhandas Karamcand iga yan umswingm d yan urgaz n tsrtit ahndi, ar fllas ttinin "Iman Mqqurn". Ilul d Gandi ɣ usggʷas 1869 immut ɣ 1948. Inkr d ɣ yat twja tamjahdiyt, iɣra ɣ Lunḍun aylliɣ sul iga anzzarfu. Ifta ilmma s Bumbay fadd s Tfriqt n iffus ɣilli ɣ rad immaɣ mgal asnaḥya aẓuṛan d ɣaylli mi ttinin "angraw n ubaṛṭayḍ".
Ɣ udwal nns s Lhnd, rad inkr f usddurri Anglizi da iɣʷin f tmazirt nns, iɣra i mddn afad ad inkr ttɣwwiɣ uɣrim bla ddafuniyt d ad bbin ayt lhnd kra d ikkan Nngliz. F mayad, ikcm mnnawt n ttwal s usqqur, idra daɣ ɣ umhawaḍ n tdrfit qbl imiɣ amaḍlan wis sin. Yut ula f ungiddi gr mddn.  
Immaɣ bla ad imurs ad itawa gr Imuslmn d Ihndusn aylliɣ t inɣa yan Uhndus azakrar ass n 30 Ynnayr ɣ usggʷas 1948.
