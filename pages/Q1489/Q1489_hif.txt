Mexico City, Mexico ke capital aur sab se barraa city hae. Ii dunia ke sab se barraa city me se ek hae. Aztec log hian pe Spanish log ke aae se paile hian pe rhat rahin. Iske 1521 me Hernán Cortés suruu karis rahaa. Abhi iske abaadi 8.5 million hae aur 18 million log Greater Mexico City urban area me rahe hae. 1928 ke baad Mexico City nai rahaa aur iske jagha ek Federal District banawa gais.
Mexico City ke abaadi, Tokyo ke baad dunia me sab se jaada hae aur ii Mumbai se thora barraa hae.
