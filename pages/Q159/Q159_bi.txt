Rasia (Toktok Rasia: Россия), hem i wan bigfala kaontri long Yurop mo Not Esia, we pipol save araon 120 toktok mo speseli toktok Rasia.
Presiden blong Rasia hemi Vladimir Putin. Kapital blong Rasia i Moskow. Praem Minista blong Rasia hemi Mikhail Mishustin. Narafala bigwan taon long kaontri ia hemi San-Peterburg, Novosibirsk, Kazan, etc. Populaesen blong Rasia i stap araon 141,927,297.
