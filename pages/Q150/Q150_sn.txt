Chifurenji (Fur: français, Nge: French) kana Chifuranzi mutauro wekuFuranzi, uye Mitauro ye Chirhoma pamwechete neChispeini, Chiputukezi, Chiitari, Chiromania neChikatara, Chiratini uye Chikwiyori (Speini-Kwiyori kana Furenji-Kwiyori). Chifurenji (pamwe nedzimwe nzvimbo dze Furanzi) chinotaurwa muChamhembe cheBherujyamu, Madokero eSwitsarendi, Rakisambhegi, neKanadha. Uye inotaurwa munyika dze Afurika senge Utemabanze weRudhende rweVakongo, Senegari, Maari, Seyisheri, Chadhi nedzimwe, uye Chamhembechemabvazuva eEzhiya neAmerika akatorwa neFuranzi.
