Ulwimi lwasiFrentshi (français, la langue française) ulwimi lwesiFrentshi.
Ulwimi lwesiFrentshi njengebali lothando. Okokuqala, isiFrentshi siye saba lisango eliya eFrance, eBelgium, eLuxembourg, eSwitzerland, eCanada, kumbindi we-Afrika, eMadagascar, eMauritius naseRéunion kodwa namhlanje kukho abantu abazizigidi ezingama-300 kwihlabathi liphela (uninzi lukhona kumazwe angama-40) ulwimi lwaseFransi.
