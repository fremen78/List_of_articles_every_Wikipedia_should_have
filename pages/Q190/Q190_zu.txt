Ekucabangeni kokukholelwa kuNkulunkulu munye, uNkulunkulu uthathwa njengomuntu ophakeme kunabo bonke, umdali, nento eyinhloko yokholo. UNkulunkulu uvame ukucatshangelwa njengonamandla onke, owazi konke, okho konke futhi onamandla onke kanye nokuba nokuphila okuphakade futhi okudingekayo. UNkulunkulu uvame ukubhekwa njengongekho emthethweni, kuthiwa isici esithile sihlobene nemibono yokudlula noma yobudoda.

Ezinye izinkolo zichaza uNkulunkulu ngaphandle kokubhekiswa ebulilini, kanti ezinye zisebenzisa amatemu aqondene nobulili futhi ahambisana nobulili. UNkulunkulu ukhulelwe njengomuntu uqobo noma ongeyena umuntu. Ku-atheism, uNkulunkulu ungumdali nomondli womhlaba, ngenkathi ese-deism, uNkulunkulu ungumdali, kepha hhayi umsekeli, wendawo yonke. E-pantheism, uNkulunkulu uyindawo yonke uqobo. Ukungakholelwa ebukhoneni bukaNkulunkulu ukungabikho kokukholelwa kuNkulunkulu, kanti ubu-agnosticism buthi ubukhona bukaNkulunkulu abaziwa noma abaziwa. UNkulunkulu ubuye wakhulelwa njengomthombo wazo zonke izibopho zokuziphatha, futhi "omkhulu kunabo bonke ongacatshangwa ukuthi ukhona" .Izazi zefilosofi eziningi ezivelele zenze izimpikiswano zokuphikisa nobukhona bukaNkulunkulu.
