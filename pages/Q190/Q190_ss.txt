Nkulunkulu lophindze atiwe ngekutsi ngumninimandla onke, leyo lehlala ikhona, ngumnikati wetintfo tonke letikhona emhlabeni, ezulwini, ngaphansi kwemanti kanye nasemkhatsi. Bantfu labanyenti bamati ngemagama lahlukene Nkulunkulu kantsi basebentisa tinkolo letehlukene kuchumana naye.
