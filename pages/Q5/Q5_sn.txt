Munhu (kana mundu uye munthu muChindau) chisikwa chakanaka chakasikwa naMwari pazuva rechitanhatu ravakasika denga nenyika nazvose zvirimo.

Mutsa (humans considered as a group; the human race).
Munhu paakaya ane mweya usingarovi. Man has this essential quality, he has an immortal soul.


== Mitauro ye Chiafurika ==
VaLenje vanoti muntu (person) vachireva munhu. VaGungu neMaZulu vanoti bantu (people) vachireva vanhu.
Lenje vanoti mwana (person) vachireva munhu. Mamwe mazwi: mwanike (child - either boy or girl, son or daughter) vachireva mwana mudiki.
Lenje vanoti mwanakashi (female person) kureva munhukadzi. Baila vanoti mumi (a living person) kureva munhu mupenyu.
Lenje vanoti mwanalume (male person, husband) kureva munhurume. Sangho vanoti zo (n. human) kureva munhu.
Digo vanoti mwanadamu (n. human) kureva munhu. Shilluk vanoti tero (n. people) vachireva vanhu
VaMambwe vanoti  mulawa (n. person, a human being) kureva munhu.
