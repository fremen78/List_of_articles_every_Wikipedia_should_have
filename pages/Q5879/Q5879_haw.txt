He haku mele kaulana ʻo Goethe o Kelemānia, o ka wā kelakikala. ʻO Johann Wolfgang von Goethe (puana Kelemānia: [ˈjoːhan ˈvɔlfɡaŋ fɔn ˈɡøːtə]) kona inoa piha. Hānau ʻia ʻo Goethe i ka makahiki 1749, ma ka lā 28 ʻAukake, ma Palanipoka (ma ka ʻōlelo Kelemānia, Frankfurt am Main). Nui loa kāna hana hihiu. Kaulana kāna keaka hopena kaumaha "Faust".
Make ‘o Johann Wolfgang von Goethe ma ka lā 22 Malaki 1832 ma Wehimala (ma ka ʻōlelo Kelemānia, Weimar).
