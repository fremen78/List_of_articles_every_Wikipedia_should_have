Johann Wolfgang von Goethe (28 August 1749 – 22 March 1832), German ke writer me se sab se achchhaa rahaa. Uu ek poet, novelist aur playwright rahaa, lekin uu actor, administrator, scientist, geologist, botanist aur philosopher bhi rahaa. Uske asar bahut 19th century ke  writer aur thinker ke uppar rahaa.  Uu Theory of colours se science me bhi yogdaan diis hae. Bahut log uske books me se quote kare hae aur uske kahawat German bhasa ke ek hissa bhi hoe gais hae. Uske kavita ke musicme karaa gais rahaa  composers jaise [[Franz
