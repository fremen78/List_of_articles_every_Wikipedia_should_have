I Johann Wolfgang von Goethe (Pangayagkas a Aleman: [ˈjoːhan ˈvɔlfɡaŋ fɔn ˈɡøːtə] ( makiramdam), 28 Agostu 1749  –  22 Marsu 1832) metung yang Aleman talasulat ampong polymath. Ding obra nang Goethe sasakupan no ring marangle ning kawatasan (poetry), drama, malagung pangyulat (literature), theology, philosofia, humanism ampong  science. Ing kayang magnum opus, mekilala ya bilang iti ing taluktuk ning pangkalibilugan yatung malagung panyulat (world literature), adua yang dake keng drama Faust. Ding aliwang bantug a malagung panyulat a obra na kayabe la reng dakal a kawatasan, ing Bildungsroman Wilhelm Meister's Apprenticeship ampo ing epistolary novel The Sorrows of Young Werther.
