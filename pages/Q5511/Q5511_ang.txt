Tangangicamere is miclu lūh on Affrican ēastdæl.  He bedǣleþ Tansania þæt land on his ēatrīme of Congolande bewesten and fēower rīcu sittað be his rīm, ðā eardon Tansania, Congoland, Burundi and Sambia. Tanganyicamere līþ in Affrican Riftdæle.
Ðis is Middangeardes mǣsta mere be his wætergemete æfter Baikalmere in Siberie and se dēoposta mere æfter Baikalmere ēac. Ofer allum is Tangangicanmere þisse woruldes langsta swētewætermere.
He wæter sigð in strēamum Congo þǣre Ēa and ðider to Westsǣ.
