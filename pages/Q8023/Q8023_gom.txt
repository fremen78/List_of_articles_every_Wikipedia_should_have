== Monddela, Nelson ==
Dokxinn afrikecho prodhanomontri 1994t tannem dokxinn afrikecho prodhanomontri mhunn soput gheun ovrnnobhedi lokxayecho purskar kelo.Gorea lokanchi sot'ta axil'li ten'na,dexantlea kallea lokankuy rajkaronnant soman mellche mhunn tannem cholloull kel'li.Haka lagun taka dokxinn afrika sorkaran 27 vorsam bondkhonnint dourolo.
Monddelacho bapuy tembu zomaticho mukheli axil'lo.Dokxinn afrikentlea olposonkhyi-i gorea lokanchi razoutt puraiponnan moddun uddoupachea hetan 1912t afrikon nexnol kangresochi sthapnnuk kel'li.Visavea xenkddeachea dusrea ordakosaun nelsonchea fuddaroponnasokoil afrikon nexnol kangrosocho purnojlm' zalo.Tannem zalo afrikon lokanchea adaran vornnobhedi gorea sorkara add avaz utthoupacho yotn kelo.Haka lagun 1956-61 hea tempar tacher dexdrohi mhunn choukoxi zaun taka bondkhonnichi kheast zali.1961t tachi suttka zale uprant legit tannem gorea sorkarachea vornnobhedi dhornnak nettan virodh chaluch dourolo. Taka lagun 1962t taka portun bondkhonnit dourolo. 1963-64 meren tachi choukoxi zaun
