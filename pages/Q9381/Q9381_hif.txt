Adam Smith (1723 - 17 July 1790) ek Scottish economist rahaa, jiske Father of Modern Economics maana jaawe hae. Uske book, An Inquiry into the Nature and Causes of the Wealth of Nations, bahut important rahaa. Admii log ii buk ke The Wealth of Nations bole hae. Nawaa economics ke kuchchh idea ii buk pe based hae.
