Adam Smith [smɪθ] (dööpt 5. Junijul./ 16. Juni 1723greg. in Kirkcaldy, Graveschop Fife, Schottland; † 17. Juli 1790 in Edinburgh) was een schottschen Philosophen in de Tied van de Upklärung un geld för den Gründer van de klassische Natschonaaalökonomie.


== Warken ==

Theory of Moral Sentiments (1759)
Considerations Concerning the Formation of Languages and the Different Genius of original and compounded Languages. (1767)
An Inquiry into the Nature and Causes of the Wealth of Nations (1776),
Essays on Philosophical Subjects (postum 1795).
Lectures on Jurisprudence (postum 1976)
Lectures on Rhetoric and Belles Lettres (postum 1976).
