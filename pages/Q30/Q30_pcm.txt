USA (Amerikan Hinglish: United States of America) or Amerika is a kontri in di Amerikas. Dem blow Hinglish. Amerika ste bai Kanada and Mexiko.
Di kontri get 50 stets.


== Politics ==
Di fedral (nashonal) govament for USA get three branches: di Executive, Legislative, na Judicial branches.
Congress go mek statutes, na di Prezident go sign dem, wey mek dem law. Laws dey give fedral agencies powers, so dem go mek regulations.


=== Executive ===
Di Prezident for USA dey Joe Biden, wey don get elected for 2020.


=== Legislative ===
Di Congress get two houses: di House of Representatives na di Senate.


=== Judicial ===
Di Judicial branch get di Supreme Court na oda courts.
