United States of America (U.S.A. bɩ USA, b sẽn boond tɩ United States, yaa tẽng sẽn be  North America. A tara tẽns 50 la tẽnga pʋga, la tẽns a nu sẽn ka tar tẽng a ye, la tẽn-kɩrems a wɛ, la nin-buiid 326. United States yaa tẽng a tãab-n-soab sẽn yaa kãseng n yɩɩdã dũniyã pʋga. A tara tẽnga noor ne Kanada la a rɩtg ne Mɛksiko, la a tara mogr ne Bahama, Cuba,  Russia, la tẽns a taab. A tara nin-buiidã sẽn yɩɩd milyõ 333, a yaa tẽng ning sẽn tar neb wʋsg n yɩɩd Amerikã la a tãab-n-soabã sẽn tar neb wʋsg n yɩɩd dũniyã. United States tẽn-kãsengã yaa Washington, D.C., tɩ tẽngã sẽn tar neb wʋsg n yɩɩdã la ligd yell gesg zĩigã yaa New York.
