Iɣ ur igi wad amgrad lli tsiggilt, kcm s tasna n Mirikan bac ad tẓrt akkʷ imgradn nna dar illa yism zun d wad !

Iwankn Munnin nɣ s yism nns iɣzzifn Iwankn Munnin n Marikan, illa daɣ ma ⴰd as ittinin ɣar Marikan (s tanglizt : United States, United States of America, US, USA, America) tga yat tmazirt bahra istawhmman ɣ umaḍal. Tlla tmazirt ad ɣ tamrikt n ugafa.
Tga tamazirt tazgrmnẓawt, amata n ikaln nns llan ɣ tmrikt n ugafa. Iwunak imunn dars tuskiwt tawsrtit n yat tgduda tawmnḍawt tafidiralt mm ungraw awslway yulɣn s smmumraw n iwunak, kumraw d tam gisn gan inaragn. Imggura ad, issuty asn ugaraw anaṭlas ɣ ugmuḍ d ugaraw imzgi ɣ utaram, tlla as d Kanada ɣ ugafa tili as miksiku ɣ unẓul. Iwunak nna nn illan iwr inn d Alaska illan ɣ utaram n Kanada, d haway yan warkabil d yusan ammas n ugaraw imzgi n ugafa. Tumy tmazirt ad altu ikaliwn igan tigzirin illuzzan ɣ yill n Karayib d ugaraw imzgi. Tarakalt d unzwi n tmazirt ad mawayn kigan, aynna d yuwyn yan umzaray imqqurn n imɣayn d imudar, aynna iskrn ɣ iwuak imunn yat zɣ 17 n tmizar wala imnawayn n umtiwg.
