Lafiya, a cewar Hukumar Lafiya ta Duniya, shine "yanayin cikakkiyar lafiyar jiki, tunani da zamantakewa ba kawai rashin cututtuka da rashin lafiya bane".  An yi amfani da ma'anoni iri-iri da dalilai daban-daban na tsawon lokaci. Ana iya habaka lafiya ta hanyar karfafa ayyukan lafiya, kama motsa jiki na yau da kullun da isasshen barci, da rage ko guje wa ayyuka ko yanayi mara kyau, kamar shan taba ko damuwa mai yawa. Wasu abubuwan da suka shafi kiwon lafiya sun kasance saboda zabin mutum, kamar ko shiga cikin halayen hadari mai hadari, yayin da wasu kuma saboda dalilai na tsari, kamar ko an tsara al'umma ta hanyar da ta fi sauki ko wuya ga mutane su samu. ayyukan kula da lafiya da suka wajaba. Har yanzu, wasu dalilai sun wuce duka zabi na mutum da na rukuni, kamar cututtukan kwayoyin cuta.
