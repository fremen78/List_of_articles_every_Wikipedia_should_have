Heal is freedom frae skaith, that is won at wi the prevention, traitment, an management o illness an the preservation o mental an pheesical weel bein throu the services gien by the medical, nouricin, an allee'd heal perfaissions. Gaun by the World Health Organisation, heal care inrowes aw the guids an services designed tae forder heal, includin “preventive, curative an palliative interventions, whither aitit at indiveeduals or tae populations”. The organised proveesion o sic services micht constitute a heal care seestem. This can include a speceefic govrenmental organisation sic as, in the Unitit Kinrick, the National Health Service or a cooperation athort the National Health Service an the Social Services as in Shared Care.
