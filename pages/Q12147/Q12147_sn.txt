Hutano (health in English) zvinotaura kuva vatano kana kusimba kwemuviri yevanhu. Munhu mtano ndiye anenge asina chirwere.


== Mamwe Mazwi ==
Mutano, dano, tano kana nhano (Healthy) apa kureva chipenyu chine hutano hwakarurama.


== Mitauro yeBantu ==
Shambala inoti tana (adj. fine; beautiful). Malinke inoti tana (adj. fine) kureva kuva mutano.
Venda inoti mutakalo (n. health) kureva hutano. Mamwe mazwi: -takala (be healthy). Nyanja inoti mlamo (health; preservation).
Giryama inoti uzima kana matsiya (health) kureva hutano. VaGiryama vanoti muzima (healthy) kureva munhu mutano.
Zigula inoti udyima (health) kureva hutano. VaKikuyu vanoti ogima kana hinya (health) kureva hutano.
Lenje inoti wuumi kana wumi (n. life, health, soundness, safety) vachireva hupenyu, hutano, chisvinu, ndaramo.
VaMambwe vanoti ulumi (good health, peace, quietness) kureva hutano.
Ko kuturikira chinonzi vital signs kuEnglish.
