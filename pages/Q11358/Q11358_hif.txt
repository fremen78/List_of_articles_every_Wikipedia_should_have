Ek carbohydrate (pronounced /kɑrbɵˈhaɪdreɪt/) ek organic compound hae jiske empirical formula Cm(H2O)n hae (jisme m, n se different rahe sake hae); iske matlab ii hae ki isme khaali carbon, hydrogen, aur oxygen rahe hae, aur hydrogen:oxygen atom ke ratio 2:1 (paani ke rakam rahe hae).
