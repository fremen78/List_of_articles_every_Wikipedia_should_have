Louis Daniel Armstrong, "Satchmo" suti (* New Orleans, 1901 mara 4 uru Thaya phaxsin yuritayna - †  New York, 1971 mara 6 uru Huillka kuti phaxsin yuriwi),  Istadus Unidos jacha marka q'uchu arsuyaña, jaylliphust'ari & Jazz trumpitista. 
Awki: William Armstrong, Jr.; Tayka: Mary "Mayann" Albert (1886–1942).
