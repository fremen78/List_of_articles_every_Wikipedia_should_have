Franz "Peter" Schubert (janam: Vienna, 31 January 1797; maut: Vienna 19 November 1828), ek Austrian composer rahaa. Jab ki uu 31 saal ke umar me mar gais rahaa, uu lagbhag 1000 music ke compose karis rahaa. Isme se kuchh abhi talak compose karaa gais music me se sab se achchhaa hae. Vienna ke aur jaana maana composer, jaise ki Haydn, Mozart aur Beethoven rahin, lekin Schubert khaali ek rahaa jon ki Vienna me paida bhais rahaa. Schubert Classical music period ke aakhri aur Romantic period ke pahila composer rahaa.
