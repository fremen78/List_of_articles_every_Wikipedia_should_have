Franz Peter Schubert (1797 nî 1 goe̍h 31 ji̍t – 1828 nî 11 goe̍h 19 ji̍t) sī chi̍t ūi Ò-tē-lī chok-khek-ka. Schubert tī 31 hòe ê sî-chūn kòe-sin, m̄-koh i it-seng chhòng-chok ê chok-phín sò͘-liōng sǹg-sī chin chē. Schubert sī lōng-bān-phài im-ga̍k ê chá-kî tāi-piáu jîn-bu̍t, iā tiāⁿ-tiāⁿ pī jīm-ûi sī kó͘-tián-phài im-ga̍k ê siāng-bóe chi̍t ūi thâu-chhiú-sai.
