Physics, me classical mechanics me se mechanics ke dui hissa mwe se ek hae. Ii physical laws  join ki motion of bodies under the action of a system of forces ke baare me hae. Ii study bahut purana hae jiske kaaran  classical mechanics science, engineering aur technology ke sab se purana topic hae.
