Desert ek bahut jhuura jagah rahe hae. Hian pe chaahe average, nai to 250 mm (10 in) se kamti paani barrse hae., Ek definition ke niche, ek desert me jetna paani barse hae se jaada paani evaporate hoe hae. Ek desert ekdam thanda nai to ekdam garam jagah rahe hae. Desert ke jaada hissa baalu nai to snow, sand dunes nai to snowdrifts, aur cacti nai to bahut chhota plants rahe hae.
Desert dunia ke 25% area ke cover kare hae. Sab se barraa garam desert Sahara aur sab se barraa thanda desert Antarctica hae.
