== Latar belakang ==
Pilotutuliyo to Venesia, Italia, 4 Maret 1678, te Vivaldi lodudu'a tota liyamoliyo moyitohu musiki, te Giovanni Battista Vivaldi, ta moyyitohe lo biola to Katedral San Marco. To usia onngo muda, te Vivaldi pilotunuliyo moyitohu biola odelo tiyamoliyo. Timongoliyo lohelili hi' na'owa lohelli to kota Venesia mopobilohu yitohu lo musik.
