The solar seestem includes the Sun an aw the objects that revolve aroond it, including planetoids, comets, muins, planets, an droich-planets (Mercur, Venus, Yird, Maurs, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, an Neptune). Pluto isnae ranked as a planet ony mair.

Ither starns an the objects that revolve roond aboot them is aften referit tae as planetary systems. When speaking aboot a specific starn's planetary system, it is usual tae shorten it tae "the <name> system" (e.g. "the Alpha Centauri system", "the Barnard system", or "the Sol system").
