Delhi (Hindi: दिल्ली) India des ke capital city hae. Ii India ke north part me hae. Iske population lag bhag 18 million hae. 2010 me hian pe Commonwealth Games bhais rahaa.  Hian pe duniya ki second sabse barraa eco friendly metro rail service hae.


== Population ==
Saal 2011 me hian ke population 16.8 million rahaa ii duniya ke 5 number ke sabase baraa population waala sahar hae. Area ke hisaab se bhi ii duniyaa ke metro cities me se ek sab se barraa hae.
