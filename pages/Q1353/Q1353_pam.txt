Ing Delhi (Hindi: दिल्ली; Punjabi: ਦਿੱਲੀ; Urdu:  دلی; IPA: [d̪ɪlːiː]; a ausan da muring Dilli) ing kaduang pekamaragul a metropolis ning India, at maki populasiun yang labimpitung (17) miliun , at metung a union territory a opisial a mayayaus a National Capital Territory of Delhi (NCT). 
Atiu iti kareng pampang ning Ilug Yamuna king Punjab (labuad) ning pangulung India, at metung ya kareng lakanbalen a pekamaluat da nang tutuknangan a alang patugut king yatu (oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world). 
Tungku (capitoliu o cabecera) de reng mapilang imperiu ning India kanitang minuna ing Delhi, at metung yang maulagang lakanbalen kareng mangatuang dalan a pangkalakal (trade routes) king pilatan ning pangulu-albugan (northwest) ning India ampo reng Kapatagan a Indo-Gangetic. Dakal a monumentung ibat kanitang minuna ampo king Edad Media, karinan a archeological, ampong aliwa pang lugal a makasalesayan. Tinikdo ne ning emperador a Mughal a i Shahjahan ing lakanbalen a ausan da ngening "Old Delhi" (Matuang Delhi), bang magsilbing tungku ning Imperiung Mughal pilatan ning 1649 ampong 1857.
