Jahaaj (Hindustani: जहाज/जहाज़/جہاز jahāz; English: ship) ek barraa watercraft hai. Jahaaj samundar (ocean aur sea) me. Ii ek boat se barraa rahe hae. Jahaaj se aaj-kal jaada kar ke cargo carry karaa jaae hae, lekin jahaaj passenger ke carry kare ke khaatir aur larrai ke khaatir bhi kaam me laawa jaae hae.


== Itihaas ==
Pahila jahaaj ke khe (row) ke, nai to hawaa se , nai to duuno se chalaawa jaawat rahaa. 4000 BC me Egypt ke log lakrri ke paal waala jahaaj banae ke suruu kare rahin. 1200 BC me Greek log aur barra boat banain jon ki 30 metres ambaa rahaa aur 180 ton chij carry kare sakat rahaa. Rome ke boat isse aur barra rahaa 1,000 ton ke log aur chij ke carry kare sakat rahin
