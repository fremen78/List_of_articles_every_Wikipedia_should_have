Ta'n Commeeys Arabagh (Arabish: الجامعة العربية‎ Jāmiʻa al-ʻArabiyya), ny Commeeys ny Steatyn Arabagh (جامعة الدول العربية‎ Jāmiʻat ad-Duwal al-ʻArabiyya) dy oikoil, ny reagheydys ardjynagh jeh steatyn Arabagh 'syn Aishey Heear Yiass as 'syn Affrick Hwoaie as Hiar Hwoaie. Va'n reagheydys bunnit ayns Cairo ny h-Egypt er 22 Mart, 1945 lesh shey olteynyn: yn Egypt, yn Earack, y Taryordaan (aa-enmyssit gys y Jordaan ny yei 1946), y Livaan, yn Araab Saudi as y Teer. Haink er y Yeaman dy ve ny oltey er 5 Boaldyn, 1945. Ec y traa t'ayn, ta 22 oltey 'sy reagheydys.
