Rodent, jiske jiu vigyan me rodentia bhi bola jaae hae, ek rakam ke jaanwar hae jiske duuno uppar aur niche waala maskur me dui daant rahe hae jon ki sab time barrte rahe hae. Lagbhag 40% jaanwar rodentia hae aur iske New Zealand aur Antartica ke chhorr ke dunia ke sab jagha paawa jaae hae. Kuchh sadharan rodentia kae; chuuha, squirrel, prairie dog, porcupine, beaver, guinea pig, hamster, rabbit
