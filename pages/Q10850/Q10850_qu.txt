Khankiq, shukllachishka kichwapi Wiskachak (ordo Rodentia) nisqakunaqa huk ñuñuq uywakunam.


== Mitan kamay ==
Kaymi huk khankiqkunam:
Subordo Anomaluromorpha
Subordo Castorimorpha
Subordo Hystricomorpha

Kichka ukucha (Echimyidae)
Kichka k'usillu (Erethizontidae)
Quwi rikch'aq ayllu (Caviidae)
Quwi (genus Cavia)
Kapiwara, karpinchu, runsuqu icha ariraña (Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris)
Chiwayru (Dasyproctidae)
Chinchilla rikch'aq ayllu (Chinchillidae)
Wisk'acha (genera Lagidium, Lagostomus)
Chinchilla (genus Chinchilla)
Subordo Sciuromorpha

Antharu / Waywacha (Sciuridae)
Subordo Myomorpha

Chupasapa ukucha (Muridae)
Hatun ukucha (genus Rattus)
Wasi ukucha (Mus musculus)
Chutuchupa ukucha (Cricetidae)
Awya Yala ukucha (Sigmodontinae)
Qachu ukucha (genus Akodon)
Hawa ukucha (Oecomys bicolor)
Palanta ukucha (Oecomys concolor)
