Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel (1770 nî 8 goe̍h 27 ji̍t – 1831 nî 11 goe̍h 14 ji̍t) sī Tek-kok ê tiat-ha̍k-ka, i tī Stuttgart chhut-sì, tùi lô-chip, hêng-siōng-ha̍k, jīn-sek-lūn, chèng-tī-ha̍k, le̍k-sú tiat-ha̍k, bí-ha̍k kap chong-kàu téng-téng lóng-ū gián-kiù, éng-hióng chin-chōe āu-sì ê ha̍k-chiá, chhin-chhiūⁿ Bertrand Russell, Karl Marx kap Friedrich Nietzsche téng-téng.
