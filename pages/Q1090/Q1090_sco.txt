Siller (seembol Ag) is a saft metal that haes been uised for mony thoosands o year bi fowk aw athort the warld, for jewellery, as money, an mony ither things forby. It is cried a white metal e'en tho it leuks gray. The wird siller is uised tae talk aboot this colour o gray.
In chemistry, siller is chemical element 47, a transition metal in Group 11. It haes an atomic wecht o 107.86 a.m.u. Its seembol is Ag, frae the Laitin wird for siller, argentum.
