Sergei Mikhailovich Eisenstein (Roushie: Сергей Михайлович Эйзенштейн; 23 Januar 1898 – 11 Februar 1948) wis a Soviet Roushie film director an film theorist, a pioneer in the theory an practice o montage. He is notit in pairticular for his silent films Strike (1924), Battleship Potemkin (1925) an October (1927), as well as the historical epics Alexander Nevsky (1938) an Ivan the Terrible (1944, 1958).
