Arctic Ocean ,North Pole ke chaaro taraf hae. Iske kinare Eurasia aur North America ke north ke hissa hae. Ii ocean jaada kar ke ice aur snow cover kare hae.
Ii ocean ke area, 14.056 million sq km hae aur isme 45,389 km ke coastline hae. Ii sab ocean me se sab se chhota hae. Hian pe kuch kanwar hae:  walruses, whales and polar bears.
