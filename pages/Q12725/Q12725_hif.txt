Electricity, electric current ke maujuudi (presence) aur bahao (flow) hae. Electricity se insaan dher kaam kare sake hae. Iske sab se jaana ruup hae, conductors, jaise ki copper wire me [[electron]s ke bahao.
EK chij jon ki electricity ke bahe ke ijaajat de hae ke conductor bolaa jaae hae, jaise ki copper aur duusra metal. Plastic, electricity ke bahe nai de hae aur iske insulator bola jaae hae.
Electrical energy praakritik (natural) hoe sake hae, jaise ki bijlii (lightning), nai to iske insaan banae sake hae, jaise ki generator. Jab electrical charge nai bahe hae tab iske "static electricity" bola jaae hae, aur jab charge bahe hae tab isme electric current bola jaae hae.
Electricity khatarnaat hoe sake hae, khaas kar ke paani me, kaaheki paani me mailaa aur nimak electricity conduct kare hae.
Electricity ke banaawa jaae sake hae jab ek magnet ke metal wire ke nigiich jaae hae. Ii rakam se generator chale hae. Electricity ke ek liquid solution me dui different metal ke duboe ke banaawa jaae sake hae. Static electricity ke dui chij ke ek duusre se ragre se banaawa jaae sake hae, jaise wool aur plastic ruler. Electrical energy ke suraj ke shakti se bhi banaawa jaae sake hae.
