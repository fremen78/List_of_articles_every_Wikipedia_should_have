Isinqandagciwane siwuhlobo lwesiqa esithiya amagciwane (antimicrobial) afana nezinungwana. Siwuhlobo lwezinqandazinungwana ezibalulekile ekulwisaneni nokuhogeleka kwezigulo zezinungwana, futhi imishanguzo yezinqandagciwane isetshenziswa kabanzi ekwelashweni nasekuthiyeni lezo zigulo. Izinqandagciwane zingakwenza kokubili ukujuqa noma ukujiyeza izinungwana. Ezinye izinqandagciwane ezimbalwa zinobuqhebeqhebe bokunqandazinanjazana. Izinqandagciwane ngeshwa azinawo amandla wokulwa namagciwanyane afana nalawo abanga umkhuhlane noma ubhadane; imishanguzo ekwazi ukujiyeza ukukhula kwamagxiwanyane yna ibizwa ngokuthi imishanguzo enganqanda ubhadane. Futhi asinamandla ngokumelene nokhunta; imishanguzo ejiyeza ukukhula kokhunta ibizwa izinqandakhunta.
