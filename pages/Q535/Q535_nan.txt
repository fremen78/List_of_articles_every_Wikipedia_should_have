Victor-Marie Hugo (IPA: /vik.'tɔʁ ma.'ʁi y.'go/, 1802 nî 2 goe̍h 26 ji̍t – 1885 nî 5 goe̍h 22 ji̍t) sī Hoat-kok lô-bān-chú-gī si-jîn, siáu-soat-ka, ke̍k-chok-ka, té-bûn (essay) chok-ka, iū-koh sī sī-kak gē-su̍t-ka, chèng-tī-ka, jîn-koân ūn-tōng-chiá. Pháiⁿ-miā-lâng (Les Misérables) sī Hugo siāng chhut-miâ ê siáu-soat.
