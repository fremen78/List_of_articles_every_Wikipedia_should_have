Polytheism me dher bhagwaan me biswas karaa jaawe hae. Ek jan, jon polytheism me biswas kare hae ke polytheist bolaa jaawe hae. Ek dharam jisme polytheism me biswas karaa jaawe hae ke polytheistic dharam bolaa jaawe hae. 
Jaada kae ke, polytheistic dharam me dher rakam ke bhagwan kebaar me khissa rahe hae. Ii rakam ke sab se jaana maana polytheistic dharam Greek mythology me paawa jaawe hae.
Duusra log jon ki polytheists rahin me Celts aur Norse rahin.
Kuchh log, galtii se ii soche hae ki Hindi dharam ek polytheistic dharam hae.
