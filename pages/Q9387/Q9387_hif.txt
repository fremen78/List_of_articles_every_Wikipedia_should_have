Maximilian Carl Emil Weber (pronounced: maks ˈveːbɐ) (21 April 1864 – 14 June 1920) ek German political economist aur sociologist rahaa, jiske modern study of sociology and public administration ke founder maana jaawe hae. Uu aapan kaam University of Berlin me suruu karis, aur baad me Freiburg University, University of Heidelberg, University of Vienna and University of Munich me kaam karis. Uske kaafiasar German politics ke uppar rahaa, kahe ki uu Germany ke Treaty of Versailles ke treaty ke advisor rahaa.
