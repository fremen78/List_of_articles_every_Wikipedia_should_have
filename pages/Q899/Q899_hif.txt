Suez Canal (Arbii: قناة السويس, Qanā al-Suways, French: Le Canal de Suez), Egypt me ek canal hae. Ii Sinai Peninsula ke west me hae. Ii canal 163 km lamba (101 miles) aur ii aapan sab se patra jagah pe, 300 m Chaurraa (984 ft) hae. Ii Port Said (Būr Sa'īd) , Mediterranean Sea pe se Suez (al-Suways), Red Sea pe jaawe hae. Iske ek French company banais rahaa jon ki iske 1859 me banae ke suruu kar ke 1869 me khalaas karis rahaa.
