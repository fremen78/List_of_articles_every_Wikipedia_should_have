Hroald Engelbregt Gravning Amundsen (16. dæg Mædmonðes 1872 – c. 18. dæg Searmonðes 1928) was aspyrgend on Normannum se for wide geond gefrorenum landum in middangeares norþernmestum dælum and his suðernmenstum. He wæs micel mann þære ylde seo het 'Þære Beorna Yld of Antarctiscum Aspyrgunge'.
In Borge in Eastfelde, Norþweges lande, wæs Hroald geboren. Fram 1903 oþ 1906 læd he se forma fare se geondhweorfede þone Norþwestgang, in Gjøa his yþlidan. In 1910 beebbode he Norwege on Fram, Friþiofes Nansenes scipe and seglode to Antarctican. Hroalde mid his fifmanna werod aræccede ðone Sūþende þǣre eaxe on 14. dæg Geolan 1911 and hie wæron þa ærrestan menn þa efnedon swylce dæd.
On on 12. dæg Þrimilcemonðes 1926 flogen Hroald Amundsen and fiftiene menn in Norge þæm lyftscipe ofer ðæm Norþende þære eaxe. He Cwanc in Searmonðe 1928 ofer þæm norðernan is.
