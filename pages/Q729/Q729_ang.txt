Deor is great heap wihta þa sind manigcleofeda and cyrnelcleofeda. Hie sculon etan oðra wihta oþþe wihta wæstmas to libbenne, þe wyrta þe deor. Mæst deora mæg self gangan, þeah þe sumu magon ane þæt don on dæle heora lifes.


== Niwu word ==

cyrnelcleofed (aj) - eukaryotic
manigcleofed (aj) - multicellular
