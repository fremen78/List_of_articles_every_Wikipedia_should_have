Sundarta (Beauty) ek sabd hae jiske kaam me laae ke koi chij ke describe karaa jawe hae. Ek chij ke sundar bola jaawe hae jab ki uu dekhe me achchhaa lage hae. 
Sundarta konchi hae ke baare me dher bichaar hae. Kuchh log bole hae ki sundarta hae:

Ek asli chij aur kalaa (art) ke biich ke antar. Jaise ki, ek sundar chhaapa uu hoe sake jon ki dekhe me sajiu (lifelike) lage hae.
Koi bhi chij aur aap ke jiske sochtaa hae ki kaise dekhae ke chaahi, ke biich ke antar. Jaise ki, ek sundar perr ke brown trunk aur haraa patti hoe sake hae aur sundar baadal ujjar rahe hae.
Jon rakam se koi chij am log ke feel kare: khusi, udaas, gussa, nai to aur kuchh. Jaise ki ek sundar sangeet koi ke khus nai to dukhi kare hae.
Koi chij ke gun (quality). Jaise ki, ek sundar khissa achchhaa se likha rahaa hoi.
Ek lekh (writing) sundar hoe sake hae jab uske gahiraai parrhe waala ke dimaagi chaapa chhaapa nai to emotion de hae. Kavita ke sundar maana jaae hae jab usse mental chhaapaa ke dekha jaae hae nai to bhaawana waala (emotional) response de hae.
