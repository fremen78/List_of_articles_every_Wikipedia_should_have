Ityubya (Tamharit: ኢትዮጵያ, Tafaṛt: Itiyoophiyaa, Tigizt: ኢትዮጵያ, Tuṛumut: Itiyophiyaa, Taṣumalit: Itoobiya, Tigrinyat: ኢትዮጵያ) tga yat tmazirt ɣ Yisk n Tfriqt. Tga yat sɣ tmizar mi akkʷ itwassan umzruy ɣ Tfriqt d umaḍal. Tga yat zɣ mnnawt drusnin nna iffrtln i umntaw f tfriqt. Tanf i usdurri aṭalyan ɣ 1935 lliɣ urmn ad fllas akʷin ddaw tnbaḍt n Biniṭu Musulini. Tkka tt inn Ityubya tettyussan s Abisinya.
