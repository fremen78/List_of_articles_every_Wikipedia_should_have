Ethiopia, wey ofishali bi di Federal Demokratik Repoblik of Ethiopia, bi a kontri wey dey for di Hon of Afrika for di rejion of Eas Afrika. E dey bodad bai Eritrea for di nort, Djibouti for di norteas, Somalia for di eas and sauteas, Kenya for di saut, Saut Sudan for di wes, and Sudan for di nortwes. Ethiopia kova a land erea of 1,112,000 sque kilometas (472,000 sq. miles). As at year 2023, e bi hom to araund 128 million inhabitants, mekin am di 13th-mos populous kontri for di wold, di 2nd-mos populous kontri for Afrika afta Naijeria, and di mos populaeted landlokd kontri for Earth. The national kapital and lajes citi, Addis Ababa, dey several kilometas wes of di Eas Afrikan Rift wey dey split di kontri into di Afrikan and Somali tetonik plaets.
