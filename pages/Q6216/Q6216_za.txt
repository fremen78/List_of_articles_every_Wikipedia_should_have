Swyouzcujyi dwg itcungj yisihingzdaih caeuq cezyoz, annaeuz swyouz dwg daej haedsim biengz. Gangj engq gvangj, Swyouzcujyi yauhqiuz sevei baujcienz boux vunz swhsiengj swyouz, yawh faplwd hanhcih cengfuj sawjyung gienzleih, bauj boux boux swyouz guh gaicawx, cihciz seivunz gunghswh loengh hawciengz ginghci, haengj gunghminz yiengjmiz cwngci tijci ronghcingx.
Swyouzcujyi fanjdui haujlai gij cwngci cidu siezcaeux, beijbaenz youz sien buet hawj vuengzdaeq gienzleih gij leixlun, seiqsiz cidu, caeuq gozgauq cidu. Cujcangh vunzgienz gihbwnj Swyouzcujyi miz gienz sengmingh, gienz swyouz caeuq gienz caizcanj. Swyouzcujyi youq seizdaih buqmoengz haijhij, daengj seizneix bauh halo haujlai cungj mboujdoengz eiqsei.
