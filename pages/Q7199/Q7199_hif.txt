Valentin Louis Georges Eugène Marcel Proust (French pronounced: maʁsɛl pʁust) (10 July 1871 – 18 November 1922) ek French novelist, essayist aur critic rahaa, aur uske sab se jaada  À la recherche du temps perdu (in English, In Search of Lost Time, jiske 1913 aur 1927 ke biich me likha gais rahaa, ke khatir jana jaawe hae.
