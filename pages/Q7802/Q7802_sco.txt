Breid is a staple fuid makkit by bakin a daich o floor an watter. Hit can be leavent or unleavent. Saut, saim an a leavenin augent sic as baurm ais common ingredients, tho breids can conteen a scowth o ither ingredients: milk, egg, succar, spice, fruit (sic as raisins), vegetables (sic as ingan), nits (sic as wawnits) or seeds (sic as puppy seeds).
