O manrho si ek bakro manrho kai si phabardo, vai rhuzhina, shudini, ai univar pravarde les. But xabe si de dumult kai sas mai anglal kerdilo but fialuria dieli la lumiake. O pervo manrho sas kerdo andel khash kai busholas Neolithic, pashte 12.000 bersh de dumult. Mai anglal sar te khash jiv amime le paiesa, kadia de baro manrho shordilo po bax kai sas shudino ai pharhadia le iagasa katar le dand. Le Egyptianuria dikhle ke mekle te barion jiv, ai kadia de baro jiv kerdias iek vediara, kai bariardia o manrho andek than ai vi won bariarenas e iag, phagle o jiv.
