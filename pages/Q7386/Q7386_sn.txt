Svosve (nge: ant, nde: ubunyonyo, swa: sisimizi) kana shoshwe kapukanana kane makumbo matanhatu.
Usonzi (general name for small black and red ants).


== Mhando dzeMasvosve ==
Chikodza kana chigaguga (Very small, black, stinging ant).
Svosve Mukange.


== Tsumo ==
Sonzi rakatuma nzou. An ant sent the elephant
Svosve rakatuma zhou. An ant sent an elephant (on an errand).


== Mitauro ye Chibantu ==
Bemba neLomwe vanoti nyelele (n. ants) kureva svosve.
MaXhosa vanoti imbovhane (n. ant) kureva svosve.
