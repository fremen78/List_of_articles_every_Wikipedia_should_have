Sorghum, jiske vigyanik naam (Scientific name) Sorghum bicolor hae, ek rakam ke grass ke paudha hae jiske uske anaaj khaatir, jaanwar ke khaana aur ethanol banae ke khaatir kaam me laawa jaae hae. Sorghum Africa se aais hae lekin iske aaj kal dunia bhar ke tropical aur subtropical des me boyaa jaae hae. Sorghum, chaaur, gehuun, makai aur barley ke baad 5th sab se jaada boe waala anaaj hae (59.34 million metric tons, 2018 me). 
Sorghum ke  har saal boe ke parree hae lekin kuchh species dher saal talak jinda rahe hae. Ii guchchhaa (clumps) me me hoe hae aur 4 metres (13 feet) uuncha hoe sake hae. Iske anaaj chhotaa rahe hae.
