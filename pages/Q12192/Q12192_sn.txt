Chibayo (kureva pneumonia; Chishongrish: nyumoniya) chirwere chinovhutitsa chemapapu chinobata pakazhinji maseki emweya madiki anonzi arivheyoli. Masimputomu anowanzo sanganisa chikosoro chine dzihwa kana chisina dzihwa, marwadzo echipfuva, fivha, uye koneko yekufema. Kugocha kwe chirwere ichi kwakasiyana-siyana.
