Tamnekda n imunguliyen d tamnekda tessnulfat-id s Ǧengis Xan deg 1206, deg Asya talemmast, ɣer tagara n lqern wis 13, teẓẓel seg Agaraw Amelwi ɣef usamar ar Abagu Afarsi ed wasif n Danub ɣef umalu. Asmi teǧhed maḍi, tamnekda n imunguliyen teṭṭef-d ahat n 23 n yimelyan km2, i d tamnekda meqqren akk deg umaḍal (akked yiwet tajumma n timnaḍin inaragen (lǧiran) mačči tadukli n timnaḍin ttwabḍant mwezzaɛen deg umaḍal).
