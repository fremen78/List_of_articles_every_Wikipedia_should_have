I Galileo Galilei (15 February 1564 – 8 January 1642) metung yang Tuscan (Italianung) physicist, matematiku, astronomer, ampong philosopher a maki maragul a papil king rebolusiung sientipiku. Kayabe  kareng kayang agawa ing pamaniamasan king teloskopiu ampo reng obserbasiun king astronomiang menibat kaniti, ampo ing suporta para king Copernicanism. Inaus deng father of modern observational astronomy ("ibpa ning makabayung observational astronomy") i Galileo"father of modern physics" (ibpa ning makabayung physics")"father of science" (ibpa ning siensia) ampong “the Father of Modern Science” (Ibpa ning Makabayung Siensia)  Ing pangimut da reng bageng para-pareu ing pamagpabilis (motion of uniformly accelerated objects), a mituturu kareng kursu king physics neng sekundaria ampo neng umpisa ning kolehiu, penigaralan neng Galileo antimo ing paksa ning kinematics. Kayabe ya kareng kayang ambag observational astronomy ing pamanuklas kareng apat a pekamaragul a bulan o satellite ning Jupiter, a mipalaguan w:Galilean moons anting pamangalang kaya, ampo ing pamagumasid ampong pamaniuri kareng w:sunspot. Menigobra ya murin i Galileo king siensiang miapse (applied science) ampong teknolohia, at semasan ne diseniu ing paraluman (compass).
