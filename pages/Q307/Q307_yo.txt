Galileo Galilei (Àdàkọ:IPA-it; 15 February 1564 – 8 January 1642) je ara Italia onimo fisiiki, mathematiiki, olutorawo, ati onimo oye to ko ipa pataki ninu Ijidide Sayensi. Die ninu awon aseyori re ni atunse si teliskopu ati awon akiyesi irawo to se, ati itileyin ise Copernicus. Won ti pe Galileo ni "baba itorawo oniakiyesi odeoni," "baba fisiiki odeoni," the "baba sayensi," ati "Baba Sayensi Odeoni." Stephen Hawking so pe, "Galileo, ju elomiran, lo sise fun ibi sayensi odeoni."
