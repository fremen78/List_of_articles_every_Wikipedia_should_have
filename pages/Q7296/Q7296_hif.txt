Kilimanjaro, nai to Kilima Njaro, (jiske matlab Swahili me "shining mountain" hae), aur jiske pahile Kaiser-Wilhelm-Spitze ke naam se jaana jaawat rahaa, northeastern Tanzania me ek pahaarr hae. Kilimanjaro, dunia ke sab se uuncha free-standing pahaarr hae, jon ki 4600 meters (15,000 ft) uuncha hae.
