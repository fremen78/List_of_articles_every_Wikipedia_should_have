Phiri la Kilimanjaro ni phiri la volcano ilo lili mu chigaŵa cha Kilimanjaro ku Tanzania. Lili na makuni ghatatu gha volkano: Kibo, Mawenzi, na Shira. Ni phiri likuru chomene mu Africa na lapachanya chomene pa charu chose chapasi ilo lili pa mtunda wa mamita 5,895 na mamita 4,900 kufuma pa malo agho lili. Ni phiri litali comene kuluska mapiri ghose gha mu Africa na Eastern Hemisphere.
Phiri la Kilimanjaro ndilo lili pachanya pa mapiri ghose pa charu chapasi. Malo agha ghali ku chigaŵa cha Kilimanjaro National Park ndipo ŵanthu ŵanandi ŵakukwerapo. Chifukwa chakuti mu 2025 m'paka 2035 chiwuvi ichi chizamumara, ŵasayansi ŵanandi ŵakuchita kafukufuku.
