Di Kilimandscharo (faan 1902 bit 1964 Keiser-Wilhelm-Toop, ingelsk Kilimanjaro) as mä 5.895 m det huuchst beragkeed uun Afrikoo. Flooksis men wi mä Kilimandscharo oober di huuchst toop, hi het Kibo of Uhuru Peak.
Trinjam a Kilimandscharo leit di Kilimandscharo Natsionaalpark.
