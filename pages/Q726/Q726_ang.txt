Hors oþþe Equus caballus is miċel gærsƿyrt-ǣt dēor, and ān seofona niƿa ȝecynda "Equus". Hors ƿeorðaþ ȝebrȳcedu tō farenne, and tō spannenne ānfornȝēan crætƿǣne.
And hors hæfþ āne tān on ǣlċum fēt, and hit is sēo þridde tā. Ac mæniȝe his ealdena foreȝengena sƿā Mesohippus and Merychippus hǣfdon þrēo tān on ǣlċum fēt. And seldcūðe ƿearþ ācenned fola mid mārum þonne ānre tān on fēt. And in þǣre Eōcēniscan tīde lifdon his ƿīdcūðe foreȝengan Eohippus þe ēac hātte Hyracotherium, and his ǣrran foreȝengan þā Condylarthra þe hæfdon fīf tān on ǣlċum fēt, and lȳtel hōf on ǣlcre tān, and hit rann þurh ealde eormenƿealdas ǣr þǣm þe ƿæs ǣniȝ græs on eorþan.
