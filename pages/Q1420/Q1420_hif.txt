Ek motar ek vehicle jisme passenger ke dhowa jaae hae. Motar me chaar pahiya aur ek ingin rahe hae.
France ke Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot pahile bhaap se chale waala gaarri ke 1769 me banais rahaa. Switzerland ke Francois Isaac de Rivaz pahila internal combustion se chalaawa gais gaarri ke 1808 me banais rahaa. Germany ke carl Benz]] pahils motar ke 1886 me banais rahaa. 1908 me jab Ford motaor company, Ford Model T ke banais tab sadhaaran log motar kharide sakat rahin. Ii kaaran se America me baggi ke jagha motar ke kaam me laae jaae lagaa.
