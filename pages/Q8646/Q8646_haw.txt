ʻO Hong Kong (Kina: 香港), kahi ma Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China (HKSAR), kahi ʻona nui a me kahi ʻāpana hoʻokele kūikawā o ka People's Republic of China ma ka hikina Delta ʻo ka muliwai Delta o ke kai Kina Hema. . Aia ma kahi o 7.5 miliona mau kamaʻāina o nā lāhui like ʻole [d] ma ka palena he 1,104-square-mile (426 sq mi), ʻo Hong Kong kekahi o nā wahi i noho nui ʻia ma ka honua.
