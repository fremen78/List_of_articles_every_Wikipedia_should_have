Lingwistikis i save i lukluk long tokples long manmeri. Nem lingwistikis ikam long Tok Inglis linguistics, i min save long tokples (lingua-).


== Dichotomies na tokples ==
Save long tokples em i gat tripela axis. 

Synchronic vs Diachronic: Synchronic em i save i lukluk tokples long wanpela taim; Diachronic em i save i lukluk tokples long longpela histori.
Theoretical vs Applied: Theoretical em i save i lukluk tokples long describing wan wan tokples na theory long yunivesal aspekt long tokples; Applied em i save long applying theory long narapela save.
Contextual vs Autonomous: Contextual em i save i lukluk olsem wanem tokples interact long graum; Autonomous em i save i lukluk tokples long em yet.


== Theoretical lingwistikis ==
Ponetikit: save long ol saun bilong tokples (olsem a, e, p...)
Ponoloji: save long dispela sauni kamapim wanem insait long het na insait long maus (olsem f i senis inap i kamap p),
Mopoloji: save long senis bilong ol vokabelari, o liklik tok (olsem kain i senis inap i kamap kainkain),
Sintaktik: save long vokabelari i bung inap i kamapim sentens,
Semantiks: save long mining bilong tok (wod),
Pramatiks: save long pasin bilong yusim tok namel long narapela manmeri i save wankain tok.


== Diachronic lingwistikis ==
Bikpela interes bilong theoretical lingwistikis em i wantaim i lukluk tokples long wanpela taim. Diachronic lingwistikis em i lukluk hau sampela tokples i kamap difren long bipo. Historikal lingwistikis i wanpela kain long diachronic lingwistikis em i lukluk histori bilong wanpela o mo tokples.
Save long lingwistikis i bin statim wantaim diachronic lingwistikis, tasol nau planti yunivesiti long Amerika tisim mo theoretical lingwistikis. Bihain Ferdinand de Saussure na Noam Chomsky, ol manmeri bilong lingwistikis i bin dilim theoretical mo long diachronic.
