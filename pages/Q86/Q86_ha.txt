Ciwon kai (Turanci: headache) wani ciwone da mutane da yawa suke yin fama dashi, wanda be shafi babba ko yaroba, Aa ya hada kowa da kowa, gajiya,yinwa, rashin isashshen bacci, rashin lafiya kaman , mura me zafi ,maleria, da ciwuka da yawa sukan iya jawo ciwan kai. Wato shi ciwan kai alamace na rashin wani abu ajikin dan Adam,kuma kowa da kalar nawa.
