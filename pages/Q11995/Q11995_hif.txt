Peet me baby, jiske janwar gaabhin bola jaawe hai, uu time hai jab ki aurat ke bhittar baby develop hoe hai. Jab dui baby ek saathe paida hoe hai tab baby log ke jurrwa bola jaawe hai.  Baby, lagbhag 40  haptaa ke baad paida hoe hai. Peet me baby ke suruu ke sign period ke miss karnaa, chhaant, naram chuuchi, jaada bhuuk aur  jaada mutti rahe hai.
