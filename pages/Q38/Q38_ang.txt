Italia (Italisc: Italia [iˈtaːlja] ( listen) oþþe on Italisc: Repubblica Italiana [reˈpubblika itaˈljaːna]) is cynewise on westernum Europan, and seo hæfð landgemære be Franclande, be Swisslande, be Eastrice, be Slofenie, be Halgan Marinus, and be Faticanbyrig. His heafodburh is Romburg. Eotolware sprecaþ Italisc.
Italia hæfþ manig iegland, þara Sicilia is þæt mæste and Sardinia þæt oðer.
