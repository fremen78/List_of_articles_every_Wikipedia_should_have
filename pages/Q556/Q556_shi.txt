Ahidrujin (s tinglizt : Hydrogen , s tfranṣiṣt : Hydrogène) iga t ufrḍis akimi lli mu gan tasdugmt n H d uṭṭun afrḍas 1. Ntta ad kullu ifssusn ɣ umaḍal. Ɣ tifadiwin tinawayn, ar ittgga uhidrujin agaz n snat tifulin d ar ttggan H2. Iga war-ikʷlan d war-aḍu, ur igi toxic, d ar ijddr s zzrb.
Ahidrujin ad iggutn ɣ umaḍal, slahwant akkʷ lliɣ iɣʷi 75% n tmtta (matter). Itran zun d tafukt nnɣ ur gan-t amr Ahidrujin ɣ waddad n plasma. Agudi n hidrujin ɣ wakal ar ittmun d ifrḍisn yaḍni ar skarn tifulin zun d  aman nɣ d uddis agman.
