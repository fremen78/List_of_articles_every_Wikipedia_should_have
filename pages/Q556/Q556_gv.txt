Ta hiddragien, ny heedrageen, ny vunstoo kemmigagh lesh yn earroo breneenagh 1. She H y cowrey kemmigagh echey. Ta'n hiddragien ny vunstoo s'eddrymey as smoo lhieenar 'sy Tuinney as eh jannoo seose 75% jeh'n chruinnaghey echey. Ta rollageyn 'sy phreeu-straih jeant seose jeh hiddragien son y chooid smoo as eh 'sy phaase plasmey. Cha nel mooarane dy hiddragien najooragh ry-gheddyn er y Chruinney.
