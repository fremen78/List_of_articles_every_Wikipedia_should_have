Musa (s taεibrit: מֹשֶׁה Moce Mošé, s teεrabt: موسى, Mūsā), iga yan ɣ mmis niɣd nbi yan ɣ immisn, yackat tigira ns Σisa d Muḥmmad, yawid yan udllis lli mmu nttini tawrat. Inna mast id izzukz Rbbi f uɣrf n ait israyil. Tga tadyant ns yat ɣ tidyanin tiqburin lli mmu nttini Tudayt. Imtfrn n Musa llan ɣ kraygat tasga n umaḍal. Mac yan ugzzum gitsn ira ad yurri s tmazirt tamzwarut n Musa lli igan Israil d Filisṭin.


== Izdayn n brra ==

(fr) Musa tabbayt n il d udlis wi-ssin
(fr) Musa d udllis ns wi-ssin d usmmaql f umzruy gr Nil dl Urdun yurat André Lemaire.
