IOstreliya lilizwe eliseOceania. Inabantu abangaphezu kwe-26,000,000 ukusukela ngo-2021. Ikomkhulu laseOstreliya yiKhanberra kwaye esona sixeko sikhulu yiSidni.


== IJografi ==
IOstreliya inemimandla esibhozo; UMzantsi Omtsha Wales (New South Wales), Viktoria (Victoria), Kwinsland (Queensland), KwiNtshona yeOstreliya (Western Australia), UMzantsi Ostreliya (South Australia), Ummandla Wekomkhulu laseOstreliya (Australian Capital Territory), Tasmaniya kwaye Ummandla osemantla (Northern Territory).


== Izilwanyana ==
I-Ostreliya ineentlobo ezininzi zezilwanyana zasendle, kuquka iikhangaru, iikoala, iiemu kunye nezinye ezahlukeneyo.
