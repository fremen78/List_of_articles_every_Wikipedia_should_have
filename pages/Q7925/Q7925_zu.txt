Imvula ingamaconsi wamanzi ajiyile asuka kumhwamuko osemkhathini abese ewela phansi ngokuna . Imvula iyingxenye enkulu yomjikelezo wamanzi futhi iyona eletha amanzi amaningi ahlanzekile eMhlabeni. Ihlinzeka ngamanzi imikhando yamazubomjilimba, ukuchelela ngezitshalo, kanye nezimo ezifanele zezinhlobo eziningi zxhaxhaemvelo .
