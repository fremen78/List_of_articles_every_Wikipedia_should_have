Rain (Weet, or Onfaw) is a kynd o precipitation, ither o whilk includes snaw, sleet, hail, an dyow. Weet is makkit whan sinder dribs o watter faw tae the yird's surface fae cloods. No aw rain launds on the grund; some evaporates whan onfawin throu the drouthie lift. Whan nane o hit launds on the grund, hit is cried virga.
