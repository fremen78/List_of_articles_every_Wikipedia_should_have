Gold is gesceaft, þǣre tācen is Au (sē is fram þǣm Lǣden ƿorde aurum) and þǣre motige rīm is 79. Goldes Lǣden nama mǣnþ "scīnende dagung". 
Hit hæfþ geƿorden sƿīðe gesōht tō andƿeorce missenlicra þinga, tō bȳsne gierelan, peningas, and ōðre cræftas siþþin þǣre onginnunge geƿritenes stǣres. Gold is gefunden on clymprum oþþe cyrnelum on stānum, on līnum and on ƿætere gesetedum ālecgungum. Gold is þicce, sōft, scīnende and se gelǣdenlicosta and gelēddosta smǣtosta blōma gecūþ mannum. Smǣte gold hafaþ bēorht geolu bleoh and lēoman geƿunelīce gesmēad lustbǣr ƿesan, þe hit hielt lēas ōmunge fram lyfte oþþe ƿætere. 
Gold is ān þāra peninga blōman and hafaþ geƿorden gebrocen tō tācne sceattes and hord ƿeorðes geond stǣr. Golda staðolas habbaþ staðolod staðol sceattes gesetednessa. Hit hæfþ ēac geƿorden gebunden mid missenlicum tācnungum and gelēafum.
