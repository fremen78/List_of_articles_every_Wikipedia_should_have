Gowd is a saft, hivy, daurk yellae, glazie metal that haes been uised fur mony thoosands o years bi fowk aw ower the warld, for jewelry, as money, an nou in tuith fillins an electronic gear sic as computers. It wis parteecular respectit as jewelry in early times acause o its great luster an brawness. In Exodus it wis uised as pairt o the claes the priests wuir. The colour o this metal is cawed gowd an aw.
