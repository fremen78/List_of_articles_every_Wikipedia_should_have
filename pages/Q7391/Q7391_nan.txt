Phang sī Apoidea chhiau-kho (superfamily) ê khun-thiông, ē-hiáu poe; kap hó͘-thâu-phang (wasp) ham káu-hiā koan-hē chin sio-oá, lóng kui tī Hymenoptera ba̍k. Lâm-ke̍k Tāi-lio̍k lia̍h-goā, sì-kè lóng ū phang, iok-liōng ū 20,000 ê chéng.
Phang ū-sî kan-taⁿ chí bi̍t-phang. Ū-sî Hymenoptera ba̍k lāi-té ē poe--ê lóng hông chún sī phang.
