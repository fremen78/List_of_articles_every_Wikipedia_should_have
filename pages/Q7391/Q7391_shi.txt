Tazzwit  nɣ Tizzwit nɣ Tazizwit nɣ Tizizwit  (s Tsrɣint : نحلة) tga yan wanaw n ibxxucn ittaylaln, tettyawssan tzzwit s tikki n tammnt igan asafar i kigan n tmuḍan n ufgan.


== Asnmarra ==
Amyiwn : Tazzwit
Asgt : Tizzwa


== Gr tamawt ==
G kra n tmnaḍin, illa ma ittinin tazzwit nɣ tizzwit i tazdwit nɣ tadggat (s unamk n yan uzmz g wass g ad tṭṭaṛ tafukt, tlla tifawt tili tazzwit).


== Tizzwa d ufgan ==
Llant tizzwa g uflla n wakal ḍaṛt 45 000 000 n usggas. Zund ukan afgan, ula tizzwa qqdimnt g uflla n wakal, mayad a iwdjan afgan a ifaqq mas tizzwa gant yan uɣbalu n umttcu d usafar, mayad af iswingm ufgan g a izzdɣ didas tizzwa.
