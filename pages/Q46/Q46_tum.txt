Europe ni chigaŵa icho chili kumanjiliro gha dazi kwa Eurasia, icho chili mu Northern Hemisphere na Eastern Hemisphere. Charu ichi chili pa chigaŵa cha Afilika na Asia. Kumpoto kuli nyanja ya Arctic Ocean, kumanjiliro gha dazi kuli nyanja ya Atlantic Ocean, kumwera kuli nyanja ya Mediterranean Sea, ndipo kumafumiro gha dazi kuli Asia. Charu cha Europe chikupambana na vyaru vya Asia chifukwa cha mapiri gha Ural, Mlonga wa Ural, Nyanja ya Caspian, Great Caucasus, Nyanja Yiswesi na maji gha ku Turkey.
