E    Eropa (Europa) si yek mashkar le 7 Barodvipenge la Phuvyake, kay, andi kadaya situatsiya si buteder yek sanskrtipnaski tay forovipnaski rikyaripen sar yek phuvipnaski. Phuvikanes, Eropa si yek Subbarodvip vay yek bari barodvip, o may oksidentutno kotor la Erasiyako.
O baripen la Eropyako si 10 390 000 km2 tay o gin le manushenge si 741,447,158 (2016), vay opretele 11% katar le manushenge la sundalake.
