Lagos nyɛla tingban'karili m-be Nigeria. Niŋsali bɛlim ban bɛ tiŋ maa ni yiɣisi ka mani 22,829,561.lagos nye tiŋ shali din pahi mali ninsali bɛlim ban galisi can be duniya. Di lan nyɛla Nigeria tiŋ karili, bee tiŋ titali din viɛlim yiri ko ka chɛ tiŋ shaŋa ŋɔ pahi pahi.Din Ninsali bɛlim daa kuli pahirimi wawayo, yuun-kɔbiga saha (20th centuries).
Achiika, di tiŋzugu yɛligiya hali ti gbai tiŋgbani din be teeku nyaaga (Gulf of Guinea inland) ka mani 1,117 square kilometers yɛlim. Pɛrila tiŋ maa galisim maa Ninsali bɛlim ban bɛ dipuni malla been ʒeni di yaŋa shaŋa ka di galisim kuli mɛriti  17,000 inhabitants per square kilometer.Hali wayo,Bɛnshagu Na bi yɛna ti wuhiri Nigeria daadam-bɛligu kalili ni yɛɣisi sham. Din mi sham nyɛla di tiŋgban shali din galisi gban sab-la tiŋgbana yaɣili
