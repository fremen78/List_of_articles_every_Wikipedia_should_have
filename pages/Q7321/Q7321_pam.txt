I Jacques Cartier (1491–September 1 1557), bindian ne ing lugal a ausan tamu ngening Canada para king Francia. Ya ing mumunang miglarawan ampong ginawang mapa ning Gulf of Saint Lawrence ampo kareng pampang ning Ilug Saint Lawrence, a pepalaguan nang "The Country of Canada(s)", kayabe no reng aduang mangaragul a balayán (settlement) a Iroquoian king Stadaconna (Quebec City) ampo king Hochelaga (Montreal Island).
