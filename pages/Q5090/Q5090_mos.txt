Mui yaa tɩɩs buud a ye, sẽn boond t'a Oryza sativa, bɩ O. glaberrima. B wae n boond-a lame t'a Zizania la a Porteresia buud fãa, baa ne b sẽn tõe n bool-a me tɩ Oryza buud a taab sẽn pa tar koodã.
A yaa kood sẽn yaa zɛɛg n yaa rɩɩb ning sẽn yaa kãseng n yɩɩd dũni wã neb sʋka, sẽn yɩɩd fãa Azi la Afirik. A yaa kood ning sẽn tar kood a tãab-n-soab n yɩɩd dũniyã gill zugu, sẽn pʋgd sũ-kẽeng la koodã. [1] Sẽn mik tɩ b rɩkda sũ-kẽeng la koodã wʋsg n maand bũmb a taab sẽn pa ninsaal rɩkr yĩnga, rãs yaa rɩɩb ning sẽn tar yõod n yɩɩdã ninsaal rɩkr la a sẽn dɩt kalori wã wɛɛngẽ, n kõt kalori wã koabg zug a ye neb sẽn rɩt dũniyã gill zugã. Rɩs buud toor-toor n be, tɩ rɩɩb ning b sẽn nong n dɩt pa yembr ye.
