Chaaur ek rakam ke khaana hae. Iske jaada kar ke garam des me bowa jaawe hae, jaise ki Asia, Africa, northern Italy, aur North America ke west coast.
Ii ek rakam ke anaaj hae, aur jaada kar ke iske daldal jagha me boyaa jaae hae.
Asia me khaae waala [[khaana] ke 80% calories chaaur se aae hae aur dunia bhar me ii 20% hae.
