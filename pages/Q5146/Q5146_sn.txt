Chiputukezi (kureva Portuguese) mutauro unotaurwa kuPutugaro. Chiputukezi Mutauro weKudanana seChifurenji, Chispeini, uye Chiratini, Chiitari ne Chirhomania. Pamusoro pezvo Chiputukezi chinotaurwa mudzimwe nyika dzeAfurika seMazambuko.
Chiputukezi pamwechete nemimwe mitauro, unoshandiswa kugadzira mashoko matsva eChishona, kune mashoko mazhinji anozivikanwa se eChishona.

== Nharaunda dzinoshandisa Chiputukezi ==

== Mashoko echishona akabva kuChiputukezi ==

Mamwe mahwi akabva kuChiputukezi anoshandiswa se Mazita eChishona:

Dhinyero (dinheiro) - gold money
Rupiya (rupia) - twenty cent piece
Tizora (tesoura) - scissors
Sinyoro (senhor) - Lord/Sir
