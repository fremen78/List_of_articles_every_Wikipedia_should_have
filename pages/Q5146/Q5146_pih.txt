Porchuguees (português or língua portuguesa [ˈɫĩgwɐ puɾtuˈɣezɐ]) esa Romance laenghwij, an es t' sole ofishol laenghwij a' Porchugal, Bresel, Mozambique, Angola, Cape Verde, Gini-Bisau, Sau Toem an Prinsiip, an tuu has co-ofishol laenghwij stetus in Macau, Shiina, Ekwatoryal Gini an in Eist Tiimor, Sowtheist Asya.
