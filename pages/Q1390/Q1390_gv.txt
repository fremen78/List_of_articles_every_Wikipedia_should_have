She y rang Insecta bun-chooish y duillag shoh. My vel anaase ayd ayns ymmyd elley yn ennym beisteig, lhaih beisteig (reddaghey).

Ta beishteigyn, beisteigyn ny shey-chassee (dy ghra myr shen, olteyryn y phossan Insecta) nyn bossan smoo ny h-arthropodyn.  T'ad jannoo magh possan dy vaaghyn smoo anchasley ny chruinney.  Ta ny smoo na millioon dy ghooie oc er nyn soilshaghey - ny smoo nah lieh jeh ooilley ny organeyn bioag as fys orroo ain - as t'ad credjal dy vel wheesh as 30 millioon elley ry-gheddyn foast, foddee.  Myr shen, foddee beishteigyn jannoo magh ny smoo na 90% jeh organeyn bioag ny cruinney.
