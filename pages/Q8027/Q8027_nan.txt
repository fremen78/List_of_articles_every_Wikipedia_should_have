Martin Luther King, Jr. (1929 nî 1 goe̍h 15 ji̍t – 1968 nî 4 goe̍h 4 ji̍t) sī Bí-kok ê bo̍k-su, mā sī siā-hoē ūn-tōng-chiá, Hui-chiu-hē Bí-kok-lâng ê bîn-khoân ūn-tōng léng-siù. I tī 1964 nî in-ūi tùi chéng-cho̍k chha-pia̍t ê hoán-khòng ūn-tōng, tit tio̍h Nobel Hô-pêng Chióng. 
I tī 1968 nî 4 goe̍h 4 ji̍t hōng chhì-sat sin-bông.
