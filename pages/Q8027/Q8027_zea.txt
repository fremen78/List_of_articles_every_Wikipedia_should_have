Martin Luther King (Atlanta (Georgia), 15 jannewari 1929 – Memphis (Tennessee), 4 april 1968), was 'n Amerikaonse doômenee en politiek leider. Um z'n speech I Have A Dream is stikke beroemd 'eworre. Op 4 april 1968 is King doôdgeschoten.
