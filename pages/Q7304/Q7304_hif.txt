Gustav Mahler (janam ke naam: Kalischt (abhi ke naam: Kali≈°te), Bohemia, 7 July 1860; maut Vienna, 18 May 1911) ek Czech-Austrian composer aur conductor rahaa. Uu Romantic period ke time ke sab se barraa composer rahaa. Uu nau [symphony likhis rahaa (daswa ke khalaa nai karaa gais rahaa. Uu German ke folk song ke bahut like karat rahaa. Uske work Das Lied von der Erde (English:The Song of the Earth) uske sab se achchhaa kaam hae. Uu ek bahut achchhaa conductor rahaa aur Vienna Opera ke banais rahaa.
