Anti UraqiPlantilla:Infobox mountain rangeAnti Uraqinakawja
Aka Anti uraqinakajax wali jach'a siqi qullunakapuniwa. Ukhamarak walja pat patanakaniwa. khaysa Kitu, Bogota, Arikipa, Medellin, Sucre, Merida, Chukiyawu uka markanakax anti uraqinakankiwa. Titiqaqa quta ukawj uraqinakax wali jaya patankapuniwa, yaqhax juk'amp patanx Tibetano ukakiw ukapachpax utji. Ukhamarak aka anti uraqinakax janiw thaya suni patankakiti, jan ukasti qhirwanakas junt'u uraqinakas utjarakiwa.
Aka Anti uraqix janw aka Tawantinsuyu ukawjankakiti, jan ukasti Awyayala uraqpachankiwa. Alayat qalltani ukatsti aynachkamaw puriraki.
