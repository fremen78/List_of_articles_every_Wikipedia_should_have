The Andes is the langest continental muntain range in the warld. It is a continual range o hielands alang the wastren coast o Sooth Americae. This range is aboot 7,000 km (4,300 mi) lang, about 200 km (120 mi) tae 700 km (430 mi) wide (widest atween 18° sooth an 20° sooth latitude), an o a average hicht o aboot 4,000 m (13,000 ft). The Andes extend frae north tae sooth throu seiven Sooth American kintras: Venezuela, Colombie, Ecuador, Peru, Bolivie, Chile, an Argentinae.
