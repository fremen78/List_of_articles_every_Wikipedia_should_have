Ua hānau ʻia ʻo Adolf Hitler ma ka lā 20 ʻApelila 1889 ma Braunau, ʻAukekulia a ua make ʻo ia ma ke ola ʻana ma ʻApelila 30, 1945 ma Berlin ʻo Chancellor o Kelemania mai 1933 a i 1945 a hoʻokumu i kahi dictatorhip totalitary, imperialist, anti-Semitic, racist a me xenophobic dictatorship i kapa ʻia ʻo ke Third Reich .
