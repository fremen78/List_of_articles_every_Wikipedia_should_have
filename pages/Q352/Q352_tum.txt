Adolf Hitler (German: [ˈad.ɔlf ˈhɪt.lɐ] ( 20 April 1889 – 30 April 1945) wakawa wandale uyo wakababikila ku Austria ndipo  kwake kukaŵa ku  Germany. Wakaŵa wakulamula chalo mu nkhongono zake kufuma 1933 mpaka kufwa kwake mu 1945.
Wakamanyikwa chomene chifukwa cha chipani icho wakayambiska cha Nazi. Iyo wakakomeska ŵayuda ŵanandi.
