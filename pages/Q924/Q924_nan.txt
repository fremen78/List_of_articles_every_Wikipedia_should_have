Tanzania Liân-ha̍p Kiōng-hô-Kok (Swahili-gí: Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania) tī Hui-chiu tang-pō͘. Pak ū Kenya kap Uganda 2 kok, sai ū Rwanda, Burundi kap Congo Bîn-chú Kiōng-hô-kok, lâm ū Zambia, Malawi kap Mozambique. Tang-hōaⁿ chiap Ìn-tō͘-iûⁿ.
Tī 1964 nî Tanganyika kap Zanzibar ha̍p-pèng chò 1 kok.
