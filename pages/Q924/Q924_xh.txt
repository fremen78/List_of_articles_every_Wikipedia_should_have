ITanzania, ngokusesikweni iRiphabhlikhi yoManyano yaseTanzania (isiSwahili: Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania), lilizwe eliseMpuma Afrika phakathi kommandla we-Afrika yeGreat Lakes. Umda waseUganda ukuya emantla; iKenya ukuya kumntla-mpuma; Iziqithi zaseComoro kunye noLwandlekazi lwaseIndiya ukuya ngasempuma; iMozambique neMalawi ukuya emazantsi; iZambia kumzantsi-ntshona; kunye neRwanda, iBurundi, neDemocratic Republic of the Congo ngasentshona. INtaba iKilimanjaro, eyona ntaba iphakamileyo eAfrika, ikumntla-mpuma weTanzania.
