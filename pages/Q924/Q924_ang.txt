Tansanie seo Geande Cynewise (Swahilisc: Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania) is rīce on middelre Ēastaffrice þe ligþ be Cenie and Ugandan benorðan hire, Rwandan, ge Burundi, ge Congoland bewestan hire, and Sambie, Malawan and Mosambican besuðan hire. Þæs rīces ēasternu mearcland licgaþ on þǣm Indie Gārsecge.
Tansanie is geāned cynewise þe stent on 26 landscipum. Se andwearda rīceshēafod is Samia Hassan Suluhu Foresittend. Siþþan þǣm 1996 gēare hæfð se Tansanie geseteda hēafodstōl gebēon Dodoma, þǣr se gemōt and sumu gewealdes ambehthūs standaþ. Betƿeox ānstandendness and þǣm 1996 gēare sēo hēafodlice rimlicgende burg Dar es Salaam hæfde gebēon þæs rīces geƿealdsciplic hēafodstōl. Hēr on þissum dagum is Dar es Salaam gīet sēo hēofodlicoste burg Tansanie and se wyrcenda stōl þæs mǣstan dǣles gewealdes gesetnessa. Hēo is se mǣsta sǣport þæs rīces and his landbundenra nēahlanda.
Se nama Tansania is wordgebundenness þara namena Tangangica and Sansibar. Þas tu ricu geānlǣhton on þǣm 1964 gēare tō macienne Tanganyice and Sansibares þa Geandan Cynewisan, þe on æfterran dæle ilcan gēares wæs genemned Tansanie þa Geandan Cynewisan.
