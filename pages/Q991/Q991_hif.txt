Fyodor Mikhaelovich Dostoevsky (Russian: Фёдор Миха́йлович Достое́вский, Fëdor Mihajlovič Dostoevskij,  (11 November 1821 – 9 February 1881) ek Russian [writer rahaa. Dher jane iske Russia ke ek bahut achchha writer maane hae.  Uske kaam 20th century ke wriitng pe bahut asar karis rahaa. Uske character logan garibi me rahat rahin aur uu logan ke dimaag chaahe ekdam achchhaa nai to ekdam kharaab rahaa.  Uske sab se achchhaa book aagae ke baare me batat rahaa.
