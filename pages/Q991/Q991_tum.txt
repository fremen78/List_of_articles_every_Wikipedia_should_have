Fyodor Mikhailovich Dostoevsky (Russian: pre-1918: ?? едоръ Михайловичъ Достоевский; after-1918: Фёдор Михайлович Достоевский, tr. Fyodor Mikháylovich Dostoyévskiy; 11 November 1821 - 9 February 1881), uyo nyengo zinyake wakuchemeka Dostoyevsky, wakaŵa mulembi wa mabuku wa ku Russia, wakulemba nkhani zicoko, mulembi wa nkhani zakudumura, na mulembi wa nyuzipepara. Mu mabuku ghake, Dostoevsky wakulongosora umo ŵanthu ŵakukhala mu nyengo ya masuzgo ya ndyali, ya umoyo wa ŵanthu, na yauzimu mu Russia. Mabuku ghake ghakumanyikwa chomene ni Crime and Punishment (1866), The Idiot (1869), Demons (1872), na The Brothers Karamazov (1880). Mu 1864 wakalemba buku lakuti Notes from Underground, ilo likuwoneka kuti ndilo likaŵa lakwamba mu mabuku ghakuyowoya vya umoyo. Ŵanthu ŵanandi awo ŵakususka mabuku ŵakumuwona kuti ni yumoza wa ŵanthu ŵakumanyikwa chomene awo ŵakalemba mabuku gha pa caru cose.
