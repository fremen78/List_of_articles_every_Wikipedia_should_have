Un material de plastico es alicun de un extension large de synthetic o semi-synthetic solidos organic usate in le manufactura de productos industrial. 
Plasticos es typicamente polymeros de alte massa molecular, e pote continer altere substantias de abonar comportamento o de reducer costos de production. 
Monomeros de plastico es compositos organic, o natural o synthetic.


== Etymologia ==
