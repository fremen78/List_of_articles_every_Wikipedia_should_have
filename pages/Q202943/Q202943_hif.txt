Brahmagupta  (c. 598 – c. 668 CE) ek Hindustani Mathematician aur Astronomer rahaa. Uu Mathematics aur Astronomy ke baare me likhis rahaa. Uu dui purana kaam  : the Brāhmasphuṭasiddhānta (BSS, "right se Brahma ke Sisharata", dated 628), ek theoretical treatise, aur Khaṇḍakhādyaka ("edible bite", dated 665), ke likhis rahaa.
628 me, Brahmagupta pahile gravity ke describe karis as an attractive force, aur term "gurutvākarṣaṇam (गुरुत्वाकर्षणम्)"  Sanskrit me kaam me laae ke iske describe karis
Brahmagupta ke quadratic formula (the solution of the quadratic equation) ke sab se pahile describe kare khatis jaana jaawe hae. in his main work, the Brāhma-sphuṭa-siddhānta.
