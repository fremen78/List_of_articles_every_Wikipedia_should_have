Bacteria (/bækˈtɪəriə/; singular: bacterium) constitute a muckle domain or kinrick o prokaryotic microorganisms. teepically a few micrometres in lenth, bacteria hae a wide range o shapes, rangin frae spheres tae rods an spirals. Bacteria inhabit sile, watter, acidic het springs, radioactive waste, an the deep portions o Yird's crust. Bacteria  an aa live in plants, ainimals (see symbiosis), an hae flourished in manned space vehicles.
