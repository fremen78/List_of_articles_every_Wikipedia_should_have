Saikolosi i save ol i lukluk long tingting bilong manmeri. Planti narapela kain long stadi lukluk tingting, olsem wanem pilosopi, theolosi, sosiolosi na moa. Tasol saikolosi i lukluk wantaim scientific method.
Saikolosi i kamap long tupela narapela stadi: pilosopi na save long laip. Bihain long manmeri i bin save samting inap long kru bilong het, neuron na pisiolosi bilong ol, manmeri i kamap inap stadim tingting wantaim saiens. Bipo, ol man bilong pilosopi i bin kamap long sampela theories tasol nogat saiens long em.


== Skul long saikolosi ==
Saikoanalisis (long ol pawa long tingting na effects long ol)
Functionalisim (hau sampela samting long mind halivim manmeri)
Behaviourisim (tingting we manmeri mas lukluk observable behaviors tasol)
Biosaikilosi (hau pisiolosi long bodi influensim manmeri)
Sosial saikolosi (hau manmeri igat sindaun long sosaiti wantaim narapela manmeri)
