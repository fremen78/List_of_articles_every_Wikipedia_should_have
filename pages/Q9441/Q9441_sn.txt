Buddha (623/563 BCE - 543/484 BCE) aive muzivi wechiIndia, meditator, mudzidzisi wezvemweya, uye mutungamiriri wechitendero. Iye anozivikanwa zvakare se Gautama Buddha, Siddhartha Gautama uye Lord Buddha. Ndiye aive muvambi wechiBuddha.
