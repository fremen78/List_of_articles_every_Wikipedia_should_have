Ezinụlọ Elizabeth Alms bụ nchịkọta ụlọ nkwakọba ihe anọ dị na Elizabeth Road, Worthing na 1860 site n'aka onye na-ewu ụlọ ụlọ William Burges .  Ọ bụ nna William, Alfred Burges, ekiri ụlọ ọrụ ebere ahụ maka izi ezi Alfred.  A na-edepụta ụlọ ahụ ọkwa nke abụọ


== Akụkọ ihe mere eme na ije ==
Alfred Burges bụ onye injinia obodo nke nnukwu akụ [1] site na ọrụ ya na James Walker maka ụlọ ọrụ injinia ha Messrs Walker na Burges .  [2] N'afọ 1860, o nyere nwa ya nwoke, bụ William, ọrụ ka o chepụta maka iwu ụlọ ọrụ ebere a na-enye onyinye Alfred.  [3] ahụ dị okpukpu abụọ n'ịdị elu ma jiri brik na-acha uhie uhie uhie.  [1] N'okpuru gablet dị n'etiti bụ ihe oyiyi St Elizabeth nke Hungary .  [5] ndị nkwari akụ ahụ bụ ụlọ a depụtara na ọkwa nke ịma, nke akọwara dị ka "ichepụta ihe atụ nke ụfọdụ akụ na aka".  [1] Burges bu n'obi ka "ụmụ dara ogbenye na-alụ osisi di, ndị na-eru afọ 50, ndị nwere ikike aha ọma ga-ebi n'ama ọrụ ebere".  Otu National Almshouses Association mechara bụrụ onye na-ahụ maka ha, na 1986 ha were ka a rụzigharị ya.  Otú ọ dị, ọ dịghị ego e nwere, a ịchụpụkwara ndị bi na ya.  Na 1992 Worthing Borough Council weghaara ụlọ ọrụ ebere ma rụzi ọrụ ha.  icon ahụ, kọmitii na-ahụ maka ebe obibi kpọchiri ha n'aka [6]
Ihe akara 1970 nke Sussex, ụzọ nke usoro ụlọ nke England, nke Ian Nairn na Nikolaus Pevsner dere, ekwughị okwu ụlọ ụlọ ọrụ alms, [7] ọ bụ ebe na Nairn, bụ onye dere ngalaba West Sussex, na-ihe dị mma.  , na-edekọ ya dị ka "obodo na-akpasu iwe (nke) ekiri site n'i ike Brighton wee ike site na i gosiri Bournemouth ".  [7] Ntuziaka (2019) ede nkọwa, Sussex: West, na-edekọ ụlọ ọrụ alms, ọ bụ ebe na ọ (na--eke ezi?) Na-riya William dị ka " nwanne nke onye nchoputa, Alfred Burges of Blackheath (London)  )"
