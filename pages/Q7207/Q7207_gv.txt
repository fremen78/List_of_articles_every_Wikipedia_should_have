Va Ealisaid I (7 Mean Fouyir 1533 – 24 Mart 1603) ny benrein Hostyn as Nerin veih 17 Mee Houney 1558 derrey laa e baaish. V'ee ny ree ny benrein jerrinagh y Reeraghtys Tiudaragh. Va ny h-enmyn Gloriana ny Good Queen Bess currit urree myrgeddin.
