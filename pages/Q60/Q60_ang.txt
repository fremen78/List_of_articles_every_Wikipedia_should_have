Sēo Burg Nīƿeoforƿīces is sēo mǣste burg þāra Geāndena Rīca American. Hiere burgealdor tōdæg is Eric Adams.
Tōforan þǣm 2001. gēare ƿæs Nīƿeoforƿīcbyrig (under manigum ōþrum landgemearcungum) ƿel gecūþ for þām tƿintorrum þæs World Trade Center, ac æfter þǣm 11. Hāligmōnðes þæs 2001. torngemōte þā hīe acƿealdon, biþ sēo burh ēac cūþ for þǣm Freedom Tower (Frēodōmtorr) þe nū under naman One World Trade Center fullmacod biþ.
