She peinteyr as jantagh prentyn Romansagh Spaainagh va Francisco José de Goya y Lucientes (30 Mayrnt 1746 – 16 Averil 1828). T'eh coontit myr y peinteyr Spaainagh smoo scanshoil 'sy 18oo as y 19oo eash. Ta e pheintalyn, e linneeaghtyn, as e ghrainnaghyn aahoilshaghey ny musthaaghyn shenndeeagh co-emshiragh as chur ad dy mooar stiagh er peinteyryn scanshoil elley y 19oo as y 20oo eash. T'eh currit sheese dy mennick er Goya dy nee eshyn y Shenn-vainshtyr jerrinagh as y chied mastey ny mainshtyryn noa-emshiragh.
