Makrraa (order Araneae) ek rakam ke aath gorr waala, aur saans le waala (air breathing) arthropod hae, jiske lage khaang (fang) rahe hae jisse ii jahar ke inject kare hae. Ii jaala (web) bhi banae hae. Arachnids me makrraa sab se jaada hae aur sab jiu me se 7th rank me hae.
Makrraa ke, Antartica ke chhorr ke, dunia ke sab continent me paawa jaae hae. November 2023 me taxonomist log 51,673 makrraa ke species, 136 family me record kare rahin.
