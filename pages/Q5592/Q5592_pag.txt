Si Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti (6 Marso 1475 – 18 Pebrero 1564) et sakey ya Italian Renaissance sculptor, painter, architect san poet, ya mas kabat ya Michelangelo. Lapur ed artistic versality to, dakel so mannununot ya sikato so perpekton ehemplo na Renaissance man.
Si Michelangelo et tampol a nagmaliw a bantog; duara ed saray kabkabat ton kimey, say Pietà tan David, et ginawa antis ya mantaon na talomplo. Anggaman agto impasen so inkasikato a sakey a pintor, si Michelangelo so nanggawa na duaran sankamaimpluensyan fresco ed awaran na Bokig ya art: saray eksena manlapud Genesis diad atep na Sistine Chapel ed Roma, tan Say Unor a Panangukom diad dingding na altar na satan. Say desinyo to ed Laurentian Library so nan-pioneer ed Mannerist architecture. Diad edad ton 71, sikatoy sinmalat ed si Antonio da Sangallo ya mas Ugaw bilang arkitekto na San Basilica nen Pedro. Impasimbalo nen Michelangelo so plano ta pian nasumpal so sagur a sampot unong ed desinyo to, a singa met ed dome, a wala so pigaran pananguman, kayari impatey to.
