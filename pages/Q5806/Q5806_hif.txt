Mecca (officially: Makkah al-Mukarramah; Arabic: مكة المكرمة‎), Saudi Arabia me ek city hae. Iske population 1,294,167 (2004 census) hae. Ii city Jeddah se 73 kilometres bhittar hae, Valley of Abraham me jon ki samundar se 277 metres uppar hae. Ii Red Sea se 81 kilometres duur hae. 
Makkah, Islam ke sab se jaada holy city hae. Har saal million ke log hian pe hajj karne  jaate hae.
