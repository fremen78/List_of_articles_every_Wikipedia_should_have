Ek Mahaataapu Ccontinent), dunia me jamin ke bhari jagha. Admii log ii bbat pe nai raaji hoe hae ki dunia me ketna continent hae lekin jaada log ii maane hae ki saat continent hae jiske naam hae:

Africa
Antarctica
Asia
Europe
North America
Australia
South America
Oceania ek jagha hae jisme Australia, New Zealand aur Pacific Islands hae. Jab ki Oceania ek continent nai hae iske ek continent ke roop main dekha jaae hae. Duusra jane iswas kare hae ki Australia apne aap ek continent hae.
Kuch log ii bhi biswas kare hae ki North America aur South America ek continent hae aur kuch log ii biswas kare hae ki Europe aur Asia ek continent Eurasia ke dui hissa hae. Jab ki Europe, Asia aur Africa jurra hae tab kuch log ii bhi biswas kare hae ki ii tiino ek continent Eurafrasia ek tiin hissa hae.
Jab British log "the Continent" (nai to "Continental") ke baare me baat kare hae, you logan ke matlab European mainland rahe hae.
