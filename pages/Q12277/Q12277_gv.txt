Ta aae, arc ny arçh çheet er strughtoor cruillit jeeragh ta goll tessen (y reaish) reaymys yrjit as foddee dy vel eh gymmyrkey yn trimmid er-e-skyn, ny ayns cooish aae chochruinnagh lheid as stangey aae, y broo ushtey-hassooagh ny noi.
Foddee yn fockle aae ve ymmydit chammah er son shamyryn lajer fo halloo chammah raad t'ad croo aae gyn scuirr as jannoo farvoalley. Va ny chied aaeyn ry-gheddyn cha leah as y nah villey-bleeaney RC ayns seyrnaght vreekey voish Mesopotamia, as hoshee yn ymmyd oardreilagh jeu 'sy Çhenn Raue.
