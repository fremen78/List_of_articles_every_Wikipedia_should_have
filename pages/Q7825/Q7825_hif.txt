World Trade Organization (WTO) kai desho ke ek sanstha hai. WTO 1995 me shuru kiya tha. 15 December 2005 ke 153 desh WTO ke member rahe. WTO me, desh ke biich me samjhaute  hue hai. “GATT” samjhauta ke matlab hai ki desh international trade ke bariers ke hatae hae . WTO ki staphna GATT ke naam se 1948 me hui thi jisme laghbhag 23 desh the 
GATT ka pura naam general agreement on tarrif and trades. Aaj ke samay WTO me kul 164 desh hai
