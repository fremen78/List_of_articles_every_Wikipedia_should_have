Heorte is lira on brēost manigra dēora. On mannum is sēo heorte on þǣre ƿynstran sīdan þæs brēostes. Sēo heorte hæfþ ānlicne liranspēn betƿeox lirena gecyndum - hire spēnas sind sƿīðe fæst.
Sēo heorte ƿyrceþ hefige on þǣre blōdes pæðes dǣlgæderunge.


== On folclicum þēaƿum ==
On folclicum þēaƿum tǣcneþ sēo heorte oft lufe.
