She banglane sheanse ta fishig (voish yn Ghreagish "φυσικός", najooragh, as "φύσις", najoor), as she sorçh d'oaylleeaght najooragh ee.  Ta fishig:

soilshaghey magh bun-chummey ny cruinney
soilshaghey magh eddyr-obbraghyn eddyr co-oltyn ny cruinney
oltscarrey coryssyn rere reillyn bunneydagh
Ta fishigee jannoo ymmyd mooar jeh maddaght dy hoilshaghey magh reillyn fishigagh.
Ta fishigee gra dy vel mass, liurid, traa, stroo lectragh as çhiassid ayn, as trooid ocsyn, foddee ad gobbraghey magh dy chooilley red fishigagh elley.
Ta mooinjerys eddyr fishig as ny h-oaylleeaghtyn najooragh elley, as kemmig er lheh, bentyn rish obbrinaght quantum, çhiass-niartallaght as magnaid-oaylleeaght lectragh.  Ny yei shen, ta ard-yindyssyn kemmigagh anchasley as crampit dy liooar dy chur kemmig myr red hene.
