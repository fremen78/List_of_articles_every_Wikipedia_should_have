Ek materialist uu hae jon ki ii biswas kare hae ki dunia material chij se khaali banaa hae. Sab chij material se banaa hae, aur jon koi chij nai banaa hae, jaise ki consciousness, bhi material ke kaaran hae. Iske matlab ii hae ki matter hi aslii chij hae.
Khaas kar ke, materialist log supernatural chij me biswas nai kare hae. Itihaas ke dher materialistic soche waala log aatma (spirit) ke bichaar ke koi matlab ke nai (meaningless), nai to vigyanik (scientific) ruup me saabit nai karaagais (unproven) maane hae.. Iske matlab hae ki materialism me biswas kare waala log atheist hae, lekin sab aethist materialist nai hae.
Aaj kal ke materialism me biswas kare waala log iske matlab ke barrhae ke aur khaas chij saamil (include) kare hae, jaise ki, urjaa (energy,) bal (force) aur antrikchh (space) ke curvature.
