Ludwig van Beethoven (i/ˈlʊdvɪɡ væn ˈbeɪˌtoʊvən/; bahasa Jerman: [ˈluːtvɪç fan ˈbeːtˌhoˑfn̩] baptis 17 Desember 1770 to Bonn, yilate 26 Maret 1827 to Wina) botiya tawu ngota komposer musik klasik lonto Jerman. Ilomataliyo u masahuru yito simfoni olimoliyo wawu otiyoliyo, wawu lahu lo piano Für Elise.
Tiyo ta otawa lotawu lowali tala ngota lo komponis uda'a wawu taapulu damango to delomo masa Zaman Klasik dan Zaman Romantik.
To'u onggo buboto, tiyo botiye pianis ta o bakat, masahuru wawu otawa lo tawu hikayawa to Wina, Austria, tambati to'utonu tiyo tola-tola. Dabo to taawunu 1801, tiyo ma didu boti mo'odungohu. Taawunu 1817 tiyo ma lowawi bungolo.
Openu didu mowali moyitohu to konser, tiyo layito hemohutu musik, wawu to masa botiye tiyo lohutu ilomataliyo u molamahu.
