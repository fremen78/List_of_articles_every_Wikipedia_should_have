Ethanol, jiske ethyl alcohol aur grain alcohol ke naam se bhi jaana jaawe hae,jon ki ek flammable, colorless chemical compound hae. Ii uu daaru hae jiske jaada alcoholic drinks me pawa jaawe hae. Iske chemical formula C2H5OH hae, aaur iske C2H6O bhi lkha jaawe hae.
