Ko rino (reo Hawaii: hao takea mai te kupu reo Marēo-Porinīhia i mua *paqet; reo Rātini: ferrum, Fe) he pūmotu. Ko te taungota, 26; ko te Takiwā, 4; ko te Rōpū, 8. 
Ko pūmotu kua whakaurua ki te mātau o te tangata whenua Aotearoa nō i hokohoko ngā whao ai te kahupapa Pākehā o Hēmi Kuki nō te tau 1770; kei te mōhio hoki te tangata Māori i tēnei rawa mai te pūrongo o te hokohoko waenga te tāngata Tahiti (me te kupu Tahiti: fao) me te ngā kauhoe o te waka Piritene HMS Dolphin i te tau 1767.
