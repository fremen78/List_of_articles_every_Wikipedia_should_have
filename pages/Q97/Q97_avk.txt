Atlantika Welfa tir dem divatce vas mon 92 000 000 km² ise tir tela toleafa logijafa welfa ke tawava.
Talteon inafa kima tir Amerika az 68° 04' taltewak kale Antarktika ; roneon kima tir Europa is Afrika is 20° ronewak kale Antarktika. Lenteon, kima do Arktika welfa tir wavidafa, wale Nunaata is Spitzberg tursia is Nord evilma valente Norga.
