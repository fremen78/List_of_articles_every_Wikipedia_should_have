Wumuti (nge. medicine; traditional & modern medicine) Vumuti kana Muti iguruzivo uye chiitwa chekuchengetedza varwere, kutariritsa mbondero, rumbondemberi, kudzivirira, kurapa, kuphaliyeta maronda ne zvirwere, uye kukurudziridza hutano hwe varwere. Wumuti hwunosanganisa tsika dzakasiyana-siyanao dzekuchengetedza hutano, kubira uye kupinda mukuchengetedza, kudzosedzera, kudzivirira uye kurapa zvirwere. 
Wumuti unonzi unokwanisa kusanganisa futi chidhakiso.
