Medicin is the science that deals wi diseases in humans, the best weys tae hinder diseases, an the best weys tae mak a body heal an hail.
Fowk that practices medicine is maist aften creid doacters/ mediciners/ physeecians or surgeons. For their wark, mediciners is gien a haund by nourices an mony ither kin o health care perfaisionals.
