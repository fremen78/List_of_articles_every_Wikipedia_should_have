Philippines ek 7,000 se jaada island waala des hae. Ii Pacific Ocean me Asia se 100 kilometers southeast me hae. Spain (1521-1898) aur United States (1898-1946), ii des pe raaj karin rahaa. Philippines, South Korea, aur East Timor, East Asia ke uu des hae jahan pe jaada log Christian hae.
Philippine Islands ke east me Pacific Ocean, west me South China Sea, aur south me Celebes Sea hae .
